 dakshin dhruv-ataken ghati chandramaa ke vimukh falak par sthit ek vishaal prahaar cretar he. takreeban 2,500 kimi chaudaa or 13 kimi gaharaa, yah soramandal ke gyaat sabase bade prahaar cretaron main se ek he. chandramaa par manyata praapt yah sabase badi, sabase puraani or sabase gahari ghati he. yah vimukh falak kii do rachanaaon par namit hua tha; uttari chor par sthit cretar ataken evam duusare chor par sthit dakshini chandra dhruv. is ghati kii bahya paridhi chandramaa ke dakshini bhujaa par sthit pahaado kii ek vishaal shrinkhalaa ke rup main prithvi se dekhi ja sakati he, jise kabhi "libenitz parvat" kaha gaya, haalanki yah naam antarrashtriya khagoleey sangh dwaara aadhikarik taur par nahin mana gaya he.
