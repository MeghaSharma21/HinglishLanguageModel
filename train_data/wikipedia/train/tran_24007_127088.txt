 swat ek pakistan ke uttar pashchimi seemaa praant kaa ek jila evam sundar ghati he. yah islamabad se 160 kimi kii duuri par sthit he. saidu sharif yahan kii rajdhani he kintu mingora yahan kaa mukhya nagar he. is ghati kii sundarata ko dekhte hue ise pakistan kaa "switzraland" bhi kaha jaataa he.
 disambar 2008 main iske adhikansh bhaag par taliban kaa kabja ho gaya. uske baad talibanon ke dabaav main aakar pakistan sarkaar ko yahan sharia lagu karne kii maang manani padi. is samay paryaton ke liye yayaan janaa bahut khatarnaak he.
