 yah 15 agast 1947 ko bharat kii swatantraaa ke baad se punjab rajya ke rajyapalon kii ek suchi he. 1985 se punjab ke rajyapalon ne chandigarh ke prashaasak ke rup main bhi kaary kiya he. punjab ke pehle rajyapal chandoolal madhavlal trivedi rahe hai jinhone 15 agast 1947 se 11 march 1953 tak kaarybhaar sambhala tha. vartmaan main punjab ke rajyapal ve pi singh badnaur he jo 22 agast 2016 se kaarybhaar sambhaal rahe hai.
