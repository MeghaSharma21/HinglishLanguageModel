 shamsher bahadur pratham  uttari bharat main kalapi or banda ke ek maratha shaasha the. vah peshwa bajirao pratham or unki duusari patni mastaani ke putr the.
 bundelkhand ke hindu raja chhatrasaal or unki ek farsi muslim patni ruhani bai kii beti mastaani se peshwa bajirao pratham ne shaadi kii. peshwa parivaar or pune ke brahmanon ne is shaadi ko sweekaar nahin kiya. unke putr the shamsher bahadur urf krishna raav jinaki shiksha or hathiyaaronkaa prashikshan bajirao ke baaki putron ke saath hua jo unki pehali patni kashibai ke the. 1740 main bajirao or mastaani kii mrityu ke baad kashibai ne hi shamsher kaa sangrakshan kiya.
 raja chhatrasaal se praapt kalapi or banda, kii jaagir shamsher bahadur ko pradaan kar di gayi thi. us vakt is jaagir se salanaa 40 lakh rupayon kii arthprapti hoti thi.
 shamsher bahadur ke vanshajon ne kalapi or banda kii jaagir par 1816 tak rajya kiya or 1817 main yahan british raaj ne kabja kar liya. tab britishon ne is rajya ke shaasha kii vaarshik pension 4 lakh rupee kar di thi.
