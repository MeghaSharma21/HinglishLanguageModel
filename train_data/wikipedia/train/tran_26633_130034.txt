 great andaman bharat ke andaman dweepasmooh kii pramukh dweepashrunkhalaa he. isamen paanch bade dweep aate hai jo uttar se dakshin kii ore hai:
 inamen se teen uttaravarti dweep is dweepasmooh ke sabase bade dwipon main se hai. dweepasmooh kii rajdhani port blair bhi isi dweepashrunkhalaa main aati he. isi andaman dweepasmooh kii duusari shrunkhlaa little andaman ko iskaa samakaksh poorak dweep bhi kah diya jaataa he.
