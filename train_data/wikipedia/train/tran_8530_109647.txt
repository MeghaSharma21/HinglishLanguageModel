 jayee ek fasal he. iskaa upyog anaaj, pashuon ke dane tathaa hare chaare ke liye hota he.
 bharat main jayee kii jatiyaan mukhyat: aivana sativa tathaa aivana sterilis vansh kii hai. yah bharat ke uttari bhaagon main utpann hoti hai. 
 jayee kii kheti ke liye sheeghra pakanevaali kharif kii fasal kaatane ke baad chaar-paanch jotaaiyaan karke, 125-150 man gobar kii khaad prati acre deni chahie. oktober-november main 40 ser prati acre kii dar se beej bona chahie. isaki do baar sinchai kii jaati he. hare chaare ke liye do baar kataai, janvari ke anrabh tathaa farvari main, kii jaati he. duusari sinchai pratham chaare kii kataai ke baad kerani chahie. hare chaare kii upaj 200-250 man tathaa dane kii 15-20 man prati acre hoti he.
