 kalpanaath raay uttar pradesh ke ghosi loksabhaa se chaar baar saansad chune jane vaale or teen baar rajya sabha main congress party ke rajya sabha sadsy chune jane vaale raajnneetijna the. iske alawa ve congress sarkaar main kai baar kendriya mantri bhi rahe.
 kalpanaath raay kaa janm uttar pradesh ke mau jile ke simarijmalpur naamak gaanv main 4 janvari 1941 ko hua tha. or unki shiksha-diksha gorakhpur vishvavidhyalay main hui. vishvidhyalay jivan main hi unhone chaatr netaa ke rup main apane rajnatik jivan kaa aarambh kiya or samaajawaadee yuvajan sabha ke sadsy or rahe or 1963 main iske jeneral secretary chune gayi. 
 raay, indira gandhi or narsinghraav sarkaron main mantri rahe. raav sarkaar main 1993-1994 main ve khaadya mantralay main rajyamantri the jab chini ghotaale main unka naam aayaa or unhen tihaad jail janaa padaa. 1996 ke chunaavon main ve tihaad jail main rahete hue hi ghosi se lok sabha ke liye nirdal pratyaashee ke rup main vijayi hue. apana aakhri chunaav unhone samta party se ladaa or isamen bhi ve vijayi rahe.
 6 agast 1999 ko raay kii mrity hridayaghat se hui. unki safal rajnatik viraasat ko unke putr or patni sudhaa raay ke antardvand kii bhent chadh janaa padaa.
