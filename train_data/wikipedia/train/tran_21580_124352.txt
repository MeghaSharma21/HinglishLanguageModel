 puraani turki bhashaa, jise orphon turki or puraani uigur bhi kehete hai, turki bhashaaon kaa sabase purana pramanit rup he. yah 7veen se 13veen sadi isvi ke goekaturk or uigur likhaaiyon main miltaa he. yah turki bhashaaon main dakshinpurvi shakha kii sadsy maani jaati he jisaki aadhunik sadsy uigur bhashaa or chaghtai bhashaa hai. yah bahut si lipiyon main likhi jaati thi, jaise kii runi lipi se dekhne main milane vaali puraani turki lipi, bharat se utpann hui brahmi lipi, sonda se mili ek lipi se viksit hui pracheen uigur lipi, ityadi.
