 fidrik august kekule vikhyath rasayanashastri. san 1850 se lekar mrityuparyant ve europe ke sabase mahaan rasayanashastri rahe. rasinic sanrachana kaa siddhanth mukhyatah unki hi dein he. kekule dwaara prastut benzin sanrachana sambandhi siddhant carbonic rasayan kii mahatvapurn kadi thi.
 iskaa janm darmastat  main 7 sintabar, 1829 i. ko hua tha. uskaa vichaar shilpi banane kaa tha, kintu gissen main shilpakala kaa adhyayan karte samay uskaa sampark tatkaaleen prasiddh rasayanajna livikh se hua. unhone kekule kii ruchi rasayan ke prati aakarshith kii. unki prerana par kekule paris aayaa or usne reno, fremi or burtj ke vyaakhyaan sune or jheraar se uski mitrataa hui. pashchaat vah switzaraland or england gaya or vahaan ke prasiddh rasayanagyon ke sampark main aayaa. germany lautne par usne hidelburg main ek choti si prayogshaala sthapit kii. 1858 i. main ghent tathaa 1865 i. main bon vishvavidhyalay main rasayan kaa adhyaaapak rahaa. 13 june 1896 i. ko baan main uski mrityu hui.
 carbon kii sanyojaktaa par francland aadi jo kaary kar rahe the, usamen kekule ne bhi yoga diya. 1858 i. main carbon kii chatu:sanyojaktaa ke aadhaar par parmaanuon ke sangyojan ko samajhane kaa praytn inhone kiya tathaa sanvruth or vivruth shrinkhalaa ke yogicon kii kalpana pehali baar prastut kii. isi silsile main inhone benazin kii prastaavna kii. yah kaary itane mahatv kaa tha ki professor jap ne, jinhone kekule kii mrityu par landan chemical sosayadi main san 1897 main bhaashan diya tha, kaha ki carbonic rasayan kaa teen chauthai bhaag pratyaksh rup se, yaa paroksh rup se, kekule ke benzin sanrachana sambandhi vichaaron or pariklpanaaon kaa rini he. 
 kekule dwaara prastut benazin sanrachana sambandhi siddhant hamaari sahaayta na karata to koltar se sambandh rakhanevaale sahasron upyogi yogicon kii sambhaavna bhi nahin prathit hui hoti.
