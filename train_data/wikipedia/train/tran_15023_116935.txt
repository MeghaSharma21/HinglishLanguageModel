 anwar jahir jamaali pakistan ke mukhya nyaayadhish hai jinhone 10 sitambar 2015 ko nyaayadhish pad kaa shapath uthaaya. anwar jahir 1951 ko hyderabad, pakistan main paidaa hue. aapke purvajon kaa sambandh bharat ke shahar jaipur se tha. apane parivaar ek dharmik gharaana he, jiskaa silsilaa vansh qutubuddin ahamed hansi se ja miltaa he. aap ginati un mansanin main hota he jo parvaiz musharraf ke daur main pisio ke tahat shapath lene se inkaar kar diya tha.
