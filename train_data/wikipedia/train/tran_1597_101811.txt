 vindhya pradesh, bharat kaa ek bhootpurv pradesh tha jiskaa kshetrafal 23,603 varg mile tha. bharat kii swatantrata ke baad central india agency ke purvi bhaag ke riyasaton ko milakar 1948 main is rajya kaa nirmaan kiya gaya tha. is rajya kii rajdhani reva thi. iske uttar main uttar pradesh evam dakshin main madhya pradesh tha. 
 vindhyas kshetra paaramparik rup se vindhyachal parvat ke aasapaas kaa pathari bhaag ko kaha jaataa he.
 1948 main bharat kii swatantraaa ke baad madhya pradesh tathaa uttar pradesh main sthit kuch riyasaton ko milakar vindhyapradesh kii rachana kii gai thi. isamen bhootpurv rivaan riyasat kaa ek bada hissaa, baghelkhand, bundelkhand aadi the. isaki rajdhani rivaan thi. iske uttar main uttar pradesh tathaa dakshin main madhya pradesh tathaa datia raaj tha. 1 navambar 1956 ko ye sab milakar madhyapradesh bana die gaye the. yah kshetra saanskrithik drushti se atyant mahatvapurn samajha jaataa he. vindhyas pradesh ke pratham mukhyamantri shri pan shambhunath shukla ji the, jo shadol ke rahane vaale the. unke naam par shadol main bada shaskeey mahavidhyalay he or reva vishvavidhyalay kaa saanskrithik haal bhi unheen ke naam par he. rajdhani sthit mantralay main bhi shri shukla ji ke naam par kai kaksh sthapit he.
