 takneekee rup se kaha jaay to nunatam varg kii vidhi kisi atinishchit tantra kaa lagbhag hal nikaalane ke liye upyog main laayi jaati he. duusare shabdon main, aise samikarnon kaa tantra jahaan samikarnon kii sankhya agyaat raashiyon kii sankhya se bhi adhik ho vahaan yah vidhi ek 'lagbhag hal' nikaalane main sahaayta karti he.
 nunatam varg kii vidhi ko ek alag tareeke se bhi dekha ja saktaa he - diye hue aankadon par koi vakr feet karnaa. isaliye yah 'karv-fitting' ke liye bahutayat main upyog kii jaati he.
