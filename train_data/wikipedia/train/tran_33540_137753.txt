 nalini bala devi asamiya bhashaa kii prasiddh kavyitri thim. ve apni rashtravaadi tathaa rahasywaadee kavita ke liye prasiddh hai. unke saahitiyik yogadan ke liye bharat sarkaar ne 1957 main unhen padmashri se sammanit kiya tathaa 1968 main unki kavyakruti alakananda ke liye unhen sahitya academy puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya.
 pati kii akaal mrityu par paanch santaanon ke saath kashtpurna jivan bitaana padaa. iske baad durbhagyabash unke do putron kii bhi akaal mrityu ho gayi. is vednaa se jarjar unhone apana jivan ishwar ko arpit kar diya. unhone apana samay veda, gita, upanishad or bhagwat puraana bake adhyayan main lagaaya. shishav main ankurit supt pratibha ab jaagrit ho uthi. vivaah ke samay unke shvasur ne unako ek swarna kalam uphaar diya tha. baad main unhone us kalam kaa maan rakhaa. pita ke upadesh or prerana ne unke jivan yuddh main unki vijay sunishchit kii. maatr das varsh kii aayu main 'pita' naamak kavitaar se unhone kavi jivan main pravesh liya.
