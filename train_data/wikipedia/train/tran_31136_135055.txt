 bhed kaa manushya se sambandh aadi kaal se he tathaa bhed paalan ek pracheen vyavsaay he. bhed paalak bhed se oon tathaa maance to praapt karata hi he, bhed kii khaad bhoomi ko bhi adhik upjaau banaati he. bhed krishi-ayogy bhoomi main charati he, kai kharpatvaar aadi anaavashyak ghaason kaa upyog karti he tathaa unchai par sthit charaagaah joki anya pashuon ke ayogy he, uskaa upyog karti he. bhed paalak bhedon se prati varsh memne praapt karte hai.
 sanyukt raashtra khaadya or krishi sanghathan ke databes ke anusaar, bhed ke pramukh sheersh paanch desh: mukhya bhoomi china, australia, bharat, iran, or purv sudan .
 2013 main, bhed sankhya ke pramukh paanch deshon main mukhya bhoomi china, australia, bharat, purv sudan, or iran the. 2013 main, bhedon kii sankhya is prakaar thi : asia main 44%, africa main 28.2%; europe main 11.2%, oshinia main 9.1%, america main 7.4%.
 bhed ke maance ke sheersh utpaadak is prakaar the: mukhya bhoomi china ; australia, newjiland, united kingdom, or turki . 2013 main bhed maance ke sheersh paanch utpaadak mukhya bhoomi china, australia, newjiland, purv sudan, or turki the.
