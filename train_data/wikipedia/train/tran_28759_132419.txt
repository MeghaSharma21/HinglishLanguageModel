 bharat main kai tithiyaan shahid diwas ke rup main manaayi jaatin hai, jinamein mukhya hai- 30 janvari, 23 march, 21 oktober, 17 navambar tathaa 19 navambar.
 30 janvari 1948 ko mahatma gandhi kii hatya hui thi.
 23 march 1931 ke din angrejon ne shahid bhagat singh, raajaguru or sukhdev ko faansee par lataka diya gaya tha.
 bhagat singh ek jat sikh parivaar se the.
 19 navambar rani lakshmibai kaa janmdin he.
