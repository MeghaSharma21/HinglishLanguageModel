 isaki sthapana 1997 main agrahari masala udyog ke binar tale kiya gaya tha. iskaa mukhyalay amethi, uttar pradesh main hai.
 rajesh masala ke diarector rajesh agrahari jo swayan ek samaajawaadee party rajnatik dal ke netaa the, unhen mukhyamantri akhilesh yadav ne anushaasan hinata tathaa party viruddh gatividhiyon main sanlipt hone ke kaaran unhen june 2014 main samaajawaadee party se 6 saal ke liye nishkashit kar diya.
