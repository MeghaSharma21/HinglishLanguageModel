 yah beesvin sadi ke aarambh main ubharne vaale ek jati aadhaarit andolan ko sanchalit karne ke liye nirmit sanghathan thi. madras main 1915-1916 ke aasapaas manzoli jatiyon kii ore se si.en. muliyaar, tii. en, nair or pi. tyagaraj chetty ne jastise andolan kii sthapana kii thi. in manzoli jatiyon main tamil vallaal, mudalial or chettiar pramukh the. inke saath hi isamen telegu reddy, kamma, baleechaa naidu or malayalam nair bhi sammalit the. inamen anek bhuswami or samrudh vyapaaree the jinhen shiksha, sena evam raajnneeti ke kshetra main brahmanon kaa varchasv dekhakar irshyaa hoti thi. brahman madras prasidency main 3.2% the kintu 1912 main 55% dipti collector, 72.6% jila munsif brahman hi the. justise party ko rajbhakti ke badale main apane sadasyon ke liye nai naukariyaan or nai gatividhiyon main adhik pratinidhitiv kii aashaa thi.
