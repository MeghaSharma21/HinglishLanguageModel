 george washington  sanyukt rajya america ke pehle raashtrapati the. unhone america sena kaa netritv karte hue britain ke upar amareeki kranti main vijay hasil kii. unhen 1789 main america kaa pehla raashtrapati chunaa gaya.
 aaj bhi america main unke naam kaa sikaa chalata he.
 unki mata kaa naam mairi ball or pita kaa naam augustin washington tha. ve dono ek sthaneey vishvavidhyalay main shikshak the. bataur balak, washington ne bahut lambey samay tak kisi vidhyalay main pravesh nahin liya.
 jorge washington ke bare main ek prachlit lekin juthi kahani he ki ek baar unhone bachapan main apane pita ke cheri vriksh ko kaat diya. jab unke pita ne poocha to unhone jhooth nahin kaha or sach-sach bataa kii ped unhone hi kaata he. yah kahani yah bataane ke liye bataai or kahi jaati he ki washington kitne eemaandaar the. lekin mazedaar baat ye hai ki yah kahani eemaandaar nahin he or yah parcen vims dwaara gadhi gai thi.
