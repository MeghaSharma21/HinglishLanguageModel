 awa yaa inva yaa ratanpur myanmar rajya kii pracheen rajdhani he jo iravadi nadi par sagang nagar ke sammukh vipareet kinare par sthit he. iskaa pracheen naam yadanpur, arthath 'bahumoolya pattharon kaa nagar' he.
 awa, burma ke mandle kshetra main sthit ek nagar he jo 14veen se 19veen shatabdi tak burma ke pracheen raajaaon kii rajdhani hua karata tha. itihas main yah nagar kai baar ujadaa or phis basaa. 1839 main ek ke baad ek kai bhucamp aane se yah nagar purnatah nasht ho gaya jisase rajdhani ke rup main iskaa parityag kar diya gaya. yadhyapi aaj iske pracheen gaurav ke bahut kam chihn bache hai kintu yah burma kaa pramukh paryaton sthal he.
 is nagar kii sthapana dhwast pagaan nagar ke uttaradhikari nagar ke rup main 1364 i. main thadomin paaya dwaara hui thi. yahan nirmit anek dharmik bhawan pagaan sthit dharmik bhawanon ke hi samaan hai. awa nagar lagbhag chaar shatabdiyon tak rajkeey centre tha. is kaal main 30 shaskaon dwaara rajsinghaasana sushobhit hua. 1839 i. ke bukamp main nagar khandhar ho gaya. parishad bhawan or rajkeey bhawan ke kuch bhaagon ke avshesh ab bhi vidyaman hai. adhikansh dharmik bhawan dhwast avasthaa main hai.
