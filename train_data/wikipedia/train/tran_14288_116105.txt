 sindhu kala ek prasiddh pracheen bhartiya kala he. yah sarvamaanya he ki sindhu sabhyataa sarvatha swadeshi he. hadappa se praapt nartki kii kaanse kii murti tathaa laal pathar kii ek murti kaa dhad, mohenjodaron se praapt bail tathaa bandaron kii akritiyaan aadi is kala kii sabase mahatvapurn kalakritiyaan hai. in kritayon main jis kala kaa parichay miltaa he, vah saili kii shashakt swaabhaaviktaa, akruti ke vibhinn anupaaton ke utkrisht gyaan or harsh evam ullas kii aisi bhaawanaaon se paripurna he jinka anubhav jivan ke spandansheel kshetra main hi ho saktaa he. is kaal kii kalakritiyon main anubhav tathaa kaushal kii paryaapt paripakvata kaa parichay miltaa he. kuch vidwaanon kaa vichaar he ki sindhu sabhyataa tathaa arya sabhyataa kuch samay tak sahvasini raheen or paraspar mail se fali phulin.
