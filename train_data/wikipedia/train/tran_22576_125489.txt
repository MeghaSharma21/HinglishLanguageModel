 aavriti modulation  modulan kaa ek prakaar jahaan ke vaahak kii aavriti ke anusaar is modulatting sanket ke saath vividh he. is vaahak kaa aayam sthir bani hui he. yah jaankaree sanket asar ko vaahak ke taatkaalik aavriti parivartan. chunki aayam rakhaa he nirantar, efem modulan ek kam shor prakriya he or jo sangeet or hai main bhaashan-vishwastata prasaaran ke liye istemaal kiya jaataa he ek ucch gunvattta modulan takanic pradaan karata he.
 hai ke alaava-vishwastata radio prasaaran main, efem takanic audio sanshleshan or kam virupan ke saath ek video sanket ke luminance bhaag recording jaise anya mahatvapurn upabhokta anuprayogon ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he. vahaan ki ek VCO yaa ek muqaabala newnaadhik jaise efem sanketon, utpaadan main sakasham hai kai upkarnon rahe hai.enFrequency modulation
