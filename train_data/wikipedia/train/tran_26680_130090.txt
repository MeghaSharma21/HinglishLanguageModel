 kidney stone yaa gurde kii pathari  gurde evam mutranalika kii beemaari he jisamen, vrikk ke andar chote-chote yaa bade pathar kaa nirmaan hota he. gurdein main ek samay main ek yaa adhik pathari ho sakati he. saamanyat: ye pathariyaan agar choti ho to bina kisi takalif mutrmarg se sharir se baahar nikaal di jaati hai, kintu yadi ye paryaapt rup se badi ho jaaen to ye mutrvahini main avrodh utpann kar sakati hai. is sthiti main mutrango evam qamar or pet ke aasapaas asahneeya pidaa hoti he jise renal colic kaha jaataa he . 
 yah sthiti aamtaur se 30 se 60 varsh ke aayu ke vyaktiyon main pai jaati he or striyon kii apeksha purushon main chaar guna adhik pai jaati he. bacchon or vridhon main mutrashay kii pathari zyada banati he, jabaki vayasko main adhiktar gurdo or mutrvahak nalee main pathari ban jaati he. aaj bharat ke prayek sau pariwaron main se das parivaar is peedaadaayak sthiti se peedit he, lekin sabase du:khad baat yah he ki inamen se kuch pratishat rogi hi iskaa ilaaj karavaate hai or log is asahneeya pidaa se guzarate he.evam ashmari se peedit rogi ko kaafii maatra main pani pinaa chahie.
 jin marijon ko madhumeh kii beemaari he unhen gurde kii beemaari hone kii kaaphi sambhaavnaaen rahati hai. agar kisi marij ko raktchaap kii beemaari he to use niyamit dawa se raktchaap ko niyantran karne par dhyaan dena chahie kyonki agar raktchaap badhata he, to bhi gurde kharaab ho sakate hai.
 kisi padaarth ke kaaran jab mutr sandra ho jaataa he to pathari nirmit hone lagati he. is padaarth main chote chote dane banate hai jo baad main pathari main tabdeel ho jate he. iske lakshan jab tak dikhaai nahin dete tab tak ye mutrmarg main badhane lagate he or dard hone lagata he. isamen kaaphi tej dard hota he jo baaju se shuru hokar uru mul tak badhata he.
 peeth ke nichle hisse main athwa pet ke nichle bhaag main achaanak tej dard, jo pet va jaangh ke sandhi kshetra tak jaataa he. dard fail saktaa he yaa baaju, shroni, uru mul, guptango tak bad saktaa he, yah dard kuch minto yaa ghanto tak bana rahata he tathaa bich-bich main aaraam miltaa he. dardo ke saath ji michalaane tathaa ulti hone kii shikaayat bhiho sakati he. yadi mutr sambandhi pranali ke kisi bhaag main sankraman he to iske lakshanon main bukhaar, kanpakanpi, pasinaa aana, peshaab aane ke saath-saath dard hona aadi bhi shaamil ho sakate hai ; baar baar or ekadam se peshaab aana, ruk ruk kar peshaab aana, raat main adhik peshaab aana, mutr main rakt bhi aa saktaa he. andakoshon main dard, peshaab kaa rang asaamaanya hona.
 gurde kii pathari ke zyaadaatar rogi peeth se pet kii taraf aate bhayankar dard kii shikaayat karte hai. yah dard rah-rah kar uthta he or kuch minto se kai ghanto tak bana rahata he ise ”reelan chronin” kehete hai. yah rog kaa pramukh lakshan he, isamen mutrvahak nalee kii pathari main dardo peeth ke nichle hisse se uthakar jaanghon kii ore jaataa he.
 gurde kii pathari ke nidaan ke liye sabase pehle sampurna jaanch zaruri he. 
 jaanch ke kai radiology tests uplabdh he. ultrasound jaanch sabase pehle karte he jo kaaphi jaankaree pradaan karaati he. 
 agar ultrasound par pathari dikhti he yaa phir gurde main sujan milti he to phir XRAY yaa rangeen XRAY kia jaataa he jise IVP bolte he. rangeen dawa yaa contrast dene se pehle kidney kaa function jaanchana jaruri he. 
 aajkal CT scan ne ultrasound or IVP kii jagah le lee he. 
 jaanche purna hone par pathari ke kai tarah ke ilaaj sambhav he.
