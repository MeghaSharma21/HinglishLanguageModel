 sar ian terrence botham england kii cricket team ke purv captain or vartmaan main cricket camentator he. vah harfanmaulaa ke rup main khelte the. apane prakhyat par kabhi-kabhi vivaadaaspad cricket kariyar main unhone kai record sthapit kiye or atyant achche pradarshan kiye. vah apane samay ke chaar mahaan ol-roundar main se ek the .
 1977 main apane test kariyar kii shuruyaat karne vaale ian ne apane pehle 11 test maichon main 8 baar pari main paanch wicket liye the or teen shatak lagaae the. unhone 19 test main 100 wicket or 41 test main 200 wicket liye the. 1980 main bharat ke khilaaf ek test match main unhone pehali pari main shatak lagaaya or 6 wicket liye. agali pari main unhone 7 wicket or liye. falswaroop wo ek hi test main shatak lagane or 10 wicket lene vaale pehle khilaadi bane .
 1980 main captain banaae jane par unki form bilkul gir gai. 1981 kii ashes shrinkhalaa ke bich main captaini chhodane ke baad agle hi test main vah form main aa gaye. unhone series kaa aisa rukh badla kii us test ko botham test or shrinkhalaa ko botham ashes kaha jaataa he. praarambhik 51 test main unhone 38.80 kii ausat se 11 shatak lagaakar 2,833 ran banaae or 23.06 kii ausat se 231 wicket liye jisamen 19 baar pari main paanch wicket shaamil he. haalanki, vahaan se unke kaushal main dhire-dhire giravat ai. 1985 kii ashes ko chhodkar unka pradarshan ausat rahaa.
 kul milakar botham ne 1977 se 1992 tak 102 test khelen jisamen unhone 14 shatak kii madad se 5,200 ran banaae or 28.40 kii ausat se 383 wicket liye jisamen 27 baar pari main paanch wicket shaamil he. 1986 se 1988 tak unke paas test cricket main sabase zyada wicket lene kaa record tha . unhone 1976 se 1992 tak 116 ek divasiy antarrashtriya bhi khelen jisamen unhone 2,113 ran banaae or 145 wicket liye. vah 1979 cricket vishwa cap or 1992 cricket vishwa cap ke final main khelen the.
