 
 alsi yaa tisi samshitoshna pradeshon kaa paudha he. reshedaar fasalon main iskaa mahatvapurn sthaan he. iske reshe se mote kapade, dori, rassi or taat banaae jate hai. iske beej se tel nikaala jaataa he or tel kaa prayog varnish, rang, sabun, rogan, pent taiyaar karne main kiya jaataa he. china san kaa sabase bada utpaadak desh he. reshe ke liye san ko upjane vaale deshon main rus, poland, nederland, france, china tathaa belgium pramukh hai or beej nikaalane vaale deshon main bharat, sanyukt rajya america tathaa argentina ke naam ullekhneey hai. san ke pramukh niryaatak rus, belgium tathaa argentina hai.
 tisi bharatvarsh main bhi paidaa hoti he. laal, shwet tathaa dhoosar rang ke bhed se isaki teen upjatiyaan hai iske paudhe do yaa dhai foot unche, daaliyaan bandhati hai, jinamein beej rahata he. in bijon se tel nikalataa he, jisamen yah gun hota he ki vaayu ke sampark main rahane ke kuch samay main yah thos avasthaa main parivartit ho jaataa he. visheshkar jab ise vishesh rasinic padaartho ke saath ublaa diya jaataa he. tab yah kriya bahut sheeghra puri hoti he. isi kaaran alsi kaa tel rang, varanish or chhapane kii syaahi banane ke kaam aata he. is paudhe ke enthlon se ek prakaar kaa resha praapt hota he jisko nirangkar linen banaaya jaataa he. tel nikaalane ke baad bachi hui sithi ko khali kehete hai jo gaay tathaa bhens ko badi priy hoti he. isse bahudhaa poultice banaai jaati he.
 ayurveda main alsi ko mandgandhyukt, madhur, balkaarak, kinchit kafavaat-kaarak, pittanashaka, snigdh, pachne main baari, garam, paushtik, kamoddeepak, peeth ke dard ore sujan ko mitaanevaali kaha gaya he. garam pani main daalkar keval bijon kaa yaa iske saath ek tihaai bhaag mulethi kaa churna milakar, kwath banaaya jaataa he, jo raktaatisar or mutr sambandhi rogon main upyogi kaha gaya he.
