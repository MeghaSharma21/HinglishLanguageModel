 butwal, dakshin-pashchim nepal ke pahari va tarai kshetra ke bich kaa pramukh shahar he. bharat kii taraf se sadak maarg se nepal jane ke liye isi kasbe se hokar pravesh karnaa padataa he. at: ise nepal ke ek praveshdwar jaisaa maan sakate hai.
 yah shahar tinau nadi ke kinare sthit he. butwal, lumbini anchal kaa mukaam hone ke ke alaava is kshetra kaa sabase bada vaanijya kendra bhi he. angrajon ke saath nepal kii ladaai main jeet kaa pratik jitgadhi kila is shahar ke upar kii chotii par sthit he. butwal, nepal ke rupandehi jile main aata he. yahan se bhairahava 22 kimi tathaa kathmandu lagbhag 250 kimi dur he. 
 yahan 'butwal bahumukhi campus', 'tilotama bahumukhi kyampus' va 'wootwal vanijya kyampus' ke alaava bahut se ucch madhyaamik vidhyalay, madhyamik vidhyalay bhi hai. lumvini anchal aspataal, siddharth bal aspataal ke alaava bahut se swasthya sanstha is shahar main he. yahan par local phone, es tii di, aai es di, pre ped, postped movil, imail internet uplabdh he. butwal, nepal kaa pramukh mahendra rajmarg va uttar-dakshin sidarth rajmarg kaa milanvindu he. yahan se desh ke har hisse main jane ke liye yatayaat saadhaan uplabdh hai. saath saath yah lumvini, gandki, va dhavalagiri anchal ke paahadi jilon kaa pramukh supplai point bhi he. nagar va jile ke andaruni yatayaat ke liye kai pakki-kachi nai sadaken ban raheen hai. paryatakon ko rahane ke liye paanch-saat achche hotel bhi butwal main hai.
