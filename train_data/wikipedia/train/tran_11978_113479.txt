 bhoomi kaa piech maan yah bataata he ki bhoomi kshareey he athwa amleey or kitni.
 uese kaa krishi vibhaag piech ke aadhaar par mrudaa kaa vargeekaran nimnlikhit prakaar se karata he:
 aisa paaya gaya he ki bhinn-bhinn padpon ke liye bhinn-bhinn piech sabase hadhik upyukt hota he. neeche ek aisi sarini di gayi he.
