 
 13 kul, 12 vartmaan
 jhandmurgi megapodius‎ jeevvaigyanik vansh ke pakshiyon ko kehete hai. yah murgi-jaise mote, madhyamaakar ke megapodidaye jeevvaigyanik kul ke pakshi hote hai. inkaa vistaar dakshinpurv asia se australia or pashchim prashanth mahaasaagar ke dwipon tak he. jhandmurgiyaan apane andon par baithkar unhen garam rakhane kii bajaae sadate hue patton va anya vanaspatiyon kii dheri bana lete hai jinamein garmi paidaa hoti he or andon ko garam rakhti he. jhandmurgi nar samay-samay par dheri kaa nirikshan karata he or taapmaana niyantrit rakhane ke liye usamen saamagree daalata-hataata he.
