 shachi indra kii patni or puloma kii kanya thim. draupadi inhen ke ansh se utpann hui thim or ye swayan prakruti kii anyatam kala se janmi thim. jayant shachi ke hi putr the. shachi ko 'indrani', 'aindri', 'mahendri', 'pulomaja', 'paulomi' aadi naamon se bhi janaa jaataa he.
 brahmahatya ke bhay se ek baar jab indra jalgarbha main chhipe hue the to devataaon ne nahush ko indrapad de diya. nahush ne shachi par kudrushti kii to brihaspati kii ajna se unhone bhubaneshwari kii aradhana kii or unase abhaya praapt kiya. phir shachi ne maansarovar jaakar chhipe hue indra se apni sari katha kahi. indra kii salaah se shachi ne nahush se kahalaaya 'yadi saptarshiyon ke kandhe par rakhi palki main baithkar aawein to main tumhaare saath chaluungi.' nahush ne aisa hi kiya. rishiyon ko dhire dhire chalate dekhakar usne aadesh diya 'sarp sarp' antatogatva rishiyon ke shaap se nahush ko sarp ho janaa padaa.
 apane vivaah ke purv shachi ne shankar se sundar pati, swechhamat rup tathaa sukh evam aayu kaa vardaan maanga tha. rugveda main shacheerachit kuch sookt hai jinamein sapatni kaa naash karne ke liye prarthana kii gai he . kuch vidwaanon ke mat se sookt bahut baad kii rachanaayen hai.
