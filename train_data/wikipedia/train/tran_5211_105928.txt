 daroga prasad raay ek bhartiya rajaneta the or bihar ke 10wein mukhyamantri rah chuke the. daaroga raay 16 farvari 1970 se 22 disambar 1970 tak bihar ke mukhyamantri rahe. 
 daroga raay congressi the lekin 1990 ke daur main jab desh main janata dal parivaar raajnneeti main prabhavi hone laga tab putr chandrika raay bhi lalu prasad yadav ke saath ho liye. daaroga raay ke paanch putron main chandrika raay duusare number ke bete hai. saaran zila ke parasa vidhansabha kshetra se rajd vidhayak or purv mantri putr chandrika prasad raay kii badi beti aishwarya raay ne rajd supremeo lalu prasad yadav ke bade bete tej pratap yadav se shaadi kii.
