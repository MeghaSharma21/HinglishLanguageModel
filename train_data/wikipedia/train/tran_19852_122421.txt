 lahore main hira mandi shahar ke aise ilaake ko kehete hai jisamen vaishyagiri kii garaz se qahabaa kaane khule hue hai. ye shahi muhalla ke naam se bhi mashhoor he. magaribi mamalik main aise ilaake ko redlite district yani laal batti zila kaha jaataa he
 mogul daur main hira mandi raqs or mausiqi ke liye mashhoor tha. ye shahar ke tawaiphi saqaaft kaa mercz tha. log basri tafarih or mausiqi ke liye yahaan jate the.baaz ravaayat ke mutabiq yahaan sukh hukumat ke hindu dogra vazeer-e-aazam dhyaan singh ke bete hira singh kii haveli thi hira singh maharaja ranjit kaa munh bolaa betaa bana hua tha.apane baap dhyaan singh kii maut ke baad wo vazeer-e-aazam ke ohade par fise hua.is kii haveli or naam kii munasbat se lahore shahar kaa ye hissaa hira mandi kehalaata he
