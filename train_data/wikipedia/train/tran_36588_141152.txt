 saint steefan college dilli vishvavidhyalay ke adhinsth dilli main sthit ek college he. yah bharat ke prasiddh shaikshik sansthaanon main se ek he, jahaan kala evam vigyaan kii oopaadhiyaan di jaati hai. is college ke kai purachaatr kaafii prasidd rahe hai or college ke chaatro ko steffenian kaha jaataa he.
 is college kaa itihaas bahut purana he. saint steffens skool ke rup main 1854 se maujuud shikshan santhaan ko 1 farvari 1881 ko cambridge mishan dwaara college kaa darja mila or samual scot alnut iske sansthaapak pradhanacharya bane. college ke kuch ullekhneey pradhanacharyon main si eff andrews kaa naam mahatvapurn he.varsh 1922 main dilli vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana ke baad yah college bhi dilli vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha ho gaya.
