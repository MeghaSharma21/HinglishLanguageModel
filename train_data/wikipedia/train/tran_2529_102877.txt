 gamd​ yaa gamid​ ek badu arabi kabila he. yah un ginati ke arab qabeelon main se he jinhen arabi prayadweep kaa mul nivasee mana jaataa he. is kabeeley ke log zahraan qabeele ke bhi sambandhi hai or unke aitihaasik mitrapaksh main rahe hai. inaki mul mathrubhumi arabi prayadweep kaa dakshini yaa dakshinpurvi bhaag, visheshkar yaman or saudi arab kaa al-bahah kshetra, kahi jaati he. yah saudi arab ke bade qabeelon main se ek mana jaataa he. islam ke shuruuaati daur main inhonen aarambhik muslim samrajya main bhoomika nibhaai thi.
