 gargyayan kaa pranavvaad bhagwaan das dwaara 1010-13 ke bich teen bhaagon main prakashit ek granth he. isamen bhagwaan das kaa kahana he ki yah granth ek maharishi gargyayan dwaara rachit ek alpagyaat pracheen granth kaa saransh he. bhagwaan das kaa yah bhi kahana he ki yah pustak unako pandit dhanraj ne apni smriti se unhen sunaaya or unhone ise likhaa he. pandit dhanraj unke thiosophical sosayadi ke mitra the or aankh se andhe the. pustak ke prakaashan ke purv hi pandit dhanraj kii mrityu ho gai thi.
