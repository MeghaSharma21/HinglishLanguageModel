  raashtrapati nivas  shimla, himachal pradesh, bharat ke obserwetary hills par sthit he.
 yah pehle bharat ke british vysarai kaa nivas hua karata tha. ye nivas british architect henry irvin dwaara dizin kiya gaya tha or lord duffrin ke shasankaal ke dauraan elizabethan style main banaaya gaya tha. iskaa nirmaan 1880 main shuru hua or 1888 main puraa hua. lord duffrin ne 23 july 1888 ko waisarigal loj main nivas shuru kiya.
 british kaal main shimla greeshmakaleen rajdhani thi or ye nivas tab kai mahatvapurn ghatnaon kaa sakshi rahaa he. shimla sammelan 1945 yahan hua tha or 1947 main bharat ke vibhaajan kaa nirnay bhi yahi liya gaya tha.
 swatantraaa ke baad, waisarigal loj bharat sarkaar ke kabje main aayaa. ise raashtrapati nivas kaa naam diya gaya or raashtrapati kaa greeshmakaleen nivas bana. lekin greeshmakaleen ghar shaayad hi kabhi istemaal kiya gaya tha isliye yah raashtrapati sarvapalli radhakrishnan dwaara indian institute of advanced study ke liye daan kiya gaya.

 nivas ke andruni bhaag
 nivas ke peeche ke bageeche
