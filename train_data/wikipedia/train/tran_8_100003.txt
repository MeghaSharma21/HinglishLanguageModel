 jackson haris, ek america pop gayak he. vah apane ekal "life thru lens" ke liye janaa jaataa he, jo billboard twitor imarging artist chart main number 20 par pahunch gaya..
 haris main paidaa hua tha, neuyork, neuyork, sanyukt rajya america, or bhaag liya reverdley desh main skool. vah shuru kiya hastakshar karne ke ek bachche ke rup main or pradarshan main kai skool nibhaataa he.
 haris darj kii gai ek cover sanskaran ke je-Z or elicia keez's hit "samrajya man kii sthiti". ek video ke liye joda gaya utyub par march 24, 2010 or praapt hua he 200,000 se adhik vichaaron ke saath. haris to darj kii gai apni pehali film eepi "lambii kahani ko kam". ke liye video "lambii kahani sankshep main" utyub par aplod kiya gaya tha or sitaron yahuda Friedlander.
