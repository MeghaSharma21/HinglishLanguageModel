 khiching odisha ke mayurbhanj jile main sthit ek pracheen aitihaasik gaanv he. yah bhanj vansh kii pracheen rajdhani he. bhanj kaa rajvansh chittod ke rajputon kii ek shakha thi. is rajya ke do khand the jo kramash: khiching or khinjali kahe jaataa the. is rajvansh ke sansthaapak virbhadra the. unhen 'aadi bhanj' kaha jaataa he. unke sambandh main janushruti he ki unka janm kisi pakshi ke andey se hua tha or vashishtha rishi ne unka paalan poshan kiya.
 khiching main do durgo ke avshesh hai jo viratgarh or kichak garh kahe jate hai. bhanj rajgaraane kii devi chakeshwari kahi jaati he. vahaan unki ek darshneey murti he. vahaan nilkantheshwar mahadev kaa mandir he jo vaastukala kii drushti se ullekhneey he. vah bhubaneshwar ke mandiron kii parampara main bana he or kadachit 11veen yaa 12veen shatee kaa he.
