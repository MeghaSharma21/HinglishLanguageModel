 petra jordan ke m'aan praant main sthit ek aitihaasik nagari he jo apane pathar se taraashi gai imaaraton or pani vaahan pranali ke liye prasiddh he. ise chhathi shatabdi isapurv main nabatiyon ne apni rajdhani ke taur par sthapit kiya tha. mana jaataa he ki iskaa nirmaan kaary 1200 isapurv ke aasapaas shuru hua. aadhunik yug main yah ek mashhoor paryatak sthal he. petra ek "hor" naamak pahaad kii dhalaan par bana hui he or pahaado se ghiri hui ek droni main sthit he. yah pahaad mrit sagar se aqaaba kii khadi tak chalane vaali "vaadi arbia" naamak ghati kii purvi seemaa hai. petra ko unesco dwaara ek vishwa dharohar hone kaa darja mila hua he. bibisi ne apni "marane se pehle 40 dekhne yogya sthaan" main petra ko bhi shaamil kiya hua he.
 6 veen shatabdi isa purv se petra naabatian samrajya kii prabhaavshaalee rajdhani thi. jiske baad samrajya 106 isvi main roman samrajya main avashoshit ho gaya tha or romanon ne shahar kaa vistaar jari rakhaa. vyapaar or vaanijya ke liye ek mahatvapurn centre, petra ne tab tak vikaas jari rakhaa jab tak ki ek vinaashkari bukamp ne imaaraton ko nasht nahin kiya or chhathi satabdi main musalamaano petra jeet liya lekin musalamaano adheen zyada samay tak nahin rahaa tha. iske baad 1189 isvi main madhya purv ke muslim sultan saladin kii vijay ke baad petra ko isaayiyo tyaag diya.
 trans-jordan ke gathan ke baad sthal kii pehali vaastavik khudaai 1929 isvi main gai thim. us samay se, petra 1989 main steven spilberg film, indiana jones or last crossed dwaara expoger ke kaaran aanshik rup se jordan ke sabase bade paryatak aakarshan ban gaya hai. petra ke shandaar ejineering uplabdhiyon or acchhee tarah se sangrakshit aayam ke kaaran, petra, ko puratathvik sthal july 2007 main duniya ke nae saat aashcharyon main se ek ke rup main chunaa gaya thi.== inhen bhi dekhen ==
