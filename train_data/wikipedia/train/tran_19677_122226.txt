 kundi bhandara yaa khooni bhandara maharashtra kii seemaa se sate madhya pradesh ke burhanpur jile main nagarvasiyon ko peyajal uplabdh karwaane kii ek jivit bhu-jal sanrachana he. madhyakaalin bharat kii ejineering kitni samrudh rahi hogi yah burhanpur ke kundi bhandare ko dekhne se hi pataa chalata he. 400 saal puraani ye jal yantriki aadhunik yug ke liye bhi ek kathin paheli he. us samay kaise satapura kii pahadiyon ke pattharon ko chirakar nagar kii jal aavashyaktaaon ko puraa kiya gaya hoga, jab na to aaj kii tarah mashine thim or na hi bhu-garbh main behate pani ke shroton kaa pataa lagane vaale yantra. 
 mughal badshaah akbar ke shasankaal main burhanpur ek pramukh sainik chhavnee thi. suprasiddh aseergarh ke kile se thodi duuri par sthit burhanpur ko dakkan kaa dwaar mana jaataa tha. dilli ke sultan isi jagah se dakkan ke bhubhag par niyantran ke liye sainya abhiyaan sanchalit karte the. aseergarh ke kile par vijay ke baad akbar ne abdurrahim khan-e-khaanaa ko burhanpur kaa subedaar niyukt kiya. lekin satapura kii pahadiyon ke bich based is shahar main peyajal kii baari killat thi jise dur karne ke liye rahim ne shahar ke aas-paas jal sroton kii talash shuru kar di.
 shahar se 6 kilometre dur satapura kii talahati main abdurrahim khan-e-khaanaa ko ek jal strot mila or unke man main is jal ko nagar tak pahunchane kaa vichaar palane laga. rahim ne is kaary ke liye abhiyantriki main kushal apane kaarigaron se mashvira kiya or is jal ko nagar tak laane ke liye san 1612 een main jameen se 80 feet neeche ghumaavdaar naharon ke nirmaan par kaary shuru ho gaya. do saal tak anwarat chale khanan kaary or pattharon se chinai ke baad teen kilometre lambii nahar ke jariye shuddh peyajal ko ko san 1915 een main jaali karanj tak pahunchaaya gaya.
