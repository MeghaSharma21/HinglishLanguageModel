 aisi vyavastha jisamen rajya ko kai prashasnic ikai main baant diya jaataa he or unhen ek seemaa tak prashasnic, nyaayik evam rajasav aadi ke sandarbh main shakti bhi de di jaati he. saath hi ek shaktishaalee kendriya sarkaar kaa astitv bana rahata he, jo sabhi ikaayiyon ke samooh kaa sanchaalan karata he.
