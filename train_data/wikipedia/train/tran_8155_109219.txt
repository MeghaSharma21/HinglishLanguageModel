 billiards ke peshewar khilaadiyon ke rup main 1968 main sthapit vishwa peshewar billiards or snooker sangh peshewar khel ke liye ek shaasi nikaay he. bristol, england main sthit isaki sahaayak sanstha, peshewar daure yojit karata he. varshon se WPBSA kii samiti kai baar badal chuki he, jiske bare main kai log yah tark dete hai ki yah khel ke bheetar hone vaali antarkalah kaa ek sanket he. peshewar khel antarrashtriya billiards or snooker mahasang dwaara sanchalit hota he.
 peshewar snooker khilaadi vishwa snooker ke mukhya daure par daure vaali variyata main khel sakate hai. pichley do sijanon main khilaadiyon dwaara apane pradarshnon ke madhyam se arjeet ank, vartmaan vishwa variyata ko nirdhaarit karte hai. ek khilaadi ki variyata yah nirdhaarit karti he ki variyata vaali pratiyogita ke liye unhen kis str kii yogyata kii zarurat hai. peshewar snooker ke vishisht varg aamtaur par "sheersh 16" variyata vaale khilaadi maane jate hai, jinhen kisi bhi pratiyogita ke liye purv main qualifi karne kii zarurat nahin hoti he. daure main 96 khilaadi shaamil hote hai - pichley do sijanon main se sheersh 64 khilaadi, 8 ek varsh main sarvocch ank praapt karne vaale khilaadi jo sheersh 64 main shaamil nahin hai, pichley sijan ke pontins international shankhalaaon ke sheersh 8 khilaadi or vibhinn kshetreey, junior or gaiyr-peshewar vijeta.
