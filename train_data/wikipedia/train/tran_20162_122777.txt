 jowarbhata bal vah bal he jo ek vastu apane gurutvkarshan se kisi duusari vastu par sthit alag-alag sthanon par alag-alag str se lagaati he. prithvi par samudr main jowar-bhaata ke utaar-chadhaav kaa yahi kaaran he.
 angrezi main "jowarbhata bal" ko "tidel force" bolte hai.
 jab chandramaa prithvi par apane gurutvkarshan kaa prabhaav daalata he to jo uske sabase sameep kaa samudr he, uskaa pani chandramaa kii ore uthta he. is se us ilaake main jowar aata he . prithvi ke daayen or baayein taraf kaa pani bhi khichakar saamne kii ore jaataa he, jis se un ilaaqon main bhaata aata he . prithvi ke peeche kaa jo pani he vah chandramaa se sab se dur hota he or khichaav kam mahasoos karata he, jis se vahaan bhi jowar rahata he. chandramaa ke jowarbhata bal kaa asar pani tak simit nahin. prithvi kii zamin par bhi iskaa asar hota he. agar puraa asar dekha jaae to jowarbhata bal se prithvi kaa gola thodaa pichak sa jaataa he.
 khagolashastra main ek rosh seemaa kaa niyam he. isamen yadi koi vastu kisi grah kii rosh seemaa ke andar aa jaye to vah grah usapar aisa jowarbhata bal daalata he ke vah vastu pichak kar tut sakati he. hamaare saura mandal ke chathe grah shani ke upgrahi challe kuch isi tarah hi bane hai. saadhaaran taur par agar kisi grah ke ird-gird malba parikrama kar rahaa ho to vah samay ke saath-saath judakar upgrah ban jaataa he. lekin agar yah rosh seemaa ke andar ho to jowarbhata bal kii pichakan use judane nahin detii. agar koi bana-banaaya upgrah yaa dhoomaketu bhi rosh seemaa ke andar bhatak jaae to todaa ja saktaa he. 1992 main jab shumekar-lavi naam kaa dhoomaketu brihaspati se ja takaraayaa to brihaspati ke bhayankar jowarbhata bal ne use tod daalaa.
