 umr rajjaz jordan ke vartmaan pradhan mantri hai. desh main upayon ke khilaaf vyapak virodh ke parinamswaroop unke purvavarti ke istipha dene ke baad 5 june 2018 ko niyukt kiya tha. 4 janvari 2017 se rajjaj shiksha mantri rahe the. vah pehle kai rashtriya or antarrashtriya sansthaanon ke nideshak the.
 rajaj 2002 se 2006 ke bich lebanon main vishwa bank ke nideshak the. vah 2006 or 2010 ke bich jordan ke soshal security corporation ke nideshak the. unhone jordan stratji forum or jordan ke ahali bank ke nideshak ke rup main bhi kaary kiya.. 
 2017 main, vah shiksha mantri ke rup main haani mulki kii sarkaar main shaamil ho gaye. unke kaarykaal main jordan kii shiksha pranali ke liye overhaal dekha gaya.. 5 june 2018 ko vah apane purvavarti mulki ke baad pradhan mantri bane, desh main tapasya upayon ke khilaaf vyapak virodh ke parinamswaroop istipha de diya tha.
