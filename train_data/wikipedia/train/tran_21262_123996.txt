 munasyari tehsil bharat ke uttarakhand rajya main pithauragarh janapad main ek tehsil he. pithauragarh janapad ke uttar-pashchimi bhaag main sthit is tehsil ke mukhyalay munasyari nagar main sthit hai. iske purv main dharchula tehsil, pashchim main bageshwar janapad kii kapkot tehsil tathaa chamoli janapad kii joshimath tehsil, uttar main china tathaa dakshin main didihat or berinag tehsil he. tehsil ke adhikaar kshetra main kul 219 gaanv aate hai, or 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar isaki jansankhya 46523 he.
