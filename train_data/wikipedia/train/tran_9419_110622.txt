 bhatiyana khushhalpur bharat desh main uttar pradesh rajya ke janapad bijanour kii dhampur tehsil ke antargat aata he. yah purv se hi ek swatantra nyaay panchayat gaanv rahaa he. aaj main rahul kumaar chouhan dinaank 21 octubar san 2016 ko apane gram bhatiyana khushhalpur ke bare main tathyaparak jaankaree se sabko avgat karvaa rahaa hoon. bhatiyana khushhalpur nyaay panchayat purv kaal se hi sampann panchayato main se ek rahi he. aas-paas ke sabhi ganvo main, kshetro main bhatiyana khushhalpur apana vishesh sthaan rakhata he. bhatiyana khushhalpur main sabhi vyakti ek duusare kaa sammaan karte hai evam paraspar bhaichaare ko banaaai rakhte hai. kisi bhi prakaar kii vipatti aane par ek-duusare kaa tan-man-dhan se sahyog bhi karte hai.
