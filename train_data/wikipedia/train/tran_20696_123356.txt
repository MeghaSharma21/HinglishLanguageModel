 sulfgiphu 955 se 957-8 tak angrej raja eadwig kii patni or england kii rani thim. unke bare main jo kuch thoda bahut pataa he wo angl-sackson ghoshnaptr jisamen ek vasiyat shaamil he, or anglon-sackson gaathaon main shatrutapurna va bair bhav se bhari laghu kahaniyon yaa upakhyanon va sacharitra varnanon se miltaa he. raja ke saath unka milan eadwig ke rajbhishek ke kuch hi saalon baad tut gaya jab pramukh padri cantarbari ke od ne ise behad najadik kaa khun kaa rishta bataate hue amaanya qaraar diya. unka vivaah sambhavat: saath ke dashak ke angrej satta kii raajnneetik khinchataan kii bhent chadh gaya. lagbhag 1000 i0 main jab benedictine sudhaarkon jaise danston or oswold ke sacharitra varnanon kaa kaal aayaa tab sulfgiphu kii smritiyon ko lagbhag bhula diya gaya. halaaki 960 ke dashak ke madhya main wo ek achche rahan sahan vaali dhanwaan va jameendaar mahila jiske raja edgar se achche sambandh the ke taur par jaani jaati thi. apni vasiyaton se use vincester ke old minster, vincester va new minster, vincester main vishaal ghar, bhoomi va shahi dhan praapt hua tha jo ki shahi parivaar se sambandhit tha.
