 james wat, ek scottish avishkaarak or yantric ejeeniyar sudhaar jiskaa Newcomen bhaap ingine dwaara laayaa parivartan karne ke liye moulik the audyogic kranti donon main or great britain ke samrajya duniya.
 jabaki ke ek saadhan nirmaataa ke rup main kaam karne ke glassgo vishvavidhyalay, wat ke praudyogiki ke kshetra main dilchaspee ban gaye bhaap ingine. unhone mahasoos kiya ki samakaleen ingine ke dizin ko baar - baar thanda or phir heating dwaara urja kaa ek bada sauda barbaad ki silendar. wat ek dizin vruddhi, alag condenser, jo urja ke is barbaadi se bachaa or moulik shakti, dakshata or bhaap ingine kii laagat prabhaavashiltaa main sudhaar kii shuruyaat kii. vah kii avadharana viksit ashwashakti . esai shakti kii ikai wat, uske baad namit kiya gaya tha.
 wat apane avishkaar kaa vyavsaayeekaran karne kaa prayaas kiya, lekin mahaan vittiya kathinaaiyon kaa anubhav kiya jab tak unhone 1775 main ke saath ek saajhedaari main pravesh kiya mathew Boulton. ke nae form Boulton or wat ke ant main atyadhik safal wat or ek amir aadmi ban gaya. sevanivritti main, wat nae avishkaar haalanki koi nahin ke rup main apane bhaap ingine kaam ke rup main mahatvapurn the viksit karne ke liye jari rakhaa. vah 83 varsh kii aayu main 1819 main nidhan ho gaya. wat maanav itihaas main sabase prabhaavshaalee aankadon ke rup main varnit kiya gaya he.
