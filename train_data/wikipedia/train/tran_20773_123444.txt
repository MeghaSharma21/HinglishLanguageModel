  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 british ekarattantr athwa british rajtantr, vrihat britain or uttari ireland kii united kingdom kii sanvaidhanik rajtantr he. british ekadhidaruk ko united kingdom samet kul 15 rashtramandal pradeshon, mukutiyaa nirbhartaaon or samudrapar pradeshon ke rajmukuton sattadharak ekarajeey samprabhu hone kaa gaurav praapt he. vartmaan satta-vidyaman shaasha, 6 farvari varsh 1952 se maharani elizabeth dwitiya hai jab unhone apane pita jorge shashtham se rajgaddi uttaradhikrut kii thi. 
 samprabhu or uske tatkaal parivaar ke sadsy desh ke vibhinn aadhikarik, aupchaarik or pratinidhi kaaryon kaa nirvaah karte hai. sattadhari rani/raja par saidhantik rup se ek sanvaidhanik shaasha ke adhikaar nihit he, parantu sadiyon puraane aam kanoon ke kaaran samprabhu apane adhiktar shaktiyon kaa abhyaas keval sansad or sarkaar ke vinirdeshon ke anusaar hi karyanvit karne ke liye baadhya hai. is kaaran se, ise vaastavik taur par ek sansadeey samrajyata maani jaataa he. sansadeey shaasha hone ke naate, shaasha ke adhiktar adhikaar, nishpaksh tathaa gaiyr-rajnatik kaaryon tak simit hai. samraat, shaasha or rashtrapramukh hone ke naate unke adhiktar sanvaidhanik shashan tathaa rajnatik-shaktiyon kaa abhyay ve sarkaar or apane mantriyon kii salaah or vinirdeshon par hi karte hai. paramparanusaar shaasha, britain ke sashastr bal ke adhipati hote hai. haalaanki, samprabhu ke samast kaary-adhikaron kaa abhyay shaasha ke raaj-parmadhikaar dwaara hota he.
 varsh 1000 ke aasapaas, england or scotland ke rajyon main kai chote praarambhik madhyayugin rajya viksit hue the. is kshetra main angl-sackson logon kaa varchasv england par norman vijay ke dauraan 1066 main samaapt ho gaya, jab antim angl-sackson raja harold dwitiya kii mritu ho gayi thi or angrezi satta vijai sena ke netaa, wiliam da conkarar or unke vanshajon ke haathon main chali gayi. 
 13veen sadi main england ne wales kii riyasat ko avashoshit kiya tathaa megna carta dwaara samprabhu ke kramik nishashaktakaran kii prakriya shuru hui. 1603 main scotland ke raja james chaturth, angreji singhaasana par james pratham ke naam se virajman hokar jo donon rajyon ko ek vyaktigat sangh kii sthiti main laa khadaa kiya. 1649 se 1660 ke liye angrezi rashtramandal ke naam se ek kshanik ganatantric kaal chalaa, jo teen rajyon ke yuddh ke baad astiv main aayaa, parantu 1660 ke baad rajshahi ko punarsthapit kar diya gaya. 1707 main parvartit act of setlement, 1701, jo aaj bhi parvartit he, catholic vyaktiyon tathaa catholic vyakti sang vivaahit vyaktiyon ko angrazi rajasatta par kaabiz hone se nishkarshith karata he. 1707 main angrezi or scotian rajshahiyon ke vilay se great britain rajshahi kii saathpana hui or isi ke saath angrazi or scotish mukuton kaa bhi vilay ho gaya or sanyukt "british ekarattantr" sthapit hui. irish rajshahi ne 1801 main great britain rajshahi ke saath jud kar great britain or ireland kii united kingdom kii sthapana kii.
 british ekarat, vishaal british samrajya ke nammaatr pramukh the, jo 1921 main apane vrihattam vistaar ke samay vish ke chauthai bhu-bhaag par raaj karata tha. 1922 main ireland kaa paanch-chhyai hissaa irish mukt rajya ke naam se, sangh se bahaar nikal gaya. balfor ghoshnaa, 1926 ne british dominionon ke aupniveshik pad se rashtramandal ke bheetar hi vibhakt, swashasit, saarvbhaumik deshon ke rup main parivartan ko maanya qaraar diya. dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke baad british samrajya simatata gaya, or british samrajya ke adhiktar purv upnivesh va pradesh swatantra ho gaye. 
 jo purv upnivesh, british shaasha ko apana shaasha maanate he, un deshon ko british rashtramandal pramandal yaa rashtramandal pradesh kaha jaataa he. in anek rashtron ke chinhatmak samanaantar pramukh hone ke naate, british ekarat swayan ko rashtramandal ke pramukh ke kitaab se bhi nawazte hai. haalanki kii shaasha ko british shaasha ke naam se hi sambodhit kiya jaataa he, parantu saiddhanthik taur par saare rashtron kaa samprabhu par saamaan adhikaar he, tathaa rashtramandal ke tamaam desh ek-dusre se purnatah swatantra or swytt hai.
 britain kii rajatantric vyavastha main, raja/rani ko rashtrapramukh kaa darj diya gaya he. british raajnneetik vyavastha, joki hazaron varshon kii kalavadhi par kramik rup se viksit va parivartit hoti rahi he, main, shaasha ke paaramparik va vaastavik shaktiyaan ghatati-badhati rahi he. british rajnatik sankalpana main, britain ke samprabhu ko rajmukut ke maanvi avatar ke rup main mana jaataa he, arthath ve sampoorn rajya va puri shasampranali ke samast shasnadhikaar ke antyant strot hai, or britain par shashan karne kaa adhikaar antytah british samprabhu ke paas hi he. atah, nyaayadhish, saansadon tathaa tamaam mantriyon samet, saare sarkari afasaron or karmchaariyon ek aadhikarik niyokta va karyaadhikaar ke ke pradaata bhi samprabhu hi hai. tathaa nishtha kii shapath maharani ke prati lee jaati he. tathaa saare sansadeey adhiniyamon ko vaidhik hone ke liye shahi sweekruti praapt karnaa anivaary he. tathaa united kingdom kaa raashtragan god seve da queen he. iske alaava sabhi dak tikaton, sikkon va mudra-noton par shaasha kii chhavi ko ankit kiya jaataa he.
 baharhaal, shashan-prakriya, niti-nirdhaaran va prashasnic nirnay lene main, adhirat kaa vaastavik yogadan nunatam tathaa nammaatr kaa chhot he, kyonki vibhinn aitihaasik sanvidhiyon or roodhiyon ke kaaran shaasha ke adhiktar adhirati shaktiyaan adhirat se mukut ke mantriyon or adhikariyon yaa anya sansthaanon ke paas pratyyojit kar di gayi hai. atah shaskaon dwaara rajmukut ke naam par kiye gaye adhiktar kaary, chaahe shaasha dwaara swayan hi kiye gaye kaary ho, jaise kii maharani kaa abhibhaashan yaa sansad kaa rajkeey udghaatan, saare kaaryo ke nirnay kahin or liye jate hai:
 vidhayik kaary maharani sasansaad dwaara, sansad, house of lords or house of comons kii salaah or sweekruti dwaara kiya jaataa he, vahin, kaaryakari shaktiya maharani ke naam par unki mahima kii sarkaar dwaara abhyayit kiye jate hai. jisame, pradhanmantri or mukut ke anya mantri tathaa mantrimandal dwaara abhyayit kiye jate hai, jo asal main, maharani kii shahi parishad kii ek samiti he. tathaa shaasha kii nyaay karne or dand dene kii shaktiyaan nyaaypalika par nihit kii gayin hai, jo sanvaidhanik taur par, sarkaar se swatantra he. vahin carch of england, jisaki pramukh rani he, kii apni swayan kii prashasnic vyavastha he. iske alaava bhi shaasha ke anya kaaryon ko vibhinn samitiyon va nikaayon ko saunpe gaye hai.
 jab aavashyak ho, tab, adhirat kaa daayitv he kii ve apni sarkaar kaa netritv karne ke liye ek pradhanmantri kii niyukti karen. alikhit samvidhan kii aitihaasik parampara ke anusaar aam taur par pradhan mantri house of comons main bahumat praapt dal ke net hote hai. pradhanmantri apana kaarybhaar, shaasha ke saath ek vyaktigat mulaaqaat ke baad karte he, jisame "haanth chuumane" kii ek parampara hote hi niyukti turant hi, bina or kisi aupchaarikta ke, paarit ho jaati he.
 trishanku sabha kii sthiti main, adhirat ke paar apane vivek ke upyog kar, apane icchaanusaar sarkaar ke pratinidhi kaa chunaav karne ke adhik avasar hota he, haalaanki aise sthiti main bhi reetinusaar sadan ke sabase bade dal ke netaa ko hi chunaa jaataa he. 1945 se aaj tak, keval do baar aise sthiti ai thi. pehali baar faivari 1974 ke aam chunaav ke baad, or 2010 ke aam chunaav ke baad, jab conzarvative party or libral-democratic party ne gathbandhan banaaya tha.
 paaramparik taur par, sansad ke satr ko bulaane va bhang karne kaa adhikaar shaasha ke vivek par tha, or shaasha swechha se sabha bulaayaa va bhang kiya karte the. atah dharnatmak rup se aaj bhi sansad bulaane va bhang karne ke adhikaar kaa abhyay shaasha hi karte hai. 2011 main paarit phixd-turm paraliyaments act ne sansad bhang karne ke ashikaar ko khatm kar diya. haalaanki satrantan karne kaa adhikaar shaasha ke paas abhi bhi he. yadi ek alpsankhyak sarkaar, sansad ko bhang kar nae chunaav ghoshit karne kii maang karti he, to shaasha aisi maang ko kaarij karne ke liye purnatah swatantra hai. dharnatmat rup se shaasha swechha se pradhanmantri ko kabhi bhi padochit kar sakate hai, parantu varttman sthiti main pradhanmantri ko stifa, mrityu yaa chunavi haar kii sthiti main hi pad se nishkashit kiya jaataa he. pradhanmantri ko kaarykaal ke bich nishkashit karne vaale antim shaasha wiliam chaturth the, jinhone 1834 main lord melbourne ko nikaala tha.
 samprabhu, sthapit carch of england ke sarvocch prashaasak hai. carch ke saare bishap va archbishpon kii niyukti shaasha dwaara, pradhanmantri kii salaah par kii jaati he. pradhanmantri, carch ayog dwaara taiyaar kiye gaye pratyaashiyon kii ek suchi main se niyukta ko chunate hai. angrezi girza main rajmukut kii bhoomika nammaatr kii he. carch ke varishthatam padri, cantarbari ke archbishap, is carch tathaa vishwa bhar ke angalikai sampradaay ke aadhyatmik netaa ke rup main dekhe jate hai. tathaa adhirat, carch of scotland ke sangrakshan kii shapath bhi lete hai, tathaa carch kii mahasabha ke prabhu ucchayukta ko niyukt karne kaa bhi daayitv rakhte hai, anyathaa, girza ke kaaryon main unki koi sarthak bhoomika nahin he, naahi ve isapar koi adhikaar rakhte hai. visthapit carch of wales tathaa carch of ireland main samprabhu kaa koi aupchaarik bhoomika nahin he.
 18 veen or 19veen sadi ke dauraan britain ke aupniveshik vistaar dwaara, britain ne vishwa ke anya anek bhu-bhaagon ve kshetron par apana kabza jamaa liya. jinamein se adhiktar deshon ne madhya 20veen sadi tak britain se swatantraaa hasil kar lee. haalaanki un sabhi deshon ne united kingdom kii sarkaar kii adhipatyata ko nakaar diya, parantu unamem se kai raashtra, british shaasha ko apane adhirat ke rup main manyata dete hai. aise dehon rashtramandal pradesh yaa rashtramandal prajabhumi kaha jaataa he. varttman kaal main, united kingdom ke adhirat keval united kingdom ke hi nahin balki uske atirikt kul 15 anya rashtron ke adhirat bhi hai. haalanki in rashtron main bhi unhen lagbhag saamaan pad va adhikaar praapt he jaisaa kii britain main, parantu un deshon main, unka koi vaastavik raajnneetik yaa paaramparik karttavya nahin he, shaasha ke lagbhag saare karttavya unke pratnidhi ke rup main us desh ke maharajyapal puraa karte hai. britain kii sarkaar kaa rashtramandal pradeshon kii sarkaron ke kaary main koi bhi bhoomika yaa hastkshep nahin he. britain ke alaava rashtramandal aayam main: antigua or barbuda, australia, bahama, barbados, belize, grenada, jamaica, canada, newjiland, papua new gini, solomon dweep, saint loosia, saint kits or nevis, saint vincent or grenadins or tuvaalu jaise desh shaamil hai.
 purvatah rashtron ke rashtramandal ke saare desh rashtramandal paribhumi ke hissaa hua karte the, parantu 1950 main bharat ne swatantraaa ke pashchaat swayan ko ganraajya ghoshit kiya, or british rajasatta kii rashtrapramukh ke rup main samprabhutaa ko bhi khatm kar diya. parantu bharat ne rashtramandal kii sadsyta barqaraar raakhi. uske baad se, rashtramandal deshon main, british samprabhu ko "rashtramandal ke pramukh" kaa pad bhi diya jaataa he, jo rashtramandal ke sanghathan kaa nammaatr pramukh kaa pad he. is pad kaa koi rajnatik arth nahin he.
 rashtramandal pradeshon ke bich kaa sambandh is prakaar kaa he kii uttaradhikaar ko anushaasit karne vaale kisi bhi bidhan kaa saare deshon kii ekamat sweekruti aavashyak he. singhasana par uttaradhikaar, vibhinn aitihaasik sanvidhion dwaara anushaasit he, jinamein bill of ritis, 1689, act of setlement, 1701 or act of union, 1707 shaamil hai. uttaradhikaar sambandhit niyam keval sansadeey adhinium dwaara parivartit kiye ja sakate hai, singhasana kaa koi uttaradhikari, swechha se apana uttaradhikaar tyaag nahin kar saktaa he. singhasana par virajman hone ke pashchaat ek vyakti apane nidhan tak raaj karata he. itihaas main ekmaatr swachhik padatyag, 1936 main edward ashtum ne kiya tha, jise sansad ke vishesh adhinium dwaara vaid qaraara gaya tha. antim baar jab kisi shaasha ko anaichhik rup se nishkashit kiya gaya tha, wo tha 1688 main james saptam or dwitiya jinhen glorious revolution ke samay nishkashit kiya gaya tha.
 act of setlement, 1701, uttaradhikaar ko sofia of hanover, james pratham kii ek potee, ke vaidhik protestant vanshajon tak simit karata he. atah rajpariwaar kaa koi bhi catholic sadsy kabhi bhi singhasana ko uttaridhikrut nahin kar saktaa he. ek shaasi shaasha ke nidhan par swayamev hi, rajpth, uske asann vaaris ke paas chalaa jaataa he, atah saidhantik rup se, singhasana ek kshan ke liye bhi khali nahin rahata he. tathaa uttaradhikaar ko saarvajanik rup se uttaradhikaar parishad dwaara ghoshit kii jaati he. atah angreji parampara ke anusaar shaasha ke uttaradhikaar ko vaid hone ke liye rajbhishek hona aavashyak nahin he. atah aam taur par rajbhishek uttaradhikaar ke kuch maheene baad hota he. nae shaasha ke rajbhishek kii parampara westminster aibe main cantarbari ke archbishap dwaara kiya jaataa he.
 aitihaasik rup se uttaradhikaar ko purush-variyati sajaateey jyeshthadhikaar ke siddhanth dwaara anushaasit kiya jaataa rahaa he, jisame putron ko jyeshtha putriyon par praathamikta di jaati rahi he, tathaa ek hi ling ke jyeshtha santanon ko pehali praathamikta di jaati he. atah uttaradhikari ke ling tathaa dharm kaa uttaradhikaar par mahatvapurn prabhaav padataa he. 2011 main rashtramandal kii baithak main british pradhanmantri david camerun ne yah ghoshnaa kii thi ki tamaam rashtramandal pradesh purush praathamikta kii parampara ko samaapt karne ke liye raji ho gaye hai, tathaa bhavishya ke shaskaon par catholic vyaktiyon se vivaah karne par roak ko bhi radd karne par sab kii sweekruti le lee gayi thi. parantu kyonki british adhirat carch of england ke pramukh bhi hote hai, atah catholic vyaktiyon ko singhasana uttaradhikrut karne par roak lagane vaale vidhaan ko yathaast rakhaa gaya he. is vidheyak ko 23 april 2013 ko shahi sweekruti mili, tathaa saare rashtramandal pradeshon main sambandhit vithan paarit hone ke pashchaat march 2015 ko yah lagu hua.
 1937 or 1953 main regency ke adhiniyamon ke mutabik, bijli samraat ke 18 saal hasil nahin kiya he, yaa to shaareerik yaa maansik rup se aksham, baahar kiya janaa chahie regent ke rup main. ginni ke pati yaa patni,: viklaangata nimnlikhit vyaktiyon ke kam se kam teen kii pushti kerani hogi lord chancelar, house of comons ke spikar, bhagwaan ucch vakil, or scrol ke rakshak. regency ko puraa karne ke liye, yah bhi ek hi teen vyaktiyon kii ek ghoshnaa kii aavashyaktaa he.
 jab aavashyak ho regency, uttaradhikaar kii uchit line ke baad regent ban jaataa he; sansadeey matdaan yaa kuch or ilaaj kii jarurat he. regent, british naagarikta he, or britain kaa nivasee hona. in niyamon ke anusaar, keval regent bhavishya tha jorge chaturth, jo rajya karata rahaa jab unke pita jorge III pagal ho gaya tha .
 haalanki, 1953 main regency ke adhinium kahata he ki agar rani ke uttaradhikari regency kii jarurat he, rajkumaar philip, edinburgh ke duk regent he. regency rani kii aavashyaktaa hogi, to regent board kii line main agle ho jayega . asthaayee shaareerik viklaangata yaa rajya se anupasthiti ke dauraan ginni ke apane kaaryon pratinidhi saktaa salaahkaar rajya, pati yaa sabase acha chaar uttaradhikaar kii line par pehle. rajya kii aavashyaktaaon ke salaahkaar regent ke rup main vahi kar rahe hai. vartmaan main, paanch rajyon ke parshadon:
 sansad, samprabhu ke adhikansh sarkari kharchon ke liye raashi sansadeey anudaan tathaa saarvajanik dhan dwaara pradaan karata he, jise "naagarik suchi" kehete hai. tathaa ek vaarshik anudaan, shahi nivaason ke rakrakhaav tathaa rani kii aadhikarik yatraaon ke liye bhi aavantit kii jaati he. karmchaariyon kii laagat, rajya kaa daura, aupchaarik patibaddhtaon or aadhikarik manoranjan sahit zyaadaatar kharchon ke liye dhan kii poorti naagarik suchi dwaara hi ho jaati he. yah raashi sansad dwaara 10 varshon kii avadhi ke liye nirdhaarit kii jaati he.
 varsh 1760 tak shaasha kii vittiya aavashyaktaayen, vanshanugat rajasav, mukutiy sampadaaon ke laabh, dwaara puri hoti thi. 1760 main raja jorge tritiy ne apane vanshanugat rajwan kaa parityag civil list ke liye karne kii sahmati de di, jo varsh 2012 tak rahaa. varttman samay main mukutiyaa esteton se ai mudra kii maatra civil list yaa adhirat ko pradaan kiye jane vaale anya anudaanon se bhi adhik he. is prakaar 2007-08 ke bich crown esteton ne rajkosh main £200 million kii vruddhi karwaai, jabaki sansad dwaara 40 lakh pound kaa bhugtan kiya gaya tha. atah 7.3 arab pound kii sampada ke saath mukutiy sampadaayen britain ke sabase badi zameendaaron main se ek he, yah sari sampatti nyaas ke antargat raakhi gayi hai or samprabhu swechha se inkaa sauda nahin kar sakate hai. 2012 ke baad se sansadeey anudaan or naagarik suchi ko mila kar ek sankukt samprabhu anudaan se badal diya gaya he.
 mukutiy sampada kii tarah hi lankaster kii dachi kii bhoomi va sampattiyaan bhi shahi nyaas ke antargat raakhi gayi he. yah sab shahi bhatte kaa hissaa he. is dachi dwaara utpann rajasav ko shaasha ke "nijee kharchon" ke liye upyog kiya jaataa he, jo naagarik suchi dwaara vahan nahi kiye jate hai. isi prakaar cornwall kii dachi ek kshetra he, jise shaasha ke jyeshtha putr ke vishwash main, unke nijee kharchon hetu, aavantit kii gayi he. samprabhu, mulya vardhit kar jaise apratyaksh karon ke paatr he, or 1993 se iskar tathaa puunji laabh kar ke bhi. is sandarbh main, naagarik suchi or sansadeey anudaan ko aay nahin mana jaataa he, kyonki unhi aadhikarik kaaryon ke liye diya jaataa he.
 rani kii kul sampatti ke anumaan kaa, is baat ke aadhaar par kii, anumaan lagane main keval vyaktigat sampatti ko gina jaa rahaa he yaa shaasha ke dwaara nyaas main yojit sampatti ko bhi liya jaa rahaa he, ke aadhaar par kaaphi fark pad saktaa he. udhaaharan ke liye, shahi sangrah adhirat kii nijee sampatti nahin he, lekin "crown collection trust", ek charity, dwaara anushaasit kiya jaataa he. forbs patrika kii 2008 ke anumaan main rani kii nijee sampatti ko $ 650 million bataaya gaya tha, parantu iske koi aadhikarik aankade uplabdh nahin hai. 1993 main lord chemberlen ne kaha tha kii press main ghoshit, maharani kii nijee sampatti kaa £100 million kaa anumaan "behad atiranjit tha."
 adhirat kaa landan main aadhikarik nivas buckingham mahal he, jo aadhikarik nivas hone ke alaava adhiktar rajkeey va shahi samarohon kaa bhi mukhya sthal he. iske atirikt ek or shahi nivas he, windsar kasal, jo vishwa kaa vrihattam adhyasit mahal he, jise mukhyatah saptahik chhuttiyon, easter, ityadi jaise maukon par upyog kiya jaataa he. scotland main samprabhu kaa aadhikarik nivas haulirud palace he, jo edinburgh main avasthit he. ise samprabhu dwaara, apni scotland yatra par upyog kiya jaataa he. paramparanusaar, samprabhu varsh main kamasekam ek saptaah ke liye haulirudehouse main nivas karte hai.
 aitihaasik taur par angl samprabhu kaa mukhya nivas westminster kaa mahal tathaa tower of london hua karata tha, jab tak henry ashtam ne whiteul ke mahal ko dabil kar liya. 1698 main bheeshan aag ke chalate whiteul mahal tabah ho gaya, jiske baad rajpariwaar saint james palace main shift ho gaya. haalaanki 1837 main samprabhu ke mukhya nivas ke rup main buckingham palace ne saint james palace kii jagah le lee, parantu aaj bhi saint james palace ko varisht mahal hone kaa darj praapt he, or aaj bhi vah reetispad awaas he. yah mahal aaj bhi uttaradhikaar parishad kaa sabha-sthal he tathaa ise rajpariwaar ke anya sadasyon dwaara bhi upyog kiya jaataa he.
 anya shahi nivaason main, clarence house or kencington palace shaamil hai. ye mahal rajmukut ke hai, or inhen mukut dwaara bhavi shaskaon ke liye rakhaa gaya he. samprabhu, swechha se in mehalon kaa sauda nahin kar sakate hai. iske alaava norfok kaa sandringham palace or aberdinshir kaa bellmoral kasal, rani kii nijee sampattiyaan hai.
 united kingdom kaa shahi kulank chaturanshiy maanak he, jisamen:"pratham or tritiy chaukhand lohit prishthbhumi par teen pit singhon pradarshit hai; dwitiya chaukhand main pit prishthbhumi par do dhari rekhankan se ghira hua ek lohit singh, centre main; tathaa chaturth chaukhand nil prishthbhumi par ek harp ko pradarshit kaary he", samarthak ke rup main ek singh or ek iksingha he, or ek pattee par francisi bhashaa main dhyeyvakya:"Dieu et mon droit" pradarshit he. kavach ko gherta hua ek garter he jisapar garter ke shaurykram kaa dhyey likhaa hota he:"Honi soit qui mal y pense". scotland main shaasha bhinn prakaar kaa kulank kaa upyog karte hai, usamen, pratham or chaturth khand scotland ko pradarshit karte hai, tathaa dwitiya main england or tritiy khand main ireland pradarshit hota he. isamen do dhyey pradarshit hote hai, neeche scotland ke dhyey "In Defens God me Defend" kaa ek chota bhaag "In Defens" pradarshit he tathaa thisal ke kram kaa dhyeyvakya "Nemo me impune lacessit"; samarthak ke rup main ek singh or ek unicorn kavach samet do lambii talavaaron kaa samarthan karte hai, jinpar england tathaa scotland ke dhvaj fahre cote hai.
 shaasha kaa aadhikarik dhvaj shahi maanak he, jisame shahi kulank ko pradarshit kiya jaataa he. is maanak ko samprabhu kii mezbaani kar rahe bhawan, pot, vimaan yaa vaahan par pradarshit kiya jaataa he, logon ko samprabhu kii upastithi se aagaah karne ke liye. shahi maanak ko kabhi bhi ardhdand par kabhi nahin fahraaya jaataa he, kyonki aisa kabhi bhi nahin hota he kii shaasha anupasthit ho, kyonki jaise hi ek shaasha kii mrityu hoti he, vaise hi yuvaraj shaasha ban jate hai.
 jab shaasha apane mahal main upasthit nahin hote he, tab buckingham palace, windsor kasal or sanderingham house main british dhvaj fahraaya jaataa he, or scotland main sthit mehalon, haulirud pales or balamoral pales main scotland ke shahi maanak ko fahraaya jaataa he.
