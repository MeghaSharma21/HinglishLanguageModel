 aana sagar jhil, anasagar jhil yaa ana sagar jhil bharat main rajasthan rajya ke ajmer sambhag main sthit ek kritrim jhil he. 
 is jhil kaa nirmaan prithviraj chouhan ke pitamah arunoraj yaa anaji chouhan ne barahavin shatabdi ke madhya karavaaya tha. anaji dwaara nirmit karawaaye jane ke kaaran hi is jhil kaa namakaran ana sagar yaa aana sagar prachlit mana jaataa he.
 jhil ke liye jalsanbharan kaa kaary sthaneey aabaadi dwaara karavaaya gaya tha. 1637 isvi main shahjahan ne jhil ke kinare lagbhag 1240 feet lambaa katahara lagavaaya or paal par sangemarmar kii paanch baradariyon kaa nirmaan karavaaya. jhil ke praangan main sthit daulat baag kaa nirmaan jahangir dwaara karavaaya gaya jise subhash udyaan ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he. aana sagar kaa failaav lagbhag 13 kilometre kii paridhi main vistrut he.
