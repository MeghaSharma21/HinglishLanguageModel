 palamu main do durg hai jo vartmaan samay main kuch jarjar ho chuke he, lekin ye aaj bhi is kshetra kii shaan hai or paryatakon ke liye pramukh aakarshan centre bhi. in kilon ko 'purana kila' or 'nayaa kila' kaha jaataa he.ye durg chiro rajvansh ke raajaaon kii dein hai. is kile ko raja medini raay ne banwaaya tha.
 palamu ke mukhyalay medininagar ke dakshin disha main koyal nadi ke tat par avasthit shahpur kila palamu itihaas ke saikadon varshon kii smritiyon ko samete badhaal avasthaa main khadaa he. sthaneey log is kile ko 'chalaani kila' bhi kehate hai. itihaas ke anusaar iskaa nirmaan 1766-1770 ke aasapaas cherovansheey raja gopal raay ne karavaaya tha or chero satta ke avsaan kaal ke dauraan palamu kaa samrajya ko yaheen se sanchalit kiya jaataa tha. san 1771 main palamu kila par angrejon ke akraman or niyantranaadhin hone ke baad shahpur kila hi raja kaa nivas sthaan bana.
 kaha jaataa he ki is kile se surang ke rup main ek gupt maarg palamu kila tak jaataa tha. is kile main chero vansh ke antim shaasha raja chudaaman raay or unki patni chandravati devi kii pratimaa sthapit kii gai he. shahpur mukhya maarg par sthit is kile se koyal nadi samet medininagar kshetra ke manohari prakritik drishy ko dekha ja saktaa he. kuch varsh purv is kile ko puratathvik mahatv kaa ghoshit kiya gaya hai lekin iske baavjood bhi yah cherovansheey samrajya ke vaibhavshaalee itihaas kaa mook sakshi apni durdasha par sisakata najar aata he.
