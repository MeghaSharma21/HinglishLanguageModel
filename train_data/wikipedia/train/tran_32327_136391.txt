 ismail darbar hindi filmon ke ek prasiddh sangeetkaar hai. vah big bos 3 ke pratibhaagi bhi rahe the. 
 ismail kai varshon tak rajesh roshan, nadeem-shravan, lakshmikant-pyarelal vagairah ke liye vaayalin-vadak rahe. unki sangeetkaar ke rup main pehali film 1995 kii rangila hone vaali thi. lekin kai saalon baad unako sanjay lila bhansaali ne 1999 kii apni film ham dil de chuke sanam main kaam diya. iskaa sangeet bahut saraahaa gaya or unhen sarvshereshth sangeetkaar kaa rashtriya film puraskaar praapt hua tha. iske baad sanjay kii 2002 kii film devdas kaa bhi bahut sangeet safal rahaa tha. iske baad ismail ne kai filmein kii lekin unhen safalataa nahin mili.
