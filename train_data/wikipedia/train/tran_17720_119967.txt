 rashtrakut rajvansh ke rajyakaal main vishaal maatra main kannada, sanskrit, praakrit tathaa apbhransh sahitya rachaa gaya.
 is rajvansh kaa prasiddh shaasha amoghvarsh pratham ne sabase puraani gyaat kannada kavita kavirajmarg ke kuch khandon kii rachana kii thi. unke shashan kaal main jain ganitagyon or vidwaanon ne 'kannada' va 'sanskrit' bhashaaon ke sahitya main mahatvapurn yogadan diya. pushpadant ne apbhransh main anek granthon kii rachana kii.
