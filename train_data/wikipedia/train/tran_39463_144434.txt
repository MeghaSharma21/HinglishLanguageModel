 kabhi alavida naa kahana 2006 main bani hindi bhashaa kii naatakeey premakahani film he. iskaa nirdesan karan johar ne kiya or dharma productions binar ke tahat ise nirmit kiya gaya. isamen pramukh bhoomikaaon main shahrukh khan, rani mukherjee, abhishek bachchan or priti jinta hai jabaki sahaayak bhoomika ko amitabh bachchan or kiran kher dwaara nibhaayaa gaya he. 
 kahani adhiktar neuyork shahar main guzarati he or vyabhichaar ke vishyon kii padtaal karti he. yah antarrashtriya str par vishesh rup se sanyukt rajya america main safalataa thi. yah relies ke samay videshon main sabase zyada kamai karne vaali bhartiya film ban gai.
 maaya ek anaath he jo apane bachapan ke dost rishi talwaar se shaadi kar rahi he. donon ko rishi ke pita samarjit urf sam ne paala he. dev saran sanyukt rajya america main ek safal footbal khilaadi he. vah neuyork shahar main apni patni riya, apane bete arjuna or apni maa kamaljit urf camel ke saath rahata he. rishi se shaadi karne se pehle maaya ne dev se mulaakaat kii. yadhyapi dev or maaya ajanabi hai, phir bhi ve ek duusare se judaa hua mahasoos karte hai. dev ek car se maaraa jaataa he or sthaayee rup se apane pair ko chot pahunchaata he.
 chaar saal baad, dev ab footbal na khilne main asamarthta ke kaaran chidchida or dweshpurn ho gaya he or riya ke safal kariyar ko dekhakar vah usase kam mahasoos karata he. maaya bhi isi tarah kii sthiti main he. usne paaya he ki vah baanjh he or rishi ke prati koi pyaar mahasoos nahin karti kyonki vah maanati he ki vah swaarthee he. dev or maaya ne sam or camel ko ek phool kii dukaan main gale lagate hua dekha. sam or camel ne samajhaayaa ki donon ne apane pati-patni ko kho diya he. unhone dosti ke madhyam se apane akelepan ko dur karne main ek-duusare kii madad karne kaa faisla kiya he. dev or maaya apane sambandhit rishton ke tanaav se brake lene kaa faisla karte hai. aisa karne main, ve khud ko ek duusare ke prati aakarshith pate hai.
