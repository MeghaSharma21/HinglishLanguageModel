 antarveshan kaa arth he kisi ganitiya sarini main die hue maanon ke bich vaale maanon ko gyaat karnaa. angreji shabd interpolation kaa shabdic arth he - 'bich main shabd badhanaa' yaa kisi ke varg yaa samooh ke bich main usee tarah kii or koi cheez baahar se laakar jamaana, baithaana yaa lagaana..
 maan lijie, nimnlikhit sarini di hui he:
 prashn yah he ki x ke sarinibaddh maanon ke bich ke kisi maan ke liye f kaa maan kis prakaar nikaala jaay. is prashn kaa uttar antarveshan siddhant dwaara miltaa he. antarveshan ke viksit siddhant se kisi sarini dwaara nirdisht falan kaa avakal gunak athwa do simaaon ke bich kaa anukal nikaalnaa bhi sambhav he.
 antarveshan kii kai vidhiyaan hai. upyukt vidhi chunate samay unki parishuddhta, dakshata, kam se kam kitne bindu chaiye, aadi baton par dhyaan diya jaataa he.
