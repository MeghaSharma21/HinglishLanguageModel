 trol internet slang main aise vyakti ko kaha jaataa he jo kisi online samudaay jaise charchaa forum, chat rum yaa blog aadi main bhadakaau, apraasangik tathaa vishay se asambandhit sandesh preshit karata he. inkaa mukhya uddeshy anya prayoktaaon ko vaanchhit bhaavnaatmak pratikriya hetu uksanyathva vishay sambandhit saamaanya charchaa main gadabadi failaana hota he.hamalavar preshak ke alaava sangya trol kaa prayog bhadakaau sandesh ke liye bhi ho saktaa he, jaise "tumne shandaar trol post kiya". yadhyapi shabd trol tathaa isse sambaddh kaary trolling mukhyatah internet sambhaashan se jude hai, parantu haalia varshon main media ke avdhaan ne in label kaa prayog online duniya se baahar bhi bhadakaau evam uksaau kaaryon se jod diya he. udhaahranswarup haalia media reporton ne trol kaa prayog "aisa vyakti jo internet par shraddhanjali dene vaali websiton ko sambandhit pariwaron ko dukh dene ke liye nasht kare" hetu kiya he.
