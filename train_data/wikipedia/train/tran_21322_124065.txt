 arkavanshi
 bhaarshiv shaasha bhagwaan surya evam bhagwaan shiv ke upaask the. ye bhaarshiv rajbhar hote hen baharich main suryakund par sthit bhagwaan surya kemurti kii ve puja karte the.
 bhaarshiv raajaaon kaa samrajya pashchim main mathura or purv main kaashi se bhi kuch pare tak avashya vistrut tha. is saare pradesh main bahut se uddhaar karne ke kaaran ganga-yamuna ko hi unhone apana rajchihn banaaya tha. ganga-yamuna ke jal se apana rajbhishek kar in raajaaon ne bahut kaal baad in pavitra nadiyon ke gaurav kaa punaruddhar kiya tha. bhaarshiv naam se nayaa rajvansh udai hua tha. saadhaaran janata inako agyanta se bhar kehane lage shaiva dharm kaa anusharan karte huye vaidic kaal main rajbhar kshatriya naam prachlit hue.
