 abisidi ek hindi bhashaa main bani bhartiya bollywood film he. yah 8 farvari 2013 ko cinema gharon main pradarshit hui.
 yah kahani ek nrity pratiyogita par aadhaarit he. jahangir khan, ke saath anban hone ke baad vishnu, apni naukari chhod mumbai se chennai chale jate hai. vahaan uske dost gopi use waapas mumbai jane kii baat samajhataa he. iske baad vishnu apana swayan kaa ek nrity samooh banane kaa sochataa he, jo dance dil se naamak pratiyogita jeet sake. use iske liye do log milte hai. pehla di or doosraa rocky milte hai. lekin keval rocky hi usase seekhney aata he or di vahaan se chale jaataa he. iske kuch hi samay main chandu uske samooh se judata he.
 jab antim pari hoti he to jahangir apane samooh ke logon ko duusare samooh ke dwaara kiye gaye nrity ko pehle hi karne bolta he. jisase duusare ke paas koi bhi chaara na rahe or vah is pratiyogita se hat jaaen. jab jahangir ke dal ke nrityak unke dwaara banaae gaye nrity ko karte hai to kuch samay ke liye vah kuch bhi nahin soch pate or baad main ganesh chaturthi ke samay ke nrity ko karte hai or jeet jate hai.
