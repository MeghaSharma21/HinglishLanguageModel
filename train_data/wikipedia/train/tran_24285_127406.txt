 siddiki, siddiqi yaa siddiqui ek bahu-prachlit muslim upnaam he. iskaa arth islam ke pehle khalipha abu bakr se sambandhit yaa unke vansh se jude log hai. islam kaa mulat: prachaar evam failaav isi jati ke logo ne shuru kiya. haalanki is upnaam ke log vishwa kai jagahon par paae jate hai, lekin in logon kii sankhya bharat-pakistan main adhik he.
