 anu ke liye sabase pracheen granth upanishdon me anuwaad athwa anu kaa ullekh kam hi miltaa he, isi kaaran se anuwaad kaa ullekh veydant sutro me kam hi miltaa he, kyonki unki darshanik udgam bhoomi upanishad hi he, anuwaad kaa ullekh sankhya or yoga me bhi nahin miltaa he, anuwaad vaisheshik darshan kaa ek pramukh ang he, or nyaay ne bhi ise manyata pradaan kii he, jain dharm ne ise sweekaar kiya he, or kii vyakhya ke nausar aajeevikon ne bhi isko manyata di he. praarambhik buddha dharm isse parichit nahin he, buddha granthon main iskaa ullekh nahin hua he, kintu vaibhashik evam sautrantik isko purna maanane vaale the, ke anusaar pratham chaar dravya vastuon kaa sabase chota antim kan jiskaa aage vibhaajan nahin ho saktaa he, anu kehalaata he. isame gandh, sparsh, pariman, sanyog, gurutv, dravatv, veg aadi gun samaaye rahete hai. atyant sookshm hone ke kaaran iskaa indriya janya pratyaksh nahin hota he, isaki sookshmata kaa abhas karne ke liye kuch sthool drashtaant diye gayi hai-"jalantargate bhanau yatsukshaman drshyate raj:, tasya shashtitamo bhaag: parmaanu: sa ucchyate, balagrashatasyabhagasya shatdha kalpitasya c", arthath-ghar ke bheetar chhidron se aate hue surya prakaash ke bich main udne vaale kan kaa saathvaan bhaag;athwa royen ke antim sire kaa hajaarwaan bhaag parmaanu kaha jaataa he, vyavahaarat: vaisheshikon kii shabdaawalee main anu sabase chota aakaar kehalaata he, anusanyog se dwaygunak, trasarenu, aadi bade hote chale jate hai, jainamtanusaar aatma evam desh ko chhodkar sabhi vastuein pudgalon se utpann hoti hai, sabhi pudgalon ke parmaanu athwa anu hote hai, prayek anu ek pradesh yaa sthaan gherta he, pudgal sthool yaa sooksham hota he, jab yah sooksham rup me rahata he, to aganit anu ek sthool anu ko ghere rahete hai, anu shaashvat he, prayek anu main ek prakaar kaa ras, gandh, rup or do prakaar kaa sparsh rahata he, yah visheshtayen sthir nahin he, or na hi bahut se anuon ke liye nishchit hai, do athwa adhik anu jo chikanahat yaa khurdarapan ke gun main bhinn hote he, aapas main milakar skandh banaate hai, prayek vastu ek hi prakaar ke anusamooh se nirmit hoti he, anu apane andar gati kaa vikaas kar saktaa he, or yah gati itani teevr ho sakati he, ki ek kshan me vah vishwa ke ek chor se duusare chor tak pahunch sakati he. badalon ke andar pani ke anuon kaa pratwaad iskaa udhaaharan he, ati dhanatmak samvednaa ke chalate hi vah rinatmak khoj ke liye chalyman hone par prithvi ke nikat aakar apane ko patalit kar shunya ho jaataa he, usee prakaar se vyakti vishesh ke dwaara lagaatar akashiya vidyut tarangon ko aakarshith karne ke prati apni jiva or maansik gati ke dwaara athwa sharir ke vibhinn bhaagon ko lagaataar udwelit karke apni aantarik shaktiyon ko jaagruth kar letaa he, or apane ko aashcharya vyakti kii paribhaasha main ginati kar detaa he.
