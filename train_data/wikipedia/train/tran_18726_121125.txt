 kaakani skool, scotland ke saahityalochakon dwaara landan ke ek vishisht lekhakvarg ke liye prayukt ek tiraskaarpurn abhivyakti thi. "kaakani' shabd kaa pragog sabase pehle angreji ke prasiddh samalochak loclert ne keats kii rachanaaon ke liye kiya tha. tatpashchaat 1818 i. "blakeuds magazine" main prakashit ek dharawahik lekh kaa sheershak "kaakani skool" ke naam se saamne aayaa. ukt patrika ne shelli tathaa hazlit ko bhi kaakani lekhakon kii shrenee main rakhaa tha, lekin akraman, mukhyatya le hunt tathaa keats par hi kiya gaya tha. kaakani lekhakon ko lekar jo kuch bhi likhaa gaya, usamen na keval ukt lekhakon ke krititv apitu vyaktitv ko bhi nimna tathaa kutsit bataaya gaya he.
