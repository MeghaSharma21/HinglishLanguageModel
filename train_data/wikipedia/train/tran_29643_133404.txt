 australian shodhakartaaon ne vishwa kaa pehla scanning helium microscope nirmit kiya. is sambandh main uniwarsity of newcastle ke vaigyanikon ne may 2016 ke tisare saptaah main ghoshnaa kii thi. 
 paaramparik microscope main prakaash kaa upyog kiya jaataa he jisase sample ko nuksaan hota he. saechaiem imaging takanic he jo helium aayan beam par kaary karata he. 
 saechaiem helium ins main bahut adhik chamak evam kam di brogli tarangadairghy kaa prayog karata he. isse, jo data praapt hota he vah paaramparik microscope dwaara praapt nahin kiya ja saktaa.
