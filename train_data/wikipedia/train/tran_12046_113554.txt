 
 ghulaam ali pakistan ke ek gajal gayak tathaa hindustani shastreey sangeet ke patiala gharaane ke jane maane gayak tathaa sangeetkaar hai. inkaa janm 1940 main sialkot ke paas hua tha. inhone ustad bade ghulaam ali khan sahab se talim lee he. ve apni gajalon main praayah khud sangeet dete hai. iske alaava bhi unhone dusron kii surabaddh gajalen bhi gaai hai.
