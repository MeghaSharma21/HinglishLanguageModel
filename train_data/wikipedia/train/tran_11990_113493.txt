 lakshminarayan gupt madhya pradesh ke ek rajaneta tathaa saamaajik kaaryakarta hai. 'nanna ji' jaise netaa virale hote hai. ve paanch baar vidhayak or do baar cabinet mantri rah chuke hai. iske baad bhi unke paas na to mobile he, na hi phone. gaadi kaa to sawaal hi nahin he
 umr 100 baras lekin hausala dekhne vaale ko dang kar detaa he. saadagi aisi ki kisi ko bhi mohit kar de. sahajata, saralata, eemaandaaree va vinamrata jaise gunon ke chalate dushman bhi unka kaayal ho jaataa he. kisi ke bhi sukh-dukh main shaamil hona, unke vyavhaar kaa ahm hissaa he. ve ek hi nirvachan kshetra se 5 baar vidhayak va do baar cabinet mantri rahe hai, lekin iske baad bhi na to koi mobile rakhte hai na awaas par telephone or chamchamati car dur kii baat. ek pushtaini makaan he jahaan aaj bhi log apni-apni samasyaon ko lekar pahunchate hai or ve samasyayon ke niraakaran ke liye jiladhikari karyaalay se lekar mukhyamantri karyaalay tak ke chakkar lagaate hue dikhaai de jate hai.
 isagarh main 6 june 1918 ko janme shri lakshminarayan gupta ke pita kaa naam shri pannalaal gupta tha. gwalior rajya main vakil rahe shivpuri jile ke pichor kshetra ke 99 varshiya lakshminarayan gupta ko kshetra kii janata nannaji naam se bulaati he. swatantraaa ke purv 1944 main unka saarvajanik jivan hindu mahasabha se prarambh hua. 1945 main gwalior rajya ke prajasabha ke nirvachan main vijayi hue. hindu mahasabha ke pratyaasi chanaavani ke dewan varajor singh ke sahyogi kii bhoomika main kshetra kii janata ne inhen janaa, mana. isi varsh pa. ramchandra sharma “vir” ne hindu jaagran kaa bigul bajaayaa. sant panchaganvkar ne pichor main vishaal hindu jaagran mahayajna kiya jisamen 30 hajaar se adhik shraddhalu jan sammilit hue. gwalior state ke tatkaaleen maharaj jivajirao sindhiya ke pratinidhi swarup panvar sahab yajna main upasthit hue. 1947 main shri lakshminarayan gupta hindu mahasabha kii rashtriya kaaryakarini ke sadsy the.
