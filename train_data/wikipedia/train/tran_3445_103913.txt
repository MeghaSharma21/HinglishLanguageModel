 forensik keetvigyaan  vigyaan kaa kshetra he jisamen ham keet or anya sandhipad praaniyon ke jivan ke bare main aapraadhik maamalon kii jaanch kii jaati he. is shetra main keet, sandhipad praani, archind, goger, sahastrapad, crusteshia ke bhi jivan ke vigyaan ko shaamil kiya gaya he. aapraadhik maamalon ko sulajhane ke liye yah kshetra mukhya rup se maut kii jaanch se jude maamle sulajhane ke liye he parantu iske dwaara ham kai or maamalon kii jaanch bhi kar sakate hai jaise- zeher or dawaaiyon kii jaanch, kisi ghatana ke hone kii jagah or kisi bhi ghaav ke hone ke samay kaa bhi jaanch bhi kii ja sakati he. forensik keetvigyaan 3 up-kshetron main baanta gaya he: shahari, sangrihit utpaada or medicolegal
