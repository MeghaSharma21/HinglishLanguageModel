 karad bharat ke maharashtra rajya ke satara jile kii ek taluka he. yah nagar krishna nadi tathaa koyna nadi ke sangam par satara nagar se 45 kimi dakshin-purv main basaa he.. karad yashavanth nagari ke naam se bhi jaani jaati he. yahaan par adhiktar muslim dharm ke log rahete he saath hi bahut sankhya main masjid bhi bane hue hai. karad pune se lagbhag 240 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he. 
 is nagar kaa swytt shashan 1885 i. main aarambh hua or ab yah ek suvyavasthit nagarpalika dwaara shasit hota he. yahan kii baudhdhakalin guphaayen, musalmaan-kaaleen masjiden or naveen mandir aakarshan ke vishesh centre hai. kuch log ise 'kardaah' yaa 'karhakada' ke naam se bhi jaanate hai.
 karad main kai jagah puraane chote-chote mandir mil jate he. karad ke chaavdi chowk main ek krishnabai naamak ek udyaan bhi jahaan par yashwantrao chavaan kii samadhi bani hui ,jahaan par dinabhar log aate-jate rahete he.
 karad nagari se do nadiyaan - krishna nadi or koyna naamak nadiyaan guzarati he. yahaan nadi ke aasapaas hameshaa logon kii bheed lagi rahati he.
 karad main 2 cinemaghar he is kaaran yahaan moolbhoot suvidhaon kii koi kami nazar nahin aati.
 karad main pracheen gufaaen bhi he ,saath hi inke aasapaas udyaan bhi kaaphi dekhne ko milte he.
 karad ek tehsil he saath hi yahaan nagarpalika bhi he, evam shri chhatrapati shivaji naam kii sabji mandi bhi he.
