 azaan yaa adaan. islam main muslim samudaay apane din bhar kii paanchon namaazon ke liye bulaane ke liye unche swar main jo shabd kahe jate hai, unhen azaan kehete hai.
 azaan suunaa kar logon ko masjid kii taraf bulaane vaale ko muazzin kehete hai. 
 madina taiyaba main jab namaaz bazmaat dene ke liye masjid banaai gai to jarurat mahasoos hui ki logon ko jamaat kaa samay kareeb hone kii soochna dene kaa koi tarika tay kiya jaae. rasulullah ne jab is bare main sahaba ikraam se paraamarsh kiya to is bare main chaar prastaav saamne aae:
 uparokt sabhi prastaav anhazarat ko gaiyr muslimon se milte julate hone ke kaaran pasand nahin aae. is samasya main anhazarat or sahaba ikraam chintit the ki usee raat ek ansaari sahabi hazarat abdullah bin zayd ne swapn main dekha ki kisi ne unhen azaan or iqaamat ke shabd sikhaye hai. unhone subah savere anhazarat seva main haazir hokar apana sapana bataaya to anhazarat ne ise pasand kiya or us sapane ko allaah kii ore se sachcha sapana bataaya.
 anhazarat ne hazarat abdullah bin zayd se kaha ki tum hazarat bilal ko azaan in shabdon main padhne kii hidaayat kar do, unki awaz buland he isliye vah har namaaz ke liye isi tarah azaan diya karenge. isliye usee din se azaan kii pranali sthapit hui or is tarah hazarat bilal raziyallahu anhu islam ke pehle azaan dene vaale ke rup main prasiddh hue.
