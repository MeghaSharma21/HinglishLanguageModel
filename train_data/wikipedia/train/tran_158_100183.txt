 awaz - dil se dil tak bhartiya hindi dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran zee tivi main 10 agast 2003 se shuru hua.
 yah kahani ek hawaizhaj ke agava kiye jane par aadhaarit he. isamen mumbai se landan jane vaala ek vimaan api 106 ko kuch log agava kar lete hai. lekin vah log us par niyantran hasil nahin kar pate kyonki uske chaalak or un agava karne waalon ke madhya ladaai ho jaati he. is kaaran vimaan haadase kaa shikaar ho jaataa he.
 isamen 426 main se keval 18 log ek dweep main pahunch pate hai. us samay unke parivaar waalon ko lagata he ki sabhi kii maut ho chuki he. kuch samay ke baad vahaan dweep main bache yatriyon ko lagata he ki ve baaki kii jindagi usee dweep main hi gujaarne vaale hai, kyon ki unhen baahar apane ghar jane kaa koi bhi maarg yaa upaay nahin sujh rahaa tha. lekin baad main un sabhi ko bachaa liya jaataa he or vah sabhi apane parivaar se mil jate hai.
