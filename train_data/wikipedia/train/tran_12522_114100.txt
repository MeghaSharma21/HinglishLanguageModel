 hemchand dasgupt bhuvigyaani the. inkaa janm san 1878 main deenajpur jile main hua tha. jila skool se praarambhik shiksha praapt karke ke uparaant 1895 main aapane kalakatta presidency kalege main pravesh kiya. yahan san 1900 main aapane em. e. kii digari praapt kii. teen varsh pashchaat aapaki niyukti isi vidhyalay main demonstrator ke pad par hui. dhire dhire unnati karte hue aap isi vidhyalay main bhuvigyaan ke professor ho gaye.
 bahut si sansthaaon se aapkaa nikat sambandh tha. bhartiya vigyaan congress ke vikaas main aapane mahatvapurn yoga diya. aap uski kaaryakarini ke sadsy the tathaa san 1928 i. main uske bhuvigyaan vibhaag ke adhyaksh chune gaye. 'jiyalogical mining and metalaregical socity ov india' ke aap sansthaapakon main se the tathaa aapane uske secretary ke rup main bhi kaary kiya. kalakatta vishvavidhyalay kii vibhinn sansthaaon ke bhi aap sadsy the. inke atirikt aap 'bangeey sahitya parishad', 'aciatic socity of bengal' tathaa 'indian association for kaltiveshan ov science' ke bhi pramukh kaaryakartaaon main se the. jamshedpur main tata steel company sthapit karne main aapkaa pramukh haath tha. aap hi kii sammati se yah company jamshedpur main sthapit hui. aapkaa jivan bahut sada tha. aapkaa dehavsaan 1 janvari san 1933 ko hua.
