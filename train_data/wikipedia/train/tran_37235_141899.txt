 ringus bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke sikar jile kii ek kasbaa he.
 ringus uttari akshansh 27.37 evam purvi deshaantar 75.57 par sthit he. isaki maasat se ausat oonchaai 480 meter he.
 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar ringus kii kul jansankhya 22,928 he. purush hissaa 54% evam mahilaayein 46% hai. ringus kii sakshartaa dar 62% he jo rashtriya ausat 59.5% se kuch adhik he jisamen purush sakshartaa 75% evam mahila sakshartaa 48% he. ringus main kul jansankhya kaa 16% log 6 varsh se kam aayu ke bachche hai.
