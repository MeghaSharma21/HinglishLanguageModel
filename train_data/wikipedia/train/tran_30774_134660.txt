 baap nambari betaa das nambari 1990 main bani hindi bhashaa kii kamedi film he. pramukh bhoomikaaon main kadar khan or shakti kapur he. anya kalakaaron main jaiki shroff, farha, aditya pancholi, jamuna or anjana mumtaz shaamil hai. film safal rahi thi or 1990 kii sabase zyada kamai karne vaali filmon main se ek thi. 
 chor or thag raman apane bete prasad ko apane kadamon pe chalaata he. uske dwaara thage gaye logon or police se dur bhaagate hue vah umrgaanv main pahunchataa. vahaan uski biimaar bahan gayatri singh apane mrit pati or eemaandaar seemaa shulk adhikari pratap ke saath rahati thim. raman sthiti kaa laabh uthaata he or apni bahan ko paagalakhaane main chodta he, apane bhattije ravi ko ek train main chhod detaa he, sampatti bechata he, or adhik dhan or sampatti ekatrit karne ke ek safar par chalata he.
