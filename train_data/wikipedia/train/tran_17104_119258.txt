 sinekadki bhashaa-prayog main ek vishesh prakaar kaa metonmi alankaar hota he jisamen kisi vastu ke ek bhaag yaa ang ke naam ko puri vastu ke liye prayog kiya jaataa he. masalan agar kisi gane main apni premika kii pratiksha kar rahaa premi kahe ki 'tarasati nigahen intazaar kar raheen hai' to yah sinekadki he. vastav main puraa vyakti hi taras rahaa he lekin kyonki vah premika ke aane kii soochna apni aankhon se paaegaa isliye keval unhen 'tarasati nigahen' kahata he. isi tarah agar kisi ko apane ghar main chaar logon ke liye kamanaa ho to vah kah saktaa he ki use 'chaar pet' paalane hai. ek or udhaaharan kisi kamare main khidki khulwaane ke liye 'sheesha khol do' kahana he, haalanki khidki ke khole jane vaale bhaag main shishe ke saath-saath aksar loha yaa lakdi bhi lagi hoti he.
