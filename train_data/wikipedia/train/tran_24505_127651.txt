 bidasar bhartiya rajya rajasthan ke sikar jile kii lakshmanagarh tehsil kaa ek gaanv he. yah lakshmanagarh se 15 kimi purv main or navalgadh se 7 kimi pashchim main sthit he. isaki seemayen khinvasar, bidodi badi, bidasar aadi gaanvon se lagati hai.

 gaanv kii sarpanch shrimati dayaal pyaari hai. gaanv main 15 ward sadsy graminon dwaara chune jate hai. 
 lakshmanagarh or navalgarh ko jodne vaali sadak bidasar ho kar nikalati he.
