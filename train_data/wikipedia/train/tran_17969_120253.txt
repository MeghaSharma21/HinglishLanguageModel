 jawahar navoday vidhyalay athwa navoday vidhyalay bharat sarkaar ke maanav sansaadhan vikaas mantralay dwaara chalaai jane vaali puri tarah se awasaiya, sah shiksha, kendriya madhyamik shiksha board, nai dilli se sambhddha shikshan pariyojna he. rashtriya shiksha niti - 1986 ke antargat aise awasaiya vidyalayon kii kalpana kii gai jinhen jawahar navoday vidhyalay kaa naam diya gaya, jo sarvshereshth gramin pratibhaaon ko aage laane kaa uttam prayaas he. is pariyojna kaa pramukh lakshya gaanv-gaanv tak uttam shiksha pahuchana he. ye vidhyalay purnatah awasaiya evam nickshulk vidhyalay hote hai jahaan vidhyaarthiyon ko ni:shulk awaas, bhojan, shiksha evam khelkud saamagree uplabdh karai jaati he. har jile main ek navoday vidhyalay hota he.
 rashtriya shiksha niti 1986 ke anusaar bharat sarkaar ne jawahar navoday vidhyalay prarambh kiye the. prayek jilon main awasaiya vidyalayon kii avadharana "nai shiksha niti" 1986 ke antargat pragat hui. sarvapratham 2 aise vidhyalay prayog hetu khole gaye jo 31.03.2007 kii sthiti ke anusaar kul 565 ho gaye.
 vartmaan main jawahar navoday vidhyalay 27 rajyon or 7 sangh shasit rajyo main sanchalit hai. yah sah shiksha awasaiya vidhyalay he, jinhe ek swayatt sanghathan, navoday vidhyalay samiti ke zarie bharat sarkaar dwaara sanchalit sampurna vittiya sahaayta praapt hai. navoday vidyalayain main pravesh, jawahar navoday vidhyalay chayan pariksha ke madhyam se kaksha 6 main kiye jate hai. in vidyalayon main kaksha 8 tak shiksha kaa madhyam maatrubhaasha athwa kshetreey bhashaa hai or iske baad se ganit or vigyaan ke liye angrezi madhyam or saamaajik vigyaan ke liye hindi madhyam hai. jawahar navoday vidhyalay ke chaatr kendriya madhamik shiksha board kii kaksha 10 or 12 kii pariksha main sammilit hote hai. yaddapi in vidyalayon ke chaatro ko ni:shulk awaas, bhojan, ganavesh, evam pathyapustak pradaan kii jaati hai, parantu kaksha 9 se 12 tak ke chaatro se shulk ke rup main prati maah ru 200 /- liye jaayenge parantu anusuchit jati, janajati, balikaon shaareerik viklang, evam un chaatro se jinke abhibhavak garibi rekha ke neeche jivan yaapan karte hai, ko is shulk se chhot pradaan kii gayi hai.
 pravesh hetu kaksha 5 ke vidhyaarthiyon ke liye pravesh pariksha hoti he evam prayek jile se 80 chaatro kaa chayan kiya jaataa he. navoday vidyalayon main 75 pratishat gramin or 25 pratishat shahari bacchon ko pravesh diya jaataa he.
