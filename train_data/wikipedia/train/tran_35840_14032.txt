 laghav chihn hindi main evam devanagari lipi aadhaarit anya bhashaaon main sankshipti chihn ke rup main prayog hota he. iskaa unicode kuta maan 0970 he.
 angrezi main sankshepikaran ke liye fulstop kaa prayog kiya jaataa he, hindi main is kaary ke liye laghav chihn hota he. praayah yah chihn kunjeepatal par athwa tankan upkaran main sulabh na hone se log iske sthaan par fulstop kaa hi prayog kar lete hai jo ki ashuddh he.
 udhaaharan: 
 hindi shunya ank kaa chihn bhi isse miltaa-julata hone se kai baar log agyantavash laghav chihn ke sthaan par hindi ke shunya ank kaa prayog kar lete hai. inamen antar yah he ki ek to laghav chihn aakaar main shunya se chota hota he doosraa shunya jahaan kshaitij rup se pankti ke madhya main hota he, laghav chihn kshaitij rup se upar kii ore hota he.
