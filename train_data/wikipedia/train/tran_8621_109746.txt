 vaigyanik evam audyogic anusandhan parishad bharat kaa sabase bada vigyaan evam praudyogiki par anusandhan evam vikaas sambandhi santhaan he. isaki sthapana 1942 main hui thi. isaki 39 prayogshaalaayein evam 50 field stesan bharat paryant faile hue hai. isamen 17,000 se adhik karmchaaree karyrat hai. haalanki isaki vitt prabandh bharat sarkaar ke vigyaan evam praudyogiki mantralay dwaara hota he, phir bhi ye ek swytt sanstha he. iskaa panjikaran bhartiya sosayadi panjikaran dhara 1860 ke antargat hua he.
 vaigyanik tathaa audyogic anusandhan parishad rashtriya prayogshaalaon/sansthaanon kaa ek bahusthanik network he jiskaa mandet vigyaan or praudyogiki ke vibhinn kshetron main anuprayukt anusandhan tathaa uske parinaamon ke upyog par bal dete hue anusandhan evam vikaas pariyojnaayen prarambh karnaa he. vatarmaan main 39 anusandhan santhaan hai jinamein paanch kshetreey anusandhan prayogshaalaayein shaamil hai. inamen se kuchhek sansthaanon ne apane anusandhan kriyakalapon ko or gati pradaan karne ke liye praayogic, surveykshan kshetreey kendron kii bhi sthapana kii he tathaa vatarmaan main 16 prayogshaalaon se sambaddh aise 39 kendra kayrrat hai.
 siesaiaar kii ginati vishwa main is prakaar ke 2740 sansthaanon main 81wein sthaan par hoti he.
