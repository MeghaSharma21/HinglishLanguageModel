 munda ek bhashapariwaar he jise bharat tathaa bangladesh ke lagbhag 1 karod log bolte hai. yah austro-asian bhashaa-parivaar kii ek shakha he. iskaa arth he ki munda bhashaa vietnami bhashaa or khmer bhashaa se sambandhit he. ho, mundari or santaali is bhashasmooh kii mukhya bhashaayen hai. bharat main munda ke alaava austro-asian parivaar kii do anya shakhaayen milti hai: nicobari bhashaayen tathaa khasik bhashaayen.
