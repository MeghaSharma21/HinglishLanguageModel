 randhisar bikaner jile ke kolayat tehsil main ek chota gaanv he, yah gaav bikaner se lagbhag 40 kimi. dur he, is gaav main mulbhut suvidhaae paryaapt rup se uplabdh nahi he, ek chota sa skool he jisame bachche 8 ve tak kaa tha ek mahila savasthay centre he, gaavan kii jansankhya lagbhag 2500 he, pine ke pani ke liye ek thakur buldaan singh bhaati dwaara khudhvaya gaya kua he, isi kue se pure gaanv kii pyaas bujhati he. 
 mashhoor pakistani gayika reshma kaa janm isi gaanv main hua he, unke vanshaj aaj bhi isi gaanv main rahete he.
 is gaanv main rajput or muslmaan meghavaal, or kumhaar jaati ke log nivas karte he, in logo ke dharm or jaati alag hone ke baavjood inamen bhaichara or prem bahut he, bhagwaan inke bhaichaare or prem ko yun hi banaaye rakhe. 
 is gaanv main piraji kii majaar, thakurji kaa mandir, kerani mata kaa mandir, masjid he.
 is gaanv ke log kolayat ke vidhayak devisingh bhaati ko bahut pyaar karte he, yah ke log rajaniti main itane prabhavi nahi he
