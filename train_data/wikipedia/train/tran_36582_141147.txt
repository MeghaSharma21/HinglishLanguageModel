 khagoleey thanda dhabba akaash main ek aisa bada sthaan he jahaan par sookshmatarangi vikiran bahut kam he. antariksh main har disha main dekhne par brahmand ke dhamaakedaar janm ke samay main paidaa hua vikiran har jagah dekha ja saktaa he or isaki vajah se har jagah ausatan 2.7 calvin kaa taapmaana rahata he jo aam taur par is ausat se 18 microcalvin hi kam-zyaada hota he. khagoleey thande dhabbe main taapmaana is ausat se 70 microcalvin kam he. vaigyanikon ko iskaa thik kaaran abhi gyaat nahin he. is dhabbe ko angrezi main "si ium bi cold spot" or "dablyoo map cold spot" bhi kaha jaataa he. "dablyoo map" wilkinson sookshmtarang shodhyan ke angrezi naam par daalaa gaya he.
 is dhabbe kaa aakaar bahut bada he or 50 karod se 1 arab prakaash varsh kaa vyas rakhata he. yah akaash main srothasvini taramandal ke kshetra main nazar aata he jiskaa angrezi naam "iridanas taramandal" he. kuch vaigyanik samajhate hai ke is kshetra main thand isliye he kyonki yah ek maharikti he . isliye kabhi-kabhi is dhabbe ko "iridanas maharikti" bhi kaha jaataa he.
 khagoleey thanda dhabbe kii upastithi brahmand kii sristi kii vartmaan vaigyanik dharanaon ke liye ek chunauti he. vaigyanikon ne yahan tak kaha he ke shaayad yah hamaare brahmand or ek anya agyaat/adrishy brahmand ke darmayaan kwantum uljhaav kaa nateeja he.
