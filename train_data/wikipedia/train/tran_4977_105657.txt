 peter "peet" sigar america ke lokagayak or saamaajik kaaryakarta the. unhone 1948 main bane samooh da vivars se prasiddhi praapt kii.
 sigar kaa janm midtown manahaton ke french hospital main hua.
 peet sigar kaa 94 varsh kii aayu main 27 janvari 2014 ko nidhan ho gaya. sigar kii patni toshi ek saamaajik kaaryakarta thim. july 2013 main 91 varsh kii aayu main unki maut ho gai thi. sigar dampatti ke teen bachche hai.
