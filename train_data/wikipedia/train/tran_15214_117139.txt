 
 fnom penh ,purv naam krong chaktomuk yaa krong chaktomuk cerimongkul, dakshin purv asia ke desh cambodia kii rajdhani evam sarvaadhik jansankhya vaali nagari he. yah tonle saap evam mekong nadi ke tat par sthit he. fnom penh cambodia ke francisi upnivesh kaa se hi yahaan kii rajdhani bani hui he or raashtra ke aarthik, audyogic evam saanskrithik kendra ke rup main viksit hua he.
 kabhi asia kaa moti naam se prasiddh, yah shahar 1920 main francisi nirmit hind-china ke mohakatam nagaron main gina jaataa tha. siam rip or sihanukville ke saath saath fnom penh cambodia ke mahatvapurn vaishvik evam antardeshiy paryatak gantavyon main aata he. 1434 main sthapana kiya gaya yah nagar apane sundar evam aakarshak aitihaasik sthapaty ke liye janaa jaataa he. yahaan grand bulichard ke nearesth bahut si francisi aupniveshiy imaaraten abhi bhi dikhaai detii hai.
 tonle sap, mikang nadi evam bassak nadi ke tat par based fnom penh mahanagareey kshetra kii kul jansankhya 15 lakh he. cambodia kii kul jansankhya 1 crores 48 lakh he.
