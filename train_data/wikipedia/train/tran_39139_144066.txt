 ham kisi se kam nahin 1977 main bani hindi bhashaa kii sangitamay film he. iskaa nirmaan or nirdesan nasir hossain ne kiya. yah film "super hit" rahi thi or ab zyaadaatar aar di burman dwaara iske sangitabaddh geeton ke liye yaad kii jaati he. film main tarik khan, rishi kapur, kaajal kiran, amjad khan, jinat amaan, om shivpuri, jalaal aagaa or tom alter kirdaar nibhaate hai.
 kahani ek amir aadmi ke saath shuru hoti he jo africa main apni puri sampatti bech rahaa he or use heere main parivartit kar rahaa he. vah unhen belt main le jaataa he. unhen vashroom main dil kaa daura padataa he or apane bete rajesh ko heere dene ke liye ek yatri se anurodh karata he.
 us aadmi kaa gundon dwaara peechha kiya jaataa he jo heere ke peeche hote hai. vah unhen ek cycle ke tulbox main chhupaata he. cycle sanjay kumaar kii hoti he, jo is baat se anjaan hai ki uske cycle box main 25 karod rupye ke heere chhipe hue hai. saudagar singh heere ke peeche he. vah or uske saathi ranbir kumaar dana ne rajesh ke liye ek juthi kahani bataate hue phasaaya.
 uske baad puri sajeesh aage badhati he. rajesh ne manajit kumaar dana ke rup main naatak karke kaajal se pyaar kiya, jo apane bachapan ke dost sanjay se pyaar karti he. donon ke bich baithkon kii ek shrinkhalaa hoti he. kuch saal pehle jab bina maan kii kaajal ke pita kishororal gahare vittiya sankat main the, sanjay ke pita ne unhen aashray diya tha. wo ab behad samrudh ho gaye hai. sanjay or kaajol kaa vivaah karaane kaa vaada bhul gaye he.
 is film ke geet "kya hua tera vaada" ke liye mohammad rafee ko sarvshereshth parshv gayan ke liye filmafere puraskaar ke alaava rashtriya film puraskaar bhi mila tha. yah unka pehla or antim rashtriya film puraskaar he. geet "bachanaa i hasino" ko 2008 kii film bachanaa i hasino main sheershak ke rup main prayog kiya gaya jisamen iskaa rimix sanskaran bhi shaamil tha. 
 sabhi geet majaruh sultanpuri dwaara likhit; sara sangeet aar di burman dwaara rachit.
