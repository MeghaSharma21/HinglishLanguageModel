 scottish galic, jise kabhi kabhi galic Gàidhlig bhi kaha jaataa he ), ek celtic bhashaa he jiskaa prarambh scotland main hua. celtic bhashaa kii shakha goedlic bhashaaon main se ek galic kaa udbhav irish bhashaa kii hi tarah madhya irish se hua.
 united kingdom ke 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar kul 57,375 scotis log us vakt tak scottish galic bhashaa bol sakate the. janaganana ke nateezon ke anusaar 2001 ke baad se galic bhashaa bolne waalon kii sankhya main 1275 vaktaaon kii kami ai he. 87056 logon ne galic se sambandhit kisi bhi tarah kaa gyaan hone kii baat kahi jo ki 2001 ke 93282 ke aankanon se 6226 kam he. is kami ke baavjood is bhashaa ko bachaane kaa kaary jari he or 20 varsh se kam aayu ke galic bolne vaale vaktaaon kii sankhya main badhottaree hui he.
 scottish qwalifikeshan athovity galic bhashaa ke liye do tarah kii parikshaayen yojit karti he. ek nausikhiyon ke liye jo kisi bhi anya maanak aadhunik videshi bhashaa ke paathyakram kii tarah hoti he va doosraa mul vaktaaon ke liye jo ki angreji bhashaa ke paathyakram kii tarah hoti he.
 An Comunn Gàidhealach maukhik galic ke liye pariksha leti he or uteern karne waalon ko kaansya, chaandi va swarna card jari karti he. iske paathyakram ke bare main vistaarit jaankaree iske An Comunn kii website par uplabdh he. haalaanki in praaanpatr rakhane waalon ko har jagah scottish galic kaa gyaata nahin maan liya jaataa he, lekin phir bhi kai sansthaaon main inhen praathamikta milti he.
 tamaam scottish or irish vishvavidhyalay galic bhashaa main purnakalik pathyakram uplabdh karaate hai saath hi galic bhashaa main snaatak ke pathyakram bhi uplabdh karaate hai.nova scotia, canada main sthit saint francis xavier vishvavidhyalay, galic cholez of celtic orts & crafts, kep breton vishvavidhyalay, hilands & islands vishvavidhyalay, moscow kaa moscow vishvavidhyalay jaise tamaam vidhyalay galic bhashaa ke paathyakram uplabdh karaate hai.
