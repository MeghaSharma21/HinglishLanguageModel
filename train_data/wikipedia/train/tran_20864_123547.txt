 kaaldarshak ek pranali he jo samay ko vyavasthit karne ke liye prayog kii jaati he. kaaldarshak kaa prayog saamaajik, dharmik, vanijyik, prashasnic yaa anya kaaryon ke liye kiya ja saktaa he. yah kaary din, saptaah, maas, yaa varsh aadi samyaavadhiyon ko kuch naam dekar kii jaati he. prayek din ko jo naam diya jaataa he vah "tithi" kehalaati he. praaia: maas or varsh kisi khagoleey ghatana se sambandhit hote hai kintu yah sabhi calandaron ke liye jaruri nahin he. anek sabhyataaon or samaajon ne apane prayog ke liye koi na koi kaaldarshak nirmit kiye the jo praaia: kisi duusare kaaldarshak se vyutpann the. 
 kaaldarshak ek bhautik vastu bhi he. kaaldarshak shabd bahudhaa isi arth main prayukt hota he.
