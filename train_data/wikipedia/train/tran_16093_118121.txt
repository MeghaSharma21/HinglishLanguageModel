 sansadeey pranali loktantric shashan kii vah pranali he jisamen karypalika apni loktantric vaidhata vidhayikta se praapt karti he tathaa vidhayikta ke prati uttaradaayi hoti he. is prakaar sansadeey pranali main karypalika or vidhayika paraspar sambandhit hote hai. is pranali main rajya kaa mukhiya tathaa sarkaar kaa mukhiya alag-alag vyakti hote hai. bharat main sansadeey shashan pranali he. iske vipareet adhyakshiya pranali main praayah rajya kaa adhyaksh sarkaar kaa bhi adhyaksh hota he. isse bhi adhik mahatvapurn yah he ki adhyakshiya pranali main karypalika apni loktantric vaidhata vidhayika se nahin praapt karti.
