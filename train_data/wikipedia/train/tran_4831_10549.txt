 maharaja ramsingh dwitiya jo 1843 isvi main maatr 16 varsh kii umr main jaipur ke raja bane the. naaballig hone ke kaaran british sarkaar ne inhen vayask hone tak apane sangrakshan main le liya tha. inke samay main major jon ludlo ne janvari 1843 isvi main jaipur kaa prashasan sambhaala tha. isane sati pratha ,das pratha ,kanya vadh or dahej pratha aadi par roak lagane ke aadesh jari kiye the. ye jaipur ke shaasha jaisingh tritiy ke putr the. 
 1857 ke swatantraaa andolan main maharaj ramsingh ne angrejon kii bharpoor sahaayta kii thi. is kaaran angrej sarkaar ne inhen sitar - e - hind kii oopaadhi pradaan kii. 
 inke samay main san 1845 main jaipur main maharaja college tathaa sanskrit college kaa nirmaan hua tha.
