 sharia, jise shariya kaanoon or islamic kaanoon bhi kaha jaataa he, islam main dharmik kaanoon kaa naam he. is kaanoon kii paribhaasha do sroton se hoti he. pehali islam kaa dharmagranth koran he or doosraa islam ke pigambar mohammad dwaara di gai misaalen hai . islamic kaanoon ko banane ke liye in do sroton ko dhyaan se dekhakar niyam banaae jate hai. is kaanoon banane kii prakriya ko 'fiqh' kaha jaataa he. sharia main bahut se vishyon par mat he, jaise ki swasthya, khanpan, puja vidhi, vrath vidhi, vivaah, jurm, raajnneeti, arthvyavastha ityadi.
 musalmaan yah to maanate hai ki sharia paramatma kaa kaanoon he lekin unamem is baat ko lekar bahut antar he ki yah kaanoon kaise paribhaashit or lagu hona chahie. sunni samudaay main chaar bhinn fiqh ke nazariye hai or shiya samudaay main do. alag deshon, samudaayon or samskritiyon main bhi sharia ko alag-alag dhangon se samajha jaataa he. sharia ke anusaar nyaay karne vaale parmparik nyaayadhishon ko 'qaazi' kaha jaataa he. kuch sthanon par 'imam' bhi nyaayadhishon kaa kaam karte hai lekin anya jagahon par unka kaam keval adhyayan karnaa-karana or dharmik netaa hona he. islam ke anuyaayiyon ke liye sharia islamic samaaj main rahane ke taur-tareeqon, niyamon or kaayadon ke rup main kaanoon kii bhoomika nibhaataa he. puraa islamic samaaj isi sharia kaanoon yaa sharia kaanoon ke hisaab se chalata he.
 muslim shaadiyon main nikaah-namaa sharia kaanoon dwaara apekshit hota he.
