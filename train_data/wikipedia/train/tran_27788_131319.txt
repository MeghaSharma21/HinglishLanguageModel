 bismil azimaabadi Patna, Bihar se ek urdu kavi the. 1921 main unhone "sarfaroshi kii tamanna naamak" deshbhakti kavita likhi thi.yah kavita ram prasad bismil, ek bhartiya mahaan krantikari netaa ne na sirf ise likhaa balki mukademe ke dauraan adaalat main apane saathiyon ke saath samuhik rup se gaakar lokapriy bhi banaaya. bismil' ne ise bhartiya swatantrata sangram ke noujvaan swatantrata senaniyon ke liye sambodhi-geet ke rup main likhaa tha. 'bismil' kii shahadat ke baad ise swatantrata senaniyon kii noujvaan peedi jaise shahid bhagat singh tathaa chandrasekhar aaazaad aadi ke saath bhi joda jaataa rahaa he. yah pehali ek jarnal "sabaah" mai dilli se prakashit hui .
