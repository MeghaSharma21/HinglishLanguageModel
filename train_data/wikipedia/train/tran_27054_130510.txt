 salaami sammaan pradarshit karne ke liye upyog kiye jane vaala ishaara yaa koi anya kriya he. salaami mukhya rup se sashastr balon se judi hui hai, lekin anya sanghathan or naagarik bhi salaami kaa upyog karte hai.
 vibhinn samay or sthanon kii sainya paramparaon main, haathon ke ishaare, top yaa raifal golibari, jhanda uthaanaa, topi hataanaa yaa sammaan dikhaane ke anya sadhano kaa upyog karke salaami dene ke kai tareeke hai. bharat main teen senaaon ke paas alag-alag salaami he. bhartiya thalsena or bhartiya nausena british sena ke tareeke par chalati hai. bhartiya thalsena main, salaami khuli hatheli ko aage rakhte hue, ungaliyon or anguuthe ko ek saath milakar or madhya ungali ko lagbhag topi yaa dahini bhun ko chhuaakar di jaati he. yah praayah regiment ke abhivadan ke saath hota he, udhaaharan ke liye: sikh regiment main "sat shri akaal".
 duniya bhar main kai alag-alag sanketon kaa upyog saral abhivadan ke rup main kiya jaataa he. bhartiya samskruti main pranaam yaa namaskara ek bahu prachlit tarika he. anya main aadab or halakaa sir jhukaana shaamil he.
