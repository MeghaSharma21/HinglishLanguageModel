 bundelon ke bare main mana jaataa he ki ve vindhyavasini devi ke upaask the. isaliye ve pehle vindha kahalaaye or isi se bundela yaa bundel kii utpatti hui. ve kshatriya jati ke shaasha the tathaa sudur ateet main unka sambandh suryavanshi raja manu se mana jaataa hai. akshvaku ke baad ramchandra ke putr lav se unke vanshajo kii parampara aage badhaai gai or isi main kaashi ke gaharwar shakha ke karttriraj ko joda gaya he. lav se karttriraj tak ke uttaradhikariyon main gagansen, kanaksen, pradhyumna aadi ke naam hi mahatvapurn hai. karttriraj kaa gaharwar hona kisi ghatana par aadhaarit hai, jisamen kaashi main upar grahon kii buree dasha ke nivaranarth uske prayatnon main grahanivaar sangya se vah pukaara jane laga tha. kalantar main grahanivaar gaharwar ban gaya.
