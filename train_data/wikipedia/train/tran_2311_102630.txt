 bal patil ek jain vidwaan, patrkaar, saamaajik kaaryakarta or jainon ko alpsankhyak darja dilaane ke liye mumbai, maharashtra main vakaalat kii thi 2001 se 2004 tak vah maharashtra rajya ke alpsankhyak ayog main sadsy ke rup niyukt kiye gaye the. jain alpsankhyak abhiyog ko prasiddhi tab praapt hui jab bal patil ne supreme court main jaino ko dharmik alpsankhyak kaa darja dilaane ke liye yachika daayar kii. bharat kii manyata ke liye ke saath samamoolya par anya bhartiya alpsankhyakon ke prati ke rup main do siforeeshon dwaara rashtriya alpsankhyak ayog he. vah pehle gaiyr-chikitsak the jo kii hriday rog kii rokthaam or punarvaas, mumbai sanstha ke rashtriya adhyaksh rahe. unhone jain dharm par kai pustakon kaa lekhan kiya or vibhinn seminaron or sammelnon main kain lekh prastut kiye.
 shri bal patil ne soli sorbaji dwaara likhe gaye ek lekh par aapatti vyakt karte hue use galat or apnirvachan bataaya jisase jain samudaay kii bhaawanaaon ko chot pahunchi he. yah maamle press council of india main gaya or psorabji ko maafee prastut kerani padi. shri patil ne gujarat vidhansabha dwaara paarit vivaadaaspad dharm kii swatantraaa bill kii bhi aalochana kii thi. is bill main jainon or baudhon ko hindu dharm ke antargat liya gaya tha. 
 2010 main, bal patil ji ko "jain duniya kaa gahanaa" puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. shri patil ko yah puraskaar unki drushti or jain dharm ke liye unke jivan bhar ke samarpan  ke liye prastut kiya gaya tha.
