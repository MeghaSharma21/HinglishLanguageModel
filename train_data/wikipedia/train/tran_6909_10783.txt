 suunji bingfa china main yuddh kii kala ke bare main likhi gai ek 13 adhyay kii ek kitaab he. is pustak main mukhya rup se sena dwaara apanaaya jane vaale niti or kaary karne kaa tarika tha.
 is chini bhashaa main likhi gai pustak kaa pehla anuwaad varsh 1772 main francisi bhashaa main kiya gaya tha. uske baad ise varsh 1905 main ek angrez ne angrezi main aanshik rup se anuwaad kiya tha. iske baad varsh 1910 main ise puri tarah se anuwaad kiya gaya.
 sun vu dwaara pehali shatabdi se chini samskruti main pehali baar sena or unase judi jaankaree ko paathya ke rup main likhaa jane laga tha. ise baad main varsh -514 se -495 ke madhya raja helu dwaara padhaa or samajha jane laga. isi ke baad se is pustak ko "yuddh kii kala" ke naam se janaa jane laga. iske lekhak ne jab apane ghar ke sthaan main gaye to unhone bataaya ki unhone apane kshamta dwaara kai bate likhi, jisamen mahal kii mahilaon ko bhi yuddh kii kala siikhaa kar or pareekshan dekar apni sena ko or bhi majboot kar sakate hai. aadi tareeke shaamil the, jise padhane ke baad raja khush ho gaye.
