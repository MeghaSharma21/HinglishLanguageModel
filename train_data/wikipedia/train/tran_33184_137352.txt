 manindra agarwal bhartiya praudyogiki santhaan kanpur ke sanganak vigyaan evam abhiyantriki vibhaag main professor he. sanganak vigyaan ke kshetra main unke yogadan ke liye san 2013 main bharat sarkaar ne unhen padam shri pradaan kiya.
 agarwal ne iaiti kanpur se bi.tech. evam piechdi kii oopaadhiyaan praapt kii.
 
 unhone niraj kayaal evam nitin saxena ke saath milakar ackees paraemility test kaa avishkaar kiya, jiske liye unhen unke sahakartaaon ke saath sanyukt rup se varsh 2006 kaa pratishthit godel puraskaar mila.
 varsh 2008 main ganit ke kshetra main unke aseem yogadan ke liye agarwal kaa chayan pratham infosis ganit puraskaar hetu kiya gaya.
