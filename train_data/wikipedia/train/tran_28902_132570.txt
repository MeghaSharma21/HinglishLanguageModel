 shivi pracheen rajasthan kaa ek janapad hua karata tha. isi naam ke ek raja ke paatranam se ek lokapriy kahani judi he jahaan par raja shivi ne ek nirdosh pakshi ko bachaane ke liye apane sharir ke tukde katkar bhakshak ko saunp diye the. baad main ishwar prakat hue or shivi ko bataaya ki unka ye balidaan ek pariksha kaa hi hissaa tha. 
 yah kahani ijaak ke balidaan se kaaphi milti julati he or yah sambhav he ki islamic prasar ke dauraan is kahani kaa uttar bharat main or prachaar hua.
