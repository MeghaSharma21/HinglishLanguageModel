 us vyakti ko lohaar kehete hai jo loha yaa ispaat kaa upyog karke vibhinn vastuen banaata he. hathaudaa, chheni, bhathi aadi aujaaron kaa payog karke lohaar phatak, gril, railing, kheti ke aujaar, kuch bartan evam hathiyaar aadi banaata he.

par is vansh kaa shaasha hua. usne apane shashan kaal main samanton ke vidroh ko kuchlaa. uske prashasan main uski patni rani suryamati sahyog karti thi. anant kaa putr kalash ek kamazor shaasha tha. harsh- kalash kaa putr harsh vidwaan, kavi evam kai bhashaaon evam vidyaon kaa jaankaar tha. rajtarangini kaa lekhak kalhan, harsh kaa ashrith kavi tha. rajya main aantarik ashaanti ke kaaran hue vidroh main lagbhag 1101 i. main harsh kii hatya kar di gayi. harsh ko kashmir kaa 'nero' bhi kaha jaataa he. uske shashan kaal main kashmir main bhayanak akaal padaa tha. uske atyachaarpurn kaaryo se trast hokar utsal evam sussal naamak bhaiyon ne vidroh kar diya. is vidroh main harsh ke putr bhoj evam harsh donon kii hatya kar di gayi. lohaar vansh kaa antim shaasha jaisingh tha. apane shasankaal main usne yawanon ko parrast kiya tha. 1339 i. main kashmir turko ke kabje main aa gaya. jaisingh kalhan kii rajtarangini kaa antim shaasha tha. usee ke samay main rajtarangini purna huyi. turk shaskaon main sarvaadhik lokapriy shaasha 'jainul abdin' tha jise 'kashmir kaa akbar' kaha jaataa he. kalhan- kalhan ke pita champak brahman kul ke the. ve lohaar vansh ke shaasha harsh ke prashasan main mantri ke pad par the. kalhan kisi rajkeey pad par tha yaa nahin, is vishay kii spasht jaankaree nahin he. kalhan ne apane prasiddh granth 'rajtargini' kii rachana lohaar vansh ke antim shaasha jaisingh ke samay main kii thi. is granth se kashmir ke itihaas ke bare main jaankaree milti he. kalhan kii rajtargini main kul aath tarang evam 8000 shloke hai. pehle ke teen tarangon main kashmir ke pracheen itihaas kii jaankaree milti he. chathe se lekar chhathwein tarang main carcot evam utpal vansh ke itihaas kaa varnan he. antim saatven evam aathven tarang main lohaar vansh kaa itihaas ullikhit he. is pustak main aitihaasik ghatnaon kaa krambaddh ullekh he. kalhan ne pakshpatarahit hokar raajaaon ke gun evam dosha kaa ullekh kiya he. pustak ke vishay ke antargat raajnneeti ke atirikt sadachaar evam naitik shiksha par bhi prakaash daalaa gaya he. kalhan ne apane granth rajtarangini main sanskrit bhashaa kaa prayog kiya he. panne kii pragati avasthaa aadhaar praarambhik
