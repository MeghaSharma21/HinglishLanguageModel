 chelnaa pracheen bhartiya samrat bimbisar kii patni thi. vah vaishali ke raja betak kii putree thi. bimbisar ne apni vriddhavastha main mugdh hokar ise byaaha tha. bimbisar buddha dharmavalambee tha or chalanaa jain dharmavalambee. kaha jaataa he ki chelnaa ne buddhichaaturya se bimbisar ko jaindharm sweekaar karne ko baadhya kar diya tha.
