 panjashir vaadi uttar-madhya afghanistan main sthit ek ghati he. yah rashtriya rajdhani kabul se 150 kimi uttar main hindu kush parvaton ke paas sthit he. yah vaadi panjashir praant main aati he or isamen se prasiddh panjashir nadi guzarati he. yahan lagbhag 1,40,000 log based hue hai jinamein afghanistan kaa sabase bada tajik logon kaa samudaay bhi shaamil he.
 'panjashir' vastav main 'panj sher' kehane kaa farsi lahaza he. farsi main 'sher' kaa matlab 'baagh' kii bajaae 'singh' hota he. is vaadi kaa naam paanch bhaiyon ke sammaan main rakhaa gaya he jinhonen 10veen shatabdi isvi main mahmud gazani le liye yahan ek durgam nadi par band daalaa tha.
 yah vaadi puraane jamaane se yahan milane vaale ratnon ke liye jaani jaati he. madhyakaal main yahan chaandi nikaala jaataa tha jis se safaari samrajya or samaani samrajya apane sikke gadha karte the. aaj bhi is kshetra main panna utpaadan kaa bada centre banane kii sambhaavnaayen hai. 1985 tak yahan bahut hi behtareen koti ke 190 carat tak ke panna mil chuke the.
