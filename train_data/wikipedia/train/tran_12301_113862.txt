 alfaa trngulaai australis, jiskaa bair naam main bhi yahi he, dakshin trikon taramandal main sthit ek tara he jo prithvi se dikhne vaale sabhi taron main se 43vaan sab se roshan tara he. yah hamase 420 prakaash-varsh kii duuri par sthit he or prithvi se iskaa ausat saapeksh kantimaan 1.91 he. vaigyanikon ko pakkaa gyaat nahin he lekin iske dohare taare hone kii sambhaavna he.
 alfaa trngulaai australis ko angrezi main "etria" bhi kaha jaataa he. yah iske naam ke teen shabdon main se angrezi ke 'a', 'tri' or 'a' aksharon ko jodakar bana laghurup he.
 alfaa trngulaai australis ek naarangi rang kaa K2 IIb yaa K2 IIIa shrenee kaa chamkila daanav tara he. isaki sataha kaa taapmaana lagbhag 4,000 calvin he.
 khagolashastriyon ne is taare kaa adhyayan kiya to iske varnakram main isaki shrenee ke taare main jitna nila rang hona chahie usase zyaada niklaa . is se unhen shaq ho gaya kii yah nila uske kisi saathi taare se aa rahaa ho saktaa he, yani kii yah ek doharaa tara ho saktaa he. yadi yah sach he, to varnakram ke aadhaar par vaigyanikon ko lagata he ki iskaa saathi tara ek F shrenee kaa bouna tara hoga jo mukhya anukram kii sthiti main nayaa-nayaa pahunchaa he. mukhya taare kii nihit chamak suraj kii 2,500 guna, iskaa dravyaman hamaare suraj ke dravyaman kaa 6 guna or isaki aayu 50 karod saal anumaanit kii gai he.
