 birsa munda vimaanashetra jharkhand sthit hawaldda he. iskaa prabandhan bhartiya vimaanapttan praadhikaran karti he. yah ranchi ke hinu mohalle ke sameep sthit he or shahar ke mukhya sthano se lagbhag saat kilometre kii duuri par he. yah bharat kaa pantisvaan sabase vyast vimaanashetra he. iskaa namakaran jharkhand ke adivasee swatantraaa senaanee birsa munda ke naam par kiya gaya he.
 vartmaan main ek hi terminal he, ise 24 march 2013 ko kholaa gaya tha. is terminal main yatra desk, lounj aadi ki suvidhaayen he. bhartiya vimaan pattan praadhikaran ne 3 or vimaan parking stand banane kaa faisla kiya he jisase yah bharat kaa pehla tear-2 vimaan kshetra ban jayega jisame 8 vimaan parking stand hon. yahan par raat main landing bhi muft he taaki subah main mahanagaron ke liye or udaan hon.
 puraane terminal ko ab ek cargo terminal badal diya gaya he jise 10 farvari 2017 ko jharkhand ke mukhyamantri raghubar das ne udghaatit kiya.
