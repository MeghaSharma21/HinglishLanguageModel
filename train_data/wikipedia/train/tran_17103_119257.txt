 gyani zel singh  kaarykaal 25 july 1982 se 25 july 1987 bharat ke saatven raashtrapati the. sikh dharm ke vidwaan punjab ke mukhyamantri rah chuke gyani ji apni drudh ichha shakti, satyanishtha ke raajnneetik kathin raaston ko paar karte hue 1982 main bharat ke gauravamayi raashtrapati ke pad par asin hue. 1987 tak ke apane kaarykaal ke dauraan inhen 'bluestar aaparishan' evam indira gandhi kii hatya jaisi durbhagyapurna paristhitiyon se guzarana padaa.
