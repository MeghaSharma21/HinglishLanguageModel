 anant geete bharat kii solahavin lok sabha ke saansad tathaa kendriya mantrimandal main baari udyog tathaa saarvajanik udhyamita mantri hai. 2014 ke chunaavon main ve maharashtra ke raigarh se nirvaachit hue. is chunaav main unhone congress ke vidhayak va sinchai mantri sunil dattatreya ko kadi shikast di. ve shivasena se sambhddha hai. 
 anant agast 2002 se may 2004 tak kendriya cabinet mantri bhi rah chuke hai. vah ab tak bhartiya sansad main chah baar nirvaachit ho chuke hai. isse pehle unhone 11veen se 14veen loksabhaa ke dauraan maharashtra ke ratnagiri sansadeey kshetra se pratinidhitiv kiya tha. 17 may ko unhen shivasena ke sansadeey dal kaa netaa nirvaachit kiya gaya he.
