 gurbachan singh bhullar punjabi bhashaa ke namvar kahanikar hai. usne kavita, safarnama, anuwaad, sampadan, patrakarita, rekhachitr, aalochana, baalsahitya aadi anek kshetron main sahitya rachana kii he. uske kahani-sangrah agni-kalash ko saal 2005 main sahitya academy, dilli kaa puruskaar mil chukaa he.
 gurbachan singh bhullar kaa janm bathinda zile ke gaanv 'pittho' main 18 march 1937 ko hua tha. usne praathmik shiksha apane gaanv ke praathmik paathshaala se praapt kii thi. aage se unchi vidya hasil karne ke baad vah adhyaaapak lag gaya. adhyaaapak union kii sargharmiyon main bad-chadh karke hissaa lene ke kaaran se das ku saal ke baad use yah naukari chhodani pad gai. phir vah dilli main soviet duutavas main kaam karne lage. baad main vah punjabi tribune kaa sampadak bhi rahaa. vartmaan main vah saamaanya rup se sahityak sargharmiyon ko samarpit he.
