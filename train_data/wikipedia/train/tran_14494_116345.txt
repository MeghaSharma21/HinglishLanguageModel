 indian vexine corporation limited kii sthapana march, 1989 main ek sanyukt udhyami company ke rup main anusandhan evam vikaas kaaryon tathaa viral tikon ke vinirmaan hetu kii gai thi. utpaada mishran main badlaav tathaa praudyogiki ke hastantaran ke kaaran company farvari, 1992 se band he.
 evekol kii purnsanrachana karne tathaa srujit parisampattiyon kaa upyog karne ke sambandh main cabinet ke nirnay ko karyanvit kiya gaya. iske parisar main rashtriya mastishk anusandhan kendra kii sthapana kar di gai he. indian petro chemical corporation li0 ke share holding kii prakriya, jo evekol kaa ek pramotor he ko rilanyas industries li0 dwaara sheyaron ko praapt karke badla gaya he. ipisiel ke nae prabandhan ne evekol ke niyantrak sheyaron ko praapt karne or company ke pun:pravartan ke liye ek prastaav prastut kiya he. prastaav par vichaar kiya gaya he or isaki sheeghra nirnay ke liye sakasham praadhikaar ko prastut kiya ja rahaa he. yah aashaa kii ja rahi he ki agle kuch maheenon main evekol kaa purnagathan or pun:pravartan ho jayega.
