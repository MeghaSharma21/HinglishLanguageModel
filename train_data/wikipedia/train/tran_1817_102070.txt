 
 da mistic nits of tir na nog, saban antertenment ke dwaara banaai gai ek irish/america televijan shrinkhalaa he jo pracheen ireland ke ek kaalpanik sanskaran main set he. yah shrinkhalaa vaastavik irish puranic kathaon par aadhaarit thi. iskaa namakaran "tir naa nog" par kiya gaya tha jo ki irish puranic kathaon kii duusari duniyaaon main se ek he. yah saban kii pehali kaalpanik shrinkhalaa thi jisamen shoorveer, dregon, or jaaduugaron ko shaamil kiya gaya. is shrinkhalaa kaa ek doosraa bhaag mistic nits: battle thunder banane kii bhi yojana thi, lekin baad main use radd kar diya gaya or iskaa bajet power rangers lost gallexi kii ore de diya gaya. jiske parinamswaroop vaastavik japani tokusatsu kii anukriti ke bajaay ek mul gaiyr japani vishesh prabhaav shrinkhalaa ban kar saamne ai.
 sanyukt rajya america main, yah mul rup se fox channel ke fox kids block par 1 sitambar, 1998 par prasaarit hua or 7 may, 1999 par samaapt hua. bharat main yah hindi main dab sanskaran toun digni par jetics block par 2006 ke aasapaas prasaarit kiya gaya. hindi dab sanskaran sound and vijan india dwaara nirmit kiya gaya.
