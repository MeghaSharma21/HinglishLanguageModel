 rishi veli yaa "rishi veli" : arthath "rushi kii vaadi" yaa "rushi kii vaadiyaan". yahaan par ek paathshaala bhi he jis ko jiddu krishnamurthi ne sthapit kiya. yah ek ashram paathshaala he. jiddu krishnamurthi ko naveen kaal kaa rishi mana jaataa he. inhon ne is pahari vaadi praant main paathshaala sthapit kii is liye is praant ko rishi veli kaha jaataa he. bharat kii riyasat andhra pradesh, chitoor jila main shahar madanpalli ke 16 kilometre sameep, shahar kadiri ke maarg par he. yah vaadi harsili hills kii vaadiyon kaa ek hissaa he. yah praant bahut hi sundar he.prakruti ke romantic or sundar drishy paryatakon ko manamohit karti he.
 vishwaprasiddh darshanik jiddu krishnamurthi dwaara sthapit yah ashram paathshaala, praathmik or madhyamik vidya pradaan kartaahe. yahaan rahane, vidyaprapt karne kii suvidha he. yahaan ke kshatro ko vidhyabhyaas ke saath saath shaareerik or maansik vikaas kaa bhi bodhan kiya jaataa he.
