 euri gagarin, bhootpurv soviet sangh ke hawaabaz or antariksh yatri the. 12 april, 1961 ko antariksh main jane vaale ve pratham maanav the.
 antariksh kii yatra karne ke baad gagarin antarrashtriya pratishthit vyakti ban chuke the or unhen kai tarah ke padak or khitaabon se sammanit kiya gaya tha. un khitaabon main se ek kitaab tha hero of da soviet union tha. unhone Vostok 1 naamak antariksh yaan main apni yatra kii thi. 
 1968 jab ve mig 15 naamak prashikshan vimaan kaa sanchaalak kar rahe the to, vimaan ke durghatnagrast hone ke kaaran unki mrityu ho gayi. 
 euri gagarin padak unke sammaan main pradaan kiya jaataa he.
