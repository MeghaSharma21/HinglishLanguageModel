 raja dhaj yaa ror kumaar yaa raay diyaa, sindhu rajya kaa ek vyakti tha jisaki katha sindh main aaj bhi chaav se suni-sunaayi jaati he. usne sorath naamak atyant sundar stri kaa haran kiya tha. raja dhaj kii katha aaj bhi haryana, rajasthan, gujarat or sindh main bahut prachlit hai.
 yah avismaraneey romance ek vaastavik itihaas par aadhaarit he jo sindh main rodi sakhkhar kii sthapana se prachlit hai. jo is kahani ke nayak hai. rodi kii sthapana ke baad dhaj rod kumaar ne rod rajvansh kii sthapana kii jisane 450 ipu sindh par agle hazaar varshon tak shashan kiya.
