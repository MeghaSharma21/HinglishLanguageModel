 patric victor martindel white  england main janme australian lekhak the, jinhen vyapak rup se 20ve sadi ke angreji bhashaa ke sabase mahatvapurn upanyaaskaaron main se ek mana jaataa he. 1935 se lekar apni mrityu tak inhone kul 12 upanyaas, do choti kahani sangrah or aath naatako ko prakashit kiya tha. varsh 1973 main apane kaary ke liye inhen sahitya ke nobel puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya tha. yah sammaan praapt karne vaale white ekmaatr australian naagarik the.
