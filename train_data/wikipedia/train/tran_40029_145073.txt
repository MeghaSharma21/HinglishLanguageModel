 ganga nadi se guzaranevaale 1680 kilometre lambey elahabaad-haldiya jalmaarg ko bharat main rashtriya jalmaarg sankhya-1 kaa darja diya gaya he. ganga nadi kaa prayog naagarik yatayaat tathaa bharavahan ke liye ke liye kaaphi samay se kiya jaataa rahaa he. is jalmaarg par sthit pramukh shahar elahabaad, varanasi, mughalsarai, baxur, aara, patna, mokama, barh, munger, bhagalpur, farkka, kolkata tathaa haldiya he. desh main jal parivahan ko badhaava dene ke liye sthapit ekmaatr rashtriya antardeshiy naukayan santhaan is jalmaarg par based patna ke gaighat main sthit he.
