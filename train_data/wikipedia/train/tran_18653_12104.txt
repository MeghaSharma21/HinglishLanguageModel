  aanjana samaaj, jise choudhary, patel, kalbi yaa patidaar samaaj ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, mukhya rup se madhya pradesh, gujarat, rajasthan main failaa hua samaaj he. inkaa mukhya vyavsaay krishi or pashupaalan he.
 aanjana samaaj ke mahatvapurn vibhootiyon main atharahavin sadi ke mahaan sant shri raja ram maharajaullekhneey hai. unhone samaaj main anyaay or saamaajik kuritiyon ke khilaaf jaagrukta paidaa kii. unhone samaaj main shiksha ke mahatv par bhi jor diya.
 aanjanaon kii sanathan dharm main aasthaa he. rajasthan main shri raja ram maharaj ne jodhpur zile kii luni tehsil main shikarpura naamak gaanv main ek math kii sthapana kii. yah ashram shikarpura ashram ke naam se prasiddh he.
