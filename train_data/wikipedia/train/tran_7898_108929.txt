 dharchula, dharchaula tehsil main bharat ke uttrakhand rajya ke antargat kumaun mandal ke pithoragarh jile kaa ek gaanv he.dharchula ek chota sa sudur qasbaa hajo himalay se hokar guzarane vaale ek pracheen vyapaar-maarg se judaa hua he.nagar chaaron ore se pahadiyon seghira hua he or vismayakari drishy paidaa karata he.dharchula nagar ke nivasee nepal main sthit seemaa paardaarchula naamak kasbe ke nivaasiyon jaise hote he.yahan ke pramukh aakarshanon main narayan ashram, maansarovarjhil, chirkila band, kaali nadi or om parvat hai.narayan ashram yahan se kareeb 98 kimi dur sthit he orek samay par yahan 40 log rah sakate hai. maansarovar jhil ekatirth he or yahan pavitra snaan ke liye anekatirthayatri pahonchte hai.
