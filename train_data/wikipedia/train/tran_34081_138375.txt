 dakshin dweep newzealand ke do mukhya dwipo main se ek he, doosraa dweep uttar dweep he jo adhik ghani aabaadi vaala he. uttar main isaki seemaa cook jalsandhi se lagati he, pashchim main tasman sagar se or dakshin va purv main prashanth mahaasaagar ke saath. dakshin dweep kaa kul kshetrafal 150,437 varg kilometre he tathaa samshitoshna jalwaayu se prabhavit he.
 dakshin dweep ko kabhi-kabhi "mukhyabhumi" ke naam se bhi sanderbhit kiya jaataa he. haalanki iskaa bhubhag uttar dweep se 33% bada he parantu yahan newjiland kii 4.4 million aabaadi kaa keval 23% hissaa rahata he. newzealand ke europea upniveshan ke shuruuaati charnon main zyaadaar europian aabaadi dakshin dweep main rahati thi tathaa swarna hode ke kaaran yah kaafii samrudh bhaag tha. beesvi sadi ke shuruaati varsho main uttar dweep kii aabaadi ne dakshin dweep kii aabaadi ko peeche chhod diya, jisamen 1911 tak desh kii 56% aabaadi uttar main nivas karti thi, tathaa is puri shatabdi main logo or vyapaar kaa bahaav uttar kii taraf jari rahaa.
