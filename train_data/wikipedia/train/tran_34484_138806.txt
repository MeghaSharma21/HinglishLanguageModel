 shehzahou kaarykram china dwaara ek maanavyukt antariksh udaan kii pahal he. is kaarykram ke tahat pehle chini naagarik yang livei ko 15 oktober 2003 ko antariksh main bheja gaya.
 iskaa kaarykram kaa vikaas pariyojna 921-1 ke naam se 1992 main shuru hua.
 pehali chaar maanavrahit pareekshan udaan 1999, 2001 or 2002 main hui. or iske baad 2003 main pehali maanavyukt antariksh udaan kii gayi. yah long march 2epa prakshepan yaan dwaara jiukuan upgrah prakshepan centre se laanch kiya gaya tha. 
 shehzahou antariksh yaan kaa dizin roosi soyus antariksh yaan jaisaa dikhataa he. lekin yah aakaar main bada he. or sidhe soyus antariksh yaan parivaar se sambandhit nahin he.
 shehzahou antariksh yaan ke teen modul hai.
