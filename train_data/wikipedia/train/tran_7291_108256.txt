 sharbhang dakshin bharat ke gautam kulotpann ek prasiddh maharishi the jinka ullekh ramayana main he. inaki gananaa un maharishiyon main he jinhone dandakaarany main godavaritat par apana ashram banaaya, uttar kii arya sabhyataa kaa prachaar tathaa vistaar dakshin ke junglee praant main kiya or ant main agni main aatmahuti dekar swarg praapt kiya tha. vanvaas ke samay ramchandra inkaa darshan karne gaye the.
