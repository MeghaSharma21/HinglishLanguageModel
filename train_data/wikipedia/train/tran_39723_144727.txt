 kojicode bandargah kochi se 144 kilometre uttar main he. monsoon ke armbh main yah band rahata he. yahan samudr chhichhlaa he. is kaaran jahaajon ko bandargah se 5 kilometre dur samudr main khadaa hona padataa he. yahan se nariyal kii rassi, khopra, kahavaa,chaay, sonth, moongfalee tathaa machhlee kii khaad niryaat kii jaati he. mukhya aayaat anaaj, mitti kaa tel, mashiinon or sooti vastron kaa hota he.
