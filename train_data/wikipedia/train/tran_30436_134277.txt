 madhya vibhaag prashanth mahaasaagar main sthit fiji desh ke chaar prashasnic vibhaagon main se ek he. yah vibhaag fiji se sabase bade dweep, viti levu, ke purvi bhaag, paas ke benga dweep or kuch anya chote dwipon par vistrut he. isamen desh ke paanch praant - nitasiri, namosi, reva, serua or tailevu - shaamil hai. rashtriya rajdhani suwa is vibhaag kii rajdhani bhi he.
