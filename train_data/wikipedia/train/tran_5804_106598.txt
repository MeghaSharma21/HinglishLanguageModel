 fahamida riyaaz urdu kii pramukh shaayara evam lekhika hai. inkaa janm 28 july 1946 ko meerut main hua. baad main inkaa parivaar pakistan jaakar bas gaya. godavari, khat-e-maramuz inke pramukh sangrah hai. 1980 ke daur main pakistan ke raashtrapati general jiya ul haq ke shashan main unako or unke pati ko nirvaasan ke baad bharat main sharan leni padi thi.
