 sabhyataa shabd kaa prayog maanav samaaj ke ek sakaaratmak, pragatisheel or samaveshi vikaas ko ingit karne ke liye kiya jaataa he. sabhy samaaj aksar unnat krishi, lambii duuri ke vyapaar, vyavsaayik visheshgyata or nagarikaran aadi kii unnat sthiti kaa dyotak he. in mul tatwon ke alaava, sabhyataa kuch madhyamik tatwon, jaise viksit yatayaat vyavastha, lekhan, maapan ke maanak, sanvida evam nuksaani par aadhaarit vidhi-vyavastha, kala ke mahaan shaileyon, smarakon ke sthapaty, ganit, unnat dhaatukarm evam khagolavidya aadi kii sthiti se bhi paribhaashit hoti he.
