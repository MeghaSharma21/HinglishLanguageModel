 kunar yaa kunad afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke purv main sthit he. is praant kaa kshetrafal 4,339 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2009 main lagbhag 4.1 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kii rajdhani asadabad shahar he. is praant main zyaadaatar log pashto bolne vaale pashtun hai. isaki dakshini or purvi seemaa pakistan se lagati he.
 kunar praant main hindu kush parvaton ke nichle pahaad, kunar nadi or kunar vaadi sabase mahatvapurn kshetra hai. puure soobe main nadi-jharne, tang ghaatiyaan, chattaane or kathin dhalaanon vaale pahaad bikhare hue hai. inaki vajah se yahan yatayaat bahut kathin rahaa he. kunar praant ke 96% log dehati kshetron main basate hai or keval 4% hi shaharon-bastiyon main. apni durgamata kii vajah se afghanistan main vidrohi or updravi giroh sadiyon se kunar kshetra main chhupate or gatividhiyaan karte aaye hai.
