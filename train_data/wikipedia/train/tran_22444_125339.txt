 yah bharat kii ek pramukh nadi ghati pariyojna hai.
 is pariyojna kaa prarambh san 2000 main shri atal bihari bajpeyi ji ne neenv kaa pathar rakhkar kiya tha. 
 yah pariyojna sutluj nadi par banaai ja rahi he. 
 is pariyojna ko himachal pradesh ke bilaspur or mandi jilon kii seemaa par banaaya ja rahaa he. satluj nadi bilaspur or mandi jilon ko alag karti he. 
 is pariyojna se himachal pradesh, dilli evam uttar pradesh labhanvit honge. 
 is pariyojna se utpann bijli kaa 12% hissaa himachal pradesh ko muft diya jayega evam 15% hissaa utpaadan mulya par diya jayega|
