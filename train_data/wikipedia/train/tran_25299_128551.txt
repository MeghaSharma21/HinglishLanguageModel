 bhartiya kritrim ang nirmaan nigam ) bharat sarkaar kaa saarvajanik kshetra kaa ek upkram he jo viklang logon ke liye kritrim ang evam anya sahaayak upkarnon kaa nirmaan karata he. isaki sthapana 1972 main kii gayi thi. iskaa mukhyalay kanpur main he. iskaa dhyey 'bharat or videshon main viklang vyaktiyon ke liye sasti keemat par rajya ke atyaadhunik sahaayak upkarnon kaa sabase bada nirmaataa or aapurtikarta hone kaa darja hasil karnaa or banaae rakhana' he.
