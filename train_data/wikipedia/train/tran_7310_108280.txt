 sarvadeshik arya pratinidhi sabha aryasamaj kii sarvocch sanstha he. isaki sthapana swami shraddhand dwaara kii gayi thi taaki vishwa ke sabhi bhaagon main sthit aryasamaj kaa samuchit prabandhan karne main suvidha ho. isaki saadhaaran sabha main vishwa ke vibhinn aryasamaj sansthaaon se chune gayi 21 sadsy hote hai. ye sadsy prayek tisare varsh ek kaaryakarini kaa chunaav karte hai.
 iskaa mukhyalay dilli main he.
