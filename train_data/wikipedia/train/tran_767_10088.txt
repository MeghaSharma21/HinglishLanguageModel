 sahu gotra ek upnaam he jo bharat or pakistan main paaya jaataa he. is upnaam vaale log vibhinn jatiyon or janajatiyon main paye ja sakate hai.
 shabd "sahu" kaa arth alag-alag kshetron par alag-alag he. iskaa sabase vyapak arth "vyavsaay" athwa "sahuukaar" hai. jhelum kshetra main iskaa arth sajjanta se liya jaataa he jabaki jaaton main iskaa arth dhairyavan se liya jaataa he.
 bharat main yah gakkhad, jat or rajput samudaayon main paaya jaataa he. iske atirikt yah upnaam dhund, dhaniyal, kaalya or satti naamak janajatiyon main bhi paaya jaataa he. ludhiana kshetra main greval jat bhi yah upnaam kaam main lete hai. jaaton kii ek janajati siyaal bhi is upnaamak ko kaam main lete hai. kabirwala kshetra main anya jat janajatiyaan yah upnaam kaam main leti hai.
 gujarat or madhya pradesh main marwari vyavsaayee sahu upnaam kaam main lete hai jo saamaanya varg main aate hai. sahu upnaam vaale bihar, jharkhand ke log soudic, rauniyaar, taili jaati ke hote hai. jharkhand main adhiktar sahu log vyavsaay kaa kaary karte hai.
 maharashtra main sahu upnaam vaale log taili jati ke hote hai. wo kuch yudhon ke kaaran bundelkhand kshetra se vidarbha kshetra main aaye huye log hai.
