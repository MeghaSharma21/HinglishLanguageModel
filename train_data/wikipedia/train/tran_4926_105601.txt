 don 1978 kii ek hindi krime thriller film he. saleem-javed dwaara likhi is film ke nirmaataa nariman irani hai, tathaa nirdeshak chandra barot hai. amitabh bachchan, zeenat amaan, iftekhaar, praan, helen, om shivpuri, satyen kappu tathaa pinchu kapur ne film main mukhya bhoomikaaai nibhaai hai. kalyanji aanandji ne film main sangeet diya he, tathaa film ke geet anjaan or indivar ne likhe hai.
 amitabh bachchan ne film main anderworld bos 'don' kii, or uske hamshakl 'vijay' kii dohari bhoomika nibhaai thi. film kii kahani bombay kii jhuggiyon ke nivasee vijay ke ird-gird gumti he, jo sanyog se don kaa hamshakl hai. bombay police ke disipi di'silva vijay se don kaa rup lene ko kehete hai, taaki vah police ke mubir ke rup main kaam kar sake, or saath hi don ke aapraadhik network kaa bhandaphod karne main police kii madad kar saken.
 'don' mumbai main ek anderworld apradhi he, jisaki talash mumbai police or interpol, donon ko he. interpol kii 'most wanted' aparaadhiyon kii suchi main shaamil don har baar police ko chakma dekar bhaag nikalne main safal ho jaataa he. apane nirdayee swabhaav ke kaaran, police ke atirikt don ke anya dushman bhi hai. pramukhtah don ne ek baar apane hi ek sahakarmi, ramesh kii hatya kar di thi, jiske baad uski mangetar 'kamini' tathaa bahan 'roma' don ke dushman ban gaye. kamini don ko bahakaane kii koshish karti he, taaki police aakar don ko pakad le, parantu don kamini ko bandhak banaakar vahaan se bhaag nikalne main safal hota he, or is puure ghatnakram main kamini kii mrityu ho jaati he. kamini kii bhi mrityu se acroshit roma apane bal kataa leti he, or judo tathaa karate sikhkar don ke giroh main shaamil ho jaati he, taaki mauka paakar vah don ko maar de. don ko pakadaney ke kai asafal prayatnon ke baad police ko akhirkaar safalataa praapt hoti he, or disipi di'silva don ko lagbhag pakad hi chuke hote hai, ki don kii mrityu ho jaati he, or don ke bos tak pahunchane kaa di'silva kaa sapana adhuraa hi rah jaataa he. iske baad di'silva don ko vahiin dafna detaa he. 
 mumbai kii jhuggiyon main rahane vaala vijay ek sidhaa-sada vyakti he, jo mumbai main naach-gaye kar apane do bacchon ke paalan kii koshish kar rahaa he. disipi di'silva kii mulaakaat ek baar vijay se hoti he, jisase unhen pataa chalata he, kii vijay don kaa hamshakl he, or vah vijay se don banane ko kehete hai, taaki vijay police kii mukhabiri kare, or uske raste di'silva is aapraadhik network kii tah tak pahunch paaen. vijay unki baat maan letaa he, or don banakar waapas uski giroh ke paas chalaa jaataa he, jahaan vah apni yaaddasht kho jane kaa bahaanaa banaata he. isi bich jasjit 'jeje' jail se chhot jaataa he. jasjit ko disipi di'silva ne hi giraftaar kiya tha; jis kaaran jeje kii patni kii mrityu ho gayi thi, or uske do bachche laapata ho gaye the. uske donon bacchon, deepu tathaa munni kaa laalan-paalan isi bich vijay kar rahaa tha. vijay don kii ek 'laal diary' le letaa he, or use lekar disipi di'silva se milane jaataa he. roma bhi uske saath jaati he, or vahaan use akelaa paakar vah us par hamalaa kar detii he. di'silva ain vakt par aakar roma ko vijay kii asaliyat bataate hai, or iske baad roma bhi vijay ke saath mil jaati he. vijay vah diary di'silva ko de detaa he, or use padhakar unhen pataa chalata he ki don ke bos kaa naam varadhan he, parantu uske bare main isse adhik jaankaree us diary main nahin likhi hoti.
 dhire dhire vijay ko don kebaare main or adhik jaankaree milti jaati he, or ek din roma kii sahaayta se vah ghoshnaa kar detaa he, ki uski yaaddasht waapas aa chuki he. isi kushi main ek party hoti he, jisamen police kii red pad jaati he. is red main golibari main disipi di'silva buree tarah ghayal ho jate hai, or police vijay ko don samajhakar pakad leti he. vijay un logon ko sach bataata he, jiske baad police vaale use disipi di'silva ke paas hospital le jate he; kintu kuch bol paane se pehle hi disipi di'silva kii mrityu ho jaati he. vijay ko ek trek dwaara hai-security jail bheja ja rahaa hota he, or vah trek se bhaag nikalne main safal ho jaataa he. iske baad vah swayan ko nirdosh sabit karne ke liye roma kii madad se khud koi tarkeeb sochane lagata he. vijay is ghatnakram main buree tarah ulajh jaataa he, kyonki police yah maanane se manaa karti he ki vah vijay hai, or saath hi don ke anderworld giroh ko yah ehsaas ho jaataa he ki vah vastav main don nahin hai. upar se vah laal diary, jo usne disipi di'silva ko di thi, use jasjit chura le jaataa he. vijay roma kii sahaayta se police or naarang, anderworld giroh ke vartmaan sargana, donon ko chakma dene main safal rahata he, or waapas apni puraani jindagi main lautne kaa prayaas karata he. 
 vijay interpol ke afasar, aar ke maalik se milane kaa aagrah karata he, or unase kuch samay kii maang karata he, taaki vah apni begunaahi kaa pramaan laa sake. tab use pataa chalata he ki jise vah ab tak interpol afasar aar ke maalik samaj rahaa tha, vah hi vastav main varadhan he, jo asli aar ke maalik ko agava kar unka bhesh lekar baitha he. use yah bhi pataa chalata he ki disipi di'silva kii hatya bhi varadhan ne hi kii thi. vijay yah baat police ke anya adhikariyon ko bataane kaa prayaas karata he, lekin ve log uski baton par vishwaas nahin karte. iske baad vijay varadhan ke sabhi gundon se swayan ladata he, or isi bich roma bhi vah laal diary dhundhakar le aati he. isi ladaai main ek gunda roma se vah diary chhinakar use jala detaa he. police un logon ko chaaron taraf se gher leti he, or us samay vijay apni jeb se laal dieriy nikaalkar unhen bataata he, kii jo diary jalaai gai, vah vastav main nakli thi. don vah diary police ko detaa he, or use nirdosh ghoshit kar us par lage sabhi prabhaar hataa die jate hai. asli aar ke maalik ko chhudaakar police varadhan ko giraftaar kar leti he, or vijay roma, jasjit, deepu tathaa munni ke saath apni puraani jindagi main laut jaataa he.
 film ke geetkaar kalyanji-aanandji hai or geetkaar indivar tathaa anjan hai. film main parshv sangeet anil mohile ne diya he. 1977 main iemai dwaara jari kii gai film kii album main kul 5 geet hai, jinhen kishor kumaar, lata mangeshkar or aashaa bhosle ne gaaya he. don se pehle kalyanji aanandji ne amitabh ke saath zanjeer main kaam kiya tha. aanandji ne film ke parshv sangeet nirdeshak anil mohile ke saath milakar vijay ke liye to bhartiya parmparik sangeet tathaa gramin samskruti par aadhaarit dhvaniyaan nirmit kii; or iske vipareet don ke liye western sangeet, beats, or brass instruments kaa prayog kiya.
 kul bikri ke aadhaar par don 70 ke dashak kii terahavin sarvaadhik bikne vaali album thi. 1979 ke filmphere puraskaron main kalyanji aanandji, kishor kumaar, or aashaa bhosle ko kramshah sarvshereshth sangeetkaar, sarvshereshth parshvagayak, or sarvshereshth parshvagayika ke puraskaar ke liye namankit kiya gaya tha. 2006 main don film ke sikval main is film ke 2 geet, "ye meraa dil" or "khaike paan banaras vaala" punah prayog kiye the. geet "ye meraa dil" kaa ek ansh 2005 main da blike oid pis dwaara apane hit geet "dont fank vid maai heart" main prayog kiya gaya tha. american dead ke tisare sijan main is film ke theme geet kaa prayog kiya gaya tha.
