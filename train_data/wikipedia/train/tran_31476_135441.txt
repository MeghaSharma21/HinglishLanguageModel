 prakaashiki ke kshetra main, paardarshita kisi padaarth kaa vah gun hota he jisamen vah prakaash kii kirnon ko apane bheetar se bina bikhere aane-jane de. parbhasakata kisi chiz kaa vah gun hota he jo prakaash ko apane se aar-paar guzarane to de lekin sambhawatah usamen zaraa-bahut rukaawat yaa bikharaav daalane se ksheen kar de.
