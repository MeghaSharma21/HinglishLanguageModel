 sipaahi bahadur sarkaar bharat ke pratham swatantraaa sangram ke dauraan 6 agast 1857 ko madhya pradesh kii sihor chhavnee main vidroh ke baad sthaneey sipaahiyon dwaara sthapit apni swatantra sarkaar kaa naam tha. meerut kii kranti kaa asar madhya bharat main bhi aayaa or malwa ke sihor main angrej polytical agent kaa mukhyalay hone ke kaaran yahaan ke sipaahiyon ne bhi vidroh kar diya or is chhavnee ko puri tarah angrejon se mukt karvaa liya. 
 kintu yah sarkaar maatr 6 maheene hi chali. jhaansi kii rani ke vidroh ko kuchalane ke liye barbar karnal heroj ko ek bade laav lashkar ke saath bheja gaya. indaur main sainikon ke vidroh ko kuchalane ke baad karnal heroj 13 janvari 1858 ko sihor pahunchaa or agle din 14 janvari 1858 ko 356 vidrohi sipaahiyon ko sihor kii sivan nadi ke kinare gher kar goliyon se chalni kar diya.
 krantikariyon ke is samuhik hatyakand ke baad sihor chhavnee par punah angrajon kaa aadhipaty ho gaya. is barbar hatyakand ke kaaran is sthaan ko malwa ke jaliyanvaala ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
