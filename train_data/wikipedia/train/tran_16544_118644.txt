 nimna raajnneeti ek aisi avadharana he jisamen aise sabhi maamalon ko shaamil kiya gaya he jo ki rajya ke astitv ke liye itane mahatvapurn nahin hai jitne kii aarthik or saamaajik maamle hai. nimna raajnneeti rajya ke kalyaan kaa anukshetra he. yah avadharana ucch raajnneeti kii avadharana ke vipareet he jo rajya ke astitv or sakht rashtriya suraksha ke bare main vichaar karti he. kohan or naayi ne varnan karte hue bataaya he ki pehle ke samay, antarrashtriya sambandh rashtriya suraksha par aadhaarit ek saral antarnirbharta yojana par aadhaarit the: ucch raajnneeti, or aajkal antarrashtriya sambandh gharelu muddon par aadhaarit ek jatil antarnirbharta dwaara sanchalit hai: nimna raajnneeti.
 antarrashtriya sambandhon kaa shastreey yathartwaadee siddhant keval ucch raajnneeti ko hi praasangik maanata he or nimna raajnneeti ko puri tarah se khaarij kar detaa he. jatil antarnirbharta kaa udaar siddhant ucch raajnneeti ko khaarij kiye bina nimna raajnneeti ko moulik samamajhata he.
