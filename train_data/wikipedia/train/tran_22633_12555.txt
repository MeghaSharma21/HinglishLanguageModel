 mike pens
 
 mike pens kaa janm columbus,indiana main 7 june san 1959 ko hua. unke pita america sena main the or korian yuddh main bahaduree ke liye unhen 1953 main kaansya padak diya gaya.
 pence ne 'uttar columbus skool' se praathmik shikshan puraa kiya. unhone indiana vishvavidhyalay se kanoon ke sandarbh main sanshodhan or padhai puri kii.
 2015 main mike pence indiana rajya ke rajyapal chune gaye.
