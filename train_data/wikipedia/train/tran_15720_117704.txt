 jaigarh durg bharat ke pashchimi rajya rajasthan kii rajdhani jaipur main aravali parvatamala main chil kaa tila naamak pahari par amer durg evam mavata jhil ke upree ore bana kila he. is durg kaa nirmaan jaisingh dwitiya ne 1726 i. main amer durg evam mahal parisar kii suraksha hetu karavaaya tha or iskaa naam unheen ke naam par rakhaa gaya he.
 
 jaigarh durg ko jeet kaa kila bhi kaha jaataa he. yah durg amer main sthit he, jaipur shahar seemaa me yah kila 1726 main banakar taiyaar hua tha. yahan par vishwa kii sabase badi top rakhi hui he.
