 raja edward kii kursi jise sant edward kii kursi or rajbhishek kursi bhi kaha jaataa he, ek lakdi kaa bana singhaasana he jisapar britain ke samraaton kaa rajbhishek hota he. iskaa nirmaan 1296 i main raja edward pratham ne karavaaya tha. unhone isamen ek vishesh kaanaa banwaaya jisamen skin kaa pathar rakhaa jaataa he. yah pathar scotland ke raajaaon ke rajbhishek ke liye istemaal hota he or ise edward pratham scot logon se chin kar laaye the. yah kursi landan ke prasiddh vastminster abi naamak giraje main rakhi jaati he. san 1308 ke baad saare british samraaton kaa rajbhishek isi kursi par hua he, sivaay meri pratham or meri dwitiya ke.
 jab se england ne scotland par qabza karke uskaa british samrajya main vilay kar liya tab se scotland main vidroh kii bhaavana chalati rahi he - jo kabhi madhyam ho jaati he or kabhi teevr. skin ke pathar kii landan main maujuudagi ko bahut se scot log apni gulaami kaa pratik maanate the. isliye san 1996 main in scoti bhaawanaaon ko dhyaan main rakhte hue angrezi sarkaar ne faisla kiya ke skin kaa pathar scotland main rakhaa jayega or landan tabhi laayaa jayega jab kisi nae maharaj yaa maharani kaa rajbhishek hona ho.
