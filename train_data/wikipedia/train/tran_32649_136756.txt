 1999 yuiefee champions lig final 26 may 1999 buudhvaar, ko hui thi ki ek footbal match tha. match 1998-99 yuiefee champions lig ke vijeta kaa nirdhaaran karne ke liye, barsilona, spain main camp nou main khelaa gaya tha. final match manchester united or bern munich bich tha. match manchester united match ke adhikansh ke liye peechha karne ke baad, 2-1 se jeetne kii chot samay main do antim kshanon main gol scoring ke liye yaad kiya jaataa he.
 is jeet ve bhi premier lig or egge cap jeeta jisamen manchester united ke liye unke tihara jeetne ke mausam kaa hissaa tha. bern munich unke grey or bargandi champions lig kit pahana, jabaki manchester united, unke paramparaagat laal shart pahani thi.
