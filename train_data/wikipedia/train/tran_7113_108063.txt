 rorima parvat, dakshin america kii pakaraima parvat shrinkhalaa ke tepui pathaar kaa ucchtam parvat he. iskaa sabase pehla varnan angrezi anveshak sar walter raaligh ke 1596 ke lekhon main miltaa he, jinhone likhaa he ki iske 31 kimi2 ke shikhar kii raksha iske chaaron ore kii 400 meter unchi khadi chattaane karti he. is parvat par venezuela, brazil or guyana kaa tihara seemaa bindu sthit he. 
 rorima parvat, venezuela ke dakshin pashchimi kone main sthit, 30000 varg kimi kshetra main faile kanaima rashtriya udyaan main sthit he. yah guyana shield par sthit he or guyana adhityaka kaa sarvocch shikhar he. udyaan ke pathari parvaton ko prithvi par upasthit kuch sabase puraani bhuvaigyanik sanrachanaon main se ek mana jaataa he, jinaki aayu purvacambrian kaal yani lagbhag do arab saal aanki gayi he.
