  limaah roberta gabovi  liberia kii shanti kaaryakarta hai. inhen 2011 main nobel shanti puraskaar se sammanit kiya he. ye puraskaar unhone liberia kii raashtrapati ellen janson sarlif or yaman kii mahila adhikaar kaaryakarta tavaakul karman ke saath sanjha kiya. unhone mahilaon ke shanti andolan kaa netritv kiya he jis kaaran 2003 main dwitiya liberian grihayuddh kaa ant karne main madad hui. is andolan main ellen sarlif ne bhi mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai or 2005 main liberia main muft chunaav sakasham ho sakaa jisamen sarlif jiti or raashtrapati bani.
