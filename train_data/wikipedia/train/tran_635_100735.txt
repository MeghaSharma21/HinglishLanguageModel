 manushyon kii anuvanshiki main pitruvansh samooh us vansh samooh yaa hapalogrup ko kehete hai jiskaa purushon ke waahe gun sutara par sthit diene kii jaanch se pataa chalata he. agar do purushon kaa pitruvansh samooh miltaa ho to iskaa arth hota he ke unka hajaaron saal purv ek hi purush purvaj rahaa he, chaahe aadhunik yug main yah donon purush alag-alag jatiyon se sambandhit hi kyon na hon.
 angrezi main "vansh samooh" ko "hapalogrup", "pitruvansh samooh" ko "waahe chromosome hapalogrup" or "matruvansh samooh" ko "amtidiene hapalogrup" kehete hai.
 samay se saath-saath kabhi kisi vyakti ke diene main aisa badlaav aata he jo aane vaali pidiyon ke diene main hameshaa ke liye aasaani se pahachaane jane vaale chinh chhod jaataa he. jab aisa hota he to us vansh samooh ke sadsy se ek nayaa upavansh samooh armbh hota he. manushya jaati africa se shuru hui or us pehle vansh samooh se nae vansh samoohon kii shakhaayen banati ja rahi hai. yah vriksh dikhataa he ke kaunsa vansh samooh kis dusre vansh samooh kii santan he.
