 itihasvid dow ramendranath mishra ke anusaar 1875 main rajanandgaanv ke raja mahant ghasidas ne chhattisgarh ke puravaibhav ke sangrah hetu isaki sthapana kii thi. yah madhyapradesh or chhattisgarh ke sabase pracheen sangrahalayo main se he. yah bharat ke aath pracheen sangrahalayon main se ek he. sangrahalay ke antargat raja mahant sarveshravaradaas granthalay rajya ke sabase puraane granthalayon main se ek he.
