 vidyutchumbakeey varnakram main un sari aavritiyon ke vikiran aate hai jo sambhav hai. kisi vastu kaa vidyutchumbakeey varnakram, us vastu se vidyut chumbakeey vikiranon kaa abhilakshanik vitaran yaa praayah keval varnakram hota he.
 vidyutchumbakeey varnakram nimna aavritiyon, jo ki nutan radio main prayog hotin hai, se lekar gamma vikiran tak hota he, jo ki sahasron kilometre kii tarangadairghy se lekar ek anu ke naap ke ek ansh ke baraabar tak kii sari aavritiyon ko liye hota he. hamaare brahmand main laghu tarangadairghy simit he plank duuri ke aasapaas tak; or deergh tarang dairghy simit he, brahmand ke aakaar tak. vaise varnakram ko aseemit evam aviraam hi kehete hai.
 spectrum ke vibhinn vibhaagon main suvidha ke liye saadhaarant: tarangadaighya ke bhinn-bhinn ekak prayukt hote hai. radio prasaaran main 1 meter ko ekak mana jaataa he, tathaa radio ke sookshm tarang vibhaag main ek millimetre ko ekak mana jaataa he. avarakt varnakram ke liye 10-4 sein.me. kaa ekak prachlit he tathaa drishy prakaash ke liye isse bhi chote 10-8 sein.me. ke ekak kii aavashyaktaa hoti he. 10-4 sein. me. ke ekak ko mu or drishy prakaash ke ekak ko 'angastram' kehete hai. prarambh main eksre ke liye bhi angastram upyog main laayaa jaataa tha, kintu eksre varnakram main adhik avishkaar hone par is ekak se bhi sookshm ekak kii aavashyaktaa hone lagi. at: eksre ke liye tathaa gamma kirnon ke liye zeegabhra ne ek nae ekak kaa upyog kiya, jise eksre ekak kehete hai. yah 10-11 sein. me. ke baraabar hota he. vidyutchumbakeey siddhant kii drushti se eksre or gamma kirnon main koi bhed nahin he; eksre prayogshaalaon main utpann kiye ja sakate hai or gamma kirane radiodharmee padaartho se praapt hoti hai . vidyucchumbakeey varnakram main atyant swalp tarangadairghya kaa vibhaag eksre tathaa gamma kirnon kaa he. tarangadairghy aavritiyon kaa pratilomanupati hone ke kaaran eksre or gamma kirnon kii aavritiyaan anya vidyucchumbakeey vikiranon se bahut adhik hoti he.
