 charbagh farsi-style main baag kaa khaakaa hota he. ek vargakar baag ko chaar chote bhaagon main, chaar paidal pathon dwaara baanta jaataa he. farsi main, hindi samaan hi chaar arthath chaar, evam baag arthath baag hota he. tajmahal ke baag bhi isi style ke utkrishtatam udhaaharan hai. adhiktar mughal makbaron ke baag isi style main bane hai. iske harek baag main chaar phulon kii bady kyaariyaan hai. charbagh kaa mul faras ke ekaeminid samrajya kaaleen he. unani itihaaskaar herodots evam jenophon iskaa vyapak vivaran dete hai, jo ki cyprus kii rajasi nagari, pasargade main bane the.. bharat main shahjahan ne apni patni mumtaj mahal kii yaad main tajmahal banwaaya. yahan is style kaa upyog bahut hi khub he. anya aise udaahranon se pruthak, tajmahal charbagh ke madhya main naa hokar iske uttari kinare par sthit he. is baag main morpankhi ke ped lage hai. saath hi falon ke ped bhi dikhaai dete hai.
