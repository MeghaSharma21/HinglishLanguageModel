 bhautikee main kaksha yaa orbit dik main sthit ek bindu ke ird-gird ek maarg ko kehete hai jisapar chalakar koi vastu us bindu kii parikrama karti he. khagolashastra main aksar us bindu par koi bada tara yaa grah sthit hota he jiske ird-gird koi chota grah yaa upgrah apni kaksha main uski parikrama karata he. yadi khagoleey vastuon kii kakshaon ko dekha jaae to kai bhinn tarah kii kakshaayen dekhi jaati hai - kuch golakaar hai, kuch andakar hai or kuch in se adhik pechida hai.
