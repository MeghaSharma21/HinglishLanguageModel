 urugve rashtriya footbal team antarrashtriya footbal toornament main urugve kaa pratinidhitiv karti he or urugve footbal association dwaara niyantrit kii jaati he. team saamanytah los kharrus yaa laa seleste ke rup main bulaayi jaati he.
 urugve vartmaan dakshin america champion hai, 2011 main kopa america jeetne ke dwaara. urugve, kopa america ek ricard 15 baar jeeta he. team ne do baar fifa vishwa cap jeeta he, udghaatan 1930 vishwa cap main pehali baar or 1950 main doosraa khitaab, jab ve brazil ke khilaaf final match main jite. ve do baar greeshmakaleen olympic main footbal main swarna padak jeeta he, 1924 or 1928 me.
 raashtra kii ek bahut choti aabaadi he or unki safalataa is tathy se parilakshith hoti he. 1930 main apana pehla vishwa cap jite samay unki jansankhya keval 12.5 lakh thi.
