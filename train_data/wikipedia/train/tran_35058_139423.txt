 khmer kambuj kaa pracheen naam. iskaa prayog itihaas main kambuj ke aarambhik rajvansh, kala evam samskruti kii abhivyakti ke liye hota he. is prakaar 5veen shatee se lekar 13veen shatee i. tak kaa kambuj kaa itihaas khmer kaa itihaas kaha jaataa he. khmer kii khyaati suryavarman dwitiya dwaara nirmit ankor wat naamak bikhyath mandir ke liye he.
