 haridwar main chandighat sethu se bijanour jate samay lagbhag aadha pul hi paar karen to bain ore ek parnkuti main "divya prem seva mishan" dikhegi.
 chandighat par hi seva kunjaj naamak parisar main is mishan kaa apana ek chikitsaalay he, jise samidha sevarth chikitsaalay ke naam se janaa jaataa he. yahaan par pratidin jhuggi-jhopadi, vangurjaron ke lagbhag 70-80 saamaanya rogi chikitsa kaa laabh uthaate hai. is chikitsaalay kii ek shakha sushrut ulcer care centre bhi he, jisamen har roj lagbhag 40-50 kushtarogiyon ke ghaavon kii safai, aaparishan va marahampattee sevavrati kaaryakartaaon dwaara ni:shulk kii jaati he. yaheen par iske madhyam se mahilaon ko hastnirmit kaagaj ke vividh utpaadon jaise diary, foulder, kairi beg, greeting card, mobile stand sahit lagbhag 80 prakaar ke utpaadon kaa prashikshan diya ja rahaa he. samaaj main seva kii bhaavana ko jan-jan tak pahunchane tathaa seva mishan ke apane kaaryon ko mishan ke sadasyon tak pahunchane hetu dwimasik patrika seva jyoti kaa prakaashan kiya ja rahaa he.

 sabase mahatvapurn baat to yah he ki yah sanstha kisi bhi sarkari anudaan kii mohataaj nahin he, balki yah pavitra saamaajik mishan, shuddh rup se aam logon ke hi sahyog ke bal par chal rahaa he.
