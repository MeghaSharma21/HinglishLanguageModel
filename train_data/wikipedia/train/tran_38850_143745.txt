 ulia timshenko ukraine kii ek rajaneta hai. 27 navambar 1960 ko tatkaaleen soviet sangh or vartmaan ukraine main janmi timshenko ukraine kii pradhanmantri rah chuki hai. 2007 ke dauraan ukraine main hui naarangi kranti kaa netritv kar timshenko ne raajnneetik parivartan main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai. inhen rus kaa virdohi tathaa pashchimi deshon kaa pakshdhar mana jaataa he. vartmaan main timshenko ukraine ke karagaar main kaidi jivan vyateet kar rahi hai. inke viruddh raajnneetik shadyantra tathaa maanavadhikaar ulanghan kaa maamla nyaayalay main lambit he.
