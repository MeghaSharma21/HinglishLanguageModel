 dow bhagwaan singh gyani ‘preetam’ bhartiya rashtravaadi tathaa gadar party ke pramukh netaa the. ve 1914 se 1920 tak gadar party ke adhyaksh rahe. pratham vishwayuddh ke samay 1915 ke gadar kranti main unki pramukh bhoomika thi. is kranti ke asafal hone par ve japan chale gayi the. unki 'hindustan gadar' main prakashit rashtrabhaktipurna kavitaayen bahut prasiddh hai. baad main in kavitaaon ko 'gadar di gunj' ke naam se sankalit kiya gaya.
 bhai bhagwaan singh kaa janm taran taaran main hua tha. unki mata kaa naam har cor tathaa pita kaa naam sarmukh singh tha. unki padhai mukhyatah unke dada baba ratan singh kii dekharekh main hui.
