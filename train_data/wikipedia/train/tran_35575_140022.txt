 parasgarh durg karnataka rajya ke belgaum jila main ek durg ke avshesh hai. yah aaleeshaan durg 10veen shatabdi main ratta vansh ke shaskaon ne banwaaya tha. 
 durg soundatti se 2 kimi dakshin main ek pahari ke dakshin-pashchimi chor se laga hua bana he. yahaan hindu panchang ke anusaar shaq samvath 901 kaa ek shilaalekh he or ek hanuman ji kaa mandir bhi he.
