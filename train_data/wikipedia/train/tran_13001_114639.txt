 pratap sahagal hindi ke kavi, naatakkaar, kahanikar or aalochak hai.
 pratap sahagal kaa janm 10 may 1945, jhang, pashchim punjab main hua. inhone hindi vishay se snatakottar kii shiksha praapt kar zakir hossain snatakottar mahavidhyalay ke hindi vibhaag main praadhyaapak ke pad par niyukt huye or isi mahavidhyalay se associat professor ke pad se sevanivruth bhi. desh bhar main mahatvpurn natya nirdeshkon yatha satish annd, ravi baswani, raja bundela, subhash sahagal tathaa akhilesh akhil dwaara baar baar manchan. anveshak tathaa rang basanti bahu-charchit, bahu prashansit anveshak kaa parishad rang mandal dwaara 2003 main, phir isi varsh sangeet naatak academy ke rang-swarna samaaroh main shaamil or isi naatak se chandigarh main rashtriya natya samaaroh kii shuruyaat anek naatak doordarshan evam radio par prasaarit anek rachanaaon kaa bhartiya evam videshi bhashaaon main anuwaad prakashit prasaarit tathaa ye kai swafun sevi sansthaaon se sambaddh hai.
