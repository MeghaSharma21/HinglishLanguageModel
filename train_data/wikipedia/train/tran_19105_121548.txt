 "kathakal", kii samiksha karte hue "da hindu" likhata he:
 ek laghu katha lekhak ke rup main, indu menon ko kamala das kaa uttaradhikari kaha ja saktaa he, jisane kavita or kalpana kii duniya ko aasaani se paar kiya yah, haalanki, yah suzav nahin de sakate ki vah 'kamala das mold' main dhale ek kahani-kalakaar hai. chalate raste par chalane to dur, vah saahsee dhang se nae ilaakon kii talash karti he. indu menon ke vishay or saath hi takanic ne dhyaan aakarshith kiya. gender or kaamuktha jaise muddon, jo yuva mahila lekhakon ko aam taur par vyast rakhte hai, se paar ja kar vah kerala samaaj ko chunauti detii he, jo vibhinn staron par pratigaman ke achook sanket dikhaa rahaa he. udhaaharan ke liye, vah sampradayikta kii shaktiyon se aise dhang se pesh aati he jaise anya lekhakon, purush yaa mahila ne kabhi hi kiya he. uski 'nai mahila' kii awaz he, jo abhi tak saarvajanik sthaan main khud ko sunai nahin de rahi he.
