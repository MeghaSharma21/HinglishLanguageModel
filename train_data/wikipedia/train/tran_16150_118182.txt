 debratsen hungary kaa doosraa sabase bada nagar he. yah hungary ke 'uttari vishaal maidan' kshetra kaa kendra he. 18veen shatabdi main yah hungary kaa sabase bada nagar hua karata tha. aaj bhi yah hungary ke sarvaadhik mahatvapurn saanskrithik kendron main se ek he. yah budapest se 180 kimi purv kii ore sthit he. 
 yah nagar us sthal par basaa he, jahaan teen bhinn prakritik pradesh pusta kaa charaagaah, nirseg kaa balua pathaar env beritio kaa daldali kshetra, milte hai. madhyakaalin yug main yah do pramukh sadakon kaa chauraahaa tha. ek sadak jetmer se pashchimi europe kii ore jaati thi tathaa duusari bohemia se tresilavenya ko milaati thi. us yug main in sadakon par kaaphi yatayaat hota tha. aaj bhi debratsen ek pramukh rail jankshan tathaa pashu or ann-vyapaar kaa centre he. yahan ek acha vishvavidhyalay tathaa ek krishi academy he. yahan meley bhi lagate hai. protestant dharmavalambiyon kaa yah garh he.
