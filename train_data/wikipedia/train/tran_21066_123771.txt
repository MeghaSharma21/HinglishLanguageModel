 shivaraj bhushan, kavi bhushan kii prasiddh rachana he. isamen kul 385 padya hai. bhushan ne apni is kriti kii rachana-tithi jyeshtha vadi trayodashi, ravivaar, sanmvat 1730 ko di he. is granth main ullikhit shivaji vishyak aitihaasik ghatnaayen 1673 i. tak ghatit ho chuki thim. isse bhi is granth kaa ukt rachanakaal thik thahartaa he tathaa saath main shivaji or bhushan kii samasamyikta bhi siddh ho jaati he. 'shivaraj-bhushan' main 385 chand hai. reetikaal ke kavi hone ke kaaran bhushan ne apana pradhan granth 'shivarajbhushan' alankaar ke granth ke rup main banaaya. dohon main alankaaron kii paribhaasha di gayi he tathaa kavitt evam savaiya chhandon main udhaaharan diye gayi hai, jinamein shivaji ke kaaryakalapon kaa varnan kiya gaya he.
