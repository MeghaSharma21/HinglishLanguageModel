 kisi vaidyut pranali ke do yaa adhik prabhaagon ko is prakaar vilgit karnaa ki unke bich main aavesh-vaahak kanon kaa pravaah nahin ho, vaidyut pruthakkaran kehalaata he. pruthakrut prabhaagon ke bich vidyut pruthakkaran ke hote hue bhi urja yaa soochna kaa vinimay sambhav he jo dharita, preran, vidyutchumbakeey tarangon, shravy tarangon yaa yantric vidhiyon se kiya jaataa he.
