 ulaan-ude rus ke buratiya pradesh kii rajdhani he, jo bykaal jhil ke dakshin-purv main 100 kilometre dur sthit he. jansankhya kii drushti se yah shahar purvi siberia kaa teesraa sabase bada shahar he. 17veen sadi ke madhya tak ulaan-ude main buryaat raajaaon kaa shashan tha. buryaat mongol jati se judi ek buddha khanaabadosh janajati he. buryaat log mul rup se khanaabadosh charvaahe hai, jinaki samskruti or bhashaa mongolian or tibeti baudhon se milti-julati he.
