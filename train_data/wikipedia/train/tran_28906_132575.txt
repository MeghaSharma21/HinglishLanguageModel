 dargah-e-alaa hazarat ahamed raja khan kii dargah he,jo 19veen shatabdi ke haniphi vidwaan, jo bharat main wahabi vichaardhaara ke kattar virodh ke liye jane jate hai .dargah uttar pradesh ke bhartiya rajya ke bareli main sthit he.  dargah kaa gumbad hajaaraat allamaa shah mahmud jaan kadri dwaara mcchasticks ke upyog ke saath taiyaar kiya gaya tha 
 dargah-e-alaa hazrat main ahamed raja khan kii punyatithi ke dauraan 2014 main, muslim maulaviyon ne taliban dwaara prachlit aatankwaad or wahabi sampradaay kii vichaardhaara kii ninda kii thi. 
 haalanki dargah ek baar urs-e-razavi ke liye mukhya site thi, aadhikarik urs bhi ab ek darjan se adhik deshon main manaai gai he. yah badi bheed or kai vidwaanon ke aagaman kii vajah se he. 
 dargah-e-alaa hazarat, bareli, uttar pradesh ke jile main carolan main sthit he or iske jile ke anya shaharon or rajya ke saath acchhee sampark he.
 bareli acchhee tarah se rashtriya rajmarg dwaara dilli or anya mahatvapurn jilon ke saath judaa hua he. dilli ke anand vihaar bas terminal se bareli tak baar-baar bas sevaayen hai. pramukh shaharon main se duuri hai: - 1- dilli: - 250 kilometre 2- lakhanu: - 250 kimi 3- agra: - 221 kimi 4- dehradun 300 kimi 5- nainital: -140 kimi
 ek dilli se gajiabad, hapur, gajraula, moradabad, rampur se bhi bareli tak pahunch saktaa he. nai dilli, mumbai, ahmedabad, kolkata, lakhanu, agra, ajmer, bhopal or bharat ke anya mahatvapurn shaharon se sidhi trainon ko bareli se joda gaya he.
