 das dharmadesh yaa das farmaan islam dharm,yahudee dharm or isai dharm ke vah das niyam hai jinke bare main un dharmik paramparaon main yah mana jaataa he ki ve dharmik netaa musa ko ishwar ne swayan die the. in dharmon ke anuyaayion kii manyata he ki yah musa ko sinaayi parvat ke upar die gaye the. pashchimi samskruti main aksar in das dharmadeshon kaa zikr kiya jaataa he yaa inke sandarbh main baat kii jaati he.
