 jhanjhunu jila 27.5' se 28.5'uttari akshansh tathaa 75 se 76 degree purvi deshaantar ke madhya he. iskaa
 rajasthan ke janaganana sanchaalan nideshaalay dwaara rajasthan ke ek jile jhunjhunun kii aadhikarik janaganana 2011 kii jaankaree jari kii gai he. rajasthan ke jhunjhunun jile main janaganana adhikariyon ne mahatvapurn vyaktiyon kii gananaa bhi kii thi. 
 2011 main, jhunjhunun kii jansankhya 2,137,045 thi, jisamen purush or mahila kramshah 1,095,8 9 6 or 1,041,14 9 thim. 2001 kii janaganana main, jhunjhunun kii aabaadi 1,913,68 9 thi, jinamein se purush 983,526 the or shesh 930,163 mahilaayein thim. jhunjhunun jila jansankhya kul maharashtra aabaadi kaa 3.12 pratishat gathit. 2001 kii janaganana main, jhunjhunun jile ke liye yah aankada maharashtra aabaadi kaa 3.3 9 pratishat tha.
 2001 ke anusaar aabaadi kii tulana main jansankhya main 11.67 pratishat parivartan hua tha. bharat kii pichhali janaganana main, jhunjhunun jile main 1 99 1 kii tulana main isaki aabaadi main 36.90 pratishat kii vruddhi darj kii gai.
 2011 kii janaganana ke liye kul jhunjhunun jansankhya main se 22.8 9 pratishat jile ke shahari kshetron main rahata he. shahari ilaakon main kul 48 9, 7 9 7 log rahete hai jinamein se purush 253,178 hai or mahilaayein 235, 9 01 hai. 2011 janaganana ke aankadon ke mutabik jhunjhunun jile ke shahari kshetra main sex anupaat 932 he. isi prakaar 2011 kii janaganana main jhunjhunun jile main bal ling anupaat 854 tha. shahari kshetra main bal aabaadi 65,951 thi jisamen se purush or mahilaayein 35,579 or 30,372 thim. jhunjhunun jile kaa yah bal aabaadi kul shahari aabaadi kaa 14.05% he. janaganana 2011 ke anusaar jhunjhunun jile main ausat sakshartaa dar 76.53% he jisamen se purush or mahila kramshah 87.3 9% or 65.03% sakshar hai. vaastavik sankhya main shahari kshetra main 323,811 log sakshar hai, jinamein se purush or mahilaayein kramshah 190,162 or 133,64 9 hai. 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar, jhunjhunun jilon kii 77.11% aabaadi gaanvon ke gramin ilaakon main rahati he. gramin ilaakon main rahane vaali kul jhunjhunun jila aabaadi 1,647, 9 66 he, jinamein se purush or mahila kramshah 842,718 or 805,248 hai. jhunjhunun jile ke gramin ilaakon main ling anupaat prati 1000 purush 956 mahilaayein hai. yadi jhunjhunun jile ke bal ling anupaat ke aankadon par vichaar kiya jaataa he, to prati 1000 ladakon kii sankhya 832 ladkiyaan hai. gramin ilaakon main 0-6 varsh kii umr main bal aabaadi 222,519 he, jinamein se purush 121,483 the or mahilaayein 101,036 thim. jhunjunun jile kii kul gramin aabaadi main bal aabaadi main 14.42% shaamil he. jhunjhunun jile ke gramin kshetron main sakshartaa dar janaganana data 2011 ke mukaable 73.42% he. ling ke anusaar purush or mahila sakshartaa kramash: 86.75 or 59.77 pratishat thi. kul milakar, 1,046,549 log sakshar the jinamein se purush or mahila kramash: 625,672 or 420,877 thim.
 bagarh, bakhtavarpura, chidawa, pilaani, udaipur vati, mandava, navalgadh aadi kasbe isi main he. jhunjhunuun kaa naam lete man josh evam shraddha se bhar jaataa he. yahaan ke jarre-jarre se uthne vaali deshbhakti kii aawaaj se josh to gaanv-gaanv main sthapit shaheedon kii pratimaaon ko dekhakar man main shraddha kaa bhav swatah hi aa jaataa he. sena main jane tathaa mathrubhumi ke liye shahid hone kaa jajba jaisaa yahaan dikhaai detaa he, shaayad hi kahin par dikhaai de. baat chaahe aazaadee se pehle kii ho yaa baad kii, jile ke jabanj sainikon ne dushmano ke daant khattey kar apni bahaduree kaa loha manvaaya. veerta ke baad jhunjhunuun kaa dharm-karm ke maamle main alag mukaam he. yahaan jila mukhyalay par sthit daadi ranisti kaa mandir to vishwa bhar main prasiddh hai. jhunjhunuun main hazrat kamruddin shah kii dargah evam chanchalnath ji tila apane aap main anuthe hai. bataaya jaataa he ki hazrat kamruddin evam chanchalnath main gahari dosti thi. donon kaa sampradayik sadbhaav bhi gajab kaa tha. tabhi to jhunjhunuun main aaj bhi donon jagah hone vaale kaaryakramon main ganga-jamuni samskruti kaa jhalak dikhaai detii he.
