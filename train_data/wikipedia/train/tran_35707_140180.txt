 ramesh maharaj trinidad ke rajanyik hai.
 shri maharaj ko kanoonee peshey main 30 saal kaa anubhav tha. iglish vakil sangh main vah 1967 se practis kar rahaa tha. vah kai prakashnon ke lekhak hai. unamem se ek he "rule off lo in trinidad and tobaggo". 
 1991 main unhen hous off reprasantatius ke liye chunaa gaya.
 ramesh maharaj, nanan maharaj or doli nanan maharaj kaa betaa tha. ramesh maharaj kii shaadi linete maharaj se hua or ek betaa or judwan betiyaan ke pita he. vah vyapaaree krishna maharaj kaa bhai bhi he. indira rambachan unki bahan he.
