 kachhi pakistan ke balochistan praant kaa ek zila he. san 2008 tak yah bolaan zila kehalaata tha. zile kii rajdhani dhadar naamak shahar he.
 kachhi ke maidan ati-pracheen mehrgarh samskruti kii mathrubhumi he. yah bhartiya upmahadweep kaa pehla kshetra he jahaan sangathit krishi or maveshi paalan ke chinh mili hai, jinaki tithi 7000 isapurv aanki gai he.
