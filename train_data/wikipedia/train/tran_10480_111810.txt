 mohini hindu bhagwaan vishnu kaa ekmaatr stri rup avatar he. isamen unhen aise stri rup main dikhaaya gaya he jo sabhi ko mohit kar le. uske prem main vasheebhoot hokar koi bhi sab bhul jaataa he, chaahe vah bhagwaan shiv hi kyon na hon. is avatar kaa ullekh mahabharat main bhi aata he. samudr manthan ke samay jab devataaon va asuron ko sagar se amrit mil chukaa tha, tab devataaon ko yah dar tha ki asur kahin amrit pikar amar na ho jaayen. tab ve bhagwaan vishnu ke paas gayi va prarthana kii ki aisa hone se roke. tab bhagwaan vishnu ne mohini avatar lekar amrit devataaon ko pilaayaa va asuron ko mohit kar amar hone se roka.
 kai vibhinn kathaon ke anusaar mohini rup ke vivaah kaa prasang bhi aayaa he, jisamen shiv se vivaah va vihaar kaa vishesh vivaran aata he. iske alaava bhasmasur prasang bhi prasiddh he.
