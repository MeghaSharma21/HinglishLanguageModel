 maanav tairaaki main aam taur par ek vishisht prakaar kii shaareerik gati yaa swimming stroke ko baar-baar doharaaya janaa shaamil hota he. stroke kai prakaar ke hote hai or prayek ek alag tairaaki style yaa crol ko paribhaashit karata he. 
 zyaadaatar strokes main sharir ke sabhi pramukh angon - dhad, baaju, pair, haath, paanv or sir kii layaaddh or samanvit harakatein shaamil hoti hai. sanson kaa talmail bhi aam taur par strokes ke saath hi hona chahie. haalanki, haathon ke bagaiyr keval pairon kii harkaton se yaa pairon ke bagaiyr keval haathon kii harkaton se bhi tairnaa sambhav he; is tarah ke strokes kaa istemaal vishesh uddeshyon ke liye, prashikshan yaa vyaayaam ke liye, yaa amputies or paralities dwaara kiya ja saktaa he.
 kai strokes kaa istemaal keval vishesh uddeshyon ke liye kiya jaataa he, jaise kisi vastu main herpher ke liye, yaa sirf tairte rahane ke liye.
