 canada har sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main bhaag liya he, or har baar kam se kam ek padak jeeta he. desh kii sabase badi pradarshan vainkuvar, jahaan canada ke athlete 24 ve pichley sheetakaaleen olympic main jeeta se jeeta 26 padak-do or, turine, italy main 2010 main sheetakaaleen olympic main haal hi main kiya gaya tha. 2010 ke khelon main bhi, canada ne ek sheetakaaleen olympic main 14 se sabase zyada swarna padak jite hai. yah 1976 main soviet sangh dwaara nirdhaarit ek game main 13 swarna padak ke pichley record ko gujarata he or 2002 main norge se mail khaataa he.
 canada ne do baar sheetakaaleen khelon kii mejabani kii he, 1988 main calgary main, or 2010 main vainkuvar main. canada ne 1976 main montrial main samar olympic khelon kii mejabani bhi kii thi.

 canada ne maujuuda sheetakaaleen khelon main kabhi bhi olympic padak nahin jeeta he: luge, nordic sanyukt, or ski jumping.
