 kendriya hindi samiti vah sheershasth samiti he, jo niti nirdhaaran main bade str par maargdarshan uplabdh karaati he or rajbhasha niti ko lagu karne ke liye antarmantralay or antarvibhageeya samanvay sunishchit karte hue nitigat vishay se sambandhit nirnyon ke karyanvayan kaa paryvekshan karti he. bharat ke grih mantri is samiti ke upaadhyaksh hai or grih rajya mantri iske sadsy hai.
 is samiti main kul 41 sadsy hai, jinamein chah mantralayon- videsh mantralay, maanav sansaadhan mantralay, sanchaar or soochna praudyogic mantralay, railway mantralay, soochna prasaaran mantralay tathaa carmik, jan shikaayat or pension vibhaag ke mantri shaamil hai. sthapit vyavastha ke anusaar is samiti main rajyon kaa pratinidhitiv chah rajyon jaise desh ke teen bhashiya kshetron namat: k, kha, va, evam prati kshetra se do mukhya mantriyon dwaara kiya jaataa he. vartmaan main chah rajyon assam, bihar, kerala, odisha, punjab or rajasthan ke mukhyamantri shaamil hai. sansadeey rajbhasha samiti ke upaadhyaksh or isaki teenon up-samitiyon ke sanyojak kul milakar chaar vyakti is samiti ke paden sadsy hai. inke atirikt desh bhar se 21 prakhyat vidyavan or sahityakar, or kendriya grih mantralay ke rajbhasha vibhaag ke sachiv bhi iske sadsy hai.
