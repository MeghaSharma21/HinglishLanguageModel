  bhautikee main veg kaa arth kisi disha main chaal hota he. yah ek sadish raashi he. ek vastu kaa veg alag-alag dishaao main alag alag ho saktaa he. kisi vastu ke sthiti badalane kii dar ko veg kehete hai. chaal yadi disha ke saath likhi jaae to wo veg ke tulya hi hoti he, udhaaharan ke liye 60 kimi/ghanta uttar kii taraf. veg gatiki kaa ek mahatvapurn bhaag he, jo yantriki kii ek shakha he jisame vastuo ke gati kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he.
 	veg ek sadish bhautik maatra he ; donon pariman or disha ise paribhaashit karne ke liye aavashyak hai. veg ke adish nirpeksh mulya chaal ko SI pranali main meter prati second main mapa jaataa he. udhaaharan ke liye, "5 meter prati second" ek adish he, jabaki "5 meter prati second purvi disha main" sadish raashi he.
 kisi vastu kii ek khaas disha main jo chaal hoti he use us vastu kaa us disha main veg kaha jaataa he. chaatro main ek saamaanya avadharana pai jaati he ki veg sirf usee disha main hi nikaala jaataa he jis disha main vastu chalati hui prathit hoti he. aisa nahin he, veg us disha main bhi nikaala ja saktaa he jis disha main vastu kii chaal prathit nahin hoti he. jabaki chaal veg kaa pariman hota he.
