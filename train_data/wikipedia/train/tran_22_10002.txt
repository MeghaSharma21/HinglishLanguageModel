 3 ninjas kik back ek 1994 america-japani agali kadi he film 3 ninjas ke liye. yah aalochakon se zyaadaatar nakaaratmak samiksha praapt kiya. yah keval he 3 ninjas film ek video game anukulan ke saath. pehle jari hone ke baavjood 3 ninjas knuckley ap, yah film vastav main ek varsh baad main goli maar di thi. se mori ke antim naam parivartan tanaaka se shinataro koi spasht kaaran ke liye is film ko mul film se main. is film ke baad kiya gaya tha, haalanki knuckley ap, yah pehle jari kiya gaya thaknuckley ap kanoonee muddon ke kaaran.
 jab rocky, colt or tum tum unki team ke saath ek mahatvapurn besabaal khel khelnaa chahie, ve japan main unke dada bachaav karne kaa faisla. haalanki baad main vah jeeta tha ek chaaku main bahut ruchi rakhte hai jo thag dwaara apharan kar liya gaya tha ek marshall arts toornament 50 saal pehle.
