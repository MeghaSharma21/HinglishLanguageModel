 ek khilaadi ek hasina bhartiya hindi film he, jiskaa nirdesan suparn verma ne kiya he. iskaa pradarshan 18 navambar 2005 ko cinemagharon main kiya gaya tha.
 ek khilaadi ek hasina do logon arjuna or uske dost rohit par aadhaarit he. sikandar don ke karodon rupayon ko le kar bhaag jaataa he. usase pehle vah rohit ko maar detaa he. arjuna duusare shahar bhaag jaataa he. iske baad arjuna apana ek dal taiyaar karata he. pehle arjuna kaa yah yojana paise ke liye lag rahaa tha. par baad main pataa chalata he ki yah uske dost rohit kaa badla lene ke liye tha. jise jahangir kaa vyakti maar detaa he. is film ke ant main yah dikhaaya jaataa he ki jahangir ke paas ek remote he jo arjuna or uske doston ke vimaan main lage bam kaa he. lekin yah nahin dikhaaya jaataa he ki vah vimaan ko udaaya yaa nahin.
