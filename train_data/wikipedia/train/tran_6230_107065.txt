 bhartiya rasinic jeevigyaan santhaan ) bharat kii un badi prayogshaalaon main se ek he, jisane apni sthapana ke samay se hi sankramak rogon, khaskar lishmaniysis evam cholera par buniyadi anusandhan kaary karne hetu bahu-anushaasanic saghan prayaas kiya he or saath hi rogon ki pareekshan, immunoprophilaxis evam chemo therapi ke liye praudyogiki kaa vikaas kiya he. 
 is santhaan kii sthapana jaevchikitsiya anusandhan ke liye bharat main pratham gaiyr sarkari centre ke rup main 1935 main hui thi or 1956 main ise siesaiaar ke sangrakshan main shaamil kiya gaya. aaj bhartiya rasinic jeevigyaan santhaan rashtriya mahatv ke rogon or vaishvik hit kii jaivik samasyaon par anusandhan kar rahaa he. iske vaigyanik staff sadsy rasayan, jaevrasayan, koshika jeevigyaan, aanvik jeevigyaan, tannika jeevigyaan or pratirakshavigyaan sahit vibhinn prakaar ki kshetron main vishesh gyaan rakhte hai. unki yah visheshgyata utpaadak antar-anushaadasnik antakriya ko badhaava detii he.
 tantrika jeevigyaan samooh merudendi jeevon ke mastishk ke vikaas or saath hi maanav gatishiltaa vikruti ke jenesis par anusandhan karne main sanlagn he. prakritik sroton se praapt jaevskriy upadan or rasinic drushti se sanshleshikrut nae anuon kii khoj sakasham aushadhiyon ke rup main kii ja rahi he. jin anya kshetron main kaary kiye ja rahe hai ve hai gastric hyper acidity evam ulcer, mansapeshiy distrophy evam usase sambandhit vikruti, brihadanvik (mechromolaculur sanrachana kaary vishleshan, lakshith aushadhi sanvitaran paddati kaa vikaas, shukranu jeevigyaan or protein rasayan tathaa anzimiki.
