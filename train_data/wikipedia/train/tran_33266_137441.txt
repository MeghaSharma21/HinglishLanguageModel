 australian adivasee australia kii mukhyabhumi or tasmania dweep ke mul nivasee hai. ve kai shakhaon main sangathit hai or inaki apni kai mul bhashaayen hai. britain dwaara upniveshi qabze se pehle ve australia main bahusankhyak the. 1970 ke dashak tak australia kii sarkaar unki sankruthik viraasat ko nasht karne kaa prayaas karti rahi jiske antargat unke bacchon ko aksar mata-pita se alag kar ke europea-style kii paathshaalaon main bandi rakhaa jaataa tha. 13 farvari 2008 main australian sarkaar ne aupchaarik rup se is samudaay se kshamaa maangi. australian adivasee sangeet va kala sheliyaan ab vishvabhar main prasiddh hai.
