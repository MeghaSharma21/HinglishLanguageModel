 vishaal dadlaani ek bhartiya gayak ,music record nirmaataa, rachanaakaar he. ab tak vishaal dadlaani lagbhag 300 se 400 gane ja chuke he. inkaa janm mumbai main san 28 june 1973 ko hua tha. inhen bachapan se hi sangeet se vishesh lagaav tha is kaaran inke mata-pita bhi bahut prem karte the. vishaal kaa janm ek sindhi parivaar main hua tha jo mumbai maharashtra main rahete the. ye ek swayan siikhe sangeetkaar he or ye rock ,electronica tathaa hip-hop style main gayan karte hai. inhone apni shiksha hill grange hai skool se shuru kii thi
