 vijayadevnarayan saahi kaa janm 07 oktober 1924 ko kabir chaura, varanasi main hua. prayag vishwa vidhyalay se angreji main mem karne ke baad teen varsh tak kaashi viapeeth main shikshan kaary kiya iske baad prayag vishwa vidhyalay main praadhyaapak rahe. hindi nayi kavita ke pramukh kaviyon-jagadish gupt, ramswarup chaturvedi or vijayadevanrayan saahi main se ek hai. saahi ji anek majdoor sanghathanon se jude rahe or anek baar jail gayi. 05 navambar 1982 ko inkaa nidhan ho gaya.
 vijayadevnarayan saahi kii kavitaaon main marmasparshi vyangy hai. machhlee ghar, tathaa saakhi inaki kavy-kritiyaan hai. ye agyeya dwaara sampadit teesraa saptak ke charchit prayogvaadi kavi hai. inhone niabandh tathaa samalochna bhi likhi he.
