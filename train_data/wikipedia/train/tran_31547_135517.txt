 khagoleey vastu aisi vastu ko kaha jaataa he jo brahmand main prakritik rup se paayi jaati he, yani jisaki rachana manushyon ne nahin kii hoti he. isamen taare, grah, prakritik upgrah, gallexi aadi shaamil hai.
 khagoleey vastu ko angrezi main "salastial body" or urdu-farsi main "ajaram falki" yaa "ajaram aasamaani" kaha jaataa he. 
 ye asi vastue hen jo prathavi ke vaataavaran se purnataya baahar he | jese kii chandramaa, surya ,tathaa duusare grah .ye sabhi to bahut hi chote khagoili pind hen . inke alaava kai to ese hen jo bahut bade bhi hen . kuch grahon ke bich-bich me ring numa saranchana me pind fele rahete he. jese kii kupoler ring me kai saare astroids bahut adhik maatr me fele hue hen .
