 shilong bhubhautikeey anusandhan centre kii sthapana bhartiya bhoochumbakatv santhaan ke tisare kshetreey centre ke rup main 18 janvari 2016 ko hui. is santhaan kaa pramukh uddeshy bukamp prakriyaaon ko ujagar karne or bukamp ke purvanumaanon ke liye ek agradoot model kaa srijan karnaa he. shilong main sthapit naye kendra kaa anusandhan main dhyaan sankhyatmak modelling upkarnon dwaara sahaayta praapt avlokan takanic kii ek kism kaa upyog karke uttar-purv main vibhinn vayumandaleey kshetron main purv or sah-bukampiy signecharon ke gudh rahasyon kaa pataa lagane par kendrit hoga. esajiarsi main lagaaye gayi upkarnon se is kshetra main aane vaale bukamp sambandhi sanketon kii chetavanee jaldi milane se is kshetra main bukamp ke padane vaale vyapak prabhaav ko kam karne main sahaayta milegi.
