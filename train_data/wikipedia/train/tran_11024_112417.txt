 
 dandpani jayakaantan ek bahumukhi tamil lekhak hai -- keval laghu-kathakaar or upanyaaskaar hi nahin parantu nibandhkaar, patrkaar, nirdeshak or aalochak bhi hai. vicitra baat yah he ki unki skool kii padhai kuch paanch saal hi rahi!
 ghar se bhaag kar 12 saal ke jayakaantan apane chachaa ke yahan pahunche jinase unhone communism ke bare main siikhaa. baad main chennai aakar jayakaantan bhartiya communist party kii patrika janashakti main kaam karne lage. din main press main kaam karte or shaam ko sadakon par patrika bechate.
 1950 ke dashak kii shuruyaat se hi vah likhte aa rahe hai or jaldi hi tamil ke jane-maane lekhakon main gine jane lage. haalaanki unka nazariyaa vam paksheeya hi rahaa. vah khud party ke sadsy na rahe or congress party main bhartee ho gaye.
 40 upanyaason ke alaava unhone kai-kai laghukathaayen, aatmakatha or romen rolland dwaara french main rachi gayi gandhi ji kii jeevani kaa tamil anuwaad bhi kiya he.
 inke dwaara rachit ek upanyaas shil nerangalil shil manitarkal ke liye unhen san 1972 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 "jatil maanav swabhaav ke gahare or samvedansheel samaj" ke hetu, unki kritiyon ko "tamil sahitya kii ucch paramparaon kii abhivriddi" ke liye 2002 main gyanpith puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
