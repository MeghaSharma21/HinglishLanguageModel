 abtabad yaa abttabad yaa abtabad pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva rajya ke hazaaraa kshetra main sthit ek shahar he. yah nagar islamabad se 50 kimi uttar-pashchim or peshawar se 150 kimi purv orash ghati main 4,120 foot kii oonchaai par basaa hua he. abtabad shahar abtabad zile kii rajdhani he. yah shahar puure pakistan main apane lubhaavne mausam, shreshth viyapeethon or bahut se faujee sansthaanon ke liye mashhoor he.
 abtabad kaa naam angrezi fuj ke major james abbat par rakhaa gaya, jisane punjab par angrezi qabza ho jane ke baad janvari 1853 main is shahar kii niv rakhi. vah punjab ke hazaaraa kshetra kaa 1849 se april 1853 tak dipti kamishnar tha. abtabad jald hi hazaaraa kshetra kaa prashasan centre ban gaya. yahan ek badi faujee chhavnee bane gayi jo abhi tak chali aa rahi he. 1901 main shahar or chhavnee milakar abtabad kii kul aabaadi 7,764 thi. 1911 tak yah badhkar 11,506 ho gayi. 2005 main isaki anumaanit jansankhya 1,20,888 thi. aajkal ise islamabad ke bahari ilaaka kaa hissaa bhi mana jaataa he.
 1 may 2011 ko osama bin laden abtabad main ek badi kothi main chupa hua paaya gaya or america chhapamaron ne use vahin maar giraaya.
 1998 kii janaganana main yahan par 81,000 log rah rahe the jinamein se 94.26% kii maatrubhaasha punjabi kii hindko upbhasha thi. yahan ke bahut se log urdu or angrezi bhi samajhate hai. kuch pashto or punjabi kii potohari upbhasha bolne vaale bhi yahan rahete hai.
