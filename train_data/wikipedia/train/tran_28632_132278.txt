  
 hindu kush uttari pakistan ke vivaadeet bhaag se madhya afghanistan tak vistrut ek 800 kimi chalane vaali parvat shrunkhlaa he. iskaa sabase unchaa pahaad pakistan ke kaibar-pakhtuunakva praant ke chitral zile main sthit 7,708 meter lambaa tirich mir parvat he. hindu kush pamir parvaton se jaakar judate hai or himalay kii ek upashakha maane jate hai. hindu kush kaa doosraa sabase unchaa pahaad noshak parvat or teesraa istor-o-nal he. hindu kush pahaado kii atyaadhik oonchaai ke baavjood is shrunkhlaa main bahut se darre hai jinase is shrunkhlaa se logon kaa aana-janaa sadiyon se bana hua he.
 hindu kush parvaton ko sanskrit main 'paariatr parvat' bulaayaa jaataa tha. jab is ilaake main sikandar mahaan kii jeet hui to in parvaton ko unani bhashaa main 'kaukasosh indikaush' yani 'bhartiya parvat' bulaayaa jane laga. baad main inkaa naam 'hindu kush', 'hindu kuh' or 'kuh-e-hindu' padaa. 'kuh' yaa 'koh' kaa matlab to farsi main 'pahaad' hota he lekin 'kush' ke matlab ko lekar vivaad he. san 1333 isvi main ebn batuta ke anusaar iskaa matlab 'maarane vaala' tha. iskaa taatpary yah nikaala gaya ki uttari bhartiya upmahadweep par arabon-turkon ke qabze ke baad hinduon ko ghulaam banaakar in parvaton se le jaaya jaataa tha or unamem se bahut yahan barf main mar jaaya karte the. iske vipareet kuch vidwaanon ke anusaar 'kush' keval 'kuh' kaa ek or rup he, yaa phir 'hindu kash' yaa 'hindu kesh' kaa bigadaa rup he, jiskaa matlab 'bharat kii seemaa' nikalataa he. yah bhi ho saktaa he ki yah 'kaukasosh indikaush' vaale unani naam kaa do hazaar varshon ke baad kaa bigadaa hua rup ho. dhyaan den ki hindu kush or karakoram parvaton ke bich ek hindu raaj naamak parvat shrunkhlaa bhi he.
