 sukhoi esayu-26 purv soviet sangh kaa ekal seat vaala aerobetic vimaan he, jo ek radial ricouproting ingine dwaara sanchalit he. sukhoi esayu-26 main sidhe-sidhe pankh or nishchit landing gear hai, mukhya gear ek thos titanium arq par lagaaya gaya he. 
 sukhoi esayu-26 ne june 1984 main apni pehali udaan bhari, shuruaati chaar vimaan main do-mote blade pranodak the. rifind tail or ek german nirmit amtivi-9 3-blade kampal propeller ke saath sukhoi esayu-26em kaa utpaadan shuru hua. iske atirikt sudhaar or kiye gaye or 1986 vishwa hawaii-yuddh championships main model ne purushon or mahilaon kii team puraskaar donon jeeta. 2003 or 2005 ke aerobetic vishwa championships ke saath-saath 2004 ke europea championships main nayaa em9epa430-echpi engine ke saath sanshodhit sukhoi esayu-26em3 kaa varchasv rahaa.
