 kelanthan malaysia kaa ek rajya he. yah purvottaree malaysia main sthit he or isaki uttari seemaa thailand se lagati he. yah rajya malaysia ke anya rajyon kii tulana main adhik gramin or kam viksit he, jis karanvash yahan kii samskruti bhi vishesh he. yahan kii malay bhashaa bhi zaraa alag he, haalaanki anya malay upbhashaayen bolne vaale ise kaafii had tak samaj sakate hai. kelanthan main malishia ke orang asli kahalaane vaale adivasee samudaay bhi milte hai.
 malaysia main alag-alag rajyon ke puraane rajpariwaron ko sanvaidhanik darja mila hua he. kelanthan kaa sanvaidhanik adhyaksh sultan kehalaata he haalaanki is pad ke saath koi vaastavik prashasnic adhikaar nahin aate. malaysia main kuch rajyon ko aupchaarik arabi bhashaa ke naam bhi diye jate hai or kelanthan kaa naam "kelanthan daar-ul-naim" he, yani "kelanthan, anand kaa ghar".
