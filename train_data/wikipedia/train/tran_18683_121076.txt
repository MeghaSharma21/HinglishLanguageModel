 chheni ek haath kaa aujaar he jiskaa upyog furniture banane main tathaa pathar or dhaatu kaa kaary karne vaale log karte he. chheni ek kathor dhaatu kii bani hoti he. 
 haath ke bal se kataai karne ke prasadhano main chheniyaan pramukh hai. sidhi chheniyon ko churaasi or gol, adhgol or J aakaar kii chheniyon ko rukhaani kehete hai. inaki noken or banaavat bhinn bhinn prakaar kii hoti he. badhai or fitron kii chheniyaan bhinn bhinn prakaar kii hoti he.
