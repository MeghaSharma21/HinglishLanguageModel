 
 utkal vishvavidhyalay odisha kaa sabase purana vishvavidhyalay or bharat kaa 17 vaan sabase purana vishvavidhyalay he. rajdhani bhubaneshwar main sthit yah vishvavidhyalay odisha kaa pramukh shaikshik centre he.
 april 1936 main odisha ke bihar se alag hokar ek nayaa praant ban jane ke baad rajya kaa ek apana vishvavidhyalay banane kii aavashyaktaa mahasoos kii gai. 1936 tak orissa ke sabhi college patna vishvavidhyalay yaa andhra vishvavidhyalay ke antargat the. iske baad shri vishwanath das, odisha main ek alag vishvavidhyalay sthapit karne kii sambhaavna kii jaanch karne ke liye apane adhyaksh ke rup main pandit nilkanth das ke saath, 2 march, 1938 ko ek samiti niyukt kii. pandit godwarish mishra, odisha kii sarkaar main shiksha ke tatkaaleen mantri odisha vidhansabha main utkal vishvavidhyalay vidheyak pesh kiya jo 30 june 1943 par odisha vidhansabha dwaara paarit kiya gaya tha. vartmaan parisar kii aadharshila bharat ke pratham raashtrapati dow rajendra prasad dwaara rakhi gai thi or vishvavidhyalay parisar bharat ke duusare raashtrapati dow sarvapalli radhakrishnan ke dwaara 2 janvari 1963 main udghaatan kiya gaya tha.
 vishvavidhyalay parisar, bhubaneshwar shahar ke dil main he. yah master canteen main sthit mukhya railway stesan or varmunda main sthit bas stand donon se lagbhag 5 kimi dur he.
