 ukraine ne pehali baar olympic khelon ko 1994 main ek swatantra raashtra ke rup main bhaag liya, or tab se athleton ne har greeshmakaleen olympic khelon or sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main pratispardhaa karne ke liye bheja he. pile-bluez ke liye swarna padak jeetne vaala pehla athlete oksana bil tha.
 isse pehle, aadhunik ukraine ke athlete zyaadaatar roosi samrajya or 1952 se 1992 tak soviet sangh ke hisse ke rup main bhaag lete the, or soviet sangh ke vighatan ke baad, 1992 main uniphid athlete uniphid team kaa hissaa the. 1992 main swatantra ukraine se tatiyana gutsu uniphid team kaa sarvshereshth athlete ban gaya.
 aitihaasik rup se, ukraine ke kuch athlete the jinhone romania, poland or anya deshon ke rangon kaa bhi bachaav kiya tha. kripaya dhyaan den ki sabhi athlete neshnal ukrainian nahin hai.
 swatantra rup se, ukraine ne greeshmakaleen khelon main kul milakar 119 padak or sheetakaaleen khelon main 7, greeshmakaleen jimnastics or sardiyon main bayathlon ke saath raashtra ke sheersh padak banane vaale khel ke rup main jeet hasil kii he.
 ukraine kii rashtriya olympic samiti 1990 main banaai gai thi or 1993 main antarrashtriya olympic samiti dwaara manyata praapt thi.
