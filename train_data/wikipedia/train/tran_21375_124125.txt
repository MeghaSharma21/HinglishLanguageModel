 ibrahimi dharm un dharmon ko kehete hai jo hazarat ibrahim ke poojit ishwar ko maanate hai. inamen isai dharm, islam dharm or yahudee dharm shaamil hai, or ye apane aap ko bible main varnit nabi ibrahim kaa vanshaj maanate hai. ye teenon dharm madhya purv ke registan main panape the or bilkul akeshwarwadi hai.
