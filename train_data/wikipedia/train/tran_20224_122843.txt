 guzia ek prakaar kaa pakwaan he jo maide or khoe se banaaya jaataa he. ise chhattisgarh main kusli, maharashtra main karanji, bihar main pidki, andhra pradesh main kazzikyaalu, kehete hai. uttar bharat main holi tathaa dakshin bharat main deepawali ke avasar par ghar main guzia banane kii parampara he. guzia mukhya rup se do tarah se banaai jaatin he, ek- maava bhari guzia, duusari ravaa bhari guzia. maava ilaychi bhari guzia ke upar chini kii ek parat chadhaakar work lagaakar isko ek nayaa rup bhi dete hai. maava ke saath kabhi kabhi haraa chanaa, mewa yaa duusare khaadya padaarth milakar, jaise anjir yaa khajur kii guzia bhi banaai jaati hai.
