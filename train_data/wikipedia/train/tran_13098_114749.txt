 vish aise padaarthon ke naam hai, jo khaae jane par shleshmal jhilli, utak yaa twachaa par sidhi kriya karke athwa parisancharan tantra main avashoshit hokar, ghaatak rup se swasthya ko prabhavit karne, yaa jivan nasht karne, main samarth hote hai.
 vishaaktata ke lakshan nimnlikhit hai :
  jathrantra uttejan - saadharantaya vaman, pet kii pidaa or atisar vishaaktata ke pramukh lakshan hai. yadi kuch hi gharon ke bheetar anek vyakti vishaaktata ke shikaar hue hon, to kisi khaas vastu ko kshobhak kaa vaahak samajha ja saktaa he. 
  pralaap - yah rasinic vish yaa upapachayee gadabadi or fever ke parinamswaroop utpann rudhirvishaktata ke kaaran hota he. thodi khuraaq main hi pralaap utpann karanevaale rasinic vishon main varviturat, bromide kaa chirkaalik mashaa, alcodaul, hiocynin aadi he. inamen se pratham teen adhik prachlit hai or pralaap praaia: atyalp nashe kaa suchak hota he. 
  sammurchha - pramastishkeey kshati adhik hone par pralaap sammurchha main parivartit hota he. saamanytah barviturat or alcodaul aise parinaam utpann karte hai. 
  aithan - ye do prakaar kii hoti hai : 
  parinah chetakope - sisa, arsenic sona, paara aadi se chirkaalik vishaaktata hone par parinah peshi kii durbalata hoti he, jisamen sharir chhijata he or jathrantra vikshobh bhi hota he.

 vishaaktata ke apati upachaar ke liye, jisamen jeevvish kha liya gaya ho, nimnlikhit kriyavidhi apnaani chahie :
  yathashighra ulti, vastikriya, virechan yaa mutrta dwaara vish ko nikaalnaa.
  vishisht yaa saamaanya pratikaarak dekar vish kaa niskriy karnaa or tab vastikriya kaa upachaar.
  sankshobh, paat or anya vishisht abhivyaktiyon ke hote hi unase sangharsh karnaa.
  shleshmal jhilliyon ko shamakon ke prayog dwaara bachaana.
 teevr amla, kshar yaa anya sanksharak padaarth dwaara vishaaktata hone par amashay nalikaaon, yaa vamankaariyon, kaa upyog nahin karnaa chahie. inase jathreey vedhan ho saktaa he. jathar main sthit antarvastu kii khaali karne kaa sabase saral upaay vaman karana he. vaman kaa prayog tabhi karnaa chahie jab rogi chikitsak ko sahyog dene kii sthiti main ho, uske sharir main atirikt vish ho or amashay nalikaaon kaa abhaav ho, yaa rogi amashay nalikaaon kaa upyog kar sakane kii sthiti main na ho. nidralu yaa achetan rogi ko vaman nahin karana chahie, kyonki uske amashay kii antarvastu ke tarlapanayan kaa bhay rahata he. sanksharak vishon ke upshamakon ke antargrahan kii sthiti main bhi vaman varjit he.
 vaman karaane ke liye gale main angulii yaa anya vastu kaa prayog karnaa chahie, yaa nimnlikhit vastuon main se koi cheez khilaani chahie : aiyomorphin hydrochloride, churnit sarson, or namak yaa prabal sabun jal .
 in kriyaaon ke uddeshy nimnlikhit hai : 
  atirikt asanksharak vishon kaa nishkasana, jinhen baad main jathrantra kshetra se avashoshit kiya ja saktaa he; 
  vaman centre ke nirbal hone par jab vaman nahin hota, kendriya tantrikatantr ko apasadit karanevaale vish kaa nishkasana; 
  vishon kii pahchaan ke liye jathreey antarvastuon ke sanchay or pareekshan ke liye tathaa 
  vishpratikaarakon ke suvidhajanak prayog ke liye.
 nimnlikhit sthitiyon main jathreey tarlapanayan or vasti kriya nahin kii jaati hai : 
  vish ke dwaara utakon kaa vyapak sanksharan, 
  teevr ni:sanjna, jadimagrast, yaa nishchetantagrast, rogi, kyonki use tarlapanayan phuffusarthi kaa khatra rahata he.
