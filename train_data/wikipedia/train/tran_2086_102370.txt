 dhanwaan 1993 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. yah kasinthuni vishwanath dwaara nirdeshit bhartiya romance film he. isamen pramukh bhoomikaaon main ajay devgan, karishma kapur or manisha koirala shaamil hai.
 kashinath or imli bachapan se premi hai or har koi unase shaadi karne kii ummid karata he. phir amir or rogiha anjali chopra kashinath ke jivan main aati he or phir sabakuch badal jaataa he. anjali kashinath kii dekhbhaal ke kaaran apni beemaari se thik ho jaati he. anjali kashinath se pyaar karne lagati he or sahaspurvak apane pita manmohan chopra ko bataati he ki vah keval kashinath se shaadi karegi. lekin uske pita chaahate hai ki vah ajit se shaadi karen.
