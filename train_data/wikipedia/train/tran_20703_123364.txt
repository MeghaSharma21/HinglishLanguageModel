 steven alan spilberg ek america film nirmaataa, nirdeshak or patakatha lekhak hai. forbs patrika ke anusaar, spilberg kii kul kamai 3.1 billian dollars he. varsh 2006 main premier patrika ne unhen film jagat kaa sabase prabhaavshaalee vyakti nirupit kiya tha. time patrika ne unhen shatabdi ke sau prabhaavshaalee logon main shaamil kiya he.beesvin shatabdi ke ant main life ne unhen unki peedi kaa sabase prabhaavshaalee vyakti qaraar diya tha. chaar dashakon ke kriyasheel jivan main spilberg kii filmon ne alag-alag vichaardhaara vaali alag-alag pidiyon ko alag tarah se chuaa he.spilberg ko 1993 main film sindlers list ke liye or 1998 main seaving privet reyan ke liye best diarector ke academy award se sammanit kiya gaya. spilberg kii teen filmon, jaas, i.tii. da extra terristerial or jurassic park ne apane-apane samay ke hisaab se baks aphis par aamadani kaa ek nayaa kirtiman sthapit kiya.
 unhen 8 oktober 2009 ko unki filmon main maanav prayaason ke kaaran liberty medal puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya.
