 chulvansh, pali bhashaa main rachit srilanka ke raajaaon kaa itihaas he. isamen 4thi shatabdi se lekar 1815 tak ke kaal kaa itihaas he. isaki rachana anekon varshon main anekon bhikshuon ke madhyam se sampann hui, atah isamen anek tarh kii kavy-sheliyaan milteen hai. saamaanya manyata yah he ki yah mahavans kaa uttar granth he.
