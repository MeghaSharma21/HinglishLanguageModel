 saan pedro de nora girjaghar ek roman catholic girjaghar he. ye purv romaniskiu style main bani hui he. ye spain main raas regulas khudamukhtiar samuday main sthit he. ye spain main nora nadi ke paas sthit he jo oviedo shahar se 12 kilometre dur sthit he. is girjaghar kaa pataa pahili baar alphonso tisare ke dastavejon se laga. ye girjaghar saan julia de los prados girjaghar se samarupata rakhata he. mana jaataa he ki ise alphonso dusre ke samay main banaaya gaya. ise 1931i. main ise rashtriya smarak ghoshit kiya gaya. is girjaghar ko 1936i. spain ke grah yudh main ye sad gaya tha. iskaa nirmaan louis mendez pedal ne karavaaya tha.
 is girjaghar ke nirmaan kii kala saan julia de los prados girjaghar ke saath milti he. iske mukhya teen bhaag hai. iske chat ko aap main milaayaa gaya he. is samay kii or girjagharon ke jaise isaki chat par bhi kamren bane hue hai. is main ghanti tower bhi bana hua he. ye pahile ke girjaghar main maujuud nahin tha. ise baad main louis mendez pidal ne romaniskiu style main banwaaya.
