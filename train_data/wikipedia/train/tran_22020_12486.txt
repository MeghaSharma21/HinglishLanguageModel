 nivesh/nirgam yaa ai/o kisi sanganak or prayogkarta yaa kisi anya soochna pranali ke bich main soochna ke sanchaar ko kehete hai. nivesh vah sanket hote hai jo bahari duniya se sanganak ko praapt hote hai or nirgam vah sanket hote hai jo sanganak se bahari duniya ko jate hai. aamtaur se in sanketon kaa prasar paridhiy yantron ke dwaara kara jaataa he.
