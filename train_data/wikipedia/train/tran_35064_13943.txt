 barkehoujen kasuti ek shart yaa sambandh he jo bataati he ki koi electranik paripath kis sthiti main dolan kar sakegaa . ise germany ke bhautikashastri ech ji barkehoujen ne san 1921 main pratipaadit kiya tha. electranik asilataron ke dizin main iskaa bahutayat se prayog hota he. iske saath hi rinatmak punarniveshyukt paripathon kii dizin main bhi iskaa khub istemaal hota he. 
 barkehoujen kii kasuti un paripathon par lagu hoti he jinamein feedback loop upasthit ho. iske anusaar,
 dhyatby he ki barkehoujen kasuti dolan ke liye keval aavashyak shart he kintu yah paryaapt shart nahin he. arthath kuch aise paripath bhi ho sakate hai jo is kasuti par khare utarate hai kintu dolan nahin karte. iske vipareet niquist kii kasuti kisi loop ke sthayitv/asthayitv ke liye aavashyak evam paryaapt shart kii vyakhya karti he.
