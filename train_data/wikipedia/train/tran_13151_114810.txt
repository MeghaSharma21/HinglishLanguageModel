 kamedi central ek america saamaanya cabel or satellite tivi channel he, jiskaa sanchaalan vycom global antertanment group kar rahi he, jo vycom media networks kii ikai or vycom kaa bhaag he. ye 14 yaa usase adhik umr vaale darshakon ko dhyaan main rakh kar apane hansane vaale kaarykram chalaati he. isamen iske khud ke kaarykram ke alaava anya channel par de chuke kaarykram bhi hote hai. iske alaava isamen filmon ko bhi dikhaaya jaataa he. 
 15 navambar 1989 ko echbio channel ke maalik, time warner ne is channel ko shuru kiya. ye us samay kaa pehla aisa cabel channel tha, jo keval hasya par aadhaarit kaarykram dikhaata tha. 1 april 1990 ko amtivi, viech1 or nicelodian jaise channel ke maalik, vycom ne iske muqaabale ha! channel nikaala, jo pehle se de chuke kuch hasya kaarykram or kuch nae hasya kaarykram ko dikhaata tha.
