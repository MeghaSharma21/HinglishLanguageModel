 amdo tibet ke teen parmparik praanton main se ek he, jinhe tibeti bhashaa main tibet ke teen 'cholka' kaha jaataa he. anya do praant kham or eu-tsang hai. yah kshetra maachu nadi se lekar drichu nadi tak vistrut he. 14wein dalai lamas kaa janm amdo kshetra main hi hua tha. yah ilaaka kai mahatvapurn lamaaon kii mathrubhumi rahaa he or isane tibet ke saanskrithik va dharmik vikaas main gahari bhoomika adaa kii he. tibet par janavaadee ganatantr china kaa qabza hone ke baad uski sarkaar ne tibet kaa prashasnic vibhaajan kar diya or amdo kaa bhushetra ab chingei or kuch had tak gansu praanton main sammilit he.
