 mark alan raflo ek america abhinayta, film nirmaataa or saamaajik kaaryakarta he. unhone "sibies samar plahouse" ke ek episod se apane kariyar kii shuruyaat kii, jiske baad unhone kuch filmon main maamuli bhoomikaayen nibhaai. vah "dis is yor youth" ke mul kalakaaron kaa hissaa tha, jiske liye unhen indapendent spirit puraskaar ke liye namankit kiya gaya tha. iske baad unhone 13 going on 30, itrenal sunshine of da spotles mind, jodiek, vat dasant kil you main bhoomikaayen nibhaai. 2010 main unhone kamedi-drama "da kids aar ol rhite" kiya, jiske liye unhen esaiji puraskaar, bft puraskaar, or sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinayta ke academy puraskaar ke liye namankit kiya gaya. vah epabiai special easant dialan rods ke rup main mistry film "nau you miss me" or "nau you miss me 2" main bhi dikhaai die.
 maarval cinematic univers ke charitra halk kaa abhinay karne ke liye raflo ko antarrashtriya khyaati praapt hui. 2008 main release hui da incredibal halk main yah bhoomika unke mitra edward norton ne nibhaai thi. raflo ne da avangers ke saath halk ke rup main shuruyaat kii, or baad main aayran main 3, avangers: age of ultron, thor: regnarok main is bhoomika kaa nirvahan kiya. unhone 2014 ke televijan naatak film "da normal heart" abhinay kiya, jiske vah sah-kaaryakari nirmaataa bhi the. is film ke liye unhen utkrisht televijan movie ke primetime emi puraskaar ke liye namankit kiya gaya, or unhone tivi movie main sarvshereshth abhinayta ke screen actors gild award ko jeeta. usee varsh, unhone foxkechar main david shulj kii bhoomika nibhaai, jiske liye unhen golden globe samet kai puraskaron ke liye namankit kiya gaya, or sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinayta kaa academy puraskaar diya gaya. 2015 main unhen infinitele polar bear ke liye sarvshereshth abhinayta ke golden globe puraskaar ke liye namit kiya gaya, or naatak spotlite main unki bhoomika ke liye bafta or academy puraskaar namakan bhi praapt hue.
