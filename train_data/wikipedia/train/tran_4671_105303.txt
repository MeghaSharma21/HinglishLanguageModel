 taichung, aadhikarik taur par taichung shahar ke naam se janaa jaataa he, yah madhya-pashchimi taiwan main sthit ek vishesh nagarpalika he. taichung kii jansankhya lagbhag 2.78 million he or yah aadhikarik taur par july 2017 se taiwan kaa doosraa sabase adhik aabaadi vaale shahar ke rup main suchibaddh he. vartmaan shahar kaa gathan tab hua jab taichung jile ko mul prantiy taichung shahar ke saath 25 disambar 2010 ko vilay kar vishesh nagar palika banaaya gaya. madhya taiwan main taichung basin main sthit yah shahar, japani shashan ke dauraan ek pramukh aarthik or saanskrithik centre ban gaya tha. yah shahar prakritik vigyaan ke rashtriya sangrahalay or lalit kala ke rashtriya taiwan sangrahalay kaa ghar he, saath hi yahan aitihaasik taichung park, lin fridaly garden or kai mandiron sahit kai saanskrithik sthalon maujud he.
 taichung shahar, mukhya pashchimi tateey maidan ke saath taichung basin main sthit he. jo pashchimi tat par uttari taiwan se lagbhag dakshini sire tak failaa hua he. shahar kii simaaen changa jile, nantau jile, hualien jile, yilan jile, sinchu jile or miaoli jile se milti hai. taichung kii jalwaayu ek ushnkaribandheey monsoon jalwaayu or ek garm nam up-ushnkaribandheey jalwaayu ke bich kii he, ausatan vaarshik taapmaana 23.3 degree celsius ke saath july or agast main varsh ke ucch taapmaana rahete hai, jabaki janvari or farvari main sabase kam taapmaana hota he. ausatan vaarshik varsha keval 1,700 millimetre se upar he, or ausatan ardrata 80% he. purv main madhya parvat shrankhla or uttar main mioli parvatamala dwaara pradutt suraksha ke kaaran, taichung samudri tufaan se kam prabhavit he. haalanki, dakshin china sagar se samay-samay par ubharate samudri tufaan se shahar ko khatra bana rahata he, kyonki 1986 main aaye ek tufaan, taiwan ke pashchimi tat par taichung tak pahuch gaya tha.
