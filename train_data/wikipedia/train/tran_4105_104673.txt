 prasar bharti bharat kii ek saarvajanik prasaaran sanstha he. isamen mukhya rup se doordarshan evam akashwani shaamil hai.
 prasar bharti kaa gathan 23 november, 1997 prasaaran sambandhi muddon par sarkari prasaaran sansthaaon ko swaittata dene ke mudde par sansad main kaaphi bahes ke baad kiya gaya tha. sansad ne is sambandh main 1990 main ek adhinium paarit kiya lekin ise antat 15 sitambar 1997 main lagu kiya gaya. 
 prasar bharti ke vartmaan adhyaksh mrinal pande evam siio jawahar sarkaar hai.
