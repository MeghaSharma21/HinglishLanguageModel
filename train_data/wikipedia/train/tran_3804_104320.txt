 chauri chaura uttar pradesh main gorakhpur ke paas kaa ek kasbaa he jahaan 4 farvari 1922 ko bharatiyon ne bitrish sarkaar kii ek police chaowkee ko aag laga di thi jisase usamen chupe hue 22 police karmchaaree jinda jal ke mar gaye the. is ghatana ko chaurichaura kand ke naam se janaa jaataa he. iske parinamswaroop gandhiji ne kaha tha ki hinsa hone ke kaaran asahyog aandolan upyukt nahin rah gaya he or use waapas le liya tha.chauri chaura kii is ghatana se mahatma gandhi dwaara chalaaye gayi asahyog aandolan ko aghat pahunchaa, jiske kaaran unhen asahyog aandolan ko sthaagith karnaa padaa, jo bardoli, gujarat se shuru kiya jane vaala tha.
