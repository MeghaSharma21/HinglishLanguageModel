 haat, hatiya yaa pethiya aamtaur par bharat ke gaanvon main laganevaale sthaneey bazaar ko kaha jaataa he. sabji, fal, taaja maance, khadyann yaa parchun saamagree aadi haat main kharid-bikri kii janevaali pramukh mad hai. gaanv ke logon kii dainik zaruraton ko puraa karne main haat kii bhoomika atimahatvapurn he. sadiyon se bharat ke gaanvon kii aatmanirbharta sunishchit karne main haat kii bhoomika ko nakaara nahin ja saktaa. krishi-vipanan ke alaave haat sthal mahatvapurn milan bindu evam saanskrithik centre bhi he.
 saamanyatya gaanvon main laganevaale haat 2-3 ghanton kaa hota he lekin us kshetra main bazaar kaa vistaar evam duuri bhi lagane vaale haat kii avadhi ko tay karti he. jansankhya ghanatv ke aadhaar par haat saptaah main do yaa teen din ke liye lagata he. kai kasbon yaa chote shaharon main yah kuch ghanton ke liye dainik bhi lagaaya jaataa he. mahanagaron main sthaayee taur par bani sabji mandi haat kaa viksit rup he.
