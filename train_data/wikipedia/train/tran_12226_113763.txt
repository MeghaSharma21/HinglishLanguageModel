 gulm kisi vriksh yaa anya vanaspati ke kate hue tane yaa tahani ko kehete hai jisamen se vah vanaspati phir se ug sakane main sakasham ho. yah kshamta kuch hi jatiyon main hoti he, masalan kewda. jin vrikshon main yah kshamta hoti he aksar unke wanon ko nichle tane tak katkar unki lakdi prayog kii jaati he. kuch varshon baad ped phir se bad jaataa he or use punah kaata ja saktaa he. aise vrikshon ke jhund jinamein katkar keval gulm hi chhod diye gayi hon unhe gulm dal kaha jaataa he. kisi vrikshon ke jhund ko katkar unke gulm chhod dene ko gulmkaari kehete hai or yah maananaa he ki maanav ise hazaron varshon se karte aaye hai. bharat main saal kaa vriksh parmparik rup se gulm kara jaataa he.
