 krishna-caspian stapi yaa pontic-caspian stapi krishna sagar ke uttar se caspian sagar ke purv ke kshetron tak vistrut vishaal stapi maidani kshetra ko kehete hai. aadhunik yug main yah pashchimi ukraine se rus ke dakshini sangheey kshetra or phir rus hi ke volga sangheey kshetra se hota hua pashchimi kazakhstan tak failaa hua ilaaka he. pracheen kaal main yah skithi logon or saramati logon kaa kshetra hua karata tha. is kshetra main sadiyon se bahut se ghudsavaar kanaabadosh qabeele rahete chale aae hai jinhonen samay-samay par europe, pashchimi asia or bhartiya upmahadweep par akraman kar ke qabza jamaakar jatiyon or deshon ke itihaas badal die hai. bahut itihaaskaar maanate hai ki yah vishwa kaa pehla ilaaka tha jahaan ghodon ko paaltu banaaya gaya. 16veen se 18veen shatabdiyon main akhirakar is kshetra par roosi samrajya ne apana niyantran jamaa liya or pehali baar yahan ke nivasee ek gair-kanaabadosh vyavastha ke adheen ho gaye.
 'pontic' pracheen unani bhashaa ke 'pontos' shabd se aayaa he jiskaa arth 'samudr' he. prachinkaal main unani log krishna sagar ko hi is naam se pukaara karte the, isliye 'pontic-caspian' kaa arth 'krishna sagar se caspian sagar tak' he.
