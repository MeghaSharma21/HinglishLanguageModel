 tigrey, jise sudan me khaasaa bulaayaa jaataa he, purvottaree africa main boli jane vaali ek sami bhashaa he. yah iritriya or uske pados main sthit sudan ke kuch kshetron main basane vaale tigrey samudaay ke sadasyon dwaara boli jaati he. dhyaan den ki yah ithiopia or iritriya main boli jane vaali tigrinya bhashaa se sambandhit lekin bhinn he. tigrey or tigrinya pracheen geeiz bhashaa kii do aadhunik vanshaj samajhi jaati hai.
