 manakon ko aapas main jodakar banaaye gayi shilp ko manakaa shilp yaa 'manakaa karm' kehete hai. yah kaary manakon ko sui-dhaage dwaara aapas main jodakar kiya jaataa he. manake anekanek padaarthon kii banaayi jaatin hai or anekon aakaar-prakaar kii hotin hai. manakon kaa prayog aabhushan athwa soundarya prasadhano ke nirmaan main bhi kiya jaataa he.
