 
 faraah pashchimi afghanistan ke faraah praant kii rajdhani he. yah shahar faraah nadi ke kinare sthit he or iran kii seemaa ke kaafii paas he.
 faraah ke adhiktar log pashto bolne vaale pashtun hai or ve sthaneey aabaadi ke 90% hai. inke alaava shahar ke 7% log tajik or 3% log baloch hai.
 iran kii sarhad ke paas hone ke kaaran faraah par us desh kaa prabhaav kaafii rahaa he or yah us desh ke ashkani samrajya kii 'aaria' naamak satrapi kaa ek pramukh shahar hua karata tha. jab sikandar mahaan kii unani senaaon ne iran par qabza kar liya to yahan bhi uskaa niyantran ho gaya. aage chalakar 5veen sadi isvi main yah iran ke saasaani samrajya kii bharat se lagane vaali purvi seemaa kaa ek mahatvapurn garh tha. is kshetra ko kuraasaan kaa hissaa mana jane laga. ek-ke-baad-ek tahiri, saffaari, samaani, gazanvi, gauri, khwarezmi, ilkaani, temuri, bukhaaraa kii khaanat or saffaavi samrajyon ne yahan apana niyantran jamaaya. 18veen sadi ke shuruuaati daur main yah pehle afkhaan​ hotki rajvansh or phir durrani samrajya ke samrajya kaa hissaa ban gaya.
 yahan mohammad jonpuri naamak islamic prachaarak dafan hai. yah san 1443 main bharat main jonpur main paidaa hue the or inhonen swayan ko mahadi ghoshit kar diya tha. yah jonpur se bharat, arab or kuraasaan bhar main prachaar karte hue ghumen or 63 kii umr par inkaa faraah main dehanth ho gaya. inke manane waalon ko 'mahadavi' bulaayaa jaataa he.
 faraah main garmiyon main taapmaana 45 centegrade tak or sardiyon main −5 centegrade tak ja saktaa he, yani garmi bhi bahut padati he or sardi bhi. ilaaka kaafii shushk he or barish kam padati he.
