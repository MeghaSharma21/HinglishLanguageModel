 bhashavigyaan main, bhashaa-dwait yaa samshti dwibhashita yaa dwibhasharoopita us dasha kaa naam he jisamen ek hi bhashaa-samudaay do alag tarah kii bhashaaon kaa vyavhaar karata he. saamaanya taur par pratidin boli jane vaali 'nimna' bhashaa ke alaava ek duusari 'ati parimarjeet' kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. 'ucch bhashaa' kaa prayog praayah sahitya, aupchaarik shiksha, aadi main hota he kintu saamaanya baatcheet main nahin.
