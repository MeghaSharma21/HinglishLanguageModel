  yah standhari-sambandhit lekh apni praarambhik avasthaa main he, yani ki ek aadhaar he. aap ise badhaakar vikipedia kii sahaayta kar sakate he. 
 
  bhartiya sehi ek krintak jaanavar he. iskaa failaav turki, bumadhya sagar se lekar dakshin-pashchim tathaa madhya asia evam dakshin asia or china tak main he. himalay main yah 2,400 me. tak kii oonchaai main paaya jaataa he.
 isaki lambaai 63-91 se.me., punch kii lambaai 15-30 se.me. or vazan 5-16 ki. hota he.iske sharir ke bal mote, majboot or nukile hote hai jo ise parabhakshiyon se bachane main madad karte hai. in baalo ko sehi ke kaante bhi kehete hai. sehi ke sharir hilaane par yah kaante jhadate hai, lekin yah dharna galat he ki sehi in kaanton ko apane dushman par phenk saktaa he.
 yah ek shaakaahaari praani he jo pattee, ghaas or chote paudhe khaataa he.
 maadaa or nar main yon paripakvata kramshah 25 tathaa 29 maheene main aati he. garbh kaal 210 din kaa hota he tathaa ek baar main maadaa 1-4 bacchon ko janm detii he.
