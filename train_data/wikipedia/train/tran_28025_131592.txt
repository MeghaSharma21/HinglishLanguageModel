 matia ek gaanv he, jo ki bharat ke bihar rajya ke vaishali jile main sthit he. yah bihar ke chaar jile se ghira hua he, iske purv main samastipur, pashchim main saaran, uttar main mujffarpur or dakshin main patna he. iske nikatatam shahar hajipur yahaan se lagbhag 23 kimi or 31 kimi dur he. yahaan se nikatatam railway stesan pashchim main desri or purv main sahadei bujurg dur he.
 janaganana 2011 kii soochna ke anusaar matia gaanv kaa sthaan code yaa gaanv code 236029 he. yah bihar, bharat main vaishali jile ke sahadei bujurg tehsil main sthit he. 2009 ke aankadon ke mutabik, chakajmaal matia ke gram panchayat he. gaanv kaa kul bhugolic kshetra 225 hectare he.
 kaali mandir is gaanv main ek sundar mandir he. yah ek bahut hi sundar mandir he, is mandir kaa adbhut najaara puure gaanv ke liye ek adwitiya pahchaan detaa he. bhagwaan or devi ke aashirwad se hameshaa sabhi gramin khush rahete hai. mandir vishaal pedo se ghire hue hai jo mithasbhari or aashirvadamay chaaya pradaan karte hai.
 rajkeey utkramit madhya vidhyalay, ek sarkari madhyamik vidhyalay he, jo vidhyaarthiyo ko kaksha 1 se 8 tak kii shiksha pradaan karata he. yah kaali mandir ke saamne sthit he.
 rajkeey ucch vidhyalay, matia ek aagaamee parisar he jo vidhyaarthiyo ko 9veen or 10veen kaksha ke adhyayan ko pradaan karegi. yah bhi kaali mandir ke saamne banaaya ja rahaa he.
