 aantarik crode yaa ant:crode, prithvi kaa antartam yaa sabase bhitari bhaag he jiskaa pataa bhucamp vigyaan ke adhyayanon dwaara lagaaya gaya he. aantarik crode ek thos vishaal gole ke aakaar kaa he jisaki trigya lagbhag 1220 kimi he or yah aakaar main chandramaa ke aakaar kaa lagbhag 70% he. mana jaataa he ki yah loha-nikal kii mishra dhaatu se nirmit he or iskaa taapmaana surya kii sataha ke taapmaana ke samaan hi lagbhag, 5700 K he.
