 mirzapur or vindhyaachal ke madhya bahane vaali is nadi ko punyajal athwa ojhal ke naam se janaa jaataa he. kaha jaataa he ki jis prakaar sabhi yagyon main ashwamegh yajna or sabhi parvaton main himalay parvat prasiddh he usee prakaar sabhi tirtho main ojhal sabase pramukh maani jaati he. is nadi kaa jal ganga nadi ke jal ke samaan hi pavitra mana jaataa he. yah jagah devi kaali kaa mandir, mahalakshmi, mahasaraswati or tarakeshwar mahadev ke mandir se ghiri hui he.
