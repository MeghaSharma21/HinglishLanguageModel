 shrunkhlaa kii kadiyaan mahadevi verma ke samasya mulak nibandhon kaa sangrah he. stri-vimarsh inamen pramukh hai. dow hriday narayan upaadhyaay ke shabdon main, "aaj stri-vimarsh kii charchaa har ore sunai pad rahi he. mahadevi ne iske liye prishthbhumi bahut pehle taiyaar kar di thi. san 1942 main prakashit unki kriti shrunkhlaa kii kadiyaan sahi arthon main stri-vimarsh kii prastaavna he, jisamen tatkaaleen saamaajik paristhitiyon main nari kii dasha, disha evam sangharshon par mahadevi ne apni lekhnee chalaayi he." isamen aise niabandh sankalik kiye gayi hai jinamein bhartiya nari kii visham paristhiti ko anek drushti-binduon se dekhne kaa prayaas kiya gaya he. yuddh or nari naamak lekh main unhone yuddh stri or purush ke manovigyaan par gambhir, vaishvik or moulik chintan vyakt kiya he. isi prakaar naritv or abhishaap main ve puranic prasangon kaa vivaran dete hue aadhunik nari ke shaktiheen hone ke kaarnon kii vivechna karti hai.
 
  pustak se kuch panktiyaan- ‘‘bhartiya nari jis din apane sampoorn praan-aaveg se jaag sake, us din uski gati rokna kisi ke liye sambhav nahin. uske adhikaron ke sambandh main yah satya he ki ve bhikshavriti se na mili hai, na milenge, kyonki unki sthiti aadan-pradaan yogya vastuon se bhinn he. samaaj main vyakti kaa sahyog or vikaas kii disha main uskaa upyog hi uske adhikaar nishchit karata rahata he. kintu adhikaar ke icchhuk vyakti ko adhikari bhi hona chahie. saamanytah bhartiya nari main isi visheshta kaa abhaav milegaa. kahin usamen saadhaaran dayaniyata or kahin asaadhaaran vidroh he, parantu santulan se uskaa jivan parichit nahin...." 
 
 sohani nira kukereja dwaara iskaa angrezi anuwaad links in da chen naam se kiya gaya he.
