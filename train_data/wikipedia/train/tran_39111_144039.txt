 audyogic gas aisi gas ko kaha jaataa he jiskaa prayog kisi audyogic prakriya main ho. aksar yah gaisen inhen istemaalon ke liye vishesh rup se banaai yaa shuddh kii jaati hai. nitrogen, oxigen, carbon dioxide, argon, hydrogen, helium or asitalin sabase zyaada prayog kii jane vaali audyogic gaisen hai.
