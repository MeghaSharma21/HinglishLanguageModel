 saraswati mahal pustkaalay thanjore main sthit he. yahaan pandulipiyon kaa mahatvapurn sangrah he. isaki sthapana 1700 isvi ke aas-paas kii gai thi. is sangrahalay main bhartiya or european bhashaaon main likhi hui 44000 se zyada tamrapatr or kaagaj kii pandulipiyaan dekhne ko milti hai. inamen se 80 pratishat se adhik pandulipiyaan sanskrit main likhi hui hai. kuch pandulipiyaan to bahut hi durlabh hai. inamen tamil main likhi aushadhi vigyaan kii pandulipiyaan bhi shaamil hai.
