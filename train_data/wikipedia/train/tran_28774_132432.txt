 australian cricket team ne 12 farvari se 26 march 2013 tak bharat kaa daura kiya, bharat ke khilaaf chaar maichon kii test series kheli. border-gavaskar trophy jeetne ke liye bharat ne 4-0 se jeet main chaar match kii test siries jiti thi. pehle test ke dauraan, mahendra singh dhoni ne ek bhartiya test captain dwaara sarvocch score banaaya, jisamen 224 ran banaae or sachin tendulkar ke pichley record ko tod diya.
