 nirdeshaank: 26°10′N 83°30′E / 26.16°N 83.50°E / 26.16; 83.50champaran bihar praant kaa ek jila tha. ab purvi champaran or pashchimi champaran naam ke do jile hai. bharat or nepal kii seemaa se laga yah kshetra swadhinata sangram ke dauraan kaaphi sakriy rahaa he. mahatma gandhi ne apni mashaal yaheen se angrejon ke khilaaf nil andolan se jalaayee thi. betiapashchimi champaran kaa jila mukhyalay he or motihaari purvi champaran kaa. champaran se 35 kilometre dur dakshin sahibganj-chakiya maarg par laal chhapra chowk ke paas avasthit he pracheen aitihaasik sthal kesaria. yahan ek vrihad baudhdhakalin stupa he jise kesaria stupa ke naam se janaa jaataa he.
