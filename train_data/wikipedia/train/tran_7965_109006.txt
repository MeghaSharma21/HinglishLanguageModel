 kita parvat japan ke yamanashi praant, "dakshini alps" main, akishi parvat kii ek parvat chotii hai. yah fuji parvat ke baad japan kaa doosraa sabase ooncha parvat hai, or ise "dakshini alps kaa raja" ke rup main janaa jaataa hai. yah japan ke 100 prasiddh parvaton main shaamil hai. yah minami alps neshnal park main minami-alps shahar ke paas sthit hai.
 kita parvat, ano parvat or notori parvat, akishi parvatamala ke mukhya chotiyaan hai. ye teen pahaad ek saath milakar "shirin-sanajan" kehalaate he, jiskaa arth "teen shwet chotiyaan" hota hai. kita parvat naam kaa shabdic arth "uttari shikhar" hai, kyoki teen pahaado main yah uttari bhaag main sthit hai.
 pahaad ke purvi hisse main kita-deck batates ek 600 meter lanbaa chattan hai. nakashire parvat or kusasuberi se hote hue mirmitata or isken saath-saath shirineo talab kii uttari disha main alpine pedo ke qatari dikhaai dete hai. chotii ke nikat sthit maidan main paudhon ke bade samooh dekha ja saktaa hai. is parvat main pedo kii kalainhenmam hondogens prajati pai jaati he.
 kita parvat ke aasapaas kaa parvat kshetra japan main sabase lokapriy hai, iskaa lokapriyata main yaari parvat or hotaka parvat kshetra ke baad doosraa sthaan hai. chadhaai ke liye purv ke ore se hiraagaaraa  or pashchim main rayomata bindu he; haalanki, adhikansh log hirogava se chadhana pasand karte hai, kyonki yahan suvidhaayen behatar hai. sheersh ke nikat do parvat jhonpadi hai jo 150 logon ke liye awaas pradaan karti hai, or aasapaas camp ke liye maidan kii suvidha bhi uplabdh hai. 
 kita parvat ke shikhar tak ke liye teen mukhya maarg hai. pehla hirogavara se shuru hota hai, or okanbajawa nadi ke madhyam se hote hue, upar daikekeki ghati ki ore jaataa hai.dusraa maarg nadi ghati ko pehle hi chhod shirieoic-kusabuberry jangal ki ore nikal jaataa hai. tisare rasta royomata se shuru nakashiranezava se hote hue pahaad kii chotii tak jaataa hai.
