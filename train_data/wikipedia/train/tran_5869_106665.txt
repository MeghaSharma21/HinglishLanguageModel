 1900 main argentina ne pehali baar olympic khelon main bhaag liya. 1 9 04, 1 9 12 ko chhodkar iske baad ke sabhi greeshmakaleen olympic main bhaag liya he, or 1 9 80 main moscow bahishkaar ke liye apane shashan ke samarthan ke kaaran, 1980 main. yah 1928, 1948, 1952 main sheetakaaleen olympic main bhaag liya or 1960 ke baad se lagaataar.
 argentina 12 deshon main se ek tha - keval ibero-america se - jisane 1894 main antarrashtriya olympic samiti kii sthapana kii thi, jiskaa pratinidhitiv 1907 tak pehali kaaryakari parishad jose binajameen jubiaur ne kiya tha, jinhone us bhoomika main kaary kiya tha. argentina ke liye rashtriya olympic samiti kaa nirmaan or 1923 main manyata praapt thi. 1924-1952 kii avadhi ke dauraan desh main safal pradarshan hue, prayek sanskaran main kam se kam ek swarna padak kaa daawa kiya.
 1956 ke greeshmakaleen olympic ke saath, argentina ko kramshah samgr giravat kaa saamana karnaa padaa, ek aisi sthiti jo 1976 or 1984 greeshmakaleen olympic main sabase mahatvapurn bindu tak pahunch gai. un khelon main argentina ne koi padak nahin jeeta tha. athens main 2004 ke greeshmakaleen olympic main, desh ne 52 varshon main pehali baar swarna padak kaa daawa kiya.
 argentina ke athlete ne greeshmakaleen olympic khelon main kul 74 padak jite hai. inamen se 24 padak mukkebaaji main aaye hai, argentina ne is khel main kisi bhi anya se zyada padak jite hai. raashtra ne sheetakaaleen olympic khelon main abhi tak koi padak nahin jeeta he. 
 bunus yrs 2018 greeshmakaleen yuva olympic kii mejabani karegaa.
