 nepal ke up raashtrapati nepal ke up rajya pramukh kaa gathan kiya gaya or may 2008 main nepali rajtantr ko samaapt kar diya gaya tha. 
 nepal ke vartmaan up raashtrapati nand bahadur pun hai. is pad par inkaa yah doosraa kaarykaal he. isse purv ve oktober, 2015 main nepal ke uparashtrapati bane the. unhone nepal main pipuls libration army ke mukhya commander ke rup main seva kii he. ve nepal ke communist party ke kendriya samiti ke sadsy bhi rah chuke hai.
