 mathamnay athwa mahanushasan aadi shankaracharya dwaara virachit granth he jisamen apane dwaara sthapit chaar mathon kii vyavastha se sambandhit vidhaan or siddhanth diye gayi hai. isamen 73 shloke hai.
 'amnaay' kaa arth he -
 is granth ke mahamy mahanushasan main is bare main spasht kiya he ki koun shankaracharya pad kaa adhikari he or ayogy vyakti ke liye kya vyavhaar hona chahie.
 mahanushasan main sanyasi ko sansar main rahete hue bhi usamen lipt na ho jane kaa nirdesh he. mahanushasan main shankaracharya ne kaha he, “mathon ko sampatti kaa sanyam karnaa chahie, itnaa ann hona chahie ki asahaay, peedit or daridrajanon ko aashray diya ja sake. mathon main daridrata kaa naam bhi na dikhaai de. acharya or sanyasi us vaibhav kaa upyog dharm-samskruti kii raksha ke liye karen, swayan nirvaah ke liye jitna aavashyak ho, utana hi grahan karen.”
 mahanushasan main aage kii dharmagyaaen hai - mathpati apane raashtra kii pratishtha ke liye va dharm-pratishtha ke liye aalasy tyagkar parishram karen. ve apane-apane shashan pradesh main sadaiv bhraman kar logon ko varnashram ke karttavyon kaa upadesh den, sadachaar badhayen. ek mathpati duusare mathpati ke adhikarashetra main na jaae. sarv mathpati bich-bich main ekatrit hokar dharm-charchaa karen va desh main dharmik suvyavastha banaae rakhane ke liye pranpatase praytn karen; vaidic dharm pragatisheel va akhandit rahe, iske liye ve daksh rahen.
 acharya ne apane is mahanushasan main desh ke vidwaan mandaliyon par bhi ek zimmedaari saunpi he. unke matanusaar vidwaan log hi dharm ke prati niamak ho sakate hai. ve in dharm-peethon par dhyaan rakhen, samay-samay par mathpatiyon ke aacharan ko parakhen. vidwaan, charitravaan, karttvyadaksh, sadguni, sanyasi ko hi peethaadhishthit banaaen. vah peethadhyaksh karttavyayut sabit ho, to vidwaan use peeth se padchyut kar den.
