 anugoonj gitanjali shri kaa pehla kahani sangrah he. isamen sankalit das kahaniyon main jo samvednaa jo vidroh or pratirodh he vah swayan ko ujagar karte vakt paatro kii kamjor hote swabhimaan andekha nahin karti, ishaaron or bimbon ke sahare chithran karne vaala shilp or shikshit varg kii aadhunik maansikta ko dikhaati unki bolchaal kii bhashaa, gitanjali shri kii vishesh lekhkeey pahchaan banaati he. yun to in kahaniyon kaa kendra lagbhag har baar-ek ‘daraar’ ko chhodkar- banataa he shikshit madhyamvargeey naariyon se, par isamen varnit hote hai hamaare aadhunik naagarik jivan ke vibhinn paksh. jaise vaivaahik tathaa vivaahettar stri-purush sambandh, paariwarik paristhitiyaan, saamaajik roodhiyaan, hindu-muslim samasya, striyon kaa parsparik maitri ityadi. yahan sidhaa, sapaat kuch bhi nahin he. har sthiti, har sambandh, har sangharsh main vyaapt rahete hai paraspar virdohi swar. yahi virdohi swar rachate hai har ek kahani kaa ek alag raag.
