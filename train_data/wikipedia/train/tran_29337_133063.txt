 bandipur zila bharat ke jammu va kashmir rajya kaa ek zila he. yah pehle baraamula zile kaa hissaa hua karata tha lekin 2006 main ise ek alag zile kaa darja de diya gaya. is zile kaa mukhyalay bandipur shahar he. is zile main adhiktar log kashmiri bhashaa bolte hai haalaanki is zile ke uttari bhaag ke gurez kshetra main shina bhashaa boli jaati he.
 bandipur zile kii teen tahseelein hai - bandipur, gurez or sumbal sonawadi.
