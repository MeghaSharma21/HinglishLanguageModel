 maulok jila ) myanmar ke ke kendriya sagang mandal kaa ek jila he. iskaa mukhyalay maulok nagar he. yah burma ke jilon main sabase bada he. iskaa kshetrafal 41,536 varg kilometre he. 
 maulok jile kaa bhubhag vishesh rup se pahari he. chhindvin nadi, jo jile main uttr se dakshin pravaahit hoti he or jisaki mukhya sahaayak uyu he, kshetreey parvaton ko do mukhya shreniyon main vibhajit karti he, jo is nadi ke purv evam pashchim main sthit hai. uttar-pashchim main verma kaa sarvocch parvatshikhar sarameti athwa vemakataang sthit he, jo 3829 me. unchaa tathaa parvatashrinkhalaaon ke us sanghatan main he, jo verma ko bharat se alag-alag karata he. nadiyon evam parvatashreniyon ke kaaran adhikansh jila, visheshkar dakshini bhaag prakritik saundarya main apurv he. prachur maatra main varsha hone ke kaaran wanon kii adhikta he, jinase imarti lakdi, visheshkar sagaun, praapt hoti he. vibhinn prakaar ke banson kii bhi adhikta he. pahari bhaagon tathaa ghaatiyon kii pramukh upaj dhan he. iske atirikt kuch chaay bhi utpann hoti he.
