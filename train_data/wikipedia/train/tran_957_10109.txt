 vadkunathan mandir, kerala ke trishur nagar main sthit ek pracheen shiv mandir he. ise 'tenkailashm' tathaa 'rishabhachalam' bhi kehete hai.
 trishshur ek khubasurat pracheen shahar or kerala kii saanskrithik rajdhani he. bhootpurv kochin riyasat ke maharaja ram verma  ke samay trishshur hi riyasat kii rajdhani bhi rahi. nagar ke madhya main hi 9 ekad main failaa oonche parcote vaala ek vishaal shiv mandir he jise vadkunathan kehete hai. vadkunathan se taatpary “uttar ke nath” se he jo 'kedarnath' hi ho saktaa he. pracheen sahitya main is baat kaa ullekh miltaa he ki is mandir main aadi shankaracharya ke mata pita ne santan prapti ke liye anushthan kiye the. ek or sabandh bhi he. yahan aadi shankaracharya kii tathakthith samadhi bhi bani he or uske saath ek chota sa mandir jisame unki murti bhi sthapit he. ullekhneey he ki aadi shankaracharya kii ek samadhi kedarnath mandir ke peeche bhi he. vadkunathan ke is mandir ke chaaron taraf 60 ekad main failaa ghanaa sagaun kaa jangal tha jise shaktan tamburan ne katwa kar lagbhag 3 kilometre gol sadak kaa nirmaan karavaaya tha. yahi aaj kaa swaraj round he. kisi wilichpad ke yah kah kar pratirodh kiye jane par ki ye jangal to shiv ji kii jataaen hai, us raja ne apane hi haath se us bege kaa sar kaat diya tha. isi mandir ke baahar april/may main puram namka utsav hota he jise dekhne videshiyon sahit lakhon log aate hai.
