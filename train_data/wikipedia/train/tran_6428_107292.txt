 sansadeey samprabhutaa kuch sansadeey loktantron ke sanvaidhanik vidhi kii ek avadharana hai. isaki yah dharna hoti he ki, vidhaayi nikaay ke paas purna samprabhutaa hoti he, or vah sabhi anya sarkari sansthaanon, jisamen karypalika yaa nyaayik nikaay samaveshit hai, se sarvocch hota hai. 
 kai rajyon main samprabhu vidhayikaaye hoti hai, jisamen united kingdom, finland, nederlands, new zealand, sweden, barbados, jamaica, papua new gini and solomon dweepasmooh sammilit hai.
