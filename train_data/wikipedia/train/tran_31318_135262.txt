 ahamed timol bhartiya mul ke dakshin africa rangabhed virdohi kaaryakarta or african neshnal congress ke sadsy the.
 unka janm 3 november 1941 ko bratan, transawaal praant, dakshin africa main hua tha. unke pita kaa naam haji yousuf timol or unki maan kaa naam hawwa ismail didaar tha. ahamed timol ke maan-baap surat, gujarat se the.
 ahamed timol ek raajnneetik kaaryakarta the jinhone khuleam dakshin africa sarkaar kii rangabhed niti kaa virodh kiya. isliye unhone transawaal indian congress main shaamil kiya. baad main unhone africa neshnal congress or communist party main bhi shaamil kiya gaya.
 timol kii jeevani ke anusaar oktober 1971 ke dauraan rangabhed sarkaar police ne badi berahami se unki hatya karavaaya tha, lekin police dastauez ke anusaar unhone jon foster police stesan main aatmahatya kii thi.
