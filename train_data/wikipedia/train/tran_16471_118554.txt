 shrimati malvika sinha bhartiya rigerv bank main ek karypalak nideshak hai. unhen 3 april 2017 ko is pad par niyukt kiya gaya. karypalak nideshak ke rup main shrimati sinha videshi mudra vibhaag, sarkari or bank lekha vibhaag or aantarik rin prabandh vibhaag dekhati hai.
 unhone mumbai vishvavidhyalay se lok prashasan main snatakottar kiya he or ve indian institute of bankers kii ek pramanit sahyogi bhi hai.
 ve 1982 main rigerv bank main shaamil hui or viniman or paryvekshan, videshi mudra or sarkari or bank lekha ke kshetra main kaary kiya. karypalak nideshak ke rup main padonnat hone se pehle, shrimati sinha reserve bank ke sahakaari banking paryvekshan vibhaag main pradhan mukhya mahaprabandhak thi.
