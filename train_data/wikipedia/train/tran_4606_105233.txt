 vine maanan kaa tara min taramandal main sthit ek safed bouna tara he. yah prithvi se lagbhag 14.1 prakaash-varsh kii duuri par he or vyaadh taare or praswa taare ke mandalon main maujuud safed boune taron ke baad hamaare saura mandal ke teesraa sab se paas vaala safed bouna he. yah pehla safed bouna mila tha jo kisi bahu tara mandal main kisi anya taare ke saath gurutvkarshak bandhan main nahin bandhaa he.
 angrezi main "vine maanan kaa taare" ko "vine maananaz star" kehete hai.
 anya safed bunon kii tarah vine maanan kaa tara bhi bahut ghanaa he. iskaa dravyaman suraj ke dravyaman kaa qareeb 70% he lekin iskaa vyas suraj ke vyas kaa sirf 1% he. iskaa satahi taapmaana 6,750 calvin he or isaki chamak suraj se 5,000 guna kam he.
