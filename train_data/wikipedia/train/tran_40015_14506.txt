 
 jaiguaar felidaye kul kaa ek shikari maansaahaari jaanavar he jo uttari america or dakshini america main pai jane vaali iklauti panthera jati he. singh or baagh ke baad jaiguaar billi kul kaa teesraa sabase bada or prithvi ke pashchimi golardh kaa pehla sabase bada sadsy jaanavar he. iskaa nivas kshetra uttar main sanyukt rajya america ke kuch dakshini hisson se lekar dakshin main argentina ke uttari hisson tak he.
 haalanki dekhne main jaiguaar kuch-kuch tendue jaisaa lagata he, lekin iskaa sharir tendue se bada or adhik shaktishaalee hota he. iskaa rahan-sahan or aadaten tendue se zyaada baagh se milti hai. yah wanon main rahanaa pasand karata he lekin maidaanon or jhaad vaale ilaakon main bhi ghumta he. ise pani ke paas rahanaa pasand he or baagh kii tarah ise bhi aasaani se tairnaa aata he. ise akelaa rahanaa or peechha karke apana shikaar maarna pasand he. iskaa jabada or daant bahut majboot hote hai or sahaj hi kavach vaale jaanwaron ko bhi grass banane main sakasham hai. aamtaur par yah apane shikaar ko kaanon ke bich kaat kar sidhaa kopal kii haddi todakar mastishk par jaanaleva ghaav kar detaa he.
