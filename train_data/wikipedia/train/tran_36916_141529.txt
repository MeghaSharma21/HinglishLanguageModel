 ve es ramadevi eme, ellem, 26 navambar 1990 se 11 disambar 1990 tak bharat kii mukhya chunaav ayukta raheen. wo pratham mahila thi jinhone bharat ke mukhya chunaav ayukta kaa padbhaar sambhaala. unke baad tii en sheshan mukhya chunaav ayukta bane.
 ramadevi kaa janm 15 janvari 1934 ko bhartiya rajya andhra pradesh ke pashchimi godavari jile ke chebrolu main hua. unhone aluru se shiksha praapt kii. eme or elelbi karne ke baad unhone andhra pradesh ucch nyaayalay main apane aap ko vakil ke rup main panjikrut karavaaya. wo 26 july 1997 se 1 disambar 1999 tak himachal pradesh kii rajyapal rahi or 2 disambar 1999 se 20 agast 2002 tak karnataka kii rajyapal raheen.
 17 april 2013 ko unka echestor le-out bangalore sthit apane nivas par nidhan ho gaya.
