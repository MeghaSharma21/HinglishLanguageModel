 shabar yaa shabar swami jaiminikrut purv mimasaa sutara ke bhashyakaar the. is par unhone shabar bhashya kii rachana kii. aisa kaha jaataa he ki shabar kaa vaastavik naam 'adityadev' tha kintu jainon ke dar se unhone apane vaastavik naam ko prakat nahin hone diya or vanavaasi kii tarah bhesh badalakar rahe. shabar-bhashya par kumaril bhatta ne bhashya likhaa he. shabar kaa jeevankaal pratham shatabdi ke aasapaas hua tha. ve patanjali mahabhashya ke pashchaat evam vatsyayan ke purv utpann hue the.
