 dilli vishvavidhyalay ek saarvajanik kendriya vishvavidhyalay he jo bharat kii rajdhani dilli main sthit he. vishvavidhyalay bharat sarkaar dwaara vitt poshit kiya jaataa tathaa iske kulapati kaa pad bharat ke uparashtrapati ke paas hota he. dilli vishvavidhyalay ke do parisar hai jo dilli ke uttari or dakshini bhaag main sthit hai or jinhen kramash: uttari parisar or dakshini parisar kaha jaataa he. vishvavidhyalay kii sthapana san 1922 main kendriya vidhaan sabha dwaara paarit kiye gaye bill ke dwaara hui thi. iske sansthaapak upkulapati harisingh gaur the. dilli vishvavidhyalay lagaataar bharat ke ucch kramakan vishvavidhyalayon main se ek rahaa he.
