 bottla house encounter jise aadhikarik taur par oparation bottla house ke rup main janaa jaataa he, sitambar 19, 2008 ko dilli ke jamiya nagar ilaake main indian mujahidin ke sandigdh aatankwaadiyon ke khilaaf kii gayi muthbhed thi, jisamen do sandigdh aatankwaadi aatif amin or mohammad sajid maare gaye, do anya sandigdh saf mohammad or aariz khan bhaagane main kaamyaab ho gaye, jabaki ek or aaropee zeeshaan ko giraftaar kar liya gaya. is muthbhed kaa netritv kar rahe encounter visheshgy or dilli police nireekshak mohan chand sharma is ghatana main maare gaye. muthbhed ke dauraan sthaneey logon kii giraftaar hui, jiske khilaaf anek raajnneetik dalon, kaaryakartaaon or vishesh rup se jamiya milia islamia vishvavidhyalay ke shikshakon or chaatro ne vyapak rup se virodh pradarshan kiya. samaajawaadee party or bahujan samaaj party jaise kai raajnneetik sanghathanon ne sansad main muthbhed kii nyaayik jaanch karne kii maang uthaai, jaise-jaise samachar patron main muthbhed ke "nae sanskaran" pradarshit hone lage.
