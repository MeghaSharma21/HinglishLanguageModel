 nepal bhashaa nepal kii ek pramukh bhashaa he. yah bhashaa chini-tibeti bhashaa-parivaar ke antargat tibeti-burmeli samooh me sanyojit he. yah devanagari lipi me bhi likhi jane vaali ek maatr chini-tibeti bhashaa he. yah bhashaa dakshin asia kii sabase pracheen itihaas vaali tibeti-burmeli bhashaa he or tibeti burmeli bhashaa main chauthi sabase pracheen kaal se upyog main laai jane vaali bhashaa.
 yah bhashaa 14veen shatabdi se lekar 18veen shatabdi ke ant tak nepal kii prashasnic bhashaa thi.
 yah bhashaa chini-tibeti bhashaa parivaar ke antargat tibeti-burmeli samooh me sanyojit he.
 nepal bhashaa bahut si lipiyon main likhi jaati he. inamen pramukh lipiyaan hai - ranjana, prachlit, brahmi, bhoojingol, devanagari aadi. ye sabhi lipiyaan baayen se daaen taraf likhi jaati he. in sabhi lipiyon main swarmala or vyanjanamala naamak do prakaar ke akshar hote hai.
 nepal bhashaa kathmandu kii mul bhashaa he. is bhashaa kaa utpatti-sthaan kathmandu hi he. kathmandu me kiraant shashan ke samay me is bhashaa par kiranti bhashaa kaa prabhaav pada. tibet ke saath shatavdiyon ke vyapaarik sambandhon ke kaaran is bhashaa me tibeti bhashaa kaa bhi ullekhya prabhaav dikhataa he. lichhavi kaal or mall kaal me sanskrit tathaa shaahakaal me nepali bhashaa kaa prabhaav bhi is bhashaa me dikhne laga.
 tibeti-burmeli bhashaa parivaar kii bhashaa hone ke baavjood varsho tak bharopeli bhashaa ke sambandh ne nepal bhashaa kaa swaroop vishesh rup se naamapad thoda bharopeli jaisaa bana diya he.
 nepali media me nijee kshetra kii bhagidaari ke baad is bhashaa ke vikaas ko gati mili he.
 nepal bhashaa sahitya ke armbh kaa koi aitihaasik pramaan nahin he. iskaa vikaas or vistaar lichhavi tathaa mall kaal me hone ke tathy kii us samay me rachit vibhinn naatak, geet, kavy tathaa tatkaaleen shilaalekh pushti karte hai.
 nepal bhashaa ke mahakavi siddhidaas mahaju hai.
