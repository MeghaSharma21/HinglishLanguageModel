 sindhu pracheen bharat kaa ek rajya tha jiskaa ullekh mahabharat tathaa harivansh puraana main aayaa he. yah pradesh sindh nadi ke kinare sthit tha. kaha jaataa he ki is pradesh kii sthapana vrishdarbh naamak raja ne kiya tha jo shivi ke putr the. mirchandani dwaara likhit 'glimces of anshient sindh' ke anusaar sindh rajya kii rajdhani vrishadarbhapur thi. sindhu rajya ke nivasee 'saindhav' kehalaate the.
