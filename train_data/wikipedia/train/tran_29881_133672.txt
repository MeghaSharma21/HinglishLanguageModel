 nairo gej railway vah rail trek hota he, jo 1,435 mimi ke maanak gej se sankara hota he. adhikansh nairo gej, jo ab bhi astitv main hai; 3 feet, 6 inch yaa isse bhi sankare hai.

 bal bhawan kii rail ke stesan par nairo gej kii line
 do feet chah inch kii is nairo gej len par nau november, 1903 se ajtak rail yatayaat jari he. kalka-shimla railmarg main 103 surangen or 869 pul bane hue hai. is maarg par 919 ghumaav aate hai, jinamein se sabase tikhe mode par train 48 degree ke kone par gumti he.
 varsh 1903 main angrejon dwaara kalka-shimla rail section banaaya gaya tha. rail vibhaag ne 7 navambar 2003 ko dhoomdhaam se shatabdi samaaroh bhi manaaya tha, jisame purv railmantri nitish kumaar ne hissaa liya tha. is avasar par nitish kumaar ne is rail trek ko vishwa dharohar kaa darja dilaane ke liye maamla unesco se uthaane kii ghoshnaa kii thi. unesco kii team ne kalka-shimla railmarg kaa daura karke halaat kaa jayja liya. 
 team ne kaha tha ki darjeeling rail section ke baad yah ek aisa section he jo apane aap main anokhaa he. unesco ne is trek ke aitihaasik mahatv ko samajhate hue bharosa dilaayaa tha ki ise valrd heritage main shaamil karne ke liye vah puraa prayaas karenge. or antatah 24 july 2008 ko ise vishwa dharohar ghoshit kiya gaya.
