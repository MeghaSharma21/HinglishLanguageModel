 unani chikitsavigyaan kaa sabase mahatvapurn vyakti hippocrates tha jise paashchaatya jagat 'chikitsavigyaan kaa janak' kahata he. hippocratus or anya logon kii kritiyon kaa islamic chikitsavigyaan or madhyakaal ke europea chikitsavigyaan par gahari chhap padi or antatah 14veen shatee ke baad ye siddhanth kalathit ho gaye.
 'unani' shabd sanskrit 'yavanani' kaa roopaantar maatr he, jo swayan 'yavan' shabd se vyutpann he. panini ke samay main �yavanani�shabd yavan kii stri ke liye prayukt haata tha. peeche yah shabd yawanon kii lipi or yawanon kii bhashaa ke liye bhi prayukt hone laga. pracheen kaal main greece desh ko yunaan kehete the or vahaan ke tathaa syria ke rahanewaalon ko unani. sanskrit granthon se pataa lagata he ki pracheen kaal main videshiyon or vidharmiyon ko yavan kehete the. yah shabd us samay se prachlit he, jab islam dharm or musalamaanon kaa sansar main kahin namonishaan bhi nahin tha. aaj unani chikitsapaddati musalamaanon ke haath main he. iske granth arabi, farsi or ab urdu main bhi milte hai. pracheen unani granth greek or latin bhashaaon main the. unheen kaa anuwaad arabon ne apni bhashaa main kiya tha. arabon ko chikitsa kaa gyaan yunaan se hi praapt hua tha or uski pratishtha, pracheenta evam pramanikta suchit karne ke liye unhone chikitsapaddati ke saath unani shabd jod diya tha. arab vaale is paddati ko hi aaj apni paddati maanate hai. unani paddati kaa aavirbhaav yunaan main hua tha, jisaki vruddhi main anek unani darshanikon kaa, jaise askalipus, woocraat pithagoras, aflatoon, arastu or peeche iskandariyaa ke irophilas, erosistratas or ant main jalinus kaa sahyog praapt hua tha.
 kalantar main unani vidya kaa hraas hona shuru hua. unani vidya ko romanwaalon ne grahan kar liya, par ve uskaa vikaas kuch nahin kar sake. is bich islam dharm kaa pradurbhaav hua. musalmaan shaskaon ne yunaan ke gyaanakosh ko arabi dhaanche main dhaal liya or uski anya deshon ke gyaan vigyaan evam vidyaon se tulana kii. isse anek deshaan ke gyanbhandar kaa arabi bhashaa main sangrah ho gaya.
 ab arabi vidvan pratilipi evam anuwaad kaary se virat hone ke uparaant, gyaan vigyaan ke purna adhikari ban gaye tathaa unke paas ek gyaanakosh sanchit ho gaya. tab aadhikarik ban gaye tathaa unke paas ek gyaanakosh sanchit ho gaya. tab aadhikarik rup se inhone in pracheen siddhanton evam samasyaon kaa parishilan, vivechan, mimasaa or anveshan aarambh kiya or unke prayek angopang main vikaas kiya. duusare shabdon main unani vaidyak, arabi vaidyak kaa paridhaan dhaaran karne laga. arabi vikaas mul gyaanakosh ke saath aisa hill mil gaya ki use pruthak karnaa dushkar evam duruh ho gaya.
 arabon ke shashan ke shaantikaal main jo vaidyak granth pranit kiye gaye, ve itane ucch koti ke siddh hue ki sabhi paashchaatya or purv deshon ke vidwaanon ne aise granthon ko patha granth ke rup main sweekrut kar liya.
 yunaan main vaidyak gyaan kaa prasar misr or phinishia dwaara hua. ayurveda kaa bahut sa gyaan bharat se buddha bhikshuon dwaara, athwa syria or babilonia haakar, misr gaya, misr se yunan gaya, athwa iran hokar yunaan gaya or vahaan se arab or anya pashchaatya deshon main failaa. aadhunik paashchaatya vaidyak vigyaan kaa aadhaar bhartiya ayurveda hi he or allopathy kaa mul mantra bharat se hi gaya. vastut: aadhunik allopathy main unani or ayurvedic chikitsapaddati donon hi mili hue hai. bharat kii chikitsapaddati pracheen kal main adhik samrudh or unnat thi. chini chikitsa paddati par bhi iskaa spasht prabhaav padaa dekha jaataa he.
 unani vaidyak ke aadharbhoot siddhant, arkan arbaa or arbalaat arbaa aadi, aryavaidyak main bahut pehle se sthir ho chuke the or inhen chaturmahabhoot, yaa panchmahabhoot or chaturdosh, yaa tridosh aadi naamon se jaanate the. ukt kaal main hi bocraat, dioscoridies or jalinus aadi ke granthon main anek bhartiya dravyon tathaa siddhanton kaa grahan ho chukaa tha. muvashshir evanfaatik ne mukhtarul hukum main likhaa he �jab sikandar ne daara par vijay pai, to usne iraniyon ke samast granth nasht kar diye, keval jyotish, darshan or vaidyak ke granth chhod die, jinka uske aadesh se unaneey bhashaantar kiya gaya.�sambhavat: bharat se bhi isi prakaar vaidyak vidya ke kosh unaniyon ke haath aae hon.
 sikandar ke akraman ne unaniyon tathaa bharatiyon ke bich sambandh paidaa kar diya tha. is meljol kaa avashyambhavi parinaam yah hua ki unaniyon ne bharatiyon se vidhi gyaan vigyaan siikhe. baad main bhi yah sambandh iran, syria or iskandariyaa se bana rahaa. dow hornle tathaa dow newberg ne likhaa he ki tijias or megasthineez naamak do unani hakim isvi san se 4 shatee purv gyaan kii khoj main bharat aae the. yah kaaran he ki uttarkalin unani granthon main branpuran kii bhartiya vidhiyon kaa ullekh miltaa he. unaniyon se unani bhashaa ke granthen dwaara europe, asia or africa kii anek jatiyon ne is vidya ko siikhaa or uskaa prachlan har sthaan par ho gaya tha.
 unani-roosi-samskruti ke haas ke pashchaat gyaan or vidya ke saath vaidyak kii dharohar bhi musalamaanon ke haath main ai jinhone ise balakh, bokhaara, turkistan, china or bharat se lekar andlus tak failaayaa. islam ke praarambhik kaal main, yadhyapi iske abhibhavak musalmaan avashya the, tathaapi yah anya jatiyon ke haath main rahi. lagbhag dedh sau varsho tak unani chikitsa paddati ko isai, yahudee, tarapujak, irani, kuldani, misri or surayani aadi vibhinn bhashabhashi jatiyaan jaananevaali thim. jab in granthon kaa arabi main anuwaad ho gaya tab musalamaanon ne is vidya ko siikhnaa prarambh kiya, tab anek vishwavikhyath, hakimon jaise raji or shaikh, kaa pradurbhaav hua.
 unani chikitsa ke aadharbhoot siddhant nimnlikhit he:
 ilmetib shaastra he jisase maanavsharir kii swasth tathaa aswasth avasthaaon kaa gyaan hota he or yah shaastra bataata he ki swasthya kii raksha kaise kii ja sati he tathaa rogavastha main rog kaa nivaaran kaise ho saktaa he. manushya kaa swasthya un tatwon kii prakritik or aprakritik sthiti par nirbhar karata he jinase maanav sharir bana he. in tatwon ko unani chikitsak umoor mukabwema, yaa ajazaai mukavvema, kehete hai. manushya kaa astitv inhin tatwon par nirbhar he. jab tak ye tatv apni prakritik avasthaa main rahete hai, tab tak maanav sharir apana kaary niyamit rup se karata he. isi kaa naam swasthya he. in mul tatwon par hi maanav prakruti kii utpatti nirbhar karti he, di in tatwon main ek bhi na rahe, to maanav sharir kii sthiti asambhav ho jaaygi.
 umoor tabiiyya ko hi ayurveda main dosha-dhaatumal-vigyaan or allopathy main phiziology kehete hai. umoor tabiiyya main saat tatwon kaa samavesh he: 
 ye hi sharir ke kool ghatak, yaa upadan he jinase manushya bana he. ilm manafedl aajaa main inhen padaartho kaa nirupan hua karata he.
 unaniyon ne sharir ke sanghatnkaaree samast padaartho ko do pradhan bhaagon, dravyabhoot or adravyabhoot, main baanta he adravyabhoot ghatak dravya nahin he. yah dravyabhoot ghatakon ke aashray se rahanevaala unka gundharm he. inhen chhod dene par dravyabhoot ghatak keval chaar thaharte hai, jinamein arkan dravyibhoot ghatakon ke bhi nirmaayak moolbhoot tatv hai. ise chhod dene par keval teen dravyabhoot ghatak, akhlaat, aaza or arvaah rah jate he.
 unaniyon ne sharir ke samast sanghatnkaaree tatwon ko swaroop kii drushti se teen virgo main baanta he: 
 shaikhurrais ne unani vaidyak ke samast pratipaadya vishyon or unke ang pratyangon kaa apane suprasidv granth, al kanoon ke praarabhik adhyyon main vishad vivaran diya hai. is granth main yah bhi batayaya gaya hai ki hakim ko kin kin bishyon ko kitna jaananaa aavashyak hai tathaa kin bishyon main pratyaksh, anubhav evam pareekshan apekshit hai. shaikh mahoday kulliyaat kanoon kii duusari fasal maujaat tib main fermaaten hai :
 'chookin unani vaidyak shaastra swasthya or aswasthya kii drushti se maanav sharir kaa nirupan karata hai or prayek vastu kaa gyaan usee samay purnataya praapt kar liya jaay, basharte ki us vastu ke kaaran kaa hon, iske liye yah aavashyak hai ki unani vaidyak shaastra main swasthya or aswasthya ke hetu, unke kaaran gyaat kiye jaae. chunki swasthya tathaa unke nidaan kaaran kabhi vyakti evam prakat hote hai or kabhi avyakt evam aprakat, jo gyanedriyon se gyaat nahin kiye ja sakate pratyut unke gyaan ke liye updrav evam lakshan ke pathpradarshan kii apeksha hua karti hai, isaliye yah bhi anivaary ho gaya ki unani vaidyak shaastra main swastheavastha evam aswastheavastha main hone vaale un updravon evam lakshanon kaa ullekh kiya jaay jinase ham swasthya evam aswasthya arthath rog ke pahchaanane main samarth ho jaate hai. �ulum hakikia main aisa kaha hai ki kisi vastu kaa gyaan uske nidaan kaarnon se praapt hota hai. yadi uske nidaan kaaran na ho, to uske nijee lakshanon ke gyaan se kuch seemaa tak uskaa pataa lag jaataa hai.
 unani grantho main asbagh ke chaar bhed bataae gaye hai. ye hai : asbab maddiya, asbab faelia, asbab suriya or asbab tamaamia, yaa gaia. basbaab maddiya se swasthya evam rog adhishthit hote hai. iske bhi do upbhed he: 1. sannikrusht or viprakrusht . sannikrusht nidaan ang pratyang, oj evam vaayu hen or viprakrusht nidaan chaturdosh or chaturmahabhoot hai. asabaab faelia ve kaaran hai, jo maanav sharir ke bheetar parirvatan karte hen, yaa unki raksha karaten hai. asbab suriya ke teen upbhed he: 1. prakruti, 2. bal or 3. sanghathan . prakruti, bal or sanghathan ke yathavat rahane se bahya swasthya paaya jaataa hai. in teenon main se kisi ek ke vikrut hone se rog hota hen. asbab tamaamia sharir kriyaayein hai.
 sutara unani vaidyak main nimna vishyon kaa pratipaadan kiya jaataa he : mahabhoot prakruti, dosha, abhishravayav, sanmishravayav, praan or oj, bal, swasthyay evam aswasthya or tanmadhyavarti halaat, aswastha drusht, yaa shareerik avasthaa main or adholikhit nidaan kaaran khaadya, peya, vaayu, jal, desh tathaa sthaan, sansaadhan, stambhan, vyavsaay, swabhaav, kayika evam maansik kaary, acarya, vibhinn vaayu lingabhed, sharir par aane vaale anya bhra vishay, swasthya sangrakshan, prayek vyaadhi ke nivaranarth khaadh evam peya kii vidhi, cheshtaa acheshtaa kaa anumaan, aushadhsewan, hastkarm evam shalyakarm se laabh uthaanaa. inamen kuch to aise hai jinhen hakim ko bina tark ke maan lenaa chahie or kuch aise hai jinhen tark or yukti se sidv karnaa hota hai.
 vaidyak shaastra main kuch vishay anya shastron se grahan kiye gaye hai, aise vishyon kaa swaroop tark evam yukti ke bina sweekaar kar lenaa vaidya ke liye anivaary hai. ang pratyang or unke karm to gyanendriyon or shavchchanden evam shalyakarm se gyaat kiye jaate hai. shaikh kehete hai, jin vishyon kaa jaananaa or jinaki vaidyak main tark evam yukti se siddh karnaa aavashyak hen, ve rog, unke nidaan kaaran or unke lakshan he tathaa yah he ki vyaadhi kaa nivaaran kis prakaar kiya jaae evam swasthya sangrakshan kis prakaar ho saktaa hai. ye vishay aise hen ki unamem se jinka astiv spasht evam nishchit na ho, unako samay or pramaan- varnanasah tark evam yukti - se vistaarpurvak siddh karnaa aavashyak hai.
 shaikh ke vivaran se hamen jahaan unani vaidyak ke samast pratipaadyon evam sidvanto kaa sankshep main gyaan ho jaataa hai, vahaan is vishay kaa bhi gyaan hota hai ki in pracheen vidwaanon ke paas pratyaksh gnanarth us praarambhik evam saral yug main jo jo upkaran uplabdh the, unase apni shakti evam samarthy bhar kaam lene main in audyogikon ne koi baat uthaa nahin rakhi hai.
 kapitay sidvanto kaa badalanaa or vibhinn anveshak kaa vibhinn kaal main vibhinn nishkarsh par pahunchnaa, ek abaadh niyam hai. paashchaatya vaigyanik vishyon ke sambandh main bhi aisa sadaa hi hota aa rahaa hai. jo baat ek samay akatya satya samajhi jaati thi, vah nae sadhano, upkarnon or khojon se anek baar asaty pramanit hui hai. pratyaksh gyaan ke baad bhi bhuyodarshan karne waalon se bhul hona sambhav he, to is niyam se pracheen vidwaan bhi nahin batch sakate hai. apni samarthy ke anusaar prakruti ke niyamon kaa adhyayan evam nirikshan unhonen kiya. pratyaksh prayog yaa anubhav ke baad kuch nishkarsh sthir kiye, unamem se kuch peeche ke prayogon or anubhavon se mithyaa siddh hue. isse ham un pracheen vidwaanon ko dosha nahin de sakaten. shaikhurais ke anusaar vyaadhi kaa pratikaar hetuvyaadhivipareet dwaara hi kiya jaataa hai. ise elaj vijjid keethan hai. yahi moolbhoot siddhant anya chikitsa pranaaliyon ke bhi hai.
