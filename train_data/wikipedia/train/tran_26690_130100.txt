 bamayaan yaa bamiyaan afghanistan kaa ek praant he jo us desh ke madhya bhaag main sthit he. bamayaan main adhiktar aabaadi hazaaraa samudaay kii he, jo mongol nasl ke vanshaj hai or shiya islam ke anuyaayi hai. bamiyaan praant kaa kshetrafal 14,175 varg kimi he or isaki aabaadi san 2006 main lagbhag 3.9 lakh anumaanit kii gai thi. is praant kii rajdhani bamayaan shahar he.
