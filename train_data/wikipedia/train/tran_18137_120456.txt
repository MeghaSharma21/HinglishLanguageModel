 vanya praani udyaan ko chidyaghar bhi kaha jaataa he. dilli kaa chidyaghar asia ke sabase achche chidyagharon main ek he. yah puraane kile ke paas hi sthit he. 1959 main bane is chidyaghar kaa dizin srilanka ke major winemen or pashchim germany ke karl hegalbek ne banaaya tha. 214 acre main faile is jaivik udyaan main jaanwaron or pakshiyon kii 22000 prajatiyaan or 200 prakaar ke ped hai. yahaan par austelia, africa, america or asia se laae gaye pashu-pakshi bhi dekhe ja sakate hai. chidyaghar main ek pustkaalay bhi he jahaan se ped, paudhon, pashu-pakshiyon ke bare main jaankaree lee ja sakati he. samay: garmiyaan main subah 8-shaam 6 baje tak, sardiyon main subah 9-shaam 5 baje tak shuukravaar ko band rahata he or khaane pine kii chijen laana manaa he|
