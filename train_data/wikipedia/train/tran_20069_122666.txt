 loard nadi canada ke ukon, british columbia or uttarpashchim praant se hokar guzarati he. dakshinpurvi ukon main peli parvat ke saint seer range se nikalne vaali yah nadi dakshin purv kii taraf british columbia, rocky parvatamala se guzarati hui ukon, uttarpashchimi praant kii ore uttar purv main mud jaati he or fort simpasan ke paas 1,115 kilometre kii duuri tay karte hue maikenzi nadi main mil jaati he. yah nadi taiga ke saath milakar lagbhag 277,100 varg kilometre kshetra kaa jal vahan karti he.
 yah nadi nichali maikenzi nadi tajepani kii paristhitiki kaa ek hissaa he. ukon praant main is nadi ke aasapaas ke kshetra ko loard nadi ghati kaha jaataa he. yah nahani ke wood bison naamak jaanavar ke pravasi raste main padati he.
 nadi ke naam kaa itihaas to agyaat he lekin yah purvi cottnwood naamak paudhe ke liye prayukt hone vaale fransisi bhashaa ke shabd par aadhaarit he jo yahan nadi muhaanon par baari maatra main ugta he. fer kaa vyapaar karne vaale shruwati vyapaaree fort nelson nadi ko purvi shakha or iske upar bahane vaali loard ko pashchimi shakha kehete the. 
 nadi ke adhikansh hisson main bhraman karne vaale pehle europea vyakti kaa naam jon macleod tha jo ek anveshk the or hadsan be company se sambandhit the. fort simpasan se 28 june, 1831 ko aath anya saathiyon ke saath nadi main bhraman ke liye nikalne vaale macleod chha: hafton main hi des nadi pahunkk gayi, uskaa namakaran kiya or phir chaar dinon baad francis nadi pahunch gayi. wo ise mukhya loard nadi samajhane kii bhul kar baithe or usamen upar kii taraf jane lage or vaastavik loard kii shruwaat tak nahin pahunch paae. nau saalon baad echbisi ke ek anya karmchaaree robert campbell loard nadi kii shruwaat saint seer range tak pahunche.
 
 shruwaat se nadimukh tak loard kii sahaayak nadiyaan nimnlikhit hai.
