 chamak, chamkilaapan yaa roshanapan drishy bodh kaa ek pahalu he jisamen prakaash kisi strot se ubharta hua yaa pratibimbit hota hua lagata he. dusre shabdon main chamak vah bodh he jo kisi dekhi gai vastu kii prakaash prabalata se hota he. chamak koi kade tareeke se maap sakane vaali chiz nahin he or adhiktar vyaktigat bodh ke bare main hi prayog hoti he. chamak ke maap ke liye prakaash prabalata jaisi avadharanaon kaa prayog hota he.
 angrezi main "chamak" ko "bretanus" kehete hai.
 koi chiz kitni chamakili he vah baaqi ke vaataavaran par bhi nirbhar karata he. khagolashastra main taron kii saapeksh kantimaan or nirpeksh kantimaan ko mapa jaataa he, lekin yah maap anumaanit hi hota he.
