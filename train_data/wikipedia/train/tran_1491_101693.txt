 == pracheen itihaas == s
 
 
 bihar bharat ke purv bhaag main sthit ek vishesh rajya he, jo kii aitihasik drushtikon se bharat kaa sabase bada centre he, yaa phir aisa kahana galat nahin hoga, kii bihar ke bina bharat adhura he. chiraand, chhapra se 11 kilometre sthit, saaran jila kaa sabase mahatvapurn puraatatv sthal he. bodh dharm ke logo dwaara yahan vihaar karne ke kaaran is rajya kaa naam bihar padaa. yahi par bhagavan budh kaa janm hua, or isi paavan bhoomi par mahabodhi mandir stith he. bihar kii paavan bhoomi par anekanek santo kaa janm hua, isi paavan bhoomi par acharya chanakya or aryabhat jaise vidwan or vaigyanik hue. jab duniya ke bahut se hisson main log padhnaa likhnaa bhi nahin jaanate the, us samay shiksha kaa sabase bada centre nalanda vishwavidhyalay bihar kii rajdhani patliputra vartmaan main patna main stith tha. nalanda vishvavidhyalay ke kulapati khud aryabhatta the, aryabhat ek ganitagya or khagol vaigyanik the. bihar kii hi paavan bhoomi par ashok, ajatshatru, bimbisar or anya bahut raajaaon kaa janm hua. azaad bharat ke pratham rashtapati doctor rajendra prashaad kaa janm bhi bihar main hi hua he, sikho ke dashave guru govid singh kaa janm bhi bihar ke rajdhani patna main hua, aaj bhi bharat ke sabase zyada aies bihar se hi nikalate hai, lekin lagaataar videshiyon ke akraman or ghatiya raajnneeti ke kaaran bihar bharat ke sabase garib or pichde rajyon main se ek ho gaya he. vishwa main shiksha ke centre kaa gaurav praapt karne vaale is rajya main sakshartaa dar anya rajyon se kam ho gai he, or rojgaar na milane ke kaaran palaayan ek bahut badi samasya ban chukaa he.
