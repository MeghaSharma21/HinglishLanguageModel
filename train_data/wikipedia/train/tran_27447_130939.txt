 mairi louis klev ek amareeki engeenior or NASA kii antariksh yatri he.
  unka janm 5 farvari 1947 ko new york ke southempton shahar main hua tha. unhone 2004 se 2007 tak vigyaan mishan nideshaalay ke nasa ke associat prashaasak ke rup main seva kii. unke mata-pita howard klev or barbara klev, donon hi shikshak the. vah great nek, neuyork main bade hue or unki 2 bahane thi, ek badi bahan trody carter or ek choti bahan barbara "bobi" klev basworth. 1965 main cleve ne great nek north hai skool, great nek, neuyork se apni skool kii shiksha praapt kii. 1969 main unhone colorado state uniwarsity se biological science main vigyaan kii degree praapt kii, or 1975 main uta state uniwarsity se microbial paristhitiki main vigyaan main masters kii degree praapt kii. 1979 main unhone uta rajya vishvavidhyalay se civil or paryaavaran ejineering main doctoret kii oopaadhi praapt kii. unhone june 1971 se 1980 tak uta state uniwarsity main paristhitiki centre or uta jal shodh prayogshaala main graduate research, research phicologist or research engeenior asin bhi yojit kiya tha.
 cleve ko may 1980 main ek antariksh yatri ke rup main chunaa gaya tha. unhone bahut se takaniki kaary kiye jiname, shatal avionix ekatrikaran prayogshaala main udaan software satyaapan; paanch antariksh shatal udaanon par capkom, prakriya pustak, kru upkaran dizin do antariksh udaanon ke ek anbhavi, unhone kul 10 din, 22 ghante, 2 minute, 24 second main antariksh main pravesh kiya, prithvi kii grahapath 172 baar kii or 3.94 mile kii yatra kii.
