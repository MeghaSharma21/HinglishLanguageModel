  sirohi pashchimi bharat main rajasthan rajya main ek shahar he. yah kaa prashasnic mukhyalay he or purv main devda chouhan rajput dwaara shasit sirohi rajya riyasat kii rajdhani thi. isamen paanch tehsil hai:abu road, shivganj, revadar, pindwada or sirohi swayan. ise devanagari bhi kaha jaataa he. sirohi ke nikatatam railway stesan sirohi road he. sirohi ko varsh 2014 main "svachh bharat abhiyaan" ke liye rajasthan ke 33 jilon main pehla sthaan mila.
 sirohi rashtriya rajmargon or rajya rajmargon ke madhyam se bharat ke sabhi shaharon se acchhee tarah se judaa hua he. areshartisi kii jaipur, udaipur, ajmer, kota, barmer, dilli, ahmedabad, surat, kalyaan sirohi central bas kii stop se ​​dainik sevaayen hai. viarel travels, emr travels, esares travels, shrinath travels jaisi vibhinn nijee company bason dwaara sirohi se bangalore or pune tak dainik sevaayen hai. nikatatam railway stesan sirohi road or abu road he. nikatatam gharelu hawaii adda udaipur or antarrashtriya hawaii adda ahmedabad he.
 sirohi 24.885 degree N 72.8625 degree E par sthit he. isaki ausat oonchai 321 meter he.
 
 sirohi kaa ek aspataal or rajasthan vishvavidhyalay se sambhddha ek sarkari mahavidhyalay he.
 sirohi nagar kii jansankhya 2001 kii janaganana ke anusaar 35,531 he or sirohi zile kii kul jansankhya 8,50,756 he.
