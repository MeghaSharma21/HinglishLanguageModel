 tandav shankar bhagwaan dwaara kiya jane vaala alokik nrity he. aisa mana jaataa he ki iske andar bhagwaan kii shaktiyaan traahi machaati hai yah nrity shiv kaali jaise dev karti hai shiv kii teesri aankh ke khulne se haahaakaar ho jaataa he.
 tandav ke sanskrit main kai arth hote hai. iske pramukh arth hai uddhat nrity karnaa, ugr karm karnaa, svachhnd hastkshep karnaa aadi. bhartiya sangeet main chaudaa pramukh talbhed main vir tathaa bibhats ras ke sammishran se bana tandavi taal kaa varnan bhi miltaa he. vanaspati shaastra main ek prakaar kii ghaas ko bhi tandav kaha gaya he. 
 shastron main pramukhataa se bhagavan shiv ko hi tandav swaroop kaa pravartak bataaya gaya he. parantu anya aagam tathaa kavy granthon main durga, ganesh, bhairav, shriram aadi ke tandav kaa bhi varnan miltaa he. lankesh ravan virachit shiv tandav stotra ke alaava vaishyavar samadhi rachit durga tandav, ganesh tandav, bhairav tandav evam shribhagavatanand guru rachit shriraghvendracharitam main ram tandav stotra bhi praapt hota he. manyata he ki ravan ke bhawan main pujan ke samaapt hone par shiv ji ne, mahisasur ko maarane ke baad durga mata ne, gajamukh kii paraajay ke baad ganesh ji ne, brahma ke pancham mastak ke chhedan ke baad adibhairav ne evam ravan ke vadh ke samay shriramchandra ji ne tandav kiya tha.
