 dandanayak pracheen bhartiya shashanyantr kaa ek adhikari tha. praaia: is padavi kaa prayog kisi senadhikari ke liye kiya jaataa tha. kintu dandanayak se taatpary sarvada kisi sainik adhikariadhikari se hi hota ho, aisi baat nahin. uskaa prayog aadhunik magistrate athwa madhyakaalin faujadaar jaise adhikariyon ke liye bhi kabhi-kabhi kiya jaataa tha. yada-kada dandanayak ke kaaryakshetra main kuch gaanvon ke samooh kii raksha kaa bhi kaary hota tha. pracheen bhartiya abhilekhon main dandanayakon ke ullekh praaia: milte hai, tathaapi unke kaaryon kaa koi nishchit swaroop bataa sakna kathin he. adhikaron kii vyapaktaa athwa badi badi senaaon ke sanchaalan ke kaarybhaar ke kaaran saadhaaran dandanayakon se bade adhikari bhi hote the, jinhen mahadandanayak, mahaprachanddandanayak athwa sarvadandanayak kaha jaataa tha.
