 vibhinn electronic madhyamon sahit paramparaagat rup se prakashit akhabaaron ko pradutt abhivyakti kii swatantraaa ko press kii swatantraaa kaha jaataa he.
 kintu is samasya kaa ek doosraa pahalu bhi he. duniyabar main media corporate ke haath main he jiskaa ekmaatr uddeshy adhik se adhik faaydaa kamanaa he. vastav main koi press swatantraaa he hi nahin. bade patrkaar motaa vetan lete hai or isi vajah se ve fansy jeevanstyli ke aadi ho gaye hai. wo ise khona nahin chahenge or isliye hi adeshon kaa paalan karte hai or talave chaatate hai.
 vibhinn deshon kii sarkarein bhi vibhinn kanoon laakar press par kaabo paanaa chaahate hai. udhaaharan ke taur par bharat sarkaar nen haal hi main online media website par nigaraanee rakhane ke liye nayaa kanoon pesh kiya he. iske tahat sarkaar online kuch bhi chhapane par niyantran karnaa chaahati he.
