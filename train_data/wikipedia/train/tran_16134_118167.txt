 ghaav padak yaa aahat medal ek bhartiya sainya sammaan he jo un sainikon ko diya jaataa he, jinhen kisi abhiyaan yaa aatank-virdohi abhiyaan main sidhe shatru se ghaav mili hon.is padak kii sthapana 1973 main bharat ke raashtrapati dwaara 15 agast, 1947 kii tithi se kii gai thi.
 bhartiya sena ke sabhi rank,yogyata kii shreniyon main shaamil hai, jinamein arakshit or pradeshik senaayen shaamil hai, or railway sangrakshan bal, police, home gards, naagarik raksha, yaa sarkaar dwaara nirdisht kisi anya sanghathan ke sadsy shaamil hai. vayuyan daste ko, jo shatrutapurna karrwai dwaara nasht kiye gaye vimaan se logo ko baahar nikalne ke dauraan, yadi choton ko rokte hai ,to unhen sammanit kiya ja saktaa he. maranoparant padak se sammanit nahin kiya ja saktaa he. baad ke puraskaron ke liye 'baar ' kaa vishisht praavdhaan kiya gaya he.
 agravarti: centre main rashtriya pratik ke saath 35 mimi paripatr rajat padak. bain ore, hindi main "aahat medal " or dahini ore angreji main "WOUND MEDAL". prishth bhaag : ek chakra ke bheetar, ashok chakra riban: 32 mimi, safed, ek 10 mimi laal kendriya pattee ke saath. safed 11 mimi, laal 10 mimi, safed 11 mimi
