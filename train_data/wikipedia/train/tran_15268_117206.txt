 vishwa ke pracheen itihaas ko jaananaa ek kathin prakriya he. us samay aadhunik vishwa ke saadhan uplabdh nahin the. lekin puraana, itivrutt, aakhyayika, udhaaharan, dharamshastra or arthshaastra main pracheen aaryon ke itihaas ko paaya ja saktaa he. kisi desh ke itihaas kaa adhyayan karne ke liye is sabhi saamagree ko do bhaagon main baanta ja saktaa he- saahitiyik tathaa puratathvik.
 saahitiyik saamagree ko pehle do bhaagon main vibhakt kiya jaataa he- dharmik sahitya tathaa ihalokaparak sahitya. isamen dharmik sahitya bhi do prakaar kaa he- brahman granth tathaa abrahman granth. ihalokaparak sahitya main aitihaasik, ardh aitihaasik, videshi vivaran, jivaniyaan, kalpana pradhan tathaa galp sahitya aate hai. puratathvik samagriyon main abhilekh, pracheen smarak or mudraayen aate hai.
