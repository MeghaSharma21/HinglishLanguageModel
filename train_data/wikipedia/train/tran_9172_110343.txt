 radioskriyata yaa radiodharmita vah prakiya hoti he jisamen ek asthir parmaanu apane nabhik se inkaree vikiran ke rup main urja phenkata he. aise padaarth jo swayan hi aisi urja nikaalte hon vikiransheel yaa radiodharmee kehalaate hai. yah vikiran alpha kan, beta kan, gamma kiran or electronon ke rup main hoti he. aise padaarth jinaki parmanvik nabhi sthir nahin hoti or jo nishchit maatra main aaweshit kanon ko chhodte hai, radiodharmee kehalaate hai.
 radioskriyata kii khoj france ke vaigyanik henry bequeral ne 1896 main kii thi. yadi yah kriya swatah hoti he, to ise prakritik radio sakriyata kehete hai, jabaki manushya ke dwaara kara jane par kritrim radio sakrita kahi jaati he. prakritik radioskriyata mukhyatah baari nabhikon se hoti he. uranium pehla khoja gaya prakritik radioskriy tatv he.
 yadhyapi pehle keval alpha, beta or gamma vikiran kii hi khoj hui thi, kintu baad main pataa chalaa ki kai anya prakaar ke utsarjan bhi hote hai.
