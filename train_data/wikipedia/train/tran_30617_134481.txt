 kotdwar uttrakhand rajya ke gadhwal mandal main sthit paudi jile kaa ek mukhya nagar he. khoh nadi ke tat par sthit yah nagar itihaas main 'khohadwar' naam se bhi janaa jaataa tha. kotdwar uttar pradesh kii seemaa se sata hua he. ise 'gadhwal kaa praveshdwar' bhi kaha jaataa he. kotdwar se dugadda or lansdon hote hue paudi tathaa srinagar tak pahunchaa ja saktaa he, tathaa kotdwar se dehradun jane ke liye uttar pradesh ke nazibabad shahar se hokar gujarata he. kotdwar se nazibabad kii duuri 25 kimi he.
 nagar kaa vikaas vaise to 1890 main rail ke aagaman se hi shuru ho gaya tha, kintu vaastavik basaavat pramukhtah 50 ke dashak main nagar palika ke nirmaan ke baad hi hui. 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar nagar kii jansankhya 33,035 thi. 2017 main uttrakhand shashan dwaara rishikesh ke saath saath kotdwar ko bhi nagar nigam ghoshit kar diya gaya. nagar nigam banane ke baad nagar kshetra main 73 graamon ko shaamil kiya gaya, tathaa isaki jansankhya 1,35,000 tak pahunch gai.
 kotdwar kaa purana nagar khoh tathaa givain shot nadiyon ke sangam par sthit tha.:134 1897 railway line ban jane ke baad nagar dhire dhire dayee ore badhane laga, or khoh nadi kii pashchimi disha kii ore viksit hone laga.:128 1901 main kotdwar ko nagar kaa darja diya gaya tha,:356 tathaa usee varsh hui pratham janaganana main nagar kii jansankhya 1029 thi. 1909 main kotdwar ko lansdon se jodne vaali sadak kaa nirmaan sampann hua.:128 iske baad lansdon tathaa dogadda nagaron ke paraspar vikaas ke kaaran kotdwar main kaaphi palaayan hua, or 1921 main kotdwar kii jansankhya maatr 396 rah gayi; jiske kaaran 1921 main hi nagar ko avargeekrut kar gram ghoshit kar diya gaya.:174 
 1940-41 main kotdwar kisi chote gaanv ke saamaan tha. dwitiya vishwa yuddh ke samay nagar kshetra main kaaphi vikaas hua, or 1949 main ise punah 'notyphoid aria' ghoshit kar diya gaya.:173 1951 main kotdwar nagar palika kii sthapana hui, or 1951-55 main nagarpalika ke pramukh ejeeniyar, ke.si. maathur ne nagar kaa pehla master plan banaaya. is master plan ke tahat sabase pehle railway stesan ke aas paas ke kshetron ko viksit kiya gaya, or uske baad nagar ko dhire dhire uttar kii ore badrinaath maarg ke donon taraf badhaayaa gaya.:173 badi masjid ke ird-gird bhi awasaiya kshetra basaaye gaye. 1955 main palika karyaalay ke sameep ek udyaan sthapit kiya gaya.:173 1958-59 main nagar kaa vidyutikaran kiya gaya.:134 
 kotdwar kii jalwaayu samshitoshna he, haalanki yah mausam ke aadhaar par badalati rahati he. paas ke pahari kshetron main aksar sardiyon main barfbaari dekhi jaati he, lekin kotdwar main taapmaana 0 degree celsius se neeche girte nahin dekha gaya he. greeshmakaleen taapmaana aksar 43 degree celsius tak pahunch jate hai, jabaki sardiyon ke taapmaana aamtaur par 4 or 20 degree celsius ke bich hote hai. monsoon ke mausam main aksar baari or lambii varsha hoti he. paas main hi sthit pahari kshetron ke kaaran sardiyon main mausam acha rahata he. bharpoor varsha or paryaapt jal nikasi ke kaaran nagar kii mitti upjau he.
 2017 main nagar kii seemaa main vistaar kar 71 graamon ko nagar kshetra main shaamil kiya gaya, jiske baad nagar kii vartmaan jansankhya lagbhag 1,35,000 ho gayi he. 2011 main hui janaganana main nagar kii jansankhya 28,859 thi. kotdwar kii kul jansankhya 33,035 he jisamen se 17,157 purush or 15,878 mahilaayein hai. kotdwar main 0 se 6 saal ke bich 4,034 bachche the, jinamein se 2,187 balak the jabaki 1,847 balikaayen thi. kotdwar nagar kaa ling anupaat 925 he, arthath kotdwar main prati 1000 purush 925 mahilaayein thi.
 2011 main kotdwar kii kul sakshartaa dar 86.29% he, jo uttrakhand kii ausat sakshartaa dar kii tulana main adhik he. jansankhya-anusaar, kul 25,024 log sakshar hai, jinamein 13,508 purush jabaki 11,516 mahilaayein hai. is prakaar nagar main purush sakshartaa dar 90.23% he, or mahila sakshartaa dar 82.08%.
 nagar main 23,306 hindu hai, jo kul jansankhya kaa 70.55% hai. isi prakaar nagar main 9,135 musalmaan, 301 isai, 215 sikh, 1 buddha, tathaa 49 jain hai. 2 log kisi anya dharm se sambandh rakhte hai, or 26 logon kaa koi dharm nahin he. 5.3% log anusuchit jati ke hai, jabaki anusuchit janajati ke log nagar kii kul aabaadi kaa 0.1% hai.
 kotdwar purvi gadhwal kshetra kaa pramukh parivahan or thok vyapaar centre rahaa he.:374 aataa mil, tel nishkarshan, printing press, chaaval ke theliyon kaa nirmaan, or plastic or rabar ke utpaada kotdwar main pramukh udyog hai.:63 
 2011 kii janaganana ke anusaar kotdwar nagar kii kul aabaadi main se 9,528 log kaary gatividhiyon main shaamil hai. 93.9% shramikon ne apane kaam ko mukhya kaary bataaya jabaki 6.1% 6 maheene se bhi kam samay ke liye aajeevika pradaan karne vaali seemaant gatividhi main shaamil the. mukhya kaaryon main lage 9,528 vyaktiyon main se 23 kisaan the, or 26 krishi majdoor the.
 agar aap kotdwar dilli se aate hai to gadhwal express or musuri express aapke liye acha vikalp he. agar aap roadways se aana chaahen to dilli se sidhi bas seva kashmiri get se har samay uplabdh he.haridwar se nazibabad ke raste yaa jiemoyu ki bason se laaldhaang ke raste ja sakate he.
