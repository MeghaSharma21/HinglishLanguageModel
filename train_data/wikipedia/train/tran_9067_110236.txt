 mithun yaa jaminai taramandal raashichakr kaa ek taramandal he. puraani khagolashastriy pustakon main ise aksar do judwaan bacchon ke rup main darshaya jaataa tha.
 mithun taramandal main satraa mukhya taare hai, haalanki vaise isamen dariinon taare sthit hai. castor or polx iske sabase roshan taare he. shaktishaalee doorabeen se dekhne par gyaat hota he ke castor vastav main chah taron kaa mandal he. polx ke ird-gird ek bada grah parikrama karata he. is taramandal main ek maqbooza naam kaa tara bhi he, jiskaa bair naam zeta jaminoram he. maqbooza kaa dravyaman hamaare suraj se do lakh guna se bhi adhik he.
