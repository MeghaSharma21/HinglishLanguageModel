 muslim tyohaar : duniya main muslim samooh, alag alag parvon ko alag alag diwason main manaate hai. har diwas kaa apana ek mahatv hota he. jaise digar dharmik samooh vishesh parv din manaate hai, thik usee tarah muslim samooh bhi anek tyohaar manaata he. maas ke anusaar dekhen to muslim tyohaar is prakaar hai.
 in ke ilaava yom al juma yani saadhaaran shuukravaar bhi "id ul mominin" yani vishvaasiyon kaa parv kahlaata he.
