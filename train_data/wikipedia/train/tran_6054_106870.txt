 puravanaspatti vigyaan bhuvaigyanik ateet ke padpon se sambandhit vigyaan he. yah padap jeevashmon athwa shailon main surakshit padap avasheshon par aadhaarit he.
 iske antargat un paudhon kaa adhyayan kiya jaataa he, jo karodon varsh purv prithvi par rahete the, par ab kahin nahin paae jate or ab fossil ban chuke hai. unke avshesh pahaad kii chattaanon, koyale kii khaanon ityadi main milte hai. chunki paudhe ke sabhi bhaag ek se jude nahin milte, isaliye har ang kaa alag alag naam diya jaataa he. inhen formginas kehete hai. puraane samay ke kaal ko bhuvaigyanik samay kehete hai. yah cambrian-purv-mahakalp se shuru hota he, jo lagbhag paanch arab varsh purv tha. us mahakalp main jeevanu shaiwal or kavak kaa janm hua hoga. doosraa peliosoik kehalaata he, jisamen kareeb 60 karod se 23 karod varsh purv kaa yug sammilit he. shuru main kuch samudri paudhe, phir parnaharith, parngaudbhid or ant main anavrutbiji paudhon kaa janm hua he. taduparant madhyajivi mahakalp shuru hota he, jo chah karod varsh purv samaapt hua. is kalp main bade bade oonche, nukili patteevale, anek anavrutbiji pedo kaa samrajya tha. jantuon main bhi atyant bhimkaay dinasor or bade bade saanp ityadi paidaa hue. sinaazoik kalp main dviviji, evam ekbiji paudhe tathaa stanadhariyon kaa janm hua.
