 kailash vaajapeyi hindi sahityakar the. unka janm hamirpur uttar-pradesh main hua. unke kavita sangrah ‘hawa main hastakshar’ ke liye sahitya academy puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya tha.
 unki pramukh kritiyon main 'sankrant', 'dehaat se hatakar', 'teesraa andhera', 'sufinama', 'bhavishya ghat rahaa he', 'hawa main hastakshar', 'shabd sansar' chuni hui kavitaayen 2004, bheetar bhi ishwar 2008 aadi shaamil hai. unhone dilli doordarshan ke liye kabir, surdaas, je krishnamurthi, ramakrishna paramhans ke jivan par filmein bhi banaai thim. vah lambey samay tak doordarshan kii hindi salaahkaar samiti ke sadsy bhi rahe.swami vivekanand ke jivan par aadhaarit naatak 'yuva sanyasi' unka prasiddh naatak he. unhone 1967 main checkoslovakia tathaa 1970 main saanskrithik vinimay kaarykram ke antargat rus, france, germany, sweden or anya europea deshon kii yatra kii.
