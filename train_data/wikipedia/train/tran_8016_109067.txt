 vishvedev se taatpary 'sabhi devataaon' se he. rugveda main anekon mantron main 'vishvadevah' kii stuti kii gai he jaise 1.89, 3.54-56, 4.55, 5.41-51, 6.49-52, 7.34-37, 39, 40, 42, 43, 8.27-30, 58, 83 10.31, 35, 36, 56, 57, 61-66, 92, 93, 100, 101, 109, 114, 126, 128, 137, 141, 157, 165, 181 aadi. rugveda ke mantra 3.54.17 main indra ko vishwedevah kaa adhyaksh kaha gaya he. 'vishwedevah' agni tathaa shraadh devtaa kaa bhi naam he or is naam kaa ek rakshas bhi hua he, par praaia: vishwedeva: un sabhi nau yaa das devataaon ke samooh ke liye aata he jinke naam veda, samhita tathaa agnipurana aadi main die gaye hai. bhagwat main inhen dharm rishi tathaa, vishwa ke putr bataaya he or inke naam daksh, vratu, vasu, kaam, satya, kaal, rochak, adrav, pururava tathaa kuraj die hai. in sabon ne raja marut ke yajna main sabhaasadon kaa kaam kiya tha.
 vartmaan manvantar main saat hi vishvedev maan gaye hai or markendeya puraana ke anusaar vishwamitra ke tiraskaar karne ke kaaran inhen draupadi ke garbh se unke paanch putron ke rup main janm lenaa or ashwatthama ke haathon maranaa padaa tha. rugveda ke kuch sookhton main vishwedeva kii stuti kii gai he or shukla yajurveda main inhen ganadevataa ke rup main mana gaya he. veda samhita main inaki sakhya keval nau he or inhen indra, agni aadi se kuch nimna shrenee kaa mana he. ye maanvon ke rakshak tathaa satkarmon ke puraskaaradaata kahe jate hai or riksanhita ke ek mantra main inhen vishwa ke adhipati kii oopaadhi di gai he.
