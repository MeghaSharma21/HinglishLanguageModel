 sanvruth gear sabase pramukh gear ruparekhaon main se ek he. is tarah kii gear main gear ke daant ek vrutt kii bahari taraf lage hote hai. sanvruth gear ko samajhane ke liye hamen do vritton ke bare main sochna hoga, jo aapas main chhu nahin rahe hai. ab ham un donon kii sparsharekha banaate hai. is tarah se ham do sparsh rekha paa sakenge. 
 ab hamen ek vrutt ko ghumana he. kalpana kijiye kii baayan gear ghadi kii disha main gum rahaa he. is tarah se ham ek sparsh rekha ko hataa sakenge. sanvrutt gear isi prakaar se chalate hai. ek gear dusre gear ko dhakka detaa he, to wo dhakka hameshaa isi sparsh rekha kii disha main hota he. 
 asli gear main in vritton ke upar daant lage hote hai, jiske kaaran ve ek dusre ko chhu pate hai or saath main gum pate hai. 
 ab hamen kuch takneekee shabd samajhane padenge :
 swar vrutt : ham apane vritton ke kendron ko sidhi rekha se jod denge. hamen ek bindu aisi milegi, jahaan par ye rekha sparsharekha ko kaat rahi he. is bindu ko swar bindu kehete hai. 
 ham ek centre par prakaar kii nok rakhkar swar bindu tak kii trigya kaa vrutt banaaenge. jab ham duusare centre ke saath aisa karenge, to hamen do swar vrutt praapt ho jaayenge. is swar vrutt kii sahaayta se ham apane gear kii koniya gati kaa anumaan laga lenge. chunki swar bindu par donon vritton kii gati samaan he 
 kha kii trigya * kha kii koniya gati = k kii trigya * k kii koniya gati
 kha kii koniya gati / k kii koniya gati=k kii trigya / kha kii trigya
 sparsh rekha or gear kendron ko jodne vaali rekha kii lamb ek rekha ke bich jo kone banataa he, use dabaav kone kehete hai. aam taur par 20 degree ke dabaav kone kaa istemaal kiya jaataa he.
