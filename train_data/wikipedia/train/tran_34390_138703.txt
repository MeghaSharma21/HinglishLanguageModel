 paramhans yoganand, beesvin sadi ke ek aadhyatmik guru, yogi or sant the. unhone apane anuyaayiyon ko kriya yoga upadesh diya tathaa puure vishwa main uskaa prachaar tathaa prasar kiya. yogaanand ke anusaar kriya yoga ishwar se saakshaatkaar kii ek prabhavi vidhi he, jiske paalan se apane jivan ko sanvaara or ishwar kii ore agrasar hua ja saktaa he. yoganand pratham bhartiya guru the jinhone apane jivan ke kaary ko pashchim main kiya. yoganand ne 1920 main america ke liye prasthaan kiya. sampurna america main unhone anek yatraayen kii. unhone apana jivan vyaakhyaan dene, lekhan tathaa nirantar vishwa vyapee kaary ko disha dene main lagaaya. unki utkrisht aadhyatmik kriti yogi kathamrita kii lakhon pratiyaa bikin or sarvada bikne vaali aadhyatmik aatmakatha rahi han.
 paramhans yoganand kaa janm mukundalaal ghosh ke rup main 5 janvari 1893, ko gorakhpur, uttarapradesh main hua.
 yoganand ke pita bhagwati charan ghosh bengal nagpur railway main upaadhyaksh ke samakaksh pad par karyrat the. yoganand apane mata pita kii chauthi santaan the. unki mata pita mahaan kriyyogi lahidi mahashay ke shishy the. 
 yukteshwar giri
