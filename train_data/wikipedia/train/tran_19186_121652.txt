 garbhashay stri jananaang he. yah 7.5 semi lambi, 5 semi chaudee tathaa isaki diwaar 2.5 semi moti hoti he. iskaa vajan lagbhag 35 gram tathaa isaki akruti naashpati ke aakaar ke jaisi hoti he. jiskaa chaudaa bhaag upar funds tathaa patala bhaag neeche isthamas kehalaata he. mahilaon main yah mutr kii thili or malashay ke bich main hoti he tathaa garbhashay kaa jhukaav aage kii ore hone par use antiverted kehete he athwa peeche kii taraf hone par ritroverted kehete he. garbhashay ke jhukaav se bachche ke janm par koi prabhaav nahin padataa he.
 garbhashay kaa upree chaudaa bhaag bari tathaa nichalaa bhaag tang bhaag gardan yaa isthamas kehalaata he. isthamas neeche yoni main jaakar khulata he. is kshetra ko aus kehete he. yah 1.5 se 2.5 semi bada tathaa thos mansapeshiyon se bana hota he. garbhavastha ke vikaas garbhashay kaa aakaar badhkar stri kii pasaliyon tak pahunch jaataa he. saath hi garbhashay kii deevaare patali ho jaati he.
 stri sharir rachana kaa saamne kaa aarekh
 garbhadhaaran ke 5-6wein saptaah main bhrun
 garbhashay ke vaisal
 mahilaon ke garbhashay kii mansapeshiyon ko prakruti ne ek adbhut kshamta pradaan kii he. iskaa vitaran do prakaar se he. pehle vitaran ke anusaar lambi mansapeshiyaan or duusare ko ghumaavdaar mansapeshiyaan kehete he. garbhavastha main garbhashay kaa vistaran tathaa bachche ke janm ke samay lambi mansapeshiyaan pramukh rup se kaary karti he. ghumaavdaar mansapeshiyaan bachche ke janm ke baad garbhashay ko sankuchit tathaa rakt ke bahaav ko rokane main pramukh bhoomika nibhaati he.
