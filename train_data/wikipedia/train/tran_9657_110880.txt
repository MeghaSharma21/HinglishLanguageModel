 faraghna praant madhya asia main sthit uzbekistan desh kaa ek vilaayaat he jo us desh ke sudur purvi bhaag kii faraghna ghati main sthit he. praant kaa kul kshetrafal 6,800 varg kimi he or 2005 main isaki anumaanit aabaadi 25,97,000 thi. isaki rajdhani faraghna shahar he or is rajya main aitihaasik khokand shahar bhi sthit he.
 'faraghna' main 'ga' akshar ke uccharan par dhyaan den kyonki yah bina bindu vaale 'ga' se zaraa bhinn he. iskaa uccharan 'galati' or 'garib' shabdon ke 'ga' se miltaa he.
