 "meraa padosi" franj kafka kii ek laghu kahani he jise 1917 main likhaa gaya tha or 1931 main berlin main Max Brod or hans-jokim Schoeps dwaara prakashit kiya gaya tha. Willa or Edwin Muir ke dwaara dwaara pehla angreji anuwaad martin Secker dwaara landan main 1933 main prakashit kiya gaya tha. yah, china kii mahaan diwaar, kahaniyon or pratibimb main chhapi thi.
 kahani kaa mukhya charitra ek yuva vyapaaree he jo shuru main aatm aashwaasan kaa dikhaava karata he, lekin apane nae padosi or sambhav pratiyogi hairas se khud ko khatre main paata he.
