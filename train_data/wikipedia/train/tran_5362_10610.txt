 shrutakewali, shrutagyaan arthath shastron ke purna gyaata hote hai. shrutakewali or kevali, gyaan kii drushti se donon samaan hai, lekin shrutagyaan paroksh or keval gyaan pratyaksh hota he. kevliyon ko jitna gyaan hota he uske atanwein bhaag kaa ve prarupan kar sakate hai or jitna ve prarupan karte hai uskaa anantvaan bhaag shastron main sankalit kiya jaataa he. isliye kevalgyaan se shrutagyaan ananthaven bhaag kaa bhi anantvaan bhaag he. shrutakewali 14 purvon ke paathi hote hai. mahavir swami ke nirvana ke pashchaat gautam, sudharma or jambuswami, ye teen kevali hue. jambuswami ke baad digambar parampara ke anusaar vishnu, nandi, aparajit, govardhan or bhadrabahu tathaa shwetambar parampara ke anusaar prabhav, shayyambhav, vashobhadra, sabhootvijay, bhadrabahu or sthulbhadra naam ke chah shrutakewali hue. sthulbhadra ko shrutakevliyon main na ginaney se shwetambar sampradaay ke anusaar paanch hi shrutakewali maane gaye hai.
