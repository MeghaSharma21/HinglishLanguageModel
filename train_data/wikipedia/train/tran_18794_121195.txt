 bombai tokies, 2013 main pradarshit bollywood kii prayogdharmi film he. vycom moshan pikchars or flying unicon antertenment kii film yah film bombai tokies bhartiya cinema ke sau varsh puure hone ke uplakshya main banaai gai he. yah film chaar alag-alag nirdeshkon dwaara nirmit kii gai he or prayek nirdeshak dwaara paraspar ek duusare se bhinn bhaag banaaye gayi hai.
 chunki film chaar bhinn nirdeshkon dwaara chaar bhinn bhaagon main nirmit kii gayi he aapas main ek duusare se juda hai. ye bhaag nimna prakaar hai:
 karan johar kii kahani ek shaadishuda jode, dev or gayatri ke ird gird gumti he. ek din gayatri ko pataa chalata he ki uskaa pati samalangik he.
 dibaakar banerjee kii short film sathyajith re kii ek laghukatha par aadhaarit he. ye kahani ek saadhaaran vyakti kii he jise ek top star ke saath do minute kaa ek seen karne kaa mauka miltaa he.
 zoya akhtar kii kahani ek chote bachche viki kii he. uske pita use footbal sikhaanaa chaahate hai. lekin viki ko katrina caff kii tarah behtareen dance karne kaa shauk he.
 anuraag kashyap kii film, ek prashansak kii apane chahete sitare ke prati junun kii kahani he.
 
 film ke sur amit trivedi tathaa bol amitabh bhattacharya or swanand kirakire dwaara diya gaya he.
