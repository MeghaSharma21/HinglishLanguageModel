 bhartiya prabandh santhaan, ranchi jharkhand ke ranchi main sthit ek saarvajanik vyapaar vidhyalay he. iskaa udghaatan 06 july 2010 ko kiya gaya tha.
 bhartiya prabandh sansthaanon dwaara prastut prabandhan shiksha main utkrushtataa kii parampara ko aage badhaate hue aathven bhartiya prabandh santhaan kii sthapana varsh 2010 main ranchi kii gari. yah bharat sarkaar ke maanav sansaadhan matraalay ke adheen kaam karte hue bhartiya prabandh santhaan, kolkata tathaa jharkhand sarkaar kii vyapak sahayog se sambhav hua. 
 vartmaan main bhartiya prabandh santhaan, ranchi mukhya kaarykram ke rup main do varshiya snatakottar prabandhan diploma sanchalit kar rahi he. paathyakramon ko saavdhaneepurvak dizin kiya gaya he tathaa ise bhartiya prabandhan santhaan, kolkata eksarlarari, jamshedpur ke vishwakhyati praapt shikshakon dwaara padhaayaa jaataa he.
