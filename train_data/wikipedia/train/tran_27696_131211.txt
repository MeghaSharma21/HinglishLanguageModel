 lats latvia kii mudra he. ise laghu rup main Ls ke rup main pradarshit kiya jaataa he. lats 100 centami se vibhajya he.
 lats pehali baar 1922 main latvian rublis ke sthaan par ek lats = 50 rublis kii dar par jari kiye gaye the. 1940 main latvia ke soviet sangh main vilay hone ke baad rubal ne samaan dar par lats kaa sthaan le liya.
 sikke 1, 2, 5, 10, 20 or 50 centimu, 1, 2 or 5 laati moolyavarg main jari kiye gaye the. ek lats or usase upar mulya varg ke sikke chaandi ke bane hue the.
 1922 se latvian banknote 20, 25, 50, 100 or 500 latu moolyavarg ke jari kiye gaye. iske alaava 10 latu ke note bhi jari kiye gaye, jo 500 rubali note ke upar nae moolyavarg main mudrit the. 1925 se sarkaar ne 10 or 20 latu moolyavarg ke note jari kiye.
 lats 1993 main latvian rublis ke sthaan par ek lats = 200 rubali kii dar par punah jari kiye gaye, jise latvia ke swatantra hone ke baad kuch samay ke liye istemaal kiye gaye.
 sikke 1 centimes, 2 or 5 centimi, 10, 20 or 50 centimu or 1 lats or 2 letu moolyavarg main jari kiye gaye. iske alaava sangraahakon ke liye 2, 10, 20, 100 latu moolyavarg ke sone or chaandi ke sikke hai.
 latvia ek may 2004 se europea sangh kaa or europea sangh ke aarthik or maudrik sangh kaa sadsy he, lekin sangh kii tisare daur ko puraa nahin kar paaya he. lats iaarem II main he or kendriya dar se 1% ke andar sthir he. latvia ne euro ko aadhikarik mudra ke rup main 1 janvari 2008 ko apana lene kii pehle pahal yojana banaai thi, lekin vahaan 1 janvari 2013 se aadhikarik taur par sweekrut kiya gaya.
