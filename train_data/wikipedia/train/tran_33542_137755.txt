 sanyukt tufaan chetavanee kendra america nausena or vaayu sena kaa sanyukt tufaan chetavanee kendra he. yah hawaii naamak sthaan par sthit he. ise america suraksha vibhaag or anya sarkari vibhaagon ke liye banaaya gaya he. isse yah sena or uske saamaanon jaise jahaaj aadi ko apane va anya deshon main bhi surakshit rakh saken. 
 is centre ke nirmaan kii aavashyaktaa june 1945 main shuru hua, jab guam dweep main aandhi tufaan kaa pataa lagane hetu ek kendra banaaya gaya tha. kyonki isse pehle vahaan kai saare tufaan aa chuke the. jisamen tufaan cobra bhi tha, jiske kaaran disambar 1944 or june 1945 main kai logon kii maut ho gai thi or jahaajon ko bhi kaaphi nuksaan hua tha. is samay is kendra main teen nausena or do vaayu sena ke log ise sanchalit kar rahe the. kuch varshon main kai baar kendron ke madhya sanchaar nahin ho pataa tha, jiskaa kaaran sanchaar vyavastha kaa sahi na hona tha. varsh 1958 main sanyukt raashtra ke mausam raksha vibhaag ne ek sanyukt mausam vigyaan samiti kaa nirmaan kiya, jisane prashanth mahaasaagar par najar rakhane ke liye ek sanyukt nausena or vaayu sena kendra ke gathan kaa prastaav rakhaa.
