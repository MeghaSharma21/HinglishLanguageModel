 digvijay singh ek bhartiya rajaneta, madhyapradesh rajya ke purv mukhyamantri or bhartiya rashtriya congress ke netaa he. vartmaan main is party main mahasachiv ke pad par he.
 digvijay singh kaa janm raghogarh main saamanti parivaar main hua tha. raghogarh, gwalior rajya ke adheen ek rajya tha.
 digvijay ne prambhik shiksha daily college indore se praapt kii. iske baad shri govindaram secosaria praudyogiki evam vigyaan santhaan, indore se hi ejineering ki degree praapt kii. digvijay sakriy raajnneeti main 1971 main aaye, jab vah raghogarh nagarpalika adhyaksh bane. 1977 main congress tikat par chunaav jeet kar raggarh vidhaan sabha kshetra se vidhaan sabha sadsy bane. 1978-79 main digvijay ko pradesh yuva congress kaa mahasachiv banaaya gaya. 1980 main waapas raggarh se chunaav jeetne ke baad digvijay ko arjuna singh mantrimandal main rajyamantri kaa pad diya gaya tathaa baad main krishi vibhaag diya gaya. 1984, 1992 main digvijay ko loksabhaa chunaav main vijay mili. 1993 or 1998 main inhone madhya pradesh ke mukhyamantri pad kii shapath lee.
