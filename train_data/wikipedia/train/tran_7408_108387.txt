 da walking ded ek america antarbhaasi samay ke baad kii television shrunkhlaa he. shrunkhlaa kaa sarjan frank darabont ne kiya he. robert karkaman, tony moore or charlie adlard nirmit comic book shrunkhlaa da walking ded par aadhaarit he.
 shrunkhlaa main andrew linkan sheriff dipti rik graimes kaa kirdaar nibhaate hai. rik achetan avasthaa se uthne ke pashchaat paata he ki duniya par "wankeras" kaa prabhutv he. ye wankeras jorge e rosemeri kii horer filmon ke zombiyon se mail khate hai. uthne ke pashchaat rik apane parivaar or anya jivit bache logon ko khojane main lag jaataa he.
 da walking ded kaa america main premier oktober 31, 2010 ko cabel television channel eemasi par hua tha. pratham satr ko mili safalata ke pashchaat channel ne shrunkhlaa ko iske duusare satr ke liye naveekrut kara jisamen 13 prakaran the tathaa iskaa premier oktober 16, 2011 ko hua. duusare satr ke khatm hone se do prakaran pehle eemasi ne ghoshnaa kari ki shrunkhlaa tisare satr ke liye waapas aegi. 
 shrunkhlaa ko aalochakon dwaara acchhee tarah praapt kiya gaya he tathaa isane kai puruskaaron ke namakan bhi praapt kiye hai jinamein riters gild of america award or golden globe puraskaar main sarvshereshth television shrunkhlaa drama bhi shaamil hai. shrunkhlaa ne majboot nilsan reating bhi praapt kii hai tathaa cabel shrunkhalaaon ke liye vibhinn record bhi tode hai. inamen se hi ek record duusare satr ke antim prakaran ne banaaya jisko 9 million darshakon ne dekha tha jo itihaas main sabase adhik dekhe jane vaala moolbhoot cabel drama prasaaran ban gaya.
