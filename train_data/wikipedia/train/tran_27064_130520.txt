 mongake khan mongol samrajya kaa chauthaa kagaan tha. usne 1 july 1251 se 11 agast 1259 main hui apni mrityu tak shashan kiya. mongake khan mongol samrajya se sansthaapak changez khan kaa pota or uske sabase chote bete tolui khan kaa betaa tha. uski mata sorboogtani beki ne use or uske bhaaiyon ko bahut nipunata se paala or parvaarik paristhitiyon par aisa niyantran rakhaa ki mongake mongol samrajya kaa kagaan ban sakaa. mongake ke netritv main mongolon ne iraq or syria par akraman karke unhen apane raaj kaa hissaa banaaya. aadhunik vietnam or naanjhaao rajya bhi mongolon ke adheen laayaa gaya. mongke kii mrityu ke baad uskaa bhai kubalai khan samrajya kaa aglaa kagaan bana.
