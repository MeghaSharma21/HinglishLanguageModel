 israel kii samskruti vividh or gatyaatmak he jisamen pashchimi saanskrithik prabhaav ke saath-saath purvi sanjateey or dharmik paramparaon kaa sanshleshan sanyojit he. 1948 main aadhunik israel raashtra kii sthapana se bahut pehle hi israeli samskruti kii jaden viksit ho chuki thim. isamen pravasi yahudiyon ke itihaas, 19veen sadi ke antim varshon main shuru hue zeonist andolan kii vichaardhaara ke saath-saath arab israeli aabaadi va anya alpsankhyakon ke itihaas or paramparaon kii jhalak dikhti he.
 tel aviv or jerusalem ko israel ke mukhya saanskrithik kendra mana jaataa he. neuyork times ne tel aviv ko "shant bumadhya kii rajdhani" varnit kiya gaya he, lonly planet ne raat ke jivan ke liye ise sheersh das shaharon main sthaan diya he or neshnal jeographic ne ise sheersh das samudr tat ke shaharon main se ek kaha he.
 israel main 200 se adhik sangrahalay hai jo duniya main prati vyakti sangrahalayon kii sankhya ke anusaar sabase adhik he. in sangrahalayon main salaana lakhon aagatuk aate hai. mukhya kala sangrahalay tel aviv, jerusalem, hifa or herzaliya main hai. israel philharmonic orcestra desh bhar ke aayojan sthalon ke saath-saath vishwa ke kai anya deshon main apni sangeet kala kii pradarshnee karata he. desh ke lagbhag har shahar ke apane orcestra hai or zyaadaatar sangeetkaaron kii prishthbhumi purv soviet sangh he. israel ke paaramparik lok nrityon main hora or yemenite nrity shaamil hai. israel kii aadhunik nrity companiyaan jaise batasheva dance company duniya bhar main atyadhik prashansit hai. rashtriya theatre habima 1917 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. israeli film nirmaataaon or abhinaytaon ne kai antarrashtriya film samarohon main puraskaar jite hai. 1980 ke dashak ke baad se israeli sahitya kaa vyapak rup se anuwaad kiya gaya he or kai israeli lekhakon ko antarrashtriya manyata hasil hui he.
