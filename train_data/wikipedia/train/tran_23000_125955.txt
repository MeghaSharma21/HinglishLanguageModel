 malawi kii chishenga basti se mafingaa pahaado kaa drishy
 mafingaa pahadiyaan aqrika ke dakshini bhaag main sthit zambia va malawi deshon kii seemaa par vistrut ek pathaar he jisapar kai pahadiyaan bhi sthit hai. is pathaar kaa sabase unchaa bindu mafingaa central kehalaata he or 2,339 meter kii oonchaai vaala yah sthaan zambia kaa sabase unchaa bindu bhi ho saktaa he .
