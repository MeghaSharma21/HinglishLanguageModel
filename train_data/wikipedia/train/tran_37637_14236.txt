 nival aay yaa shuddh laabh ek prakaar kaa aay yaa laabh hota he, jo kisi vastu ko bechney se vikray karne vaale ko kray karne vaale vyakti dwaara diya jaataa he. jisamen vikray karne vaale ke kul kharch ko uske kul mili hue paison se hataaya jaataa he to yah aay milti he.
 saamanytah yah aay kar dene ke baad bachane vaale paison ko kehete hai. jise hamen kisi ko bhi nahin dena hota he. yah ham chahen to rakh bhi sakate hai. yaa ise kisi or ko bhi de sakate hai. is par puraa niyantran use kamaane vaale kaa hi hota he.
