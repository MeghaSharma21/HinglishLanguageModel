 sura ek arabi shabd he. iskaa aksharshah arth he "koi vastu, jo kisi diwaar yaa badah se ghiri he". yah shabd quran ke adhyay ke liye prayog hota he, jo ki parampara anusaar kam hoti lambaai ke kram se likhe hai. prayek sura kaa naam ek shabd par he, jo us sura ke ayaah main ullekith he. kuch sura muslimon ke liye apane rahasyodghatan kaal main vismayakari the. udhaahrantah ishu messiah kii maan - mariyam kaa ati ucch str, jo ki isiyat kii maan thim - jaisaa sura 19 main ullekith he.
