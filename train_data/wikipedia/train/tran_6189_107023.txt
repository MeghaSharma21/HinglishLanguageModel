 nirdeshaank: 34°05′N 74°47′E / 34.09°N 74.79°E / 34.09; 74.79
 burzhome, srinagar kaa puratathvik mahatv vaala kashmir kaa pramukh aitihaasik sthal he. yahaan par kai pracheen bhumigat gadde paaen jate hai. in gaddhon ko chapparon se dhank kar unamem rahete the. yahaan ke gharon kaa nirmaan jamini str se upar eent or gaare se bhi kiya jaataa tha. yahaan ke log kheti bhi kar rahe the or prayek ghar main kuta paala hua tha. burjahome main maalik ke mar jane par kute ko bhi saath hi dafnaaya jaataa tha. yahaan se haddi ke vishisht aujaar, ytaakar chhidrit pathar ke chaaku, bartan, pashu kankal or upkaran bhi praapt hue hai. isi tarah kashmir ke gufakaraal main bhi pashupaalan or krishi karne ke pramaan milte he. yah kashmir kii ghati main srinagar se chah mile uttar-purv kii ore sthit he. yah shalimaar baag ke uttar-pashchim disha main padataa he. sthaneey bhashaa main burzhome ko 'place of birch' kaha jaataa he.
