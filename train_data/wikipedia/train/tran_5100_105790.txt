 pani se santrupt bhubhag ko ardrabhumi kehete hai. kai bhubhag varmbhar ardra rahete hai or anya kuch vishesh mausam main. jaivvividhta kii drushti se ardrabhumiyaan antyant samvedansheel hoti hai. vishesh prakaar kii vanaspatiyaan hi ardrabhumi par ugane or phalne-phoolne ke liye anukulit hoti he.
 iran ke ramsar shahar main 1971 main paarit ek abhismay ke anusaar ardrabhumi aisa sthaan he jahaan varsh main aath maah pani bharaa rahata he. ramsar abhismay ke antargat vaishvik str par vartmaan main kul 1929 se adhik ardrabhumiyaan hai..
 bharat sarkaar main shushk bhoomi ko bhi ramsar ardrabhumiyon ke antargat hi shaamil kiya he. vartmaan main bharat main kul 26 ramsar ardrabhumiyaan adhishuchet hai. bharat dwaara 2010 main 38 naye ardrabhumiyon ko shaamil karne ke liye chihnit kiya gaya he. ramsar ardrabhumi ke register montreks records ke tahat un ardrabhumiyon ko shaamil kiya jaataa he, jo khatre main hai athwa aa sakati hai. iske anusaar bharat main keoladev or loktak jhil khatre main padi ardrabhumiyaan hai. chilka jhil ko is record se baahar kar diya gaya he.
 varsh 2011 main bharat sarkaar ne ardrabhumi sangrakshan or prabandhan adhinium 2010 kii adhisuchana jari kiya he. is adhinium ke tahat ardrabhumiyon ko nimnlikhit chah virgon main baanta gaya he. 
 is adhinium ke tahat kendriya ardrabhumi viniamak praadhikaran kii sthapana kii gayi he. is praadhikaran main adhyaksh sahit kul 12 sadsy honge. isi adhinium ke tahat 38 nayi ardrabhumiyaan pahachaani gayi hai.
