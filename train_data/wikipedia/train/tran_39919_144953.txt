 bengal sultanate 1352 se 1576 san tak rajya karne vaala ek turki mul kaa ek muslim rajvansh or bhartiya upmahadeep par muslim satta sthapit karne ke dauraan nirmit ek rajya tha. yah bengal main pehla ekikrut or swatantra muslim rajya bhi tha jiske adheen is ilaake ko bengalah naam se ek alag pahchaan mili. in myanon main ise aadhunik bengal kaa purvavarti kah sakate hai.
