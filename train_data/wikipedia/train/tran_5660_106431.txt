 isisi cricket haul of fame samooh yaa suchi he jiskaa uddeshy cricket ke itihaas se diggaj khilaadiyon kii uplabdhiyon ko pahchaan kar unhen sammanit karnaa he. yah samooh antarrashtriya cricket parishad  ne federation of international cricketrs association ke sahyog se shuru kiya gaya he. shuruyaat main isamen 55 khilaadi shaamil the. isisi awords samaaroh ke dauraan prayek varsh aage ke sadasyon ko joda jaataa he. shuru kii suchi main dablyoo ji gres jo 1899 main ritair hue the or graham gooch jaise khilaadi maujuud the jinhone 1995 main aakhri test khelaa tha. anya deshon ke khilaadiyon kii tulana main haul of fem main adhik angrej khilaadi hai. bairi richards ne apane karier ke dauraan chaar ke saath sabase kam test match khelen. jabaki aktubar 2009 main shaamil australian steve wa ne 168 ke saath sabase adhik test khelen. haul of fame main chah mahilaayein khilaadi bhi shaamil hai.
