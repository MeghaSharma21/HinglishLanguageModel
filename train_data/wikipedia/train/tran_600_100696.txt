 i-mitra, rajasthan sarkaar kii ek mahatvakankshi i-shashan pahal he, jisame sarkaar kii vibhinn sevaaon kaa laabh lene ke liye nagarikon ko suvidha or paardarshita hetu rajya sarkaar dwaara saarvajanik-nijee bhagidaari model kaa upyog karke rajya ke sabhi 33 jilon main karyanvit kiya ja rahaa he.
 i-mitra rajasthan sarkaar ne sarkaar ke vibhinn kaaryon kaa phayda uthaane ke liye sabhi 33 zilon main online tathaa ofline kaary karne ke liye banaai gayi ek i-gavarnence he. i-mitra main mulnivaas ,jati pramaan patr ,janm pramaan patr ,rashan card bhamashah card ityadi banaaye tathaa sudhaare jate he. inke alaava i-mitra se pani kaa bill, bijli kaa bill, mobile tathaa television online arthath sidhe hi bhar sakate he. 
 i-mitra kaa praathmik uddeshy sabhi vibhaagon se ek chat ke neeche janata ko ek kushal, paardarshee, suvidhajanak or maitreepurn tareeke se ekikrut naagarik sevaayen pradaan karnaa he naagarik i-mitra kiosk yaa internet ke madhyam se vibhinn sevaaon kaa laabh uthaa sakate hai.
 panjikaran idi: panjikaran idi prayek aise naagarik ke liye ek adwitiya panjikaran sankhya he jo net-aadhaarit or kiosk i-mitra sarwar kaa upyog karne ke liye taiyaar he.internet dwaara i-mitra ke aavedan ke liye pehali baar kisi bhi naagarik ke liye mail dwaara adhishuchet kiya gaya. kiosk main pehali baar panjikrut hone par naagarik ko avgat karaayaa gaya.kisi bhi naagarik ko internet ke madhyam se kisi bhi seva kaa laabh lene ke liye panjikaran idi anivaary he.
 kiosk main panjikaran kii kya aavashyaktaa he?
 pehali baar kiosk / internet par i-mitra dwaara kii jane vaali kisi bhi seva kaa laabh uthaane ke liye, ek naagarik ko panjikaran karnaa hoga.
 note: i-mitra main panjikrut har naagarik ke paas ek adwitiya panjikaran idi he. ek naagarik dwaara praapt kii jane vaali prayek seva ko ek adwitiya token idi dwaara acces kiya jaataa he.
