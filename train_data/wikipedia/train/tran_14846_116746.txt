 misoarkian mahakalp prithvi ke bhuvaigyanik itihaas main orkian eon kaa ek mahakalp tha, jo aaj se 320 karod varsh pehle armbh hua or 280 karod varsh pehle ant hua. prithvi par paye gayi sabase pracheen stromatolite jeevashm isi yug main utpann hue the. prithvi ke vayumandal main carbon dioxide kii sandrata vartmaan se kuch guna adhik thi jis kaaran greenhouse prabhaav adhik shaktishaalee tha. suraj aaj kii tulana main 70% kam kantimaan tha, isliye is greenhouse ugrata ke baavjood taapmaana aaj se zyaada adhik nahin tha. is mahakalp se pehle peleoarkian mahakalp chal rahaa tha or iske baad nioarkian mahakalp armbh hua.
