 bhurishrevas somadatt kaa putr tha or kauravon tathaa pandavon kaa chachaa tha. uskaa janm satyaki ko parrast karne ke liye hua tha. apane bhai shall ke saath usne kai yoddhaon se yuddh kiya. bhurishrevas ne satyaki ke 10 putron kaa vadh kiya or phir satyaki ko bhi parrast kiya. 
 shall kaa vadh sahadev ke putr shrutakarman ne kiya. 14 wein din ke yuddh main bhurishrevas ke baaju uske bhattije arjuna dwaara kaat die gaye or phir satyaki ne uskaa vadh kiya.
