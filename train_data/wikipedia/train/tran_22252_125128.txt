 kasarpal bharat ke goa rajya ke uttar goa jile ke bicholim kshetra kaa ek kasbaa he. yah mapsa kasbe se 14 kimi kii duuri par sthit he.
 is gram kaa mul naam pallika he jo varsh 1436 ke ek tamra-patr shilaalekh par ankit he jo goa puraatatv vibhaag ke adhikaar main he. is sthaan ko kasarpal ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa tha kyonki yaha ke mul nivasee tamrakaar the. is gaanv ko baad main kisi nagdev naamak brahman ne shetiyon ko uphaar-swaroop de diya tha. kasarpal 800 varsh puraane kalkidevi mandir kaa sthal bhi he. iske atirikt yahan shivaji or bhoomika devi ke mandir bhi hai.
