 midzu bhashaayen yaa dakshini mishmi bhashaayen tibeti-burmi bhashaa-parivaar kii ek prastaavit shakha he jisaki boliyon ko arunachal pradesh or dakshinpurvi tibet ke kaman mishmi samudaay main bolaa jaataa he. isaki do mukhya bhashaayen hai: kaman or zekhring .
 sambhav he ki zekhring ek tibeti-burmi bhashaa ho jabaki kaman ek bhashaa viyojak ho. blench ne 2015 main yah bhi sambhaavna prakat kii he ki shaayad kaman or zekhring donon bhashaa viyojak hai jinamein shabdon kii samanta aapas main len-dein se ho gai he. unke anusaar sambhav he ki zekhring ne pehle kaman or tibetai bhashaaon se or phir naga bhashaaon or jingpo se shabd liye hai. yah sthapit ho chukaa he ki donon bhashaayen uttari mishmi bhashaaon se sameepi sambandh nahin rakhti.
