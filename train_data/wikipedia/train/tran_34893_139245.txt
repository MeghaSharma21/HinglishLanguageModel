 bhartiya chandra anveshan kaarykram jise chandrayan kaarykram ke rup main bhi janaa jaataa he. chandrayan kaarykram, dwaara bahya antariksh mishan kii ek shrinkhalaa he. is kaarykram main chandramaa ke liye kakshiyan or bhavishya chandra lander or rover antariksh yaan ko shaamil kiya gaya.
 chandrayan kaarykram ek bahu mishan kaarykram he. tathaa ab tak keval ek hi kakshiyan isro ke pieselvi rocket kaa upyog chandramaa ke liye bheja gaya. tathaa duusari antariksh yaan ko 2018 main jieselvi rocket kaa upyog prakshepan ke liye taiyaar kiya ja rahaa he.
 pehle charan main pratham chandra kakshiyan kaa shubharambh shaamil kiya gaya.
 duusare charan kii taiyaari kii ja rahi he. jisame chandramaa par landing karne main sakasham antariksh yaan ko shaamil kiya jaayegaa. tathaa chandramaa kii sataha par robot rover tainaat kiye jaaenge.
