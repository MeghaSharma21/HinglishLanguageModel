 beemaa viniamak or vikaas praadhikaran bharat sarkaar kaa ek praadhikaran he. iskaa uddeshy beemaa kii palsi dharakon ke hiton ki raksha karnaa, beemaa udyog kaa krambaddh viniman, sanvardhan tathaa sambadhit va aakasmik maamalon par kaary karnaa he. iskaa mukhyalay hyderabad main he. isaki sthapana sansad ke adhinium aiardie adhinium, 1999 dwaara kii gai thi.
 yah praadhikaran viniyamon ko jari kar rahi he jisamen beemaa agenton, vileyata laabh, pun: beemaa, bimakartaon kaa panjikaran, gramin or saamaajik kshetra ke bimakartaon ke daayitv, lekhankan kii prakriyaaon aadi sahit beemaa udyog ko lagbhag sampoorn bhaag shaamil he. 
 beemaa viniamak vikaas praadhikaran upabhokta ke hiton ko sunishchit karne ke liye beemaa companiyon kaa nirikshan karti he. vah aiardie adhinium, 1999 kii dhara 14 dwaara pradutt shaktiyon ke anusaar, sabhi companiyon kaa maukey par or maukey ke alaava nirikshan karata he. aiardie maukey ke alaava nirikshanon ke madhyam se unke shodh kshamta maamalon or vittiya rirpoting manadadon kii niyamit nigaraanee karata he.
 aiardie ne beemaa companiyon ke karyaalayon kaa sthal par nirikshan karne ke liye ek nirikshan vibhaag kii alag se sthapana kii he. sthal par nirikshanon ke dauraan, aiardie, beemaa companiyon ke macirt sanchaalan, parichaalan ke tareeke or abhishasan ke mandandon sahit unke sanvidhik praavdhaanon or viniamak nirdeshon ke anupaalan kii seemaa kaa nirikshan karata he. 
 aiardie par beemaa policy dharakon ke hiton kii raksha karne kii zimmedaari he. is uddeshy ko hasil karne ke liye, praadhikaran ne nimnlikhit upaay kiye hai :-
 beemaa udyog main pehle 26% videshi nivesh tha ise badha kar 49% kiya gaya tha. vartmaan main videshi nivesh kaa praavdhaan 100% kar diya gaya he.
