 tribhuvan anterrashtriya vimaanashetra ) kathmandu, nepal kaa anterrashtriya vimaanashetra he. yah nepal kaa ekmaatr anterrashtriya vimaanashetra he or yahaan ek antardeshiy tathaa ek anterrashtriya terminal hai. yah vimaanashetra kathmandu ghati main based shahar ke kendra se lagbhag 6 ki.me dur sthit he. yahaan radison hotel, kathmandu pratham evam bijnes shrenee yatri lounj kaa sanchaalan karata he evam kuch vayusevaon tathaa star ellins gold card dharakon ke liye thai airways bijnes shrenee kaa lounj sanchalit karata he. haal hi main hue anterrashtriya terminal ke ek vistaar ke kaaran vayusevaon ko jane vaali duuri kam ho gayi he. vartmaan main lagbhag 30 vayusevaon dwaara nepal ko asia, europe tathaa madhya purv ke kai gantavyon se joda jaataa he. 
 is vimaanashetra kaa aarambh gauchar vimaanashetra ke naam se hua tha, jo kathamandu kaa ek kshetra tha, jahaan yah vimaanashetra bana tha. nepal main aadhikarik rup se vimaanan kaa aarambh 1949 main ek lone 4-seater bichakraft bonanza vimaan ke avataran se hua tha. isamen ek bhartiya rajdoot savaar the. gauchar evam kolkata ke bich pehali charter udaan himalayan aviation ke ek dacota vimaan main 20 farvari 1950 ko chali thi.
 1955 main vimaanashetra kaa udghaatan maharaj mahendra dwaara kiya gaya or ise punarnamakaran kar maharaj ke pita ke naam par tribhuvan vimaanashetra kar diya gaya. vimaanashetra kaa naam punah 1964 main badalakar vartmaan tribhuvan anterrashtriya vimaanashetra kar diya gaya. 1957 main mul ghaas udanpattee ko kancreet se banaaya gaya or 1967 main 3,750 feet se badhaakar lambaai 6,600 feet kar di gayi. 1975 main punah udanpattee kii lambaai 6,600 feet se badhaakar 10,000 feet kii gayi.
 1967 main pratham jet vimaan kii udaan jo tribhivan vimaanashetra main avatarit hui lufthansa kii ek bowing 707 thi jo 6,600 feet udanpattee par utari thi. nepal airlines corporation ne apana jet prachaalan 1972 main bowing 727 vimaan se aarambh kiya.
