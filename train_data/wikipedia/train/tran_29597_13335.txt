 namakeen 1982 main pradarshit hone vaali hindi film he jise gulzaar ne nirdeshit kiya tha. namakeen, bangla sahityakar samaresh basu kii kahani par aadhaarit he.
 himachal pradesh ke ek chote se parvatiy gaanv main ek trek driver gerulaal aata he. sthaneey dhabas vaala dhaniram gerulaal ko ek vriddha jyoti ke ghar par ek cameraa kiraaze par dilava detaa he. jyoti kii teen betiyaan hai- sabase badi... nimaki, manzali... mitthu or sabase choti chinaki .
 dhire dhire gerulaal, chaaron mahilaon ke najadik aa jaataa he or ghar ke sadsy jaisaa ho jaataa he. vah nimaki se prem karne lagata he parantu vah shaadi karne se inkaar kar detii he or gerulaal se kahati he ki use mitthu se shaadi kar leni chaiye. gerulaal ko vahaan se janaa padataa he.
 kuch baras biit jate hai or ek baar gerulaal kii mulaakaat nautanki main naachne vaali stri se hoti he. yah chinaki he jo ab apane pita kishanlaal ke saath rahati he. chinaki se gerulaal ko pataa chalata he ki pichley teen barason main nimaki or bahanon kii koi khoj khabar na lekar usne galati kii he.
 vah gaanv main jaataa he to nimaki ko akele rahete paata he. jyoti or mitthu, donon hi mar chuki hai. gerulaal nimaki ko apane saath le aata he.
 film ko aar di burman ke achche sangeet ke liye bhi janaa jaataa he.
 rahul dev burman
