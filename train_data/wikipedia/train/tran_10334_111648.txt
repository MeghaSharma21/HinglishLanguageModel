 huran jhil sanyukt rajya america kii ek jhil he. uese kii badi jhiilon main iskaa superior jhil ke baad doosraa sthaan he. michigan or eri jhiilon ke bich sthit yah 400 kilometre lambii evam 248 kimi chaudee he. iskaa kshetrafal 58,880 varg kimi he. is jhil kaa 34,008 varg kimi bhaag canada main padataa he. huran jhil kaa sabase gaharaa bhaag 227 me. he. superior evam michigan jhiilon se pani huran jhil main aata he tathaa saint cleyar nadi, saint cleyar jhil evam ditrit nadi main se hokar iskaa pani iri jhil main chalaa jaataa he. huran jhil main april se lekar disambar tak jalayaan chalaa karte hai. iri, superior evam michigan jhiilon ke bandargahon se vyapaar hota he. vyapaar kii mukhya vastuen louhakhanij, anaaj, chuunaapatthar evam koyala hai. racoport evam rogers city pashchimi tat par mukhya bandargah hai jahaan bade-bade jalayaan chale aate hai. iskaa pani bahut svachh he or anek prakaar kii machhliyaan is pani main pai jaati hai. jhil ke uttari bhaag main kuch chote chote dweep bhi hai.
