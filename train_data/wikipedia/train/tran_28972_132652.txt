 
 yudicott​ sapushpak paudhon kaa ek samooh he jinke bijon ke do hisse hote hai, jiske vipareet monocott paudhon ke bijon main ek hi bijpatr hota he. phooldhari paudhon kii yahi do mukhya shreniyan hai.
 inhen pehle dwibijapatri bulaayaa jaataa tha lekin vah ek monopheltic samooh nahin he jabaki yudicott​ main vahi dwibijapatri shaamil kiye jate hai jo ek monopheltic gut kii jatiyaan hai. yudicott​ samooh main aane vaale sabhi ke phulon ke paraag kii yah bhi visheshta he ki uske kanon main aksh ke saath chalane vaali teen naaliyaan hoti hai - yadi paraag ke kan ke dhruv se dekha jaae to uske teen hisse dikhte hai. anuvaanshiki anusandhan se pataa chalaa he ki in paudhon main or bhi bahut se saanjhe gun hote hai.
