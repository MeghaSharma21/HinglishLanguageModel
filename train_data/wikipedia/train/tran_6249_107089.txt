 'iroj international media limited mumbai main sthit ek bhartiya chalchitr film or vitaran company he. 1977 main arjan lulla dwaara sthapit yah company bharat main agrani utpaadan or vitaran company maani jaataa he. vartmaan main, unke bete kishor lulla or sunil lulla company ko sambhalate hai.
 iroj ne theatre, television sindication or digital plateform sahit duniya bhar main kai praroopon main bhartiya bhashi filmon kaa nirmaan, unka adhigrahan or vitaran kiya he.
 group distribution network main 50 se adhik desh shaamil hai, jisamen 25 se adhik bhashaaon main dab filmein hai or bharat, euke, america, dubai, australia, fiji or oil of main main karyaalay hai. aros kii apni librari main 2,300 se adhik filmein hai, saath hi ek atirikt 700 filmon ke liye samooh ke paas digital adhikaar hai.
 2006 main, aros international pielsi, iras group kii holding company, landan stock exange ke vaikalpik nivesh bazaar par soochibaddh hone vaali pehali bhartiya media company ban gai. iske baad, company ne november 2013 main em se neuyork stock exange kii suchi main soochibaddh kiya.
 2010 main, iaroes ne apni bhartiya sahaayak company iroj international media limited ko bharat main bombai stock exange or neshnal stock exange main soochibaddh kiya tha.
