 chuurma jise chuurma ke laddu bhi kaha jaataa he, rajasthan kii sarvaadhik lokapriy mithai he. saamanytah ise daal baati ke saath parosa jataahe. chuurma rajasthan ke har tyohaar, shaadi byaah, yaa samuhik bhoj main parosa jaataa he.
 saamanytah ise mote aate se bani, bina namak kii baati se banaaya jaataa he. chuurma ke laddu banane ke liye baati ko conde kii aag yaa bhunne ke bajaay, tal kar bhi banaaya jaataa he. bani hui baati ko phod kar baarik chura bana diya jaataa he or isamen ghi, good yaa chini evam sookhe mewa mila kar bade sies ke laddu bana liye jate hai.
