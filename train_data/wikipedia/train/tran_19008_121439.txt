 shulb sutara yaa shulbasutra sanskrit ke sutaragranth hai jo srout karmon se sambandhit hai. inamen yajna-vedi kii rachana se sambandhit jyamitiya gyaan diya hua he. sanskrit ken shulb shabd kaa arth naapane kii rassi yaa dori hota he. apane naam ke anusaar shulb sutron main yajna-vediyon ko naapnaa, unke liye sthaan kaa chunanaa tathaa unke nirmaan aadi vishyon kaa vistrut varnan he. ye bhartiya jyamiti ke pracheenatam granth hai.
 shulbasutra, shrautasutron ke bhaag hai ; srautasutra, vedon ke upang hai. shulbasutra hi bhartiya ganit ke sambandh main jaankaree dene vaale pracheenatam strot hai.
