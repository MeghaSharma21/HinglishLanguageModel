 sashastr bal vishesh shaktiyaan adhinium bhartiya sansad dwaara 11 sitambar 1958 main paarit kiya gaya tha. arunachal pradesh, assam, manipur, meghalaya, mizoram, nagaland ke ‘ashaant ilaakon’ main tainaat sainya balon ko shuru main is kanoon ke tahat vishesh adhikaar hasil the. kashmir ghati main aatankwaadi ghatnaon main badhotari hone ke baad july 1990 main yah kanoon sashastr bal vishesh shaktiyaan adhinium, 1990 ke rup main jammu kashmir main bhi lagu kiya gaya. haalanki rajya ke ladaakh ilaake ko is kanoon ke daayre se baahar rakhaa gaya.
 is kanoon ke antargat sashastr balon ko talaashi lene, giraftaar karne va bal prayog karne aadi main saamaanya prakriya ke mukaable adhik swatantraaa he tathaa naagarik sansthaaon ke prati javabdehi bhi kam he.
 is kanoon kaa virodh karne waalon main manipur kii kaaryakarta irom sharmila kaa naam pramukh he, jo is kanoon ke khilaaf 16 varshon se upwas par thi.
