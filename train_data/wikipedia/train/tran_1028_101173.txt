 baldada ek gaanv he jo ki pashchimi bharat ke rajasthan rajya ke baaraa jile main basaa hua he yahan aasapaas ke kai kshetron kaa gram panchayat kaa head offis sthit he.
 baldada rajya kii rajdhani jaipur se dakshini ore 310 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he or baaraa se uttar pashchimi ore 32 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he or yah kota shahar se pashchim kii ore 70 kilometre kii duuri par he.
 → baldada rajya main sabase aakarshak nadiyon main se ek kalisindh ke liye janaa jaataa he. 
 -> baldada ke paas ek hanuman mandir he jise kheda ke balaji ke naam se janaa jaataa he jo apni pracheenta va chamatkaar ke kaaran vikhyath he 
 -> yahan kareeb 2 mandir he jo hindu aasthaa kaa pratik he ye mandir bhi kareeb 100 varsh pracheen he 
 -> baldada hindu-muslim bhaichaare ke kaaran aas-paas ke kshetra main charchit he 
 -> yahan jangam baba kanhaiya laal ji maharaj kii samadhi he 
 → hazrat dadaji maulavee sahab kii majaare sharif baldada ke kabristaan main sthit he. 
 → hazrat sufi saiyad mahmud shah kanpuri summaa baldadi kii dargah sharif jagaapura or bashirpura ke bich sthit he. 
 → marhoom haji nihaal ahamed baldada ke ek or gatisheel, saamaajik, raajnneetik vyaktitv the. ve takreeban 35 saalon tak baldada ke sarpanch rahe. vah samaaj ke samaaj sudhaarak the. har koi unhen "sarpanch sahab" kahata tha.
 → marhoom haji abdul rajjak baldada ke gatisheel, yaadgaar, udaar vyaktitv main se ek the. unhone shiksha or krishi ke kshetra main is gaanv kaa maahaul badal diya he. vah har jaruratmand kii madad kiya karte the gaanv vaale unhen garibon kaa maseehaa maanate the sabhi unhen bhayya ji kahkar pukaarte the.
 → moulana namdaar ahamed baldada ke gatisheel hastiyon main se ek the. vah acchhee tarah se moulana or kaaji e baldada kii shakl main jane jate the. or saiyad mahmud shah sahab ke pehle khalipha bhi the. vah acchhee tarah se vyavhaar or saadagi ke liye jane jate the. vah panchagaana namaji or prasiddh samaaj sudhaarak the.
 → marhoom ejeeniyar rukhsar ahamed mahamudi baldada ke ek or gatisheel vyaktitv the. vah ek acchhee tarah se shikshit, ekikrut, or ek mahaan tarah se maanavtaa ke chintak the. rukhsar ahamed trust evam memorial bhawan kaa gathan unki yaadon ko kaayam rakhane ke liye kiya gaya. yah trust shiksha, garibon, jaruratmand, anaath or kai kshetron main adhik kalyaan kaarykram ke liye kaam karata he.
