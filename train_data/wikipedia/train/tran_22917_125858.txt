 tariq
 yah shabd arabi kriya se liya gaya he jiskaa arth he hadtaal karnaa, or agentive rup main jiskaa matlab "hathoda maarna" he. ise 711 isvi main iberia par vijay praapt karne vaale ek sainya netaa tarik ebn-jiyaad ke naam ke rup main istemaal karnaa shuru ho gaya.
 shorkik arabi main raat ke samay yatra karne vaale vyakti ke liye main upyog kiya jaataa he- ek raatri aagatuk yaa manodasha- kyonki badaayun arabon ko aam taur par yah paaya jaataa he ki lambii duuri se ek yatri aamtaur par ushnkaribandheey garmi se bachane vaali raat main pahunch jayega.
 yah kisi aise vyakti ko sanderbhit karata he jo raat ke madhya main aata he or daravaaje par dastak detaa he. iske peeche bhashaai vichaar yah he: raat main aa rahaa he, or dhyaan yaa aashcharya karne ke liye bulaava.
 khagolkeey main ek chamakate hue taare ko bhu "tariq" kaha jaataa he. 
 yah ek baar subah ke taare shukr grah ko bhi sanderbhit karata he, jisamen ab ek grah ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
 tarik kaa matlab sapane main ek aagatuk he.
 arabi sahitya main, shabd kaa upyog kai sthanon par dikhaai detaa he jinamein sabase vishesh rup se quran shaamil he, jahaan tariq ne raat main shandaar sitaron ko sanderbhit kiya . sitaron ko spasht rup se tarik ke rup main janaa ja saktaa he kyonki ve raat main baahar aate hai, or yah quran ke kaaran aajkal shabd kii saamaanya samaj he. ham ise kai kavitaaon main bhi dhund sakate hai. udhaaharan ke liye, prasiddh kaviyon imru al-qais or jaarir ebn atyaayaah se .
