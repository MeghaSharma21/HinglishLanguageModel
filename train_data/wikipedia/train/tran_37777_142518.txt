 sakya tibeti buddha dharm ke chah mukhya sampradaayon main se ek he. anya paanch nyingma, kagyu, jonang, gelug or bone hai. inamen se sakya, gelug or kagyu ko vajrayan kaa navaprasar yaa sarma kaha jaataa he, kyonki yah tibet main buddha dharm ke failaav kii dwitiya shrunkhla main utpann hue. nyingma, sakya or kagyu laal topi sampradaay hai kyonki aupchaarik samarohon par inke anuyaayi laal rang kii tapiyaan pahante hai.
 tibeti bhashaa main "sakya" kaa arth "halke rang kii mitti" he. is sampradaay kaa armbh or pehla math tibet ke shigatse vibhaag ke ponpori pahari kshetra main hua tha jahaan kii mitti halke rang kii he.
