 lakhanu main sthit yah imambada mohammad ali shah kii rachana he jiskaa nirmaan 1837 i. main kiya gaya tha. ise chota imambada bhi kaha jaataa he. mana jaataa he ki mohammad ali shah ko yaheen dafnaaya gaya tha. is imambara main mohammad ali shah kii beti or uske pati kaa makbara bhi bana hua he. mukhya imambara kii chotii par sunaharaa gumbad he jise ali shah or uski maa kaa makbara samajha jaataa he. makbare ke vipareet disha main satkhand naamak adhuraa ghantaghar he. 1840 i0 main ali shah kii mrityu ke baad iskaa nirmaan roak diya gaya tha. us samay 67 meter unche is ghantaghar kii chaar manzil hi bani thi. moharram ke avasar par is imambara kii aakarshak sajaavat kii jaati he.
