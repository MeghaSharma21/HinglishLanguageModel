 krishnan nair shaantikumari chitra ek bhartiya parshv gayika he. ve bhartiya shastreey sangeet, bhakthi or lokapriy sangeet bhi gaati he. unhone malayalam, kannada, tamil, telugu, oriya, hindi, asamiya, bengali, badaga, sanskrit, tulu, urdu or punjabi geeton ke liye aawaaj di he. ve chah rashtriya film puraskaar, paanch filmafere puraskaar-dakshin or 31 alag -alag rajya film puraskaron ke praptakarta he. ve sabhi dakshin bhartiya rajya film puraskaar jeet chuki he. unako dakshin bharat kii choti bulbul evam kerala kii bulbul kaha jaataa he.
 sangeetkaaron ke ek parivaar main kerala ketiruvanantpuram main 27 july 1963 ko janme chitra kii pratibha ko choti umr se hi unke pita swargeey krishanan nair ne poshit kii. unke pita unke pehle guru the. chitra ne karnataka sangeet main shiksha praapt karne ke baad kerala vishvavidhyalay se sangeet main snaatak kii. ve vijaishankar naamak ek vyapaaree se shaadi kii. unki iklauti beti nandana kaa nidhan aath varsh kii aayu main april 2011 ko dubai main ek talab durghatana main ho gaya.
