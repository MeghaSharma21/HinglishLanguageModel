 antargrahiy antariksh udaan yaa antargrahiy yatra grahon ke bich kisi yaan kii antariksh udaan ko kehete hai. yah vaastavik yaa kaalpanik ho sakati he, haalanki aamtaur par "antargrahiy" shabd ek hi grahiy mandal ke do grahon ke bich kii yatra ko kaha jaataa he. bharat ke mangalayan kii prithvi se mangal grah kii yatra iskaa ek udhaaharan he. do alag taron yaa unke grahon ke bich kii yatra ko antartaarkeey antariksh udaan kehete hai, haalanki aisi yatraayen abhi tak keval kaalpanik hi hai.
