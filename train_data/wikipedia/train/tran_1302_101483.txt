 lakshmi kumari chudaavat bhartiya lekhika or rajasthan se raajnneetijna thim.
 unka janm 24 june 1916 ko mewar main hua. ve rajasthan main mewar rajgaraane kii ek badi riyasat devgarh ke ravat vijaisingh kii putree thim. unka vivaah 1934 main rawatsar ke ravat tej singh se hua. 24 may 2014 ko unka nidhan ho gaya. unhone rajasthani bhashaa kii manyata dilaane ke bharsak prayaas kiye.
 ve bhartiya rashtriya congress kii sadasya thim or unhone devgarh vidhaan sabha kaa 1962 se 1971 tak pratinidhitiv kiya. ve 1972 se 1978 tak rajyasabha kii sadasya raheen. ve rajasthan pradesh congress samiti kii adhyaksh bhi raheen.
 rajasthani sahitya main unke yogadan ke liye 1984 main unhen padmashri puraskaar se puraskrit kiya gaya. isi tarah unhen sahitya mahamhopadhyaay, rajasthan ratna tessitary gold award, maharana kumbha puraskaar, soviet land nehru award aadi se bhi puraskrit kiya gaya.
 unhone rajasthani or hindi main anek pustakon kii rachana kii. rajasthani main unki pramukh pustaken mumal, devnarayan bagadaavat mahagatha, rajasthan ke reeti-rivaaj, antardhvani, lenin ri jeevani, hindukush ke us paar hai.
