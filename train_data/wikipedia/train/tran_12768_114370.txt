 prakritik rup se praapt uranium main uranium-235 kii maatra keval 0.07% hoti he shesh 99.284% uranium-238 hota he. jis uranium main uranium-235 kaa kii pratishat maatra kisi vidhi se badha di gayi ho use sanvardhit uranium kehete hai. U-235 hi prakritik rup se praapt ekmaatr isotope he jo ushmiya neutranon dwaara vikhandit ho saktaa he. 
 sanvardhit uranium nabhikeey riector banane athwa sainya hathiyaar banane ke liye ati aavashyak he. antararashtriya parmaanu urja agency vishwa bhar main sanvardhit uranium par najar rakhti he. 
 isamen uranium-235 kii maatra 20% se kam hota he. saamanytah 3 se 5 % uranium-235 hoti he. yah nabhikeey riector ke liye upyukt hoti he. 
 isame uranium-235 kii maatra 20 % se lekar 80 % tak hoti he. yah nabhikeey astron ke vikaas ke liye upyukt hoti he.
