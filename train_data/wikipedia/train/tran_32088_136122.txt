 manohar singh ji pradhyumna singh ji jaadeja ek bhartiya rajkot shahi gharaane ke thakur or raajnneetijna the. 
 manoharsinghji kaa janm 14wein thakur sahab pradyumsinghji lakhajirajsinghji ke jyeshtha putr ke rup main rajkot ke ranjit vilaas mahal main hua tha. unhone rajkumaar kalege, rajkot, or elfinston mahavidhyalay, mumbai se shiksha praapt kiya. manoharsinghji ke paas kala snaatak ke saath kanoon snaatak kii degree thi. unhone kanoon se master degree landan vishvavidhyalay se praapt kii.
 apane pita or dada, kii tarah manoharsinghji cricket main utsuk the, or apane pratham shrenee kariyar kii shuruyaat saurashtra kii taraf se gujarat ke khilaaf 1955-56 ranjai trophy, se kii thi, jahaan unhone apni pehali pari main 59 ran banaaye the. manoharsinghji 1957-58 satr main team ke captain ke rup main rahen, or 1963-64 satr ke baad unki sevanivritti ke pehle tak team ke ek niyamit khilaadi rahen. aamtaur par sheersh kram ke ballebaaj ke rup main, unka ucchtam pratham shrenee score disambar 1957 main gujarat ke khilaaf ek pari main 144 ran tha. kul milakar, manoharsinghji ne 14 pratham shrenee maichon main 29.23 ke ausat se 614 ran banaaye the.
 1967 main, bhartiya rashtriya congress kii taraf se rajkot nirvachan kshetra se chunaav ladte hue manoharsinghji gujarat vidhaan sabha ke liye nirvaachit hue, or 1971 seva main rahe. november main 1973 main unke pita kii mrityu ke baad, unhone pustaini oopaadhi "thakur sahab" kaa dhaaran kiya. haalanki 1947 main bharat kii aazaadee ke baad se yah oopaadhi koi atirikt adhikaar yaa visheshadhikaar nahin rakhti thi, or yah keval ek shishtachar, ke rup main sambodhit kiya jaataa tha, haalanki sari shahi sampatti isi oopaadhi ke saath sanlagn rahati he. manoharsinghji aage do baar rajkot nirvachan kshetra se -1980 se 1985 or 1990 se 1995- ke bich gujarat vidhansabha ke sadsy rahe or isi dauraan unhen cabinet main, vitt mantri, yuva kalyaan, or swasthya mantri sahit pad diye gayi. 1998 se, ve gujarat pradesh congress samiti ke upaadhyaksh bhi rahe. manoharsinghji raajnneetik main apane vanshavaadi naam ke vajaay, aam taur par "manoharsinghji jaadeja" ke naam se jane jate the. 
 manoharsinghji kaa vivaah 1949 main alvar ke maharaja tej singh prabhakar kii suputree mankumari devi sahiba se hua tha, or unke ek putr or teen putriyaan bhi hai. navambar 2010 main unhone ek rolls royas car jise "bharat kaa sitara" kaha jaataa he, kharida, joki 1934 main unke dada dharmendrasinghji lakhajiraj ke liye banwaaya gaya tha. car, bill meredith-ovence' sangrahalay ke ek bhaag ke rup main hone ke kaaran 42 varsho se parivaar se dur rahaa tha. ise kabhi duniya kii sabase mahangi kaaron main gina jaataa tha.
 manoharsinghji kaa 27 sitambar 2018 ko apane nivas ranjit vilaas pales par nidhan ho gaya.
