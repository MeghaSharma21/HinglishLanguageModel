 shyamsundar das, mahamahopadhyaay sudhakar dvivedi,
 nagariprachaarini patrika kaa prakaashan nagariprachaarini sabha dwaara 1896 main armbh hua tha. us samay yah hindi kii tremasik patrika thi. shyamsundar das, mahamahopadhyaay sudhakar dvivedi, kalidaas or radhakrishna das iske sampadak the. 1907 i. main yah masik patrika main parivartit kar di gai or iske sampadak shyamsundar das, ramachandra shukla, ramachandra sharma or veniprasad banaae gaye.
 nagari prachaarini patrika hindi kii sabase pracheen shodh patrika he. iskaa saare jagat main khoj jagat main maan he. iskaa sheershak hota tha, 'nagariprachaarini patrika, arthath pracheen shodhasambandhi tremasik patrika'. is patrika ke sampadak mandal main babu shyaam sundar das, gaurishankar hirachand ojha, acharya ramchandra shukla, chandradhar sharma guleri, jayachand vidyalankar, dow. sampoornanand, acharya narendra dev, hajaari prasad dvivedi jaise vidwaan rahe.
