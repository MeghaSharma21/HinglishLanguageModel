 tamaluk bharat ke pashchim bengal rajya ke purvi medinapur janapad kaa mukhyalay he. vidwaanon kaa vichaar he ki tamaluk hi pracheen tamralipt he jo pracheen bharat main ek prasiddh vyapaarik kendra tha. yah nagar bengal kii khadi se sate hue rupanarayan nadi ke kinare sthit he.
 uski pracheen samruddhi athwa gaurav ke koi chinh ab vahaan nahin dikhaai dete. ise hindu or buddha donon hi apana tirthasthan maanate hai.
