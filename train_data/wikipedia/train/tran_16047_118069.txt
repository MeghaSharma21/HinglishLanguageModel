 brownfield investment yaa brownfield nivesh ke antargat ranneetik rup se kisi puraani audyogic ikai ko hi vistaarit kar use samunnat banaaya jaataa he jisase pehle se acha pratifal praapt kiya ja sake. brownfield nivesh main koi niveshak company yaa sarkari ikai kisi vartmaan utpaadan suvidha yaa sthaan kaa kray karti he jis par nayi kriyaayein shuru kii ja saken. is prakaar brownfield nivesh, harith kshetra nivesh main pratyaksh videshi nivesh kii ek vaikalpik vyavastha he jisamen na to nayi site hoti he or na hi nayaa plant.
