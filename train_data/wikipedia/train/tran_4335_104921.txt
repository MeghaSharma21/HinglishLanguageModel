 sadak parivahan evam rajmarg mantralay, bharat sarkaar kaa ek mantralay he. yah, niyamon, viniyamon or sadak parivahan se sambandhit kanoonon, rashtriya rajmargon or parivahan anusandhan ke nirmaan or prashasan ke liye sheersh nikaay he. sadak parivahan desh ke aarthik vikaas ke liye ek mahatvapurn buniyadi dhaancha he. yah gati, sanrachana or vikaas ke praturup ko prabhavit karti he. bharat main kul maal kaa 60 pratishat or yatri yatayaat ke 85 pratishat, sadakon par le jaaya jaataa he. isliye, is kshetra kaa vikaas bharat ke liye sabase zyada mahatvapurn he or bajet main ek mahatvapurn bhaag banaata he. may 2014 se nitin gadakari, sadak parivahan evam rajmarg mantralay ke mantri he.
 july 1942 main sanchaar vibhaag ko do bhaagon main vibhajit kiya gaya tha:
 yuddh ke parivahan vibhaag ko aavantit kaaryon main pramukh bandargahon, railway praathamiktaaon, sadak or jal parivahan, petrol rashan or produsar gas ke upyog shaamil hai. mote taur par dekha jaae to yuddh ke parivahan vibhaag kaa kaary-yuddh ke samay main parivahan ke liye jahaajon kii maang, tateey shipping kaa prashasan or pramukh bandargahon kaa vikaas tha. baad main, niryaat kii yojana bananaa parivahan vibhaag kii praathamiktao main shaamil kiya gaya.
