 us falan ko abijiy falan kehete hai jo kisi bahupadiy sameekaran ko santusht nahin kar saktaa. . abijiy falnon ke maan f ko iske char x ke yoga, ghataana, gunan, bhaag, ghaat evam mul kii simit bijiy sankriyaaon ke dwaara abhivyakt nahin kiya ja saktaa. iske vipareet bijiy falan ve hot hai jinako simit bijiy sankriyaaon ke rup main abhivyakt karnaa sambhav ho.
