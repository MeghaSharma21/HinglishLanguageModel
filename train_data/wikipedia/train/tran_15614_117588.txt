 vrikshayurveda ek sanskrit granth he jisamen vrikshon ke swasthyapurna vikaas evam paryaavaran kii suraksha se samandhit chintan he. yah surpal kii rachana maani jaati he jinke bare main bahut kam gyaat he.
 san 1996 main dow vaay el nene ne euke ke boldian pustkaalay se isaki pandulipi praapt kii. dow nalini saadhale ne iskaa anuwaad angreji main kiya.
 vrikshayurveda kii pandulipi devanagari ke pracheen rup vaali lipi main likhi gayi he. 60 prishthon main 325 paraspar sugathit shloke hai jinamein anya baton ke alaava 170 paudhon kii visheshtaayen di gayin hai. isamen beej kharidne, unka sangrakshan, unka sanskaar karne, ropane ke liye gaddha khodne, bhoomi kaa chunaav, seenchne kii vidhiyaan, khaad evam poshan, paudhon ke rog, aantarik evam vahya rogon se paudhon kii suraksha, chikitsa, baag kaa vinyas aadi kaa varnan he. is prakaar yah vrikshon ke jivan se sambandhit sabhi muddon par gyaan kaa bhandar he.
