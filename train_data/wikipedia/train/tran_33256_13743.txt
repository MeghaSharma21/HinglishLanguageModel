  raajnneeti praveshdwar
 raaj-parmadhikaar, kuch vishesh prathagat adhikaron, visheshadhikaron or pratiraksha kaa samooh he, jise united kingdom main keval samprabhu par nihit hai. yah visheshadhikaar, britirah sarkaar kii kai sanvaidhanik shaktiyon kaa strot bhi he. paramadhikaron ko purvatah shaasha dwaara apane vivek par prayog kiye jate hai. baharhaal, 19veen sadi ke baad se, prathagat rup se parmadhikaar ko pradhanmantri or anya cabinet mantriyon kii salaah par hi prayog karnaa aavashyak mana jaataa he. haalaanki, adhirat sanvaidhanik rup se pradhanmantri or mantrimandal ke kilaaf shahi parmadhikaar kaa prayog karne ke liye shasakt evam swatantra hai, parantu vyavhaarik rup se, aisa keval apatkaaleen sthiti main, yaa aisi paristhitiyon main, jahaan maujuud misaal, paryaapt rup se prashn main ai pasthitayon par lagu nahin hoti hon.
 aaj raaj-parmadhikaar hi united kingdom kii sarkaar ke kaarykaran ke liye, videsh maamle, raksha or rashtriya suraksha sambandhit kai vishyon ke liye uplabdh he. rajasatta kaa in tathaa anya maamalon main sarthak sanvaidhanik upasthiti he, parantu rajasatta kaa in maamalon par simit vaastavik shaktiyaan he, kyonki, visheshadhikaar kaa prayog karte pradhanmantri or anya mantriyon yaa anya sarkari adhikariyon ke haathon main he.
