 hiranyakeshikalp ke 26 wein tathaa 27 wein prashnon kii manyata dharmasutra ke rup main he, kintu yah vastav main swatantra kriti na hokar aapstamb dharmasutra kii hi punah prastuti prathit hoti he. antar keval itnaa he ki aapstamb dharmasutra ke anek aarsh prayogon ko isamen prachlit loukik sanskrit ke anuroop parivartit kar diya gaya. udhaaharan ke liye aapstamb 'prakshalayati' or 'shaktivishyen' sadrush shabd hiranyakeshi dharmasutra main kramshah 'prakshalayet' or 'yathashakti' rup main praapt hote hai. sutron ke kram main bhi bhinnata he. aapstamb ke anek sutron ko hiranyakeshi dharmasutra main vibhakt bhi kar diya gaya he. 
 is par mahadev dikshitkrut 'ujjwala' vritti uplabdh he. sambhawatah abhi tak iskaa koi bhi sanskaran prakashit nahin ho sakaa he.
