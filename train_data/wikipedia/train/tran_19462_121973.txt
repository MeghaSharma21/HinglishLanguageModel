 upite  pracheen roman dharm ke pramukh devataaon main se ek the or devataaon ke raja the. wo varsha, bijli or purushatv ke devtaa the. unke samatuly pracheen unani dharm ke devtaa the kheus . roman dharamshastra main akaash kii aatma ko 'jupitar' kii sangya di gai he. varsha or vidyut ke devtaa ke rup main isaki puja kii jaati he. vah roman jatiyon kaa rakshak mana gaya he.
 sampurna italy main pahari kii chotiyon par jupitar kii puja hoti he. rome ke dakshin main alban kii pahari par jupitar kii puja kaa sarvaadhik pracheen centre he. yah tees laathini nagaron ke us sangh kaa kendriya poojasthal tha jiskaa rome bhi saamaanya sadsy tha. swayan rome main capilolin kii pahari par jupitar ke pracheenatam mandir ke avshesh mili hai.
 vastav main jupitar na keval jatiyon ke sangrakshak ke rup main puja jaataa he, apitu use ek maatr mahaan naitik shakti rup main bhi manyata praapt he, jiskaa sambandh shapath, sanghathanon or sandhiyon se he. rome ke anek pracheen pavitra vivaah-sanskaar jupitar ke pujaariyon kii hi upasthiti main sampann hue.
 lekin samay ke anusaar jupitar sambandhi dharanaon or manyataon main parivartan tathaa vikaas hota rahaa. roman rajtantr ke antim dinon main jupitar ke nae mandiron kaa nirmaan hua or ek naveen sanskaar ke dwaara use sarvashaktiman 'jupitar aptimas maximus' kii sangya di gai. yah sanskaar samaaroh prati varsh 13 sitambar ko manaaya jaataa tha jisamen kansul, seenet-sadsy, nyaayadhish or purohit sammilit hote or ateet main kii gai shapathon ko puraa hone kii prarthana karte the. us avasar par is mahaan devtaa ko pashubali di jaati or ateet main samrajya kii raksha ke liye uske prati kritagyata prakat kii jaati thi tathaa bhavishya ke liye nai shapath grahan kii jaati thi. baad main yah din khel kud ke samaaroh kaa din ban gaya, jo shatruon par vijay kii khushi main manaaya jaataa tha. shatruon par vijay ke pashchaat khushi ke jaloos nikaale jate or jupitar ke mandir par jaakar uske prati kritagyata swaroop uski puja kii jaati thi.
