 bharat main prithvi ke vibhinn ghatakon ke bich majboot sambandhon, udaahranarth vaataavaran, mahaasaagar, siro‑sphere or jeeyo‑sphere, ke mahatv ko sweekaar karte hue prithvi vigyaan mantralay ko 2006 main gathit kiya gaya tha. iske turant baad varsh 2007 main ek vaastavik sanghathan astitv main aayaa. yah tha - prithvi pranali vigyaan sanghathan jo mantralay kaa prashasnic ang tha. 
 iske antargat bhu vigyaanon kii teen pramukh shakhaaen hai – 
  mahaasaagar vigyaan evam praudyogiki, 
  vaataavaran vigyaan evam praudyogiki or 
  bhu vigyaan evam praudyogiki. 
 is prayaas kaa ekmaatr uddeshy tha prithvi kii prakriyaaon sambandhi vibhinn pakshon par amul vichaar karnaa taaki prithvi pranali kii vibhinnataaon ko samajha ja sake or mausam, jalwaayu tathaa jokhimon kii bhavishyavaanee main sudhaar kiya ja sake.
 iskaa pramukh kaary mausam, jalwaayu or jokhim kii bhavishyavaanee kaa vikaas or sudhaar tha taaki saamaajik, aarthik or paryaavarniya phayda mil sake. iske alaava jalwaayu parivartan, jalwaayu kaaryon or ekikrut himalayan mausam vigyaan sambandhi pakshon kaa adhyayan karnaa bhi isaki paridhi main tha. esso kaa ek daayitv yah bhi he ki vah samaaj ke saamaajik‑aarthik laabh ke liye saamudrik sansadhano kii khoj uske dohan kaa bhi kaary kare. iske liye saamudrik paryaavaran ke kshetra main hone vaale vikaason ko maddenjar rakhte hue kaary karnaa he.
