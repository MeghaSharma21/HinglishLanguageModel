  kaiff bhopali ek bhartiya urdu shaayar or filmi geetkaar the. ve 1972 main bani kamaal amrohi kii film paaqiza main mohammad rafi dwaara gaaye geet "chalo dildaar chalo....." se lokapriy hue.
 kaiff bhopali ne kai hindi filmon main geet likhe, kintu 1972 main bani paaqiza unki yaadgaar film rahi. is film ke lagbhag sabhi gane lokapriy hue, jaise "tire nazar..", "chalo dildaar chalo, chaand ke paar chalo...." aadi.
 sattar-assi ke dashak main ve lagaataar mushaairon kii jaan bane rahe. unhone kai prasiddh gazalen kahi he, jaise "tera cheharaa kitna suhana lagata he", jhum ke jab rindon ne pilaa di aadi jise awaz di he jagajeet singh ne.kamaal amrohi kii ek or film razia sultan main unke dwaara likhaa ek gaanaa "i khuda shukr tera...." kaaphi lokapriy hua. unki putree "parveen kaiff" bhi urdu kii mashhoor shaayara hai.
