 abrahim benjamin di williars ek dakshin africa ke cricket khilaadi hai, jo dakshin africa kii ek divasiy antarrashtriya team ke captain the. wo indian premier lig main royal chalangers bangalore kii taraf se bhi khelte hai. july 2014 kii isisi ranking main unhen test or ek divasiy cricket main pratham sthaan diya gaya tha.
 18 janvari 2015, abi di williars ne ek divasiy antarrashtriya cricket main sabase tej shatak lagane vaale khilaadi ban gaye the. di williars ne newjiland ke kori anderson ke kirtiman ko dhwast kiya karke yah bada record banaaya. ve west indies cricket team ke khilaaf apana shatak 31 gend main puraa kiya evam out hone se purv usne 44 gend main 149 ran kaa vishwa kirtiman banaaya. inhone 23 may 2018 ko intaraashtriya cricket se sanyas kii ghoshnaa kii.
 abi di williars ne saal 2008 ke ipel ke pehle sijan main hi padaarpan kar diya tha. us samay se ye 2010 ke ipel tak dilli dairedevills ke liye khele the. is dauraan inhone teen saalon main 96, 465 or 111 ran banaaye the.
 iske baad abi di williars ko saal 2011 ke ipel main royal chalangers bangalore ne 7,43,65,500.00 main kharida tha iske baad se hi ye isi team main khelte aa rahe he.
