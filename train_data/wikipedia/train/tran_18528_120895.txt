 deviprasad munshi pracheen itihaas ke pandit tathaa mahaan hindi sevi the.
 deviprasad ji kaa janm saxena parivaar main hua tha. inke pita kaa naam munshi natthanlaal tha. inkaa parivaar bahut bada tha, parantu hindi kaa gyaan keval inke mata-pita ko tha. anya log urdu-farsi ke gyaata the. inhone urdu-farsi kii shiksha pita se or hindi kii shiksha mata se praapt kii. sanwat 1920 main inhone tonk riyasat main naukari kii. tadnantar kuch din ajmer main rahe, phir 1936 vi. se jodhpur main naukar ho gaye. 
 tonk main hi inhone 'kaab rajasthan' naamak urdu pustak kii rachana kii thi or 'swapn rajasthan' ke naam se uskaa hindi anuwaad bhi kiya tha. jodhpur main un dinon samast rajkaaj urdu-farsi main hota tha, parantu munshi ji ne maharaj kii anukulata paakar samast raajkaal ke liye hindi nagari kaa prachlan kiya. sanwat 1940 main ye vahiin munsif ho gaye or atyant kushaltapurvak kaary karte rahe. sevakaal main inhen kai puraskaar or padak aadi mili the.
 hindi jagat main munshi ji kii khyaati mukhyat: pracheen itihaas ke prakand pandit ke rup main he. in vishyon par hindi or urdu main inhone 50-60 granthon kii rachana kii thi jinamein akbarnama, humayunnama, babarnama, jahangirnama jaise suprasiddh aitihaasik aakargranth ullekhya hai. inako kai granthon par puraskaar bhi mila tha. inaki prasiddhi kaa doosraa kaaran kaashi nagariprachaarini sabha ke antargat aitihaasik granthon ke prakaashanarth 'deviprasad aitihaasik pustakamala' kii sthapana he jisamen ab tak bees se adhik uttamottam granth nikal chuke hai.
 sanwat 1980 vi. main inkaa nidhan hua.
