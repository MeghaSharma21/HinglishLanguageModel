 thap yantra
 mrudang dakshin bharat kaa ek thap yantra he. yah karnataka sangeet main praathmik taal yantra hota he. ise mrudang khol, mrudangam aadi bhi kaha jaataa he. gaanvon main log mrudang bajaakar kirthan geet gaate he. iskaa ek sira kaaphi chota or doosraa sira kaaphi bada hota he. mrudang ek bahut se pracheen vaadya he. inako pehle mitti se hi banaaya jaataa tha lekin aajkal mitti jaldi foot jane or jaldi kharaab hone ke kaaran lakdi kaa khol banane lag gayi hai. inako upyog, ye dholak hi jaise hote he. bakre ke khaal se donon taraf chaaya jaataa he inako, donon taraf syaahi lagaaya jaataa he. haath se aghat karke inako bhi bajaayaa jaataa he. chhattisgarh main navaratri ke samay devi puja hoti he, usamen ek jas geet gaaye jate hai. usamen inkaa upyog hota he.
