 saanchaa:Infobox football biography 2jevier harnandes cross, inhen aamtaur par zavi ke rup main janaa jaataa he  ek spanish footbal midfilder hai or vartmaan main spanish laa liga club epasi barcelona ke liye khelte hai. 
 unhen duniya ke behtareen khiladiyon main se ek mana jaataa he, zavi ko 2009 kii champions lig final kaa adhikarik main of da match ghoshit kiya gaya, kyonki unhone menchester united ko haraane main barcelona kii madad kii or tisare champions lig kaa khitaab jeeta. 
 yuiefee euro 2008 main yuiefee ke dwaara unhen player of da toornament ghoshit kiya gaya. 
 or ve 2007–08 fifa world player of da year ke liye chaynit paanch final khiladiyon main se ek the. kul milakar unhone spanish neshnal team ke liye 98 baar khel kar use gaurav pradaan kiya he or 2010 kaa fifa world cap bhi jeeta he. 
 ve niyamit rup se vartmaan khiladiyon main se sarvshereshth midfilder bane hue hai. 
  
 zavi barcelona youth system kii upaj hai or 11 varsh kii umr ke baad se ve niyamit rup se camp nou main rahen hai. unhone jaldi hi yuvaaon ke bich apana sthaan bana liya or team ko sangrakshit kar liya. ve jordi gonzalvo kii epasi barcelona bi team ke mukhya sadsy the, jisane duusare divijan ke liye padonnati arjeet kii thi. 
 tiimon main unki pragati ke kaaran unki pehali team kii shuruyaat 18 agast 1998 ko super cap final main hui, jisamen unhone arsidi melorka ke khilaaf score banaaya. velensia sief ke khilaaf laa liga main unki shuruyaat 3 oktober 1998 ko hui, jisamen barcelona ne 3-1 se jeet hasil kii. zavi ne shuru main reserve or senior donon prakaar kii tiimon main rahete hue, keval ek gol karte hue rial veladolid par 1–0 se ek jeet hasil kii, jab barcelona lig main 10 wein sthaan par tha, isane club main punarjagran kii shuruyaat karne kaa kaam kiya. nirantar prabhaavshaalee pradarshnon ke kaaran ve jaldi hi louis vaan gaal kii khitaab jeetne vaali team ke pramukh sadsy ban gaye. agle laa liga ke 1999-00 ke satr main pep gardiola ko chot lagane ke kaaran zavi barcelona kii pramukh khiladi ban gaye, is pad ko zavi ne hameshaa banaaye rakhaa he. 
 
 2004-05 ke khitaab jeetne vaale satr ke baad se, ve team ke up-captain bane hue hai. 2005-06 ke satr main, prashikshan ke dauraan zavi ke baayen ghutane kaa snaayu fat gaya; ve paanch maheene ke liye khel se baahar ho gaye, is dauraan ve kai khel satron main nahin khel paae, lekin april main ve waapas laute or ab ve 2006 yuiefee champions lig final ke liye vikalpi bench par the. 
 pradarshan or uplabdhiyon kii drushti se, 2008-09 kaa satr zavi ke liye ab tak kaa sabase acha satr tha. ve barcelona ke trable kaa mukhya hissaa the, unhone teenon pratispardhaon main utkrisht pradarshan kiya: 
 unhone athlettic bilbao ke khilaaf kopa del re 2008–09 ke final main ek free kik ke saath chauthaa gol karte hue, 4–1 se jeet hasil kii. 
 laa liga main, kai achche pradarshnon main sambhavatya sabase mahatvapurn pradarshan tha, 2 may ko rial madrid ke khilaaf 6–2 ke saath al-clasico vijay. unhone 6 main se 4 gol main yogadan diya or nishchit rup se isi pradarshan or isi jeet ke kaaran barcelona ne 2009 kaa khitaab jeeta. 
 ant main, zavi ne menchester unaited ke khilaaf 2009 kii champions lig final ko jeetne main barcelona kii madad kii, jo 2–0 ke saath khatm hui, isamen zavi ne 69 minute ke baad lyonal messi ke header ko gend paas kar ke duusare gol main madad kii. unhone lagbhag score bana hi liya tha, kyonki unka shot penlti box ke thik baahar se aayaa or usne post ko hit kiya. barcelona ke 2008-09 ke yuiefee champions lig vijay abhiyaan ke dauraan zavi ke yogadan ke liye unhen "yuiefee champions lig kaa sarvshereshth midfilder" chunaa gaya. zavi 20 yogadanon ke saath, laa liga ke ucchtam sahaayak khiladi the. ve champions lig main bhi 7 yogadanon ke saath ucchtam sahaayak khiladi the. 
  
 zavi 2014 tak barka ke saath anubandh main hai, is anubandh kii samay avadhi ko 2008–09 ke satr ke dauraan badhaaya gaya tha. is nae anubandh se ve club main sabase zyada kamaane vaale logon main se ek ban jaayenge or unki kamai €7.5 million prati varsh ho jaayegi. 
 zavi ne barcelona ke liye ol-time apiyarence list main carles rickech kii barabari kii jab ve 14 farvari 2009 ko club ke gyarahawein satr main 452 veen baar saamne aaye. ab ve migueli ke baad duusare sthaan par hai, jo 548 baar prakat hue hai. 
 vartmaan main ve laa liga sahaayak sarani main sheersh sthaan par hai. 
  
 2009-2010 ke satr ke dauraan, patrakaron ne barcelona ke paksh main zavi ke yogadan kaa kaaphi ullekh kiya. udahaaran ke liye: 
 2009-10 ke satr main, zavi ne pichley satr kii tarah apana utkrisht pradarshan jari rakhaa. ek baar phir se ve sahaayak sarini main sheersh sthaan par rahe or centigo bernebyu main rial madrid ke khilaaf barcelona kii 2-0 kii jeet main sahaayta kii. puure satr ke matdaan main unhen barcelona kaa doosraa sarvshereshth khiladi ghoshit kiya gaya, kyonki barcelona ne record 99 ankon ke saath lig khitaab jeeta. 
  3 june 2010 ko, spanish akhbaar mark ne laa liga main sarvshereshth khiladi ke liye vaarshik tropho alphredo di stephano puraskaar main zavi ko teesraa sthaan diya. jabaki pehle sthaan par lyonal messi or duusare sthaan par christiano ronaldo the.
  
 9 june 2010 ko zavi ne club ke saath chaar saal ke ek anubandh par hastakshar kiye, jo khele jane vaale khelon kii sankhya ke aadhaar par 30 june 2016 tak swatah hi navinikrut ho jayega. 29 navambar ko unhone apane hi desh main ek prabhavi 5-0 kii jeet ke saath prabhaavshaalee pratidwandvi rial madrid ke khilaaf apana teesraa gol kiya. 
 
 footbal pich par zavi harnandes kii kshamtaaon ne unki team ko asankhya khitaab dilaaye hai or ve sabhi samay ke sabase susajjit khiladi ban gaye hai. 
 is khiladi ne fifa you-20 world cap, fifa club world cap or fifa world cap main spain or epasi barcelona ke saath kai pramukh antarrashtriya khitaab jite hai. europea manch par unki safaltaaon ko kam nahin aanka ja saktaa, unhone yuiefee european footbal championships ko to jeeta hi he, saath hi do baar yuiefee chapions lig jeet kar in uplabdhiyon main chaar chand lagaaye hai. in sanyukt uplabdhiyon ne unhen antarrashtriya or club donon staron par europe ke mukhya khitaabon kaa vijeta bana diya he. zavi paanch baar liga bibivie champion rahen hai or barcelona kii ore se ek star khiladi hai jinhone ek prasiddh abhootpurv spanish trable jeet kar, ek calendar varsh main 6 record trophiyaan, sextupal, arjeet kin. 
  
 2010 kii garmiyon ke dauraan, zavi ke khel ne spain ko pehali baar world cap kaa khitaab dilaayaa. is safal abhiyaan ke peeche zavi kaa hi haath tha. is anbhavi midfilder ne toornament ke dauraan ek 89 pratishat paas puure kiye. is dauraan spain ke kul 7 khelon main 669 main se 599 paas puure kiye gaye. jaavi kii passing kii yah maatra kaaphi prabhaavshaalee thi, yahaan tak ki jo final main yaa tisare sthaan ke match main pahunch gaye, jaavi ne atempt kiye gaye paas, puure kiye gaye paas, atempt kiye gaye cornor, puure kiye gaye cornor or penlti kshetra main delivery main field kaa netritv kiya. 
 jaavi ekmaatr khiladi the jinhone toornament main 600 se zyada paas atempt kiye or isi tarah se ekmaatr khiladi the jinhone 500 se zyada paas puure kiye. unhone kul 669 paas atempt kiye the, jo world cap of da year ke agle saphaltam pasar se 104 adhik the, yah pasar germany kaa bastian shwenstigar tha. ye aankade jaavi kii passing kushalta ko to darshaate hi hai, saath hi yah bhi bataate hai ki gend vitaran ke liye spain ko un par kitna bharosa he.
