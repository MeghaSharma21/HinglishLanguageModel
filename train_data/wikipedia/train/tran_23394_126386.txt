 ludwig vaan bithoven ek german sangeetkaar the. pashchimi kala sangeet main shastreey or prempurna yug ke bich sankraman ke ek mahatvapurn hisse he, vah sabase prasiddh or prabhaavshaalee sangeetkaaron main se ek the. unki sabase prasiddh rachanaaon me unki 9 simphnia, 5 piano consrto, ek vaayalin consarton, 32 piano sonta, 16 string qwartes shaamil hai.bon, pavitra roman samrajya kii to colon ke matadaataaon kii rajdhani or bhaag main janme, bithoven choti umr se hi ek achche sangeetkaar the, unhe unke pita johaan vaan bithoven ne sangeet sikhaaya. 21 saal kii umr main unhone kaha ki vah joseph heden ke saath rachana kaa adhyayan shuru kiya he, jahaan vienna main le jaaya gaya he, or ek guni pianovadak ke rup main khyaati praapt kii. unhone kaha ki jab tak unki mrityu vienna main rahete the. uske der se 20 tak uski sunwaayee kharaab karne ke liye shuru kiya, or apane jivan ke antim dashak se vah lagbhag puri tarah bahere the. 1811 main vah yojit karne or saarvajanik rup se pradarshan kar chhod diya, lekin shant karne ke liye jari rakhaa.
