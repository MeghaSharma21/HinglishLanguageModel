 cyprus, aadhikarik taur par cyprus ganatantr purvi bumadhya sagar par greece ke purv, lebanon, syria or israel ke pashchim, misr ke uttar or turki ke dakshin main sthit ek urasian dweep desh he. isaki rajdhani nicosia he. isaki mukhya- or rajbhashaayen greek or turki hai.
 cyprus bumadhya kaa teesraa sabase bada dweep he or lokapriy paryaton sthal he, jahaan prati varsh 2.4 million se adhik paryatak aate hai. yah 1960 main british upnivesh se swatantra hua ganraajya he, jo 1961 main rashtramandal kaa sadsy bana or 1 may 2004 ke baad se europea sangh kaa sadsy he. cyprus kshetra kii unnat arthvyavasthaaon main se ek he.
