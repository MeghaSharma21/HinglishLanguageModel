 phiza 2000 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he jise khalid mohammad dwaara likhit or nirdeshit kiya gaya he. film main karishma kapur, ruthik roshan or jaya bachchan hai.
 yah film phiza naam kii ladki kii he, jiskaa bhai amaan 1993 ke bombai ke dange ke dauraan gaayab ho gaya. phiza or uski maa nishatbi ummid karte hai ki ek din vah waapas aayega. haalanki gaayab hone ke chah saal baad phiza anischitata kii jindagi se tang aa gai he. vah apane bhai kii talash main jane kaa sankalp karti he. apni maa nishatbi kii utsaahi aashaa or apane drudh sankalp se prerit, phiza apane bhai ko khojane ke liye jo kuch bhi kar sakati he karti he. vah kanoon, media, yahaan tak ​​ki rajanetaaon kaa bhi upyog karne kaa faisla karti he.
 jab vah use miltaa he, to vah dehshat main aakar dekhati he ki vah ek aatankwaadi samooh main shaamil ho gaya he. vah use ghar llane ke liye majaboor karti he, or antat vah apni maa ke saath ekajute ho jaataa he. haalanki uski nishtha or vichaar use muraad khan ke netritv main aatankwaadi network par lautnaa chaahate hai. phiza ko ched rahe do purushon ke saath ek takaraav main amaan apni bahan, maa or police ke saamne aatankwaadi network ke saath apni bhagidaari kaa khulaasa karata he. uski maa dukh or niraashaa se antat aatmahatya kar leti he.
 aniruddh kii madad se phiza apane bhai ko khojane kii ek baar or koshish karti he. amaan ko do shaktishaalee rajanetaaon ko maarane ke mishan par bheja gaya he. jab vah unhen maarane main safal hota he, to uskaa khud kaa aatankwaadi samooh use maarane kii koshish karata he. vah bhaag jaataa he or phiza uskaa peechha karti he. ve ek duusare se saamana karte hai or police ke najadik aate hue dekhne par vah use apane ko maarane ke liye kahata he. use ek sammanjanak ant dene ke aakhri upaay ke rup main, phiza apane bhai ko maar detii he.
 
 film main aath gane hai jisamen 6 ko anu malik ne sangeet pradaan kiya he. 1 ko e aar rahamaan dwaara sangeet diya gaya he.
