 
 2017 netwest tii 20 blast england or wales main maujuuda twanti-20 cricket lig he. yah gharelu tii-20 pratiyogita kaa chauthaa sizan he, jo isibi dwaara chalaaya jaataa he, jise netwest tii 20 blast kaha jaataa he. lig main 18 pratham shrenee vaali county timen shaamil hai jinamein nau tiimon ke do divijanon main vibhajit kiya gaya tha jinamein se july or sitambar ke bich khelaa jane vaala judanar tha. final 2 sitambar, 2017 ko birmingham ke edbeston cricket maidan main yojit hone vaala he.
 18 timen tii -20 khitaab ke liye pratispardhaa karenge. pratiyogitaayen shuruuaat main pratiyogita ke samooh charan ke liye 2 divijanon main prayek, 9 yukt tiimon main vibhajit hai. samooh str ke dauraan prayek club ek hi vibhaajan main duusari tiimon main se do baar kheltaa he, ek baar apane ghar stadium main or ek baar unke virodhiyon ke mutabik. kul 14 khel prayek ke liye ve keval ek baar do anya tiimon ko khelte hai. tiimon ko ek taai ke liye jeet or ek bindu ke liye do ank praapt hote hai yaa yadi match chhod diya jaataa he. kisi nuksaan ke liye koi ank nahin die jate hai. timen kul ankon se krambaddh hai, phir net ran rate. samooh str ke ant main, prayek samooh kii sheersh chaar team nokout charan main pravesh karti he.
