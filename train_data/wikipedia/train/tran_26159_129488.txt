 ishmit singh bharat ke sabase pratibhashali navdith gayakon main se ek the. star plus ke reality sho wais of india ke varsh 2007 ke vijeta gayak ludhiana ke ishmit singh 24 november 2007 ko lakhanu ke harsith saxena ko haraakar hindustan kii aawaaj bane the. ve is pratiyogita main vijayi hone ke baad mahaj 18 saal kii umr main apni dilkash aawaaj ke jariye lakhon dilon kii dhadkan ban gaye the. swar kokila lata mangeshkar ne ishmit ko swayan apane haathon se vijeta kaa khitaab saunpa tha. 
 buudhvaar 30 july 2008 ko taran taal main dub jane ke kaaran maldive main unka aakasmik nidhan ho gaya. ishmit ke nidhan par lata mangeshkar, aashaa bhosle, abhijit bhattacharya or alka yaganik samet sangeet jagat kii kai jaani-maani hastiyon ne gaharaa dukh vyakt kiya he.
