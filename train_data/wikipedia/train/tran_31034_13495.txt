 dharmanirpekshata, panthanirpekshata yaa secularvaad dharmik sansthaanon va dharmik ucchpadadhariyon se sarkari sansthaanon va rajya kaa pratinidhitiv karne hetu shasnadeshit logon ke pruthakkaran kaa siddhanth he. yah ek aadhunik rajnatik evam samvidhani siddhanth he. dharmanirpekshata ke mulat: do prastaav he 1) rajya ke sanchaalan evam niti-nirdhaaran main majahab kaa hastkshep nahin honi chaiye. 2) sabhi dharm ke log kanoon, samvidhan evam sarkari niti ke aage samaan he.
 dharmanirpekshata shabd kaa pehle-pahal prayog birmingham ke jorge jacob holiya ne san 1846 ke dauraan, anubhavon dwaara manushya jivan ko behatar banane ke taur tareeqon ko darshane ke liye kiya tha. unke anusaar, “aastikta-naastikta or dharm granthon main uljhe bagaiyr manushya maatr ke shaareerik, maansik, charitrik, bauddhik swabhaav ko ucchtam sambhavit bindu tak viksit karne ke liye pratipaadit gyaan or seva hi dharmanirpekshata he”.
 dharmanirpeksh deshon main dharmanirpekshata ko banaae rakhane ke liye tamaam tarah ke samvidhanik qaayade kanoon hai. parantu praayah rashtron ke ye qaayade kaanoon samay-samay par apana swaroop bahusankhya janata ke dharmik vishwaason se prerit ho badalte rahete hai, yaa uchit str par in kanoonon kaa paalan nahin hota, yaa pratyaksh apratyaksh str par inamen dheel di jaati rahati hai. yah chhadam dharmanirpekshata he.
