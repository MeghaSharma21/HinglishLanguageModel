 stri shakti puraskaar, bharat kaa rashtriya sammaan kii ek shrinkhalaa he, jo apni asaadhaaran uplabdhi ke liye vyaktigat mahilaon ko pradaan kiya jaataa he. yah puraskaar mahilaon or bal vikaas mantralay, bharat sarkaar dwaara chah shreniyon main diya gaya he. yah kathin paristhitiyon main ek mahila kii himmat kii bhaavana ko pahachaanata he, jisane apane nijee yaa peshewar jivan main saahas kii bhaavana sthapit kii he. yah puraskaar mahilaon ke shashaktikaran or mahilaon ke muddon ko badhaane main ek vyakti ke agrani yogadan ko bhi pahachaanata he. 
 1991 main sthapit, antarrashtriya mahila diwas ke avasar par raashtrapati dwaara prayek varsh nai dilli main 8 march ko puraskaar pradaan kiya jaataa he. is puraskaar main ek lakh rupee or ek pramaan patr kaa nakad puraskaar diya jaataa he.
 bharat ke raashtrapati pranab mukherjee ne 2012 main rani lakshmibai stri shakti puraskaar mrityu ke baad dilli main hue samuhik balaatkaar maamla peedit nirbhaya ko diya gaya tha.
 yah puraskaar bharat kaa itihaas main prakhyat mahilaon ke naam par diya gaya he, or nimnlikhit shreniyon main diya gaya he: 
 vyaktigat mahilaon main anukaraniya uplabdhi ko pahchaanane ke liye, bharat sarkaar ne 1991 main stri shakti puraskaar kii sthapana kii.
