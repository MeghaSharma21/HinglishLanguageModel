 zarda bhartiya upmahadweep kii ek paaramparik mithai kaa pakwaan he, jo uble chaaval, doodh or chini ke saath banaaya jaataa he, or isamen saath main ilaychi, kishmish, kesar, pista yaa baadaam bhi milaai jaataa he. zarda naam farsi or urdu shabd 'zaarad' se aata hajiskaa arth he 'pilaa', isliye iskaa rang pilaa hota he. zarda aamtaur par bhojan ke baad parosa jaataa he. bhartiya upmahadweep main, zarda shaadi ke vishesh avasaron par ek lokapriy mithai ke rup main banakar ubhari he.
