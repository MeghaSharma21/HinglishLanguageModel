 forensik bhugarbhashastra ek adhyayan he sabooton kaa jaise ki khanij, tel, petroleum, or anya saamagree se sambandhit saboot jo prithvi main paae jate he, jo kanoonee pranali dwaara uthaae gaye sawaalon kaa jawaab dene ke liye istemaal kiye jate he. san 1975 me re mare or saathi ratgars vishvavidhyalay ke professor jon tedro ne forensik bhugarbhashastra ko prakashit kiya tha. ab 2008 me alastrir ruffell or jenifer mchkinle, donon kuyins uniwarsity belafast, UK ke bhu-forensik ko prakashit kiya. 2012 me, elisa bergslien, sani bhens rajya main, ek saamaanya pathyapustak vishay par forencik jeeosince ke liye ek parichay prakashit kiya.
