 dow. upindarjeet cor ek bhartiya raajnneetijna hai or bhartiya punjab kii purv sattadhari shiromani akali dal kii netaa hai. oktober 2010 main, vah swatantra bharat main pehali mahila vitt or yojana mantri ban gai.
 unke pita es. aatma singh punjab ke ek mantri or akali dal ke netaa the. unki maan kaa naam bibi tej cor he. unhone dilli vishvavidhyalay se arthshaastra main em. e. or punjab vishvavidhyalay, chandigarh se punjabi main em. e. kiya. unhen punjabi vishvavidhyalay, patiala se arthshaastra main pi ech di kii oopaadhi mili.
 unhone punjabi vishvavidhyalay main arthshaastra padhaayaa he or arthshaastra kii professor hai. vah guru nanak khalsa college, sultanpur lodhi or jila kapoorthala kii pradhanacharya bhi thi. unhone do pustaken 'deveopment of theory of demand' or 'sikh relijan and economik deveopment' ko likhaa he. unki duusari pustak gaiyr-aarthik karkon, vishesh rup se aarthik vikaas main dharm kii bhoomika ke bare main he. unhen mul shodh patr 'da place and states of vimen in sikh socity' ke liye dow. ganda singh memorial puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 1997 main vah pehali baar sultanpur se akali dal kii tikat par punjab vidhaan sabha ke liye chuni gai. unhen prakaash singh badal sarkaar main ek cabinet mantri banaaya gaya tha or takneekee shiksha or audyogic prashikshan, saanskrithik kaary or paryaton, awaas or shahari vikaas ke mantralay die gaye the. 
 vah 2002 or 2007 main sultanpur se phir se nirvaachit hui thi. 2007 main unhen phir se cabinet mantri banaaya gaya tha or shiksha mantri, naagarik uddyan, satarkta or nyaay mantri thi. oktober 2010 main, manpreet singh badal ko hataane ke baad unhen vitt mantri banaaya gaya tha. vah swatantra bharat main pehali mahila vitt mantri he. vah vibhinn vidhansabha samitiyon kii sadsy rahi hai, jaise lok lekha samiti, anumaan samiti, saarvajanik upkram samiti, house cameti. 2012 main punjab chunaav main vah mahilaon main 72 varsh kii umr main, vah sabase puraani ummidavaar thim.
