 alphanjo legros france kaa chitrakaar evam murtikar tha. 
 iske chitron kaa pratham pradarshan 1857 main hua lekin ise protsaahan nahin mila. parinamswaroop vah landan chalaa aayaa. 1870 main university kalege main praadhyaapak bana or 17 varsh tak rahaa. iske adhikansh chitron kaa vishay francisi graamy jivan he. isaki katipay prasiddh rachanaayen hai - tirthayatri, samudr kaa aashirwad, baptism, mrit isa tathaa upasika.
