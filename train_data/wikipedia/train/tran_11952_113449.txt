 jahana yorkina ek roosi soviet antariksh yatri thi. jahana kaa janm 6 may 1939 ko hua tha. jab disambar 1961 main soviet sarkaar ne mahila antariksh yatriyon ko chunane kaa faisla kiya tha, us samay farvari 1962 main paanch mahila antariksh yatriyon ke samooh se chuni gayi thi. vah ek shaukia parachutist thi. wo vodog 5 kii yojana kii byaap chaalak ke madhyamik sadsy ke rup main shaamil thi, jo ki ek mahila avadhi or eva mishan ke liye tha. vodog kaarykram ke radd hone ke baad, vah gagarin cosmonat training centre par kaam karne lagi. or sarpil spesplane ke vikaas main shaamil ek antariksh yatri ban gayi.or 1 oktober 1969 ko unhone antariksh kaarykram se or 1989 main sakriy sainya duty se sevanivrutt ho gai.
