 daavat athwa party logon ke us ikattha hone kii prakriya ko kehete hai jisamen wo kisi mejabaan dwaara aamantrit kiye jate hai jisase saamaajik, vaartalap, manoranjan athwa kisi mahotsav athwa kisi vishesh avasar par anya smaranotsav hota he. ek daavat main saamanytah bhojan or peya parose jate hai tathaa aksar naach-gane athwa anya prakaar ke manoranjan bhi uplabdh hote hai. vibhinn paashchaatya deshon main kishor evam vayaskon se sambandhit daavatein alcohoul jaise bear, sharaab athwa anya peya ke saath kii jaati he.
