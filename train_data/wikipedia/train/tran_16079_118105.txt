 garaara uttari bharat ke hindi bolne vaale kshetron kii musalmaan auraton dwaara pahana jane vaala ek rivaayati lakhanvi libaas he. yah libaas kurti, dupatta or chaude panton se bana hua he. ghutane ke kshetra, jise gota kaha jaataa he, aksar zari or zaradozi kii kadhai se sajaayaa jaataa he. rivaayati garaaraon kaa prayek charan 12 meter se adhik kapadon, aksar resham brocad, se bana hua he.
 uttar pradesh ke avadh kshetra ke nawabon ke jamaane main garaaron kii vyutpatti hui. 19veen sadi ke uttarardh or 20veen sadi kii shuruyaat main, garaara libaas hindi-urdu bolne vaale kshetron kii musalmaan auraton ke darmyaan rozmarra kaa libaas tha.
 haalanki ab ye rozmarra pahanaave ke rup main nahin pahane jate hai, phir bhi ye hindi-urdu bolne vaale kshetron kii musalmaan auraton or pakistan or bangladesh main urdu-bhashi apravasiyon ke darmyaan shaadi ke libaas ke rup main lokapriy hai.
