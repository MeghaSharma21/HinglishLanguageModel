 robin hud sarai 124 or 126 mouno street, monmouth, monmouthshire, wales, main sthit ek madhyayugin maykhaanaa he.
 sarai kii imaarat madhyayug ke ant main nirmit hui thi. iskaa nirmaan pathar se hua tha or yah monmouth main durlabh madhyayugin astitv kii imaaraton main se ek he. sarai kii imaarat 27 june 1952 se united kingdom kii grad dwitiya soochibaddh imaarat he. 
 monmouth catholic sampradaay kaa ek centre tha. jab 1770 ke dashak main maaikel watkins sarai ke maalik the tab unhone ek upree kamare main maas yojit karne kii anumati di thi. 1778 ke papists adhinium se pehle catholic isaayiyon ke khilaaf dand vidhi prabhavi thi. watkins un kuch logon main se the jinhone safaltaapurvak monmouth magistrate ke samaksh yachika daayar karke ek imaarat nirmaan kii anumati praapt kar lee thi, jo aage chal kar saint mairi roman catholic carch bana. yah maykhaanaa or carch jinke liye watkins ne pairvi kii thi, monmouth heritage trail kii 24 imaaraton main se do hai.
 da lonly planet guide sarai kaa moolyankan "monmouth main sabase adhik parivaar-anukool va garmjoshi ke vaataavaran or bacchon ke khel kshetra ke saath ek bade bear udyaan vaale pub" ke rup main karata he.
