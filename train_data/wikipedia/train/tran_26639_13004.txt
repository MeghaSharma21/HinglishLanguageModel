 ,farrukhabad bhartiya rajya uttar pradesh kaa ek jila he. farrukhabad uttar pradesh rajya, ganga ke baane kinare par sthit pracheen nagar he or relon kaa jankshan he. yahan par pital ke bartanon ke kaarakaane, sheet bhandar or tel kii milen hai. taanbe-pital ke bartan, parde, sari, chhiton aadi kii chapai yahan par acchhee hoti he.amaruud, aalu, tambaaku or kharaboozon ke liye bhi yah nagar prasiddh he. hindi kii prasiddh kavyitri or sahityakar mahadevi verma kaa janm yaheen hua tha. pandit puttu laal dube farrukhabad kii tahasheel kaaymganj ke gram kinder nagala ke rahane baale the jile kaa mukhyalay fatehgarh he.
