 bartolomiyu dias  jise batholomiyu dias ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he, ek portugali anveshak tha or sarvapratham europea tha jisane kep of good hope se hote hue samudr maarg se purv kii ore yatra kii.
 san 1487 main, portugal ke maharaj king jon dwitiya ne dias ko purv main ek isai raja prester jon kii dharti kii khoj karne ko kaha. kyonki prester jon vastav main koi nahin tha, dias ko koi dharti nahin mili lekin use 1488 main atlantic mahaasaagar se hind mahaasaagar hote hue asia tak jane kaa maarg avashya mil gaya. san 1500 main pidro alvares kabrol kii brazil kii khojayatra kaa niyog karte samay ek samudri toofaan main uski mrityu ho gai. baad main uski ek murti kep toun, dakshin africa main sthapit kii gai.
 is khojayatra kaa ek anya uddeshy un deshon main jaakar, jinke bare main jaankaree joao afonso di aviro jinke saath portugali achche sambandh chaahate the. dias ko dakshin asia main vyapaar par musalamaanon ke ekadhikaar ko chunaiti dene ke liye bhi bheja gaya tha.
