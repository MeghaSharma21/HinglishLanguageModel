 happy bhaag jaaegi  ek 2016 bhartiya, hindi bhashaa, romantic kamedi film he jise mudassar ajij dwaara likhit or nirdeshit kiya gaya tha, or anand el raay or krishka lulla dwaara nirmit. yah nammaatr charitra ke rup main dyna painti ko tarankit karata he; ek swatantra utsaahi dulhan jo amritsar se hokar apni shaadi samaaroh se bhaag leti he or anjaane main lahore, pakistan main aati he. is utpaadan main panti, abhaya deol, jimi shergill, ali fazal or momel shaikh shaamil hai.
 ajij ne 2012 main film ke liye lekhan shuru kiya or baad main raay banane ke liye raay se sampark kiya. happy bhaag jaaegi ko "cross-border" romantic kamedi ke rup main mana jaataa tha or mul rup se doli lahore me namit tha. yadhyapi film kaa ek bada hissaa lahore main set kiya gaya he, filming ko photographeri ke nirdeshak ke rup main kaam karne vaale saurabh goswami ke saath punjab ke vibhinn hisson main kiya gaya tha. is film main pakistani abhinaytaon ko shaamil kiya gaya or shaikh kii bollywood kii shuruyaat hui. happy bhaag jaaegi ke liye sangeet sohail sen dwaara rachit tha. iroj international ne utpaadan ke liye duniya bhar main vitaran adhikaar hasil kiye, jo ki antim cat main 126 minute tak chalata he.

 harprit "happy" cor ko amritsar, bharat main vyavsaayee daman singh bagga se shaadi karnaa he, lekin unhone apane sangeetkaar premi guddu se milane kii yojana banaai he. vah anjaane lahore ke liye nirdhaarit lorie par khud ko paati he; vah ek falon kii tokari ke andar funs gai he jise sthaneey governor ke bete bilal ahamed ke ghar main diya jaataa he. is bich amritsar main, guddu ko bagga ne bandhak bana liya he.
  mudassar ajij happy bhaag jaaegi ke liye 2012 se patakatha par kaam kar rahe the; pariyojna ke liye pre-production kaa kaam shuru hua jab anand el raay ne ise ajij ke saath rang pilaa productions ke tahat nideshak ke rup main prastut karne par sahmati vyakt kii. yah ek nirmaataa ke rup main raay kaa pehla upkram tha or chaar saal main ajij ek nirdeshak ke rup main aaye. film ko "cross-border" romantic kamedi ke rup main mana gaya tha or mul rup se film kii setting, lahore, pakistan ke sandarbh main, doli lahore me, namit kiya gaya tha. baad main sheershak ko vartmaan main badal diya gaya kyonki kendriya charitra kaa naam harprit "happy" cor tha. raay ne ek nirdeshak se nirmaataa ke parivartan ke bare main kaha, ek nirmaataa ke rup main ek pariyojna se jude hone se unhen "ek purna film nirmaataa" banane main madad milegi. raay ne kaha ki vah ajij kii rachanaatmak prakriya main hastkshep nahin karenge or nirdeshak ke rup main unhen puri aazaadee denge. ajij ne film kii baatcheet bhi likhi.
