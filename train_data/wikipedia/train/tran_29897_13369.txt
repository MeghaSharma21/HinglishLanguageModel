 yukta mukhi ek bhartiya abhinetri hai. ve vishwa sundari vijeta rah chuki hai.
 yukta mukhi kaa janm 7 oktober, 1979 ko hua tha. vah mumbai main rahati thime. ab yukta mukhi kii shaadi ho chuki he. filmon main asafalataa kaa kaaran yukta kii lambaai bhi he. yukta kaa kad 6 foot 1 inch he. jabaki hamaare baliwood ke adhiktar abhinayta thigane kad ke hai.
