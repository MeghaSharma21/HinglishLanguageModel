 
 leftinent sachin payalat ek bhartiya raajnneetijna tathaa bharat sarkaar kii pandrahavin loksabhaa ke mantrimandalmen sanchaar evam soochna praudyogiki mantri main mantri rahe he. ye chaudaavin loksabhaa main rajasthan ke dausa loksabhaa kshetra kaa bhartiya rashtriya congress kii ore se pratinidhitiv karte hai.
 sachin payalat gujjar samudaay se hai. unke pita swargeey rajesh payalat the jo congress ke diggaj netaaon main se the. payalat kii praarambhik shiksha nayi dilli ke air force bal bharti skool main hui. unhone apane snaatak kii degree dilli vishvavidhyalay ke saint steffens mahavidhyalay se hasil kii. iske baad payalat ne america sthit pencilwania vishvavidhyalay ke wharton skool se emb kii degree bhi hasil kii.
 
 bharat lautne par san 2002 main apane pita ke janmdin 10 farvari ko shri sachin congress party main shaamil hue. is avasar par ek bade kisaan sabha kaa aayojan bhi kiya gaya. 13 may 2004 ko payalat chaudaavin loksabhaa ke liye dausa seat se chune gayi jisamen unhone apane nikatatam pratidwandi ko 1.2 lakh maton se haraaya. 26 saal kii umr main ve bhartiya saansad banane vaale sabase yuva vyakti the. payalat centre sarkaar ke grih vibhaag ke standing kamity ke sadsy evam naagarik uddyan mantralay ke salaahkaar samiti ke sadsy bhi hai. 
 shri payalat kaa vivaah sara abdullah se san 2004 main hua, jo kashmiri netaa faruk abdulla kii suputree hai.
