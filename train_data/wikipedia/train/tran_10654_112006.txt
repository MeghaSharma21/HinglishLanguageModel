 antareep, kisi samudr yaa kabhi kabhi bhoomi se bhi upar kii ore ubhara huye bhoomi ke bhaag ko kehete hai. jab yah jal se ubharta he to ise prayadweep yaa raas kaha jaataa he. 
 adhiktar antareep, yaa to kisi chattan ke mulaayam hisson ke apradan ke uparaant bache sakht yaa kathor hisson se nirmit hote hai, yaa phir yah do nadi ghaatiyon ke bich jahaan unka sangam hota he, par shesh bachi ucchbhumi hote hai.
 aitihasik rup se apni vishesh sthiti or swaabhaavik rakshaatmak gun ke kaaran kai kilon or durgon kaa nirmaan antareepon par kiya gaya he. ireland ke antareep kile is kaa ek udhaaharan he.
