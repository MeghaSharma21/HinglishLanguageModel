 nyaayalayik jeevigyaan ek aavedan he jeevigyaan kaa jo kanoon pravartan se judaa hua he. yah nyaayalayik maanav vigyaan, vanaspati vigyaan forencik, nyaayalayik keet vigyaan, nyaayalayik odontalji or vibhinn diene yaa protein aadhaarit takanic is me shaamil he. aparaadhik sthaan par mili sabuto kaa vishleshan karnaa or unase aparaadh sabit karnaa forensik kaa kaam he. aparaadhik sthaan par mili khun, koi janwar yaa vyakti kaa sharir in sab se aparaadh or apradhi kaa pataa lagaaya ja saktaa he. 
 forensik maanavvigyaan
 forensik maanavvigyaan me avshesh kii pahchaan karnaa or use baraamad karnaa karnaa. bas itnaa hi nahi balkee avshesh se pataa lagaana kii vah kone the. jati, ling, umr or kad donon aksar avshesh maapne or haddiyon main sanrachanaatmak suraag kii talash ke dwaara nirdhaarit kiya ja saktaa he.
 vanaspati vigyaan forencik
 laash par mili phool, pattee yaa pushp-renu yaa paraag se yah pataa lagayaa ja sakhta he kii aparaadh kab or kaha hua he. yaa mrityu ke baad agar jagah badali gai ho. pushp-renu yaa paraag na nasht hone vaali padaarth he us ke vishleshan se pataa lagayaa ja saktaa he vah kose ped yaa podhe kaa he us samay vah podha waha tha.forenisk me pushp-renu yaa paraag kii adhyayan ko forensik palyanology kaha jaataa he. 
 forensik odontalji 
 forensik kii sakha jisame vishleshan hota he daaton kaa. daat ek asi dhaatu he jo sadti nahi he yaa jalaane se nasht nahi hoti he. aparaadhik sthaan par daat se vyakti kii pahchaan kii yaa sakhti he. dantavalk manuye kii sabase thos padaarth he. 
 forensik diene
 diene se kisi bhi vyakti kii pahchaan kii ja sakhti he. har vyakti kaa alag diene hota he. aparaadhik sthaan par mili thodese mili khun se bhi diene kaa vishleshan kar ke vyakti kii pahchaan pataa lagayaa ja sakhaata he.
