 heraat afghanistan kaa ek nagar he or heraat praant kii rajdhani bhi he. yah desh ke pashchim main he or aitihaasik mahatv ke shahar he. yah vrahat koraasaan kshetra kaa hissaa he or sabase bada shahar bhi. resham maarg par sthit hone ke kaaran yah vaanijya kaa kendra rahaa he jahaan se bharat or china se pashchimi deshon kaa vyapaar hota rahaa he. parsi granth avesta main iskaa zikr miltaa he. isa ke purv paanchavin sadi ke hakhaamani kaal se hi yah ek sampann shahar rahaa he. yahan islam saatvin sadi ke madhya main aayaa jiske baad kai vidroh hue. heraat hari nadi ke kinare basaa hua he.
