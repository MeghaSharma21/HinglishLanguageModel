 netbuk ek chota, portable, computer he jo bina taar ke sanchaar karne evam antarjaal se judane kii saral suvidha se yukt hota he. ise 'mini-laptop' bhikah sakate hai.
 vaise to 'netbuk' naam se tarah-tarah ke aakaar-prakaar evam sanrachana vaale computer uplabdh hai kintu unki kuch samarupata bhi he jo nimnavat he-
 Asus, Acer, Hewlett Packard, Dell, Sylvania, Lenovo, MSI, Samsung, and Sony.
