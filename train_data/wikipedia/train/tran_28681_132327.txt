  punjab ke mukhyamantri uttar bharat ke punjab rajya ke sarkaar ke pramukh hai. bharat ke samvidhan ke mutabik, punjab ke rajyapal rajya ke nyaaypalik he, lekin vaastavik kaaryakari praadhikari mukhyamantri he. punjab vidhaan sabha ke chunaavon ke baad, rajyapal aam taur par sarkaar banane ke liye bahusankhya paksh ko aamantrit karata he. rajyapal mukhyamantri ko niyukt karata he, jinke mantrimandal main samuhik rup se vidhansabha ke liye jimmedaar he. yah dekhte hue ki unhen vidhansabha kaa vishwaas he, mukhyamantri kaa kaarykaal paanch saal ke liye he or yah koi avadhi seemaa nahin he.
