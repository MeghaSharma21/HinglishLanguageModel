 lison... amaaya avinaash kumaar singh dwaara nirdeshit 2013 kii hindi naatak film he. isamen mukhya bhoomika main farukh sheikh, deepti naval or swara bhaskar ne abhinay kiya he.
 film dilli main lila krishnamurthi ne armbh hoti he jo ek vidhwaa he or apni svachhand vichaaron vaali beti amaaya ke saath rahati hai. donon milakar ek librari kam coffee shop chalaati hai. lila ko apana sachcha saathi jayant sinha urf jaiz main miltaa he jo ek seva nivruth photographer or vidhur hai. amaaya ko bhi jaiz pasand he lekin jab use pataa chalata he ki uski maa or jaiz shaadi ke bare main soch rahe hai to uski pasand naapsand main badal jaati he. iske baad amaaya or jaiz ke bich tension badhata jaataa he. kuch samay baad amaaya apni maa se bhi badtamizi karne lagati he.
