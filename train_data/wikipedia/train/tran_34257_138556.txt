 sajan re phir jhooth mat bolo bhartiya hindi dharawahik he, jiskaa prasaaran sab tivi par 23 may 2017 se somvaar se shuukravaar, raat 9 baje ho rahaa he. yah sajan re jhooth mat bolo kii duusari kadi he. isamen mukhya kirdaar main hossain kuwajarawala or parvati vajhe hai. 
 jaiveer chopra ek bahut amir baap kaa betaa rahata he, jo apane pita, paramveer chopra kii hi tarah apane jeevanasathi kii parakh bhi garib ladke ke rup main karata he. use jaya lokhande pasand aa jaati or apane dost ke saath naatak kar wo usase uski man kii baat jaanane main safal ho jaataa he ki wo bhi usase pyaar karti he.
 jaya bolti he ki shaadi ke liye use uske pita se baat kerani hogi. jai use apane ghar apane parivaar waalon se or apni amir hone kii sachai bataane le jane kii koshish karata he, lekin baad main use pehle jaya ke ghar janaa padataa he. par jab wo uske pita ke ghar jaataa he to use pataa chalata he ki uske pita lalitrao lokhande amir logon se nafrat karte hai or khaane main do roti se zyada bhi lenaa unhen pasand nahin he. jab jai ko is bare main pataa lagata he to wo dar jaataa he ki yadi uske amir hone kaa raaj uske pita ko pataa chal gaya to uskaa kya hoga. 
 iske mukhya kirdaar ke liye hossain kuwajarawala ko liya gaya. jab unase baat kii gai to unhone kaha ki "haan, main is sho main ek mukhya kirdaar nibhaa rahaa hoon. main bahut lambey samay se tivi se dur rahaa or yah pehali baar he jab main soni sab ke saath kaam kar rahaa hoon. main isse pehle bhi optimistic production ke saath kaam kar chukaa hoon."
