 suchithra sen hindi evam bangla filmon kii ek abhinetri thim. vah munmun sen kii maan thi. 
 visheshkar uttam kumaar ke saath abhinay karan ke kaaran ve saare bengal main atyant janpriya huin. uttam-suchithra kii jodi aaj bhi bangla chalchitr kii shreshth jodi maani jaati he. 
 ve pratham bhartiya abhinetri hai jinako kisi anterrashtriya chalchitr mahotsav main puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya .
 suchithra sen ne keval chaar hindi filmon main abhinay kiya he jo neeche di gayi hai:-
 23 disambar 2013 ko unhen shwasan tantra main sankraman kii vajah se kolkata ke ek aspataal main bhartee karaayaa gaya tha. 17 janvari 2014 ko dil kaa daura padane se 82 varsh kii aayu main unki mritu ho gayi.
