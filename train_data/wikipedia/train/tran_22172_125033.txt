 pramaatra yantriki kuch vaigyanik siddhanton kaa ek samucchy he jo parmanviya paimaane par urja evam padaarth ke gyaat gundharmon kii vyakhya karte hai. isamen up-parmaanu paimaane par jo prakaash or up-parmanviya kanon main tarang-kan dvirup dekha jaataa he, uskaa ganit aadhaar sammilit he. kwantem yantriki main urja or padaarth ke gahare sambandh kaa bhi ganit aadhaar sammilit he.
 robert hook, christian hugence or leonhard ular jaise vaigyanikon prayogtmak tippaniyon ke aadhaar par prakaash kii ek lahar siddhant kaa prastaav kiya gaya, jab prakaash kii tarangi swabhaav par vaigyanik jaanch jo 17 veen or 18 veen shatabdi main shuru hua. 1803 main, thomas yang, ek angreji bahushrut, unki "prakaash or rang kii swabhaav par" sheershak se ek paper main varnit he prasiddh do chhed prayog kaa pradarshan. is prayog ne prakaash ke tarang siddhant ke saamaanya sweekruti main ek pramukh bhoomika nibhaai he.
 apni sthapana ke baad se, kai asahaj pahaluon or kwantum yantriki ke parinaamon darshanik vaad-vivaad or vyakhyavon ko uksaaya he. aise pehalu jose ki sambhaavna or prayikta vitaran ke vishay main maiks born kii buniyadi niyam ke rup main aayaa. yahaan tak ki buniyadi muddon, samaaj or kai pramukh vaigyanikon dwaara saraahna kii ja karne ke liye kai dashak lag gaai. richard phenman ne ek baar kaha "mujhe lagata he main surakshit rup se kah saktaa hu ki koi bhi kwantum yantriki samajhataa nahi he." steven venberg ke anusaar, "meri raay main kwantum yantriki kii koi vyakhya puri tarah se santoshjanak vyakhya nahin he."
 copenhegan vyakhya - bade paimaane par denmark ke saidhantik bhautik vigyaani niles bohr ki he - aaj sabase vyapak rup se bhautik vigyaniyon ke bich sweekaar kiya, kwantum yantric ki reeti bani hui he, jo iske nirupan ke kuch 75 saal baad huwa tha. is vyakhya ke anusaar, kwantum yantriki ke sambhavya prakruti samay ke saath ek niyatatmak siddhant kijageh me badal diya jayega ,jo ek asthaayee suvidha he.
