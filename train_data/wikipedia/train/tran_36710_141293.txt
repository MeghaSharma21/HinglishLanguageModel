 zeen kisi jaanavar kii peeth par sawari karne yaa boj dhone ke liye pahanaai jane vaali chiz ko kehete hai. yah aksar chamade yaa kapade kii bani hoti he or jaanavar ke pet va chaati par kasi jane vaali patteeyon se uski peeth par sthai rahati he. yah ghode, oont, hathi, gadhe or khachchar jaise jaanwaron ko pahanaai jaati he. agar iskaa prayog sawari ke liye kiya ja rahaa ho to aksar neeche paanv tikaane ke liye rakaab bhi lage hote hai. zeen ko kabhi-kabhi lathi bhi kehete hai lekin dhyaan den ki lathi shabd ke anya arth bhi hote hai. oont kii zeen ko kajava bhi kaha jaataa he.
 'zeen' ko angrezi main 'sedal' or arabi bhashaa main 'sirj' kehete hai.
 halanki 'zeen' ko 'lathi' bhi kaha jaataa he, dhyaan den ki lathi se taatpary kisi bhi dhaanche kaa ho saktaa he or iskaa sahi angrezi barabari kaa shabd 'frem' he. masalan tagade sharir ko 'majboot lathi' kaha jaataa he. zeen ghode par chadhaane vaala ek prakaar kaa dhaancha he isliye ise 'lathi' bhi kaha jaataa he.
