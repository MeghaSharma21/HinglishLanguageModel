 adam malik  indonesia ke ek varisht rajaneta or indonesian patrakarita ke agraduton main se ek the. ve apane jivan main indonesia ke vyapaar mantri, videsh mantri or baad main up-raashtrapati rahe. ve sanyukt raashtra kii mahasabha ke 26wein adhyaksh rahe.
 vah batubaara kabeeley ke ek batak muslim parivaar se hai.
 unhone aupchaarik rup se keval praathmik shiksha grahan kii thi. unhone apane rajnatik jivan kii shruwaat sumatra ke yuva sanghathan partindo se kii or 1934 main uske kshetreey adhyaksh bane. unhone 1946 main partaai rakyaat or partaai murba kii sthapana kii.
 grihnagar chhodane ke baad malik ne ek patrkaar ke rup main ek naukari kii. malik ne indonesia kii swatantraaa ke liye hui pramukh ghatnaon main ek mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai. ve november 1959 se 1963 tak indonesia ke soviet sangh or poland ko rajdoot rahe. wo indonesia ke vyapaar mantri rahe. unhen march 1966 main indonesia kaa up-pradhanmantri evam videsh mantri banaaya gaya. wo dakshin purvi asian rashtron ke sanghathan ko janm dene vaale 8 agast 1967 kii bangcock ghoshnaa par hastakshar karne vaale paanch videsh mantriyon main se ek the. ve 1971 se 1972 tak sanyukt raashtra kii mahasabha ke 26wein adhyaksh rahe. ve 29 march 1978 se 1983 tak indonesia ke tisare up-raashtrapati rahe.
