 alexandar dwitiya rus kaa zaar tha. vah nicholas pratham kaa jyeshtha putr tha. 
 2 march 1855 ko nicholas pratham kii jab sevestopal main baari paraajay ke baad mrityu hui or jab kreemia kaa yuddh chal hi rahaa tha, yah rus ke singhaasana par baitha. turki se mili paraajay ne sena ke sanghathan or rajya main aantarik sudhaar kii aavashyaktaa ko anivaary kar diya tha. yadhyapi alexandar swabhaav se komal tha, tathaapi kam sahishnu or pratigaami tha. itihaas main yah 'muktidaata' or 'mahan sudhaaron kaa yugpravartak' ke naam se prasiddh hua. mukti kanoon dwaara usne ek karod bhudaason ko swadhin kar diya, kaashtkaaron ko bina muaavja die vaiyaktik swadhinata de di. 1864 main jila or prantik kaunsilon kii or 1870 main nirvaachit nagarpalikaon kii sthapana hui. isi kaal sthaneey swaittashasan kaa vikaas, nyaay ke kanoonon main sanshodhan, jurypranali kaa prarambh or shikshapranali main sanshodhan hua. sainik shiksha anivaary kii gai.
 rus kii audyogic kranti kaa aarambh alexandar dwitiya ke shasankaal main hi hua. vyavsaay or railway kaa vistaar hua. kakeshas par aadhikaar jam gaya. madhya asia main rus ke rajyavistaar se rus or britain ke sambandhon main tanaav aa gaya.
 kintu alexandar ke shashansudhar pyaase ke liye os ke samaan the. krantikari dal isse santusht nahin tha. uski shakti baraabar badhati gai. usee maatra main zaar bhi pratikriyaavaadi hota gaya or jivan ke pichley saalon main uskaa praytn apane hi sudhaaron ko vyarth karne main laga. 1863 main poland se vidroh hua jo krurtapurvak kuchal diya gaya. turki se 1877 main pun: yuddh chhid gaya. sudur purv main amur nadi kii ghati kaa pradesh bladivostak tak or japan se sakhalin tak lene main zaar bhi safal hua.
 naradanaya volya naamak vampanthi aatankwaadi sanghathan dwaara 13 march 1881 ko saint petersburg main jameen ke neeche bam rakhkar zaar alexandar kii hatya kar di gai.
