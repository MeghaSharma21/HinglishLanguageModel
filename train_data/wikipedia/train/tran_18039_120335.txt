 pani kaa chashma aisi jagah ko bolte hai jahaan zamin main bani daraar yaa chhed se zamin ke bheetar ke kisi jalashay kaa pani anayas hi baahar bahata rahata he.
 chashme aksar aise kshetron main banate hai jahaan dharti main kai daraaren or kataav ho jinamein barish, nadiyon or jhiilon kaa pani pravesh kar jaae. phir yah pani jameen ke andar hi prakritik naaliyon or gufaaon main safar karata hua kisi or jagah se zamin se chashme ke rup main ubhar aata he. kabhi-kabhi zamin ke andar pani kisi bade jalashay main hota he jo dabaav ke kaaran yaa pahari ilaaqon main oonchai se neeche aate hue zamin ke upar chashmon main se fatkar baahar aata he.
