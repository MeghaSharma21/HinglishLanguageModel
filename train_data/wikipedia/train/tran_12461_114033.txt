 dravid style dakshin bhartiya hindu sthapaty kala kii teen main se ek style he. yah style dakshin bharat main viksit hone ke kaaran dravid style kehalaati he. tamilnadu va nikatavarti kshetron ke adhikansh mandir isi shrenee ke hote hai. 
 is style main mandir kaa aadhaar bhaag vargakar hota he tathaa garbhagrih ke upar kaa shikhar bhaag prijmavat yaa pyramidnuma hota he, jisamen kshaitij vibhaajan liye anek manzilen hoti hai. shikhar ke sheersh bhaag par amalak va kalash kii jagah stupika hote hai. is style ke mandiron kii pramukh visheshta yah he ki ye kaaphi unche tathaa vishaal praangan se ghire hote hai. praangan main chote-bade anek mandir, kaksh tathaa jalkund hote hai. parisar main kalyani yaa pushkarini ke rup main jalashay hota he. pragann kaa mukhya pravesh dwaar gopuram kehalaata he. praayah mandir praangan main vishaal deep stambh va dhvaj stambh kaa bhi vidhaan hota he.
