 pamir-alaay madhya asia main tajikistan, kirgizstan, uzbekistan or purvi turkmenistan main parvat-shrunkhalaaon kaa ek gut he, jo pamir parvatamala kaa ek hissaa samajhe jate hai. yah uttar main sir dariyaa kii vaadi se lekar dakshin main vaksh nadi tak vistrut hai. in parvaton kii sabase unchi chotii turkistan shrunkhlaa main sthit 5621 lambaa pick skalisti he, jiskaa arth 'patthariilaa parvat' he.
