 bhartiya 2000 rupee kaa note bhartiya rupee kaa mulya he. ise 8 november 2016 ko ₹ 500 or ₹ 1000 bankenoton kii bandi ke baad bhartiya reserve bank dwaara jari kiya gaya tha or 10 november 2016 se parisancharan main rahaa he. yah puri tarah se nae dizin ke saath bankenots kii mahatma gandhi nai shrinkhalaa kaa hissaa he.bhartiya ₹ 2000 rupee kaa note arabiai dwaara mudrit ucchtam mudra note he jo sakriy parisancharan main he, is note ko november 2016 main 1,000 rupee ke note kaa vimudrikaran ke vaad lagu kiya gaya tha..varsh 2016 ko bhartiya rigerv bank dwaara nae 2,000, ₹ 500, ₹ 200, ₹ 50, or ₹ 1 ke noton kii ghoshnaa kii gai thi.
 nayaa ₹2000 banknote 66 mimi × 166 mimi magenta rangeen note he, jisamen mahatma gandhi, ashok stambh pratik, or bhartiya reserve bank ke governor ke hastakshar hai. isamen braille print he, mudra kii pahchaan karne main drushti se chunauteepurn sahaayta karata he. reverse said main mangalayan kaa ek adarsh chitra he, jo bharat ke pehle interplanetary spes mishan kaa pratinidhitiv karata he, or svachh bharat abhiyaan ke liye logo yaa chinh or tag line he.
 ₹2000 banknote main kai suraksha visheshtaayen hai, jo neeche soochibaddh hai:
 is note main anya dizine, jyamitiya pattern samgr rang yojana ke saath sanrekhit hai, donon vipareet or vipareet hai. nae note kaa aakaar 66 mimi x 166 mimi he.
 anya bhartiya rupaya kii tarah 2000 ke note par bhi iskaa mulya 17 bhashaaon main likhaa he. agr bhaag par yah mulya angreji tathaa hindi bhashaaon main likhaa he jabaki pasch bhaag main ek bhashaa pattica he jisame bharat kii 22 aadhikarik bhashaaon main se 15 ankit hai. yah bhashaayen angreji varnmaala ke krmanusaar hai. pattica par asamiya, bengali, gujarati, kannada, kashmiri, konkani, malayalam, marathi, nepali, oriya, punjabi, sanskrit, tamil, telegu tathaa urdu ankit he.
