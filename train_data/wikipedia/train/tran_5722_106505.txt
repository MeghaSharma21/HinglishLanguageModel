 devnoor mahadev kannada ke suprasiddh sahityakar hai. unka janm karnataka main nanjaguda taluk ke devnoor gram main hua tha. unka pehla kahani sangrah dyavanuru 1973 main prakashit hua. 1981 main prakashit unke upanyaas oudalala ko bhartiya bhashaa parishad dwaara tathaa karnataka sarkaar dwaara rajyotsav prashasti se sammanit kiya gaya he. unke laghu upanyaas kusumbale ko sahitya academy kaa puraskaar praapt hua he.
