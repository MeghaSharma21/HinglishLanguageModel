 chilamkuri raja mohan bhartiya academik or videsh niti vishleshak hai. vartmaan main ve neshnal uniwarsity of singapore ke think tanks ‘‘dakshin asian adhyayan santhaan’’ ke nideshak ke pad par karyrat hai. 
 isse purv ve varsh 2012 se santhaan ke visiting professor ke rup main karyrat rahe hai. ullekhneey he ki dakshin asian adhyayan santhaan kii sthapana neshnal uniwarsity of singapore ke ek swytt shodh santhaan ke rup main kii gai thi. isse purv ve carnegi india ke sansthaapak nideshak the. ve research foundation, nai dilli ke objarwar tathaa centre for policy research, nai dilli main senior phelo rah chuke hai, saath hi ve purv main es rajtaratn skool of international studies main professor, nanyang technological vishvavidhyalay, singapore or dakshin, madhya, dakshinpurv asian or dakshinpaschim prashanth adhyayan centre ke professor, jawaharlal nehru vishvavidhyalay, nai dilli, bharat ke antarrashtriya adhyayan skool main bhi adhyaapan kaa kaary kar chuke hai. unhone institute for dephence studies and analysis, nai dilli se apane academik kariyar kii shuruyaat kii. ve 2009 -10 ke dauraan congress librari, washington, di.si. ke jon dablyoo claj centre main henry alfred kisinger ke saath scollar rah chuke hai. 
 dow mohan kii patrakarita main bhi sarthak upasthit rahi he. ve indian express, nai dilli ke samrik maamalon ke sampadak rah chuke hai, or usase pehle unhone di hindu akhbaar main rajanyik sampadak or washington samvaadadaata ke rup main kaary kiya. ve indian express ke stanbhakaar rah chuke hai.
