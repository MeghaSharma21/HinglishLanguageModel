 menier rog antajkarna kaa rog he jiske kaaran shravan shakti prabhavit ho sakati he or santulan banane kii kshamta kaa hraas ho saktaa he. iskaa pataa pehle francisi chikitsak menier ne 1861 i0 main lagaaya tha jinke naam par is rog kaa naam padaa.
 is rog kaa akraman adhikansh yuvavastha main hota he. rogi ko ekaek chakkar aane lagata, kaanon main jhanjhanaahat kii aawaaj hoti, ulti aati or anshat: bahraapan ho jaataa he. aise rogi swasth bhaag kii ore lete milte hai. duusari karavat letne se ulti aane lagati he, aankhon kii kaali putali vikrut parshv kii ore ho jaati he or rogi purnataya behosh ho jaataa he. achche hone par rogi ke bahere hone kaa bhay rahata he.
 iske utpann hone kaa kaaran teevr aantkarna raktasrav tathaa teevr pusjanak aantarkarnshoth he. kisi kisi vyakti main aadharbhoot dhamni ke asaadhaaran sphit, athwa abhyantar shravan dhamni ke bad jane, athwa anumastishk karnaktak arbud ityadi kaarnon se bhi yah rog ho saktaa he. in sabhi main mastishk kii aathvin naadee kaa pradhan kshetra avashya aakraant hota he.
 rogi ko purna vishram karne dena chahie, sir par barf kii topi rakhni chahie or taral aahaar dena chahie. namak bilkul na dena chahie. vaman ityadi kaa upachaar upyukt aushadhiyon dwaara karnaa chahie.
