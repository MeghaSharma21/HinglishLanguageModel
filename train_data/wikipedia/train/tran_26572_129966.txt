 suniti solomon ek bhartiya chikitsak he. 1985 main chennai main pehle bhartiya aids ke maamalon kaa nidaan karne ke baad microsibologist ne bharat main aids anusandhan or rokthaam kaa bidaa uthaaya tha. unhone chennai main aids anusandhan or shiksha ke liye vaai aar gayatonde centre kii sthapana kii. bhartiya sarkaar ne use rashtriya mahila jaev vaigyanik puraskaar se sammanit kiya. 25 janvari 2017 ko, bharat sarkaar ne aushadhi ke prati unke yogadan ke liye "padam shri" puraskaar se sammanit kiya. 
 vah chennai ke chamade ke vyapaariyon ke ek maharashtriyan hindu parivaar main paidaa hue thi. 2009 kii saakshaatkaar main usne kaha ki vah vaxin ke liye apane ghar main vaarshik swasthya adhikari se milane vaali dawaaon main ruchi leti hai. usne madras medical college main chikitsak kaa adhyayan kiya. sulaiman ne kings college hospital, landan main ek kanishtha chikitsak ke rup main kaam kiya tha. phir vah bharat me madras medical college main ek sookshm jeevigyaan ke rup main kaam kiya.
