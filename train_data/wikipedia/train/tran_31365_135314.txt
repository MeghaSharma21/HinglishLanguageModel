 aapla maanoos 2018 main bani marathi bhashaa kii film he.
 aapla manus 2018 ke ek bhartiya chalchitr he. yah marathi bhashaa main satish rajvaade dwaara nirdeshit kiya gaya he. is film ke nirmaataa ajay devgan he. film ke pramukh bhoomikaaon main naanaa patekar, iravati harshe or sumit raghavan jaise kalakaar hai. ek nirmaataa ke rup main ajay devgan kii yah pehali marathi film he. yah 9 farvari 2018 ko release hui he. is film ke kahani vivek bele ne likhaa he or iskke praayojak ajay devgan kii saath naanaa patekar, abhinav shukla, manish mishra, or rohit choudhary he. 
 film aapla manus ek shahari kshetra main rahane vaale yuva dampati kii kahani pe aadhaarit he. yah jodi apni pita ke saath rahata he, or ve rishte kii jatiltaaon ko samajhane kii saath hi shahari jivan se talmail banaaye rakhane kii koshish karte rahete hai. kuch apratyaashit ghatnaayen unke jivan main hoti hai, jisase unke vishwaas or jivit rahane ke tareeke main badlaav aata he.
