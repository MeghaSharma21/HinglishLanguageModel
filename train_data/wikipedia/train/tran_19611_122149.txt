 zimbabwe cricket team disambar, 2017 main dakshin africa daure par boxing de se shuru hone vaale din/raat ke match ke rup main chaar divasiy test match khilne ke liye nirdhaarit he. test match se pehle, zimbabwe or dakshin africa ke nimantran XI ke bich teen divasiy din/raat kaa match bhi hoga.
 chaar divasiy match hone ke baavjood cricket dakshin africa ne antarrashtriya cricket parishad se test match kii sthiti kaa darja dene ke liye manjuuri maangi he. isisi ne oakland main apni board kii baithak main oktober 2017 main test kii sthiti ke liye anurodh ko manjuuri di thi. isisi dwaara chaar divasiy test formet kaa pareekshan 2019 cricket vishwa cap tak chalegaa. isisi kii khilne kii sthiti ke anusaar, prayek din kam se kam 98 overon ko bold kiya jaataa he, paanch-divasiy match main 200 ran kii bajaay 150 ran kii badhat par follow-on set.
 haalanki, dakshin africa ke cricketron phalf du placi or dean algar ne chaar divasiy test match ke bare main chintaa vyakt kii. captain du plasis ne kaha ki "mujhe vishwaas he ki mahaan test maichon main paanchave din aakhri ghante ke aakhri ghanton tak chale gaye hai" or salaami ballebaaj algar ne kaha, "mujhe nahin lagata ki aapako aisa kuch nahin karnaa chahie jo tutaa nahin he".
