 mitali madhumita, bhartiya sena kii pehali mahila adhikari hai jinhen bahaduree puraskaar diya gaya hai. 26 farvari, 2010 ko afghanistan ke kabul main aatankwaadiyon dwaara bhartiya duutavas par hue hamale ke dauraan dikhaae gaye anukaraniya saahas ke liye leftinent karnal mitali madhumita ne 2011 main sena padak praapt kiya. iske alaava ve jammu-kashmir or bharat ke purvottar rajya main bhi apni seva de chuki thi. leftinent karnal madhumita bhartiya duutavas ke andar jaakar, jahaan hamalaa hua tha, kai ghayal nagarikon or senya karmiyon ko malbe se bachaaya tha. 2010 ke kabul duutavas hamale main saat bharatiyon sahit lagbhag unniis logon ne apni jaan ganvaa di.
 madhumita varsh 2000 main ek laghu seva kamishan ke tahat sena main shaamil hui. vah sainya shiksha kor kaa hissaa thim or sena ke angreji bhashaa prashikshan kaarykram ke hisse ke rup main afghanistan ke kabul main sevarat thim. madhumita ko jammu-kashmir or bhartiya rajya ke purvottar jaise samvedansheel kshetron main bhi tainaat kiya gaya tha. madhumita ke laghu seva kamishan ke adhikari se sthaayee kamishan ke liye sena se anurodh kiya, lekin raksha mantralay ne unke anurodh ko sweekaar karne se inkaar kar diya, madhumita ne raksha mantralay ke faisle ke viruddh march 2014 main sashastr bal nyaayaadhikaran se apil kii. nyaayaadhikaran ne paaya ki unke paas anurodh kii yogyata thi or farvari 2015 main raksha mantralay ko use behaal karne kaa nirdesh diya gaya tha. haalanki raksha mantralay ne bharat ke supreme court main sashastr bal nyaayaadhikaran ke aadesh ke khilaaf apil kii thi jisamen kaha gaya tha ki madhumita ne ek laghu seva kamishan par sena main shaamil hui thi. 2016 main bharat kii supreme court ne unhen bhartiya sena main sthaayee kamishan dene ke khilaaf raksha mantralay kii yachika ko khaarij kar diya.
