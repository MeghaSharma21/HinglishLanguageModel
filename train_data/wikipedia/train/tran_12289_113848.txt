 ghanchakkar 2013 main pradarshit bollywood hasya or rahasyamay film he jiskaa nirdesan rajkumaar gupta ne kiya he tathaa yah roni scruwala or siddharth raay kapur ne utivi moshan pikchars ke adhin nirmit kii. film main imran hashmi or vidya baalan mukhya bhoomika main hai. yah 28 june 2013 kp pradarshit hui.
 film main sanjay aatre, nitu kaa pati he. wo pandit or idris kii ek 30 karod rupye kii lut main madad karata he. teen maheene baad iske luti hui rakam ke bantwaare kii baat hoti he. sanjay, ko luti hui rakam ko hifaazat se rakhane kii zimmedaari saunpi jaati he. lekin baad main sanjay kii yaaddaasht chali jaati he. wo is lut ke saath-saath pandit or idris ke bare main bhi sab bhul chukaa hota he. vah ye bhi bhul jaataa he ki lut ke paise usne kahaan rakhe hai. or fir film aage badhati he.
 ghanchakkar ke geeton ko shabdabaddh amit trivedi ne kiya he or geet amitabh bhattacharya dwaara taiyaar kiya gaya.
 
 sabhi geet amitabh bhattacharya dwaara likhit.
