 bada imambada lakhanu kii ek aitihaasik dharohar he. ise bhulbhulaiya bhi kehete hai. ise asif uddaula ne banwaaya tha. lakhanu ke is prasiddh imambara kaa aitihaasik or saanskrithik mahatv he. 
 is imambara kaa nirmaan asafuddaula ne 1784 main akaal raahat pariyojna ke antargat karavaaya tha. yah vishaal gumbadnuma haul 50 meter lanbaa or 15 meter ooncha he. anumaanatah ise banane main us jamaane main paanch se das lakh rupye kii laagat ai thi. yahi nahin, is imaarat ke puraa hone ke baad bhi nawab isaki saaj sajja par hi chaar se paanch lakh rupye salanaa kharch karte the.
 is imambara main ek affphi masjid bhi he jahaan gaiyr muslim logon ko pravesh kii anumati nahin he. masjid parisar ke angan main do oonchi minaaren hai.
 isamen vishwa-prasiddh bhulbhulaiya bani he, jo anachaahe pravesh karne vaale ko rasta bhula kar aane se rokatii thi. iskaa nirmaan nawab ne rajya main pade durbhiksh se nibatane hetu kiya tha. isamen ek gaharaa kuna bhi he.ek kahawat he ke jise na de mola use de asafoouddaula.
