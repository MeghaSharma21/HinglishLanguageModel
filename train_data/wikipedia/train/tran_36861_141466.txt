 kits berlin or germany ke uttari bhaag ke kai shaharon ke muhallon ko kaha jaataa he.
 madhyakaal main kai german samudaay purv kii ore jaakar pashchimi slav logon ke kshetron main pravesh karke vahaan bas gaye. slaavon or jarmaini logon ke mishran se german bhashaa main kai slavi bhashaaon ke shabd daakhil ho gaye. inamen 'khitza' bhi ek tha, jiskaa arth 'kutiyaa' yaa 'ghar' he. yahi shabd german baseron ke saath lagi slavi bastiyon ke liye prayog hone laga or dhire-dhire isane 'muhalle' kaa arth dhaaran kar liya.
 germany main kits shabd aupchaarik rup se keval berlin or kuch anya shaharon main hota he. germany ke anya bhaagon main iskaa prayog kathboli mana ja saktaa he. vishwa ke anya bhaagon se germany main aakar basane vaale logon ke muhallon main unki puraani bhashaa se prabhavit german boli ko bhi kabhi-kabhi 'kits-german' yaa 'kits-doyech' kaha jaataa he.
