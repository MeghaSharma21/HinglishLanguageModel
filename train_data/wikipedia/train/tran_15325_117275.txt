 dandyuddh ek pracheen bhartiya marshall art he jisamen baans kii lathi kaa upyog hathiyaar ke taur par kiya jaataa he. isamen pratidwandi ko dur bhagane ke liye takat tathaa kala kaa prayog kiya jaataa he. aatmaraksha ke atirikt iskaa prayog anushaasan, sahyog tathaa aatmaniyantran kaa vikaas karne ke liye bhi kiya jaataa he.
 dand kii lambaai abhyaasakarta kii oonchaai par nirbhar karti he. yah kandhe se kaan kii oonchai tak honi chaiye. shuru main abhyaas ke liye halkaa tathaa chota dand upyukt hota he. acchhee tarah sikh jane par asli yuddh main baari tathaa apekshaakrit lambi lathi kaa prayog hota he.
 dandyuddh ke devtaa kartikeya tathaa sansthaapak acharya maharishi agastya maane jate hai. kartikeya devataaon ke senapati the tathaa unhen yuddh kala kaa gyaan apane pita bhagwaan shiv se praapt hua tha. kartikeya kaa ek naam dandpani bhi he. mana jaataa he ki maharishi agastya ne shiv kii krupa se kartikeya se yuddh kalaon kaa gyaan praapt kiya tathaa varmkallai, marm ati, silmbum aadi yuddh kalaon kii sansthapana kii. maharishi agastya ne yuddh kala par anek pustaken bhi likhi jinamein dand par 'compusutram' naamak pustak shaamil he. maharishi agastya ne yah kala anya sidhon ko sikhaayi jo ki kaalaantar se shishy parampara dwaara aage badhati rahi.
 vartmaan main dandyuddh kaa abhyaas mukhyatah rashtriya swayamsevak sangh evam iske anushangik sanghathanon dwaara apane prashikshan shiviron main kiya jaataa he. dand sangh ke purna ganavesh kaa ek hissaa he. sangh kii dandyuddh style tamilnadu ke marshall art silmbum par aadhaarit he.
