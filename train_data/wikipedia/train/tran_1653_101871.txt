  faisal toun, pakistan ke punjab praant kii rajdhani lahore shahar kaa ek ilaaka or ek union parishad he. yah lahore kaa ek pramukh ilaaka he. shahar ke anya ilaaqon ke samaan hi yahan bole jane vaali pramukh bhashaa punjabi he, jabaki urdu praayah har jagah samajhi jaati he. saath hi angrezi bhi kai logon dwaara samajhi or shiksha tathaa vyavsaay ke kshetra main upyog kii jaati he. prabhukh prashasnic bhashaayen urdu or angrezi hai. lahore ke vanijyik evam aarthik mahatv ke kaaran yahan pakistan ke lagbhag saare praanton ke log vaas karte hai.
