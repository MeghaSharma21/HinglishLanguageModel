 razmanama mahabharat kaa farsi anuwaad he jo ki mogul badshaah akbar ke samay main karavaaya gaya tha. 1574 main akbar ne fatehpur sikri main makatabakhaanaa shuru kiya. iske saath akbar ne rajtarangini, ramayana, vakaira jaise sanskrit granthon ko farsi main anuwaad karne ke liye himaayat di.
 razmanama kii teen pratiyaan he pratham jaipur ajaayabaghar main, dusri 1599 main taiyaar jo vartmaan samay main vividh sangrahalay main rakhi gai he or antim razmanama rahim kaa he jo 1616 main pura hua tha. razmanama apane chitron ke kaaran mahatvapurn he. jaipur prat ke mukhya chitrakaar dashavanth, vashavant or laal he. i.sa. 1599 kii prat ke mukhya chitrakaar asee, dhanu or fatu he.
 mahabharat ko farsi main anuwaad karne kaa hukum 1582 main diya gaya or iske 1 lakh shlokon kaa anuwaad 1584 se 1586 tak kiya gaya. is anuwaad ke dauraan mushafiq ne tasveeron banane kaa kaary kiya jiske saath mahabharat kii katha ko samajhane main suvidha hoti he. is samay is anuwaad kii ek nakal jaipur shahar ke "mahal" ajaayabaghar main maujuud he. 
 is kitaab kaa doosraa anuwaad 1598 se 1599 ke dauraan main kiya gaya. pehle anuwaad kii tulana main doosraa anuwaad main zyaada vistaar he. isamen 161 chitra shaamil the. is anuwaad kii kai nakal shahi khandaan ke sadasyon ko tohafe ke taur par deen gai thim.
