 aadmi or insaan san 1969 main pradarshit va yash chopra dwaara nirdeshit hindi film he jisamen dharmendra, syra bano, firoz khan, mumtaz tathaa madan puri aadi mukhya bhoomika main he. ye iklauti film he jisamen dharmendra va yash chopra ne saath kaam kiya he.
 munish or jaykishan do gahare mitra he or ek hi ladki minaa se pyaar karte he lekin durbhagyavash galatfahami ke kaaran donon ek-duusare ke shatru ban jate he. munish ek jaanch adhikari ke taur par justise bean desai ke katl kii jaanch-partal main jaykishan kaa haath paata he. tab vah apane dost ko bachaane ke liye khud kii qurbaani dena chahta he lekin ant main jaykishan sari sachai jaanane ke baad adaalat main apana jurm kabool kar letaa he
