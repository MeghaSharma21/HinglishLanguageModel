 padpo main vibhaajan kii kshamta vaale avibhedit koshikaon ke samooh ko vibhajyotak utak yaa merristami utak kehete hai. vibhajyotak kii koshikaaye saamaan vyas vaali ytaakar yaa bahubhuji hoti hai. in koshikaon main kendrak bada, jeevadravya saghan, tathaa riktikavihin hoti hai. in koshikaon ke bich main khaali sthaan yani intervakoshik sthal anupasthit hota he tathaa in koshikaon main mitocandria ko sankhya adhik hoti he.
