 peter gustaaf lajan diricklay germany ke mahaan ganitagya the. diricklay ne berlin tathaa gotingene main shikshan kaary kiya tathaa mukhyatah ganitiya vishleshan evam sankhya siddhanth ke kshetra main kaary kiya.
 diricklay kaa janm 13 farvari, 1805 i0 ko dhuran main hua. praarambhik shiksha bon or colon main praapt karke ye ganit ke adhyayan ke liye paris gaye. vahaan inhone gouse kii "discuijisyoness aritmetike" kaa adhyayan kiya or uske anek sthalon ko ganitagyon ke liye sahaj bodhgamy kar diya. 1824 i0 main inhone siddh kiya ki n = 5 ke liye pherma kaa antim prameya satya he, arthath unhone siddh kiya ki a, b, c ke kisi bhi ashoonya purnank maanon ke liye, a5 + b5 = c5 santusht nahin hoga. 1849 i0 ke lekh, eubar di bestimmung dair mittaleran bairthe in dair tshrontheori main, inhone aise vyapak siddhant die jinpar dhanatmak evam rinatmak saranikon ke dwivarnak vargatmak rup kii ausat sankhya nirbhar he. abhajya sankhyaon kii ore bhi inhone dhyaan diya or seemaant maan ke prashnon ko hal karne ke diricklay niyam kaa avishkaar kiya, parantu virustrus kii aalochana ke kaaran iskaa ab keval aitihaasik mahatv rah gaya he. 5 may, 1859 i0 ko inkaa swargvaas ho gaya.
