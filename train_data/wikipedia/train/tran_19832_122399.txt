 rajyapal yaa governor shashan karne vaale aise vyakti ko kehete hai jo kisi desh ke shaasha ke adheen ho or us desh ke kisi bhaag par shashan kar rahaa ho. sangheey deshon main sangh ke rajyon par shashan kane vaale vyaktiyon ko aksar rajyapal kaa kitaab diya jaataa he. kuch sanyukt rajya america jaise ganatantron main rajyapal sidhe us rajya kii janata dwaara chune jate hai or vah us rajya ke sarvocch prashasnic adhyaksh hote hai, jabaki bharat jaise sansadeey ganatantron main aksar rajyapal kendriya sarkaar yaa shaasha niyukt karti he or vah naam maatr kaa adhyaksh hota he .
