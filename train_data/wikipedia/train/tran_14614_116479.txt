 ritu menon ek bhartiya nariwadi or vampanthi lekhika tathaa prakaashak hai. unhone 1984 main urvashi butalia ke saath milakar bharat kii pehali feminist publicing house kii sah sthapana kii. yah sanstha lambey samay tak mahila adhikaron ke liye sangharshrat rahi hai. varsh 2003 main menon or butalia ke bich kattar virdohi vyaktigat matbhedon ke falswaroop yah sanstha band ho gayi or uske baad menon ne swatantra rup se mahilaon ke aseemit adhikaron ke alok main ek or nariwadi publicing house kii sthapana kii. unhone vibhinn samachar patron main apane lekhon ke madhyam se mahilaon ke khilaaf hinsa tathaa dharm kii aad main mahilaon ke adhikaar hanan ke saath-saath samaaj main ling vibhaajan par khulkar apni awaz buland kii hai. 
 varsh-2011 main sahitya or shiksha ke kshetra main ullekhneey yogadan hetu unhen urvashi butalia ke saath padmashri pradaan kiya gaya.
