 brick
 brick yani biaraisi vishwa kii sarvaadhik teji se viksit hoti arthvyavasthaaon vaale vikasshil deshon- brazil, rus, bharat or china kaa sanghathan he. is shabd kaa pehali baar prayog 2001 main goldman shash ne kiya tha. ye chaaron desh sanyukt rup se vishwa kaa ek-chauthai kshetra gherte hai or inaki jansankhya vishwa kii kul aabaadi kaa 40 pratishat se adhik he. in deshon kaa sakal gharelu utpaada 15.435 trillian dollar he.
 goldman shash kaa tark tha ki brazil, rus, china or bharat kii arthvyavasthaaen aarthik rup se itani majboot hai ki 2050 tak ye chaaron arthvyavasthaaen vaishvik paridrishy par haaavee hogi. haalanki shash kaa ye uddeshy katai nahin tha ki ye chaaron desh raajnneetik gathjod kar kisi sanghathan kaa nirmaan karen. shash kaa akalan tha ki bharat or china utpaadit vastuon or sarvice ke sabase bade pradaata honge, vahiin brazil or rus kachche maal ke sabase bade utpaadak hai. brazil jahaan louh ayskon kii aapurti main pratham he to rus tel evam prakritik gas ke maamle main sheersh par he.
