 patiganit shridharacharya dwaara sanskrit main rachit ganit granth he. 'paati' se taatpary gananaa-vidhi se he.
 patiganit kavy ke rup main he. koi niyam bataane ke baad shridharacharya ek-do aankik prashn poochhate hai kintu ve na to inkaa hal bataate hai na uttar. 
 iskaa armbh mangalacharan se hota he. iske baad 'paribhaasha' he jisamen mudra, dravyaman, lambaai, aaytan aadi ke maatrakon or unke aapasi sambandhon kaa varnan he. iske baad 'parikrm' kaa adhyay he jisamen varg, ghan, vargamul, ghanmul aadi nikaalane ke 16 parikrmon ke algoridm varnit hai. iske aage ke adhyyon main kalaswarna, trairashik, vyastatrairashik, panchasptanvarashik, bhandpratibhandam, jeevvikray ke naam ke adhyay hai. iske baad 'vyavahaara' ke antargat dhanaprayogh, suvarnganitam, prakshepaganitam, vividhvishyah, shredhivyavahaara, kshetravyavahaara naamak adhyay hai
 patiganit main shridhar ne gole kaa aaytan nikaalane kaa ek bahut acha niyam diya he. isamen shunya ke saath parikrm ke aath niyam die gaye hai. iske alaava vibhinn samaantar shreniyon or gunottar shreniyon ke yoga kii vidhi bataai gai he.
