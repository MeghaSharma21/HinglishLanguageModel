 vishwa maamalon kii bhartiya parishad bhartiya buddhijiviyon ke ek samooh dwaara ek think tanks ke rup main 1943 main sthapit kiya gaya tha. yah sosayadi adhinium 1860 ke panjikaran ke antargat ek gaiyr-sarkari, gaiyr raajnneetik or gaiyr laabhakaari sanghathan ke rup main panjikrut kiya gaya tha. 2001 main sansad ke ek adhinium dwaara, ise 'rashtriya mahatv kii sanstha' ghoshit kiya gaya he. bharat ke uparashtrapati isidablue ke paden adhyaksh hai.
 yah antarrashtriya sambandhon or videsh maamalon ke adhyayan ke liye vishesh rup se samarpit he.
