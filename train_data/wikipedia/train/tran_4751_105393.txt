 disambar gregorian calendar ke hisaab se varsh kaa barahwaan or aakhri maheenaa he, saath hi yah un saat gregory mahino main se ek he jiname 31 din hote hai. latin main, disem kaa matlab "das" hota he. disambar bhi roman calendar kaa dasvaan maheenaa tha jab tak ki masvihin sardiyon kii avadhi ko janvari or farvari ke bich vibhajit nahin kar diya gaya. disambar se sambandhit phool holly yaa narsisus he. disambar ke ratna firoza, lapis lajuli, jircon, pukhraj, yaa tenzant rahe hai. disambar maah main uttari golarddh main din ke sabase kam or dakshini golarddh main sabase adhik ghante hote hai. disambar or sitambar saptaah ke ek hi din se shuru hote hai.
