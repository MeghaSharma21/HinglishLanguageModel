 tikt kaa arth "kadava"
 samtal deshon se aa-akarpavas kii umas se akulatikt-madhur bisatantu khojatehanso ko tirte dekha habadal ko ghirte dekha he.
 tikt kaa ek arth "mirch ke swaad jaisaa" bhi hota he.tikta kaa arth neem kaa ped hota he.
 tikt shabd kaa prayog hindi or maithili ke pramukh lekhak va kavi aadarneey nagarjun ji kii rachana "badal ko ghirte dekha he" naamak kavita main hua he.
