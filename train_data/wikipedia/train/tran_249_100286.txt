   hind irani  hind arya  romani
 romani bhashaa hind europea bhashaai parivaar ke andar aati he. ye hind irani shakha ke hind arya upashakha ke antargat vargeekrut he. hind-arya bhashaayen wo bhashaayen hai jo sanskrit se utpann hui hai. hindi, urdu, kashmiri, bengali, oriya, punjabi, marathi jaisi bhashaayen hind-arya bhashaayen hai.
 romani europe ke banjaaron dwaara boli jaati he. ye baari maatra main anya europea bhashaaon se mishrit he.romani bhashaa ke vishleshan se pataa chalaa he ki yah madhya or uttari bhartiya upmahadweep main boli jane vaali bhashaaon se sambandhit he. is bhashaai sambandh ko romani logon ke bhugolic mul kaa sanket mana jaataa he. ve mul rup se bhartiya upmahadweep ke yaa ab ke uttari bharat or pakistan ke kuch hisson aae the. romani bhashaa ko aamtaur par madhya hind-arya bhashaaon main shaamil kiya jaataa he. romani bhashaa kabhi kabhi boliyon ke ek samooh yaa sambandhit bhashaaon kaa ek sangrah, jisame ek anuvanshik upasamooh ke sabhi sadsy shaamil hai, mana jaataa he.romani bhashaa kisi bhi raashtra kii adhikarik bhashaa nahin he, par kai deshon main ise alpsankhyak bhashaa kaa darja diya jaataa he. ve desh jinamein romani jansankhya adhik he, vahaan ab romani bhashaa kii vibhinn boliyaan samhitabaddh kii jane kii prakriya main hai.
