 randhir kapur hindi filmon ke ek prasiddh abhinayta hai.raaj kapoorji ke suputr tathaa karishma or karina kapur ke pita jinhone sattar,assike dashakme kaaphi dhooma machaayi thi. . ek behtareen or unke bahot jolly andaajako chaahne vaala unka ek khaas darshak varg bana. unki filme jaise .. javaani divaani,haath ki safai,rampur kaa lakshmana,dhongi,khalipha,kaccha chor,kal aaj or kal,ponga pandit,kasme vaade,bivi o bivi,bhavar,harjaai,humraahi,chachaa bhatija,ram bharose,aaj kaa mahatma,lafange,khaliph,aakhri daaku,pukaar yah filme,unke gane logonko bahot pasand aaye. . randhiraji ne nirdesan diya dharam karam,hina,prem granth in filmonko bhi acchhee safalataa mili. .. 'kishor kumaar'unke hameshaa chahete gayak rahe hai. randhir kapoorji zyaadaatar apane khudke plabac ke liye kishorada kaa aawaaj lete rahe. .is jodi ke kai gane jaise 'rampurka kaa vaasi hu main lachman meraa naam','gum he kisiike pyarme dil subah shaam'or film javaani divaani kaa 'ja ne ja dhundata phir rahaa' tathaa 'bhavare kii gunjan e meraa dil' yah gane log aajbhi gunagunaate rahete hai. .
