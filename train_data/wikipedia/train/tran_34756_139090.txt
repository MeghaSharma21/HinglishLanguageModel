 udar, jise anaupchaarik rup se kabhi-kabhi pet bhi kah diya jaataa he, maanav or kuch anya kasheruki praaniyon main sharir ke aage kaa chaati or kulha ke bich kaa hissaa hota he. udar main sharir ke andar udar guha kahalaane vaali ek shaareerik guha sthit hoti he, jisamen amashay, yakrut, pittashay, tilli, agnyashay, aante, gurde or adhivrikk granthi jaise mahatvapurn ang sthit hote hai.
