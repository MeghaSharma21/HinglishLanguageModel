 ienes kadamtt bhartiya nausena kaa ek pandubbi nirodhi yudhpot he jiskaa jalavataran 7 janvari 2016 ko kiya gaya. yah pariyojna 28 ke antargat doosraa pot he. is pariyojna main isse pehle ienes camorta ko 23 agast 2014 ko nausena main shaamil kiya gaya tha.
 ienes kadamtt kaa naam bharat ke pashchimi chor par sthiti lakshyadweep dweep samoohon ke ek dweep par rakhaa gaya he. lakshyadweep dweep samoohon or nausena kaa vishesh sambandh he or yahaan ienes dweeparakshak kaa base sthit he.
 ienes kadamtt total atomospherick control system, integrated platform menagement system, integrated brij system, battle damege control system or parasenal loketor system se las he. ullekhneey he ki is pot ko ‘mek in india’ ke lakshya ke tahat nirmit kiya gaya he. jahaaj kaa lagbhag 90 pratishat hissaa desh main hi taiyaar kiya gaya he or ise parmaanu, jaivik or rasinic yuddh ke halaat se nipatane ke yogya banaaya gaya he. jahaaj ke hathiyaar or samvedi upkaran desh main hi taiyaar kiye gayi hai, jinamein combat management system, rocket launture, tarpedo tub launture or inphra-red signechar sapressant system shaamil hai.
