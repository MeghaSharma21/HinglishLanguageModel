 samay yatra, ek avadharana he jiske anusaar, samay main vibhinn binduon ke bich thik usee prakaar sanchalan kiya ja saktaa he jis prakaar antariksh ke vibhinn binduon ke bich bhraman kiya jaataa he. is avadharana ke anusaar kisi vastu ko samay main vartmaan kshan se kuch kshan peeche ateet main yaa phir vartmaan kshan se kuch kshan aage bhavishya main, bina do binduon ke bich kii avadhi ko anubhav kiye, bhej sakate hai. . 
 haalanki samay yatra 19veen shatabdi ke baad se hi kaalpanik kahaniyon kaa ek mukhya vishay rahaa he, parantu bhavishya kii ekatarfa yatra to samay failaav kii ghatana ke kaaran saidhantik rup se sambhav he, yah ghatana vishesh saapekshata ke siddhant main varnit veg par aadhaarit he . yah yatra saamaanya saapekshata ke siddhant main gurutvkarshan samay failaav ke anusaar bhi sambhav he, par abhi tak yah agyaat he ki bhautikee ke niyam is prakaar kii pashchgaami samay yatra kii anumati denge yaa nahin.yatra karne ke liye kuch vaigyanico ne samaantar bramhand kii kalpana kii he;koi bhi takneekee yantra jisase samay main ek bindu se duusare bindu par bina kisi samay main deree ke jaaya ja sake samay yantra kehalaata he.
