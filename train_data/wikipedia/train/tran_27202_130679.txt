 susheel kumaar scindia
 
 susheel kumaar scindia bhartiya rashtriya congress se sambanddh ek bhartiya raajnneetijna he. purv main bharat ganraajya ke kendriya grih mantri the tathaa pandrahavin loksabhaa ke maharashtra se saansad he. ve purv main maharashtra ke mukhyamantri or andhra pradesh ke rajyapalrah chuke he.
 sanyukt pragatisheel gathbandhan kii sarkaar main kendriya bijli mantri bhi rah chuke he.
