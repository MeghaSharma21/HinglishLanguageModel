 angushth yaa angutha yaa haath kii anguliyon main se ek he.
 maanav jaise kuch praaniyon main haath kaa angutha modakar anya ungaliyon kii ore laayaa ja saktaa he. yah sammukh angutha vastuen pakadaney or prayog karne kii kshamta pradaan karata he or narwanar gana ke praaniyon ke panpane kaa or maanviya sabhyataa kaa ek mul kaaran samajha jaataa he.
