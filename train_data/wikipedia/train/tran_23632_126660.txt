 zarafashaan parvat shrunkhlaa madhya asia main tajikistan or uzbekistan kii ek parvat shrunkhlaa he jo pamir-alaay parvat mandal kaa ek bhaag he. yah 370 kimi lambi shrunkhlaa zarafashaan nadi se dakshin main vistrut hai or purv-pashchim disha main tajikistan ke subaad praant ke dakshin hisse se guzarati he. zarafashaan parvaton kaa sabase unchaa pahaad 5,489 meter unchaa chimtarga parvat he. subaad praant ke punjakant shahar se aage yah shrunkhlaa uzbekistan main daakhil ho jaati he jahaan iske pahaado kii oonchaai 1,500 se 2,000 meter tak kam hoti jaati he. uzbekistan main zarafashaan parvat samarkand praant or surakhandariyaa praant ke bich kii prantiy sarhad maane jate hai. yahan se aage jaakar yah pahaad dhire-dhire samarkand se dakshin-pashchim ke registani kshetra main lupt ho jate hai.
