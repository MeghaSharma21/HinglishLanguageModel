 antariksh se barf-dhaki lebanon parvatamala or purvi lebanon parvatamala 
 purvi lebanon parvatamala madhya purv ke syria or lebanon deshon ke bich dakshinpaschim se uttarpurv 150 kimi tak chalane vaali ek parvat shrunkhlaa he. is shrunkhlaa ke adhiktar pahaad syria main hai or lebanon-syria antarrashtriya seemaa isi shrunkhlaa kii chotiyon par chalati he. yah shrunkhlaa lebanon parvatamala se purv main he or uske baraabar chalati he.
 purvi lebanon parvaton ke dakshin main golan unchaaiyaan hai, jo syria or israel ke bich ek vivaadit kshetra he. is kshetra main is parvatamala se sabase unche parvat hai: 2,814 meter unchaa jabal al-sheikh or 2,669 meter unchaa talat musa. iske pashchim main uttar kii taraf beqaaa vaadi he or dakshin main hasabani nadi kii ghati he. yah donon ghaatiyaan is purvi lebanon shrunkhlaa ko lebanon shrunkhlaa se alag karti hai. purvi lebanon parvaton ke purv main syria kaa purvi pathaar he jisapar us desh kii rajdhani damishq sthit he. uttar main yah parvatamala syria ke homes shahar se zaraa pehle jaakar khatm ho jaati he.
