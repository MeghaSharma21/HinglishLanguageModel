 rajasthan sarkaar ne balika ke paidaa hone ko shubh maanane or kanya bhrun hatya ko rokane ke liye shubhalakshmi yojana shuru kii he. yojana ke antargat betiyon ke paidaa hone par ladki ke mata-pita ko 2100 rupiye sarkaar dwaara die jaayenge. isi prakaar se jab betiyon kii umr padhane kii ho jaayegi tab unhen 3100 rupiye kaa dhanadesh yaa chack diya jayega. ek or vishesh baat yah he ki is yojana ke antargat ladki ke vivaah ke samay ladki kii mata ko 7300 rupiye kaa dhanadesh die jane kaa praavdhaan he.
 kanya bhrun hatya ko rokane tathaa betiyon ko shikshit karaane ke liye is yojana ko shuru kiya gaya he.
