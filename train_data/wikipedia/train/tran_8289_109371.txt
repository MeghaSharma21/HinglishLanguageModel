 kabir panth yaa satguru kabir panth bharat ke bhaktikalin kavi kabir kii shikshaaon par aadhaarit ek sampradaay he. kabir ke shishy dharmadas ne unke nidhan ke lagbhag sau saal baad is panth kii shuruyaat kii thi. prarambh main darshanik or naitik shiksha par aadhaarit yah panth kalantar main dharmik sampradaay main parivartit ho gaya. kabir panth ke anuyaayiyon main hindu, musalmaan, buddha or jain sabhi dharmon ke log shaamil hai. inamen bahutayat hinduon kii he. kabir kii rachanaaon kaa sangrah bijak is panth ke darshanik or aadhyatmik chintan kaa aadhaar granth he.
 apane kavy main kabir ne panth ko mahatvaheen bataate hue uskaa uphaas udaaya he. "aisa jog na dekha bhai. bhuulaa phirai liye gaphilai. mahadev ko panth chalaavai. aiso bado mahanth kahavai." kabir panth kaa adhyayan karne vaale kedarnath dvivedi ke anusaar iskaa koi spasht pramaan nahin he ki panth kii sthapana kabir ne swayan kii. kabir kii mrityu ke pashchaat unke shishyon ne yah kaary kiya. 
 dvivedi kabir panth kii sthapana kii prishthbhumi kaa ullekh karte hue likhte hai ki, "kaha jaataa he ki sabase pehle santon main nanak ne hi panth rachana kaa sutarapaat kiya tha or unhone uske kuch niyam bhi banaaye the. sambhawatah nanakdev ke ananthar hi kabir panth kii sthapana hui hogi....dadupathi raghavdas ne apane hastlikhit granth bhaktamaal main dharmadas ko kabir kaa shishy kaha he. chhattisgadhi shakha kaa itihaas prastut karte samay aage chalakar dharmadas ke aavirbhaav kii tithi satrahavin shatabdi ke pratham charan ke lagbhag siddh karne kaa prayaas kiya gaya he. sambhawatah dharmadas ne hi panth ko vyapak banane ke liye sarvapratham thos kadam uthaaya tha."
 bharat main kabir panth kii mukhyatah teen shakhaayen maani jaati hai. kaashi vaali shakha, dhanauti vaali bhagatahi shakha or chhattisgarh vaali shakha. in shakhaon ke sansthaapak kramshah shruti gopal sahab, bhagwaan gosai tathaa muktamani naam sahab ko mana jaataa he.
