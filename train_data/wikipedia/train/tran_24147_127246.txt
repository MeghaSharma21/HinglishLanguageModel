 japan 47 prashasnic praanton main bata hua he jinhen japani bhashaa main "todophukan" or angrezi main "prifakchar" kaha jaataa he -
 har praant kaa ek rajyapal hota he jise japani main "chiji" kehete hai. ise us praant ke naagarik chunaavon main chunate hai. har praant kii ek "gikaaye" naam kii vidhaan sabha hoti he jiske daayre main nae kaanoon kaa bananaa or aarthik bajet hota he. iske sadsy chaar saal ke kaarykaal ke liye nagarikon dwaara chune jate hai.
 har praant ko nagaron or zilon main baanta jaataa he. prayek zile main shahar or gaanv hote hai.
