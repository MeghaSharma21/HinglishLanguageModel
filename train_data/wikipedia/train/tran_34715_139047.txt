 loktantr ke upyog kaa itihaas lanbaa or oonchanich se bharaa hua he. bharat ke pracheen ganatantron athwa europe ke athensi loktantr kaa itihaas hi do hajaar varsh se bhi adhik purana he or ham dekhte hai ki us kaal ke maanav samaaj main bhi loktantreey sansthaaen kisi-na-kisi rup main vidyaman thim.
 maanviya gatividhi jaise-jaise vyapak rup leti gai, maanav drushti main bhi vyapaktaa aati gai. naagarik samaaj nirmaan karne kii aakanksha ne manushya ko loktantr kii ore agrasar hone ke liye prerit kiya kyonki yahi aisi vyavastha he jisamen sarsaadhaaran ko adhiktam bhagidaari kaa avasar miltaa he. isse keval nirnay karne kii prakriya hi main nahin apitu kaaryakari kshetra main bhi bhagidaari uplabdh hoti he. apane vikaas kram ke vibhinn charnon main loktantr ne bhinn-bhinn paristhitiyon ko anyaanya maatra main sunishchit karne kaa prayaas kiya he.
