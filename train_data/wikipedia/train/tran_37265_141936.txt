 1971 europea cap final june 1971, 2 par, wembali stadium main landan main yojit ek footbal match tha, yunaan kii panthinakos ko 2-0 se netherlands kii ajaks haraa diya. panthinakos ek europea cap ke final main pahunchane vaale pehle yunaan paksh ban gaya. is ajaks ke liye ek pankti main teen europea cap jeet kii ek shrinkhalaa ke pehle tha.
