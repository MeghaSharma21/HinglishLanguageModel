 bill or melinda gates santhaan jise gates santhaan bhi kaha jaataa he. varsh 2000 main bill gates or melinda gates ne isaki shuruyaat kii thi. iskaa mukhya lakshya america main swasthya ke kshetra main sudhaar karnaa tha. jise baad main shiksha ke kshetra main bhi badha diya gaya. yah vishwa ke kuch sabase bade nijee sansthaaon main se ek he.
 1997 main yah wiliam gates santhaan sthapit kiye. is samay vah 2 billian dollar is santhaan ko us varsh diya tha. 15 june 2006 ko gates ne kaha ki ve dhire dhire ab microsoft se hatenge. 31 july 2008 ko vah santhaan ko or bhi samay dene lage. bill or melinda ko varsh 2005 main unke daan ke kaaran 2005 varsh kaa vyakti ke rup main naam diya gaya.
