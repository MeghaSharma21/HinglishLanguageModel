 lalitvistar sutara, mahayan buddha sampradaay kaa granth he. isamen bhagwaan buddha kii lilaon kaa varnan he. isaki rachana kisi ek vyakti ne nahin kii balki isaki rachana main kai vyaktiyon kaa yogadan he. iskaa rachana kaal isa ke pashchaat teesri shatabdi mana gaya he. isamen 27 adhyay hai.
 lalitvistar ke ek aakhyaan main bataaya gaya he ki kaise ek baar gautam buddha ko 1 se lekar 421 shunya vaali sankhya tak ko ginane ke liye kaha gaya tha. isamen sabase badi sankhya '10 par 145 ghaat' kaa ullekh miltaa he. sabase badi baat he ki badi sankhyaon ke naam bhi diye gayi hai.
