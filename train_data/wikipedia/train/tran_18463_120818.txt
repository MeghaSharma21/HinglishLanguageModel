 sujangadh rajasthan ke thali kshetra main sthit churu jile kaa ek nagar he. yah bikaner se 72 mile purv-dakshin main marwar kii seemaa se mila hua basaa he. is sthaan kaa purana naam 'kharboo ji kaa' kot tha. maharaja surat singh ne 1835 i0 main is kshetra ko adhikaar main lekar iskaa naam sujansingh ke naam par sujangadh rakhaa. yahaan purana kila ab bhi vidyaman he. yahaan 27 mandiren, 17 masjiden or kai dharamshalaein he.aazaadee se pehle sujangadh jila tha.vaikataishwar balaji kaa utar bharat kaa sabase khubasurat mandir  bana hua he.
