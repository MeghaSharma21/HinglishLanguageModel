 benin main atakora pahadiyaan
 togo pahadiyaan africa ke pashchimi bhaag main sthit togo desh main ek parvatamala he jo us desh ke madhya se guzarati he or purv main benin desh or pashchim main ghana desh main bhi jaati hai. ghana main yah pahadiyaan akvapim pahaad or benin main atakora pahaad ke naam se jaani jaati hai. bhootkaal main africa junglee kutta is parvatamala main rahata tha lekin ab use is bhubhag se khadeda ja chukaa he.
