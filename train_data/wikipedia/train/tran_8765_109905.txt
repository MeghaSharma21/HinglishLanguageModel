 rahamaan hindi filmon ke ek abhinayta the. jinka karier 1940 ke dashak ke uttarardh se lekar 1970 ke dashak ke ant tak failaa tha. vah guru dutt team kaa ek abhinn hissaa the, or zyaadaatar pyaar kii jeet, bady bahan, pyaasa, sahib bibi or gulam, dil ne fir yaad kiya, or vakat jaisi filmon main unki bhoomika ke liye jane jate the.
 rahamaan, college ke baad royal indian vayusena main shaamil ho gaye or puna main payalat ke rup main prashikshit hue. jald hi bombai main filmon main kariyar ke liye unhone vayusena kii naukari ko chhod diya. vah apane sauhardpurn parishkrut bhoomikaaon ke liye sabase zyada jane jate hai, jo unke vyaktitv ke anukool hai. pune main vishnam bedekar ke tisare sahaayak nideshak ke rup main unke film karier kii shuruyaat hui. vishnam ko ek afgaan kii jarurat thi jo pashtun pagadi baandh saktaa tha. rahamaan aisa kar sakate the, ek pashtun hone ke naate, or vah unhen kuch pramukh bhoomikaaon ke liye screen par laayaa. nayak ke rup main unki pramukh hits main se ek pyaasa ke saath pyaar kii jeet thi, or geet- "ek dil ke tukede hazaar hu, koi yaaha gira, koi vaha gira" ek pramukh hit tha. 
 shuruyaat main unhone mukhya bhoomika nibhaai, lekin samay bitne ke baad, unhone sahaayak bhoomika nibhaai or kuch hit filmon jaise pyaasa, chaudaavin kaa chaand, sahib bibi or ghulaam or vakt jo unki yaadgaar bhoomikaaon main se kuch the.
 1977 main unhen teen dil ke daure kaa saamana karnaa padaa, jiske baad unhen gale kaa cancer bhi ho gaya or 1984 main lambii or dardanaak beemaari ke baad unki mrityu ho gai.
