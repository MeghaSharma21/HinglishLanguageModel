 ram nath shastri dogri kavi, naatakkaar, koshkar, nibandhkaar, shikshashasthri, anuvadak tathaa sampadak the. unhone dogri bhashaa ke utthan or vikaas main mahatvapurn bhoomika nibhaai. unhen 'dogri kaa janak' kaha jaataa he. inke dwaara rachit ek kahani–sangrah badanaami di chhaan ke liye unhen san 1976 main sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya.
 san 2001 main unhen sahitya academy fellowship pradaan kiya gaya tha.
 pro. shastri ne dogri bhashaa kii asmita kii raksha ke liye andolan shuru kiya or san 1944 main jammu main dogri sanstha kii neenv rakhi. uske baad unhone peeche nahin dekha or apana puraa jivan dogri ke liye arpit kar diya. san 1953 main unhone dogri magazine 'namin chetana' ko shuru kii. jammu vishvavidhyalay main dogri research centre gathit kiya gaya jiskaa shrey bhi pro. shastri ko jaataa he. yahi centre baad main dogri vibhaag main badla. unke vishesh yogadan ke liye sahitya academy ne san 1976 main laghu kahaniyon ke liye kitaab ‘badanaami da chann’ or san 1989 main anuwaad ke liye sahitya academy puraskaar se sammanit kiya. 24 march 1990 main unako padmashri se sammanit kiya gaya tha. pro. shastri ko sahitya academy kii faillowship bhi mili he. 
 jameendaari pratha ke khilaaf aawaaj uthaane vaale duggar ke mahanayake kisaan baba jitto ke jivan se zyada se zyada kisaanon ko prarena mili, iske liye pro. shastri ke likhe naatak baba jitto kaa manchan 1948 main udhampur ke tikri kshetra main yojit kisaan sabha main kiya gaya.
