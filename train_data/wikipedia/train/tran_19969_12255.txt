 indira sagar band madhya pradesh main narmada nadi par khandawa jile main narmadanagar sthaan par nirmit band he. yah ek bahuddeshiy he. is band kii neenv 23 oktober 1984 main bharat kii tatkaaleen pradhanmantri indira gandhi ne rakhi thi. mukhya band kaa nirmaan 1992 main armbh hua. iske aage narmada nadi par omkareshwar, maheshwar or sardaar sarovar pariyojnaon ke antargat band he.
