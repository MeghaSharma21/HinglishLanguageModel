 bani khalid yaa al-khaldi parivaar, ek arab janajaatiy sangh he. is janajati ne 15 veen shatabdi se 18 veen tak iraq or purvi saudi arab ke dakshini kshetra par shashan kiya tha. or phir 1 9veen shatabdi kii shuruyaat main turk samrajya ke anudaan ke tahat. isaki sabase badi seemaa par, bani khalid kaa kshetra uttar main iraq se dakshin main oman kii seemaa tak failaa, or bani khalid ne shashan karke raajnneetik prabhaav banaae rakhaa madhya arab main nejad kaa kshetra. janajati ke adhikansh sadsy vartmaan main purvi or madhya saudi arab main rahete hai, jabaki anya kuwait, katar, bahrain, philistin, syria or sanyukt arab emirate main rahete hai. bani khalid shiya muslim or sunni muslim donon hai..
