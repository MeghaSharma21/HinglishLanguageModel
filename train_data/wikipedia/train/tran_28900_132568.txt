 europea parishad, europea sangh kaa sarvocch raajnneetik nikaay he. is nikaay main europea ayog ke adhyaksh ke saath saath sabhi sadsy deshon ke raashtra pramukh yaa sarkaar ke pramukh shaamil hai. isaki baithkon kii adhyakshata us sadsy desh kaa pratinidhi karata he jiske paas us samay europea sangh kii parishad kii presidency ho.
 yadhyapi parishad ke paas koi aupchaarik kaaryakari yaa vaidhanik adhikaar nahin he, par yah sanstha un pramukh muddon or faislon se sambhddha he jo "europea sangh ke saamaanya raajnneetik disha nirdeshon ko paribhaashit karne main ek pramukh prerana" hon. parishad kii baithak ek saal main kam se kam do baar brusselles kii jastus lipsius imaarat main hoti he, jo europea sangh kaa koncelium he.
