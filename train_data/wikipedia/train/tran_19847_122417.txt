 ranbir kapur, , mumbai maharashtra,bharat) ek bhartiya abhinayta hai jo bollywood cinema main abhinay karte he. ranbir kapur ne apane filmi jeevan kii shuruyaat ek sahyogi nirdeshak ke rup main nirdeshak sanjay lila bhansaali ke saath kii. iske turant baad unhone saanwariyaa film main bataur abhinayta apana pehla abhinay kiya jiske liye unhone filmpher dwaara yojit sarvshereshth purush film abhinayta kaa khitaab jeeta. iske baad unhone vek ap seed, ajab prem kii gazab kahani or rokket singh: celsman of da year, in filmo main ek hi saal main abhinay karke sakaaratmak pratikriya praapt kii. in abhinayon ke liye unhen filmpher dwaara sarvshereshth abhinayta critics puraskaar diya gaya. iske baad film raajnneeti main ek mahatvakankshi rajaneta ke rup main unki bhoomika ke liye unhone manyata praapt kii jo unki sabase badi vyavsaayik safalataa maani jaati he. ranbir kapur kaa janm 28 sitambar 1982 jo hindi cinema ke ek abhinayta he, unhone apane kariyar kii shruwaat sanjay lila bhansaali ke saath kaam karke kii .2007 main unhone vahi diarector ke saath apni pehali film main kaam kiya wo ek punjabi parivaar se sambandh rakhte he. unhone apana shikshan mumbai ke mahim kii bombai scottish skool main purna kiya. phir unhen acting sikhane ke liye nevyork ke lee strasburg theatre and film institute bheja gaya. apane kariyar kii shruwaat karne se pehle unhone do short filme bhi banaayin. phir unhone sanjay lila bhansaali kii film balack main unki madad kii.
 ranbir ke mata pita apane samay ke lokapriy film sitare rishi kapur or nitu singh hai, ranbir kii ek riddhimaa naam kii bahan bhi he. yah prithviraj kapur ke padpote or abhinayta or filmanirmaataa raaj kapur ke pote hai. unke taaoo randhir kapur or chachaa rajeev kapur hai, karishma kapur or karina kapur unki chacheri bahanen hai or nikhil nanda unka fuphera bhai he.
 haal main ek saakshaatkaar main kapur ne deepika padukon ke saath apane sambandh kii pushti kii or media ko kaha, "pichley kuch saptahon ke dauraan ham dating par gaye hai", main is sambandh se bahut khush hoon, main hameshaa aise hi hana chahta hoon. "
 apane filmi carrier kii boliwood main shuruyaat karne se pehle ranbir kapur ne do laghu filmon main abhinay kiya: pishan tu lav, jiskaa unhone khud nirdesan bhi kiya; or india 1964 . ranbir kapur ne pehali baar navambar 2007 me navagantuk sonam kapur ke saath sanjay lila bhansaali kii film saanvaaria main pratham abhinay kiya tha. is film me uske ek lovasi gayak ke sakaaratmak chithran ke liye unako bahut prashansa milane ke baavjood ye film box offis par vifal rahi thi. samalochak, taran adarsh ne tippanee kii thi, " ranbir kapur atyaadhik pratibhashali he, is par do raay nahin he, ve achche lagate hai, par jo cheez ham ghar le jate hai, vah hai pradarshan main eemaandaaree agar inaki pehali film main unka pradarshan itane oonche str kaa pradarshan rahaa to yah balak aane vaale varshon main kapur khandaan ko garvit kar sakate hai. inke pehle pradarshan ke liye inhen 10 main se 10 ank die jate hai".
 aajkal yah rajkumaar santoshi kii film azab prem kii gazab kahani me katrina kaiff ke saath kaam kar rahe hai.
