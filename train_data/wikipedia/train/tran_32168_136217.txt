 12 oktober san 1938 i. ko mumbai nagar main raajbahadur singh thakur, dow moti chandra, shri bhanu kumaar aadi ke sahyog se bombay hindi viapeeth kii sthapana hui. viapeeth kaa apana ek mudranalay or vishaal bhawan he. prathama, madhyamaa, uttama, bhasharatna or sahitya-sudhakar viapeeth kii parikshaayen nirdhaarit hai, jisaki manyata metric, inter or bi.e. ke samakaksh hai. iskaa kaaryakshetra akhil bhartiya he. prativarsh vibhinn kshetron main sanstha kii ore se prachaar-shiviron evam sammelnon kaa aayojan kiya jaataa he. viapeeth kaa masik mukhyapatr 'bharti' niyamit rup se prakashit ho rahaa he.
 bharti, sampadak : shri dattatraya bhi. gurjar
