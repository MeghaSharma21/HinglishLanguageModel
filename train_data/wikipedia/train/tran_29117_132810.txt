 nandan bacchon kii ek hindi patrika he jo pichley 40 varsho se prati maas prakashit hoti he. is patrika kii shuruyaat 1964 main pandit nehru kii smriti main hui thi. nandan kaa pehla ank pandit nehru ko hi samarpit tha. pramukh rup se nandan kii kahanio puranic evam pari kathaayen hoti thi par samay ke saath evam apane bal pathako kii badalati ruchi ko dhyaan rakh kar nandan main samakalin vishyo evam prasidh jivaniya bhi prakashit karnaa shuru kar diya. aaj tak nandan main 10,000 se bhi zyada kahaniyaan prakashit ho chuki he. patrika main niyamit chapne vaali susheel kalra dwaara banaai comic chitrakatha chitu-nitu kaafii lokapriy thi.
