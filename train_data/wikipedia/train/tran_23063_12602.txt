 gandharv swarg main rahane vaale devtaa the bhartiya sangitajna ke rup main jane jate hai,gandharv swarg main rahete hai tathaa apsaraon ke pati hai. gandharvaraj pushpadanth indra kii sabha ke gayak the.
 mukhyat: gandharv kaa isai dharm tathaa buddha dharm se sambandh he. jain dharm main gandharv shabd kaa upyog tirthankar kunthanath se jodakar kiya gaya.
 gandharv swarg main rahete hai tathaa apsaraon ke pati hai. yah nimna varg ke devtaa hai.yahi som ke rakshak bhi hai, tathaa devataaon ke sabha ke gayak hai.hindu dharamshastra main yah devataaon tathaa manushyon ke bich doot hote hai. bhartiya parampara main aapasi tathaa paariwarik sahmati ke bina gandharva vivaah anubandhit hota he.narinakaa ullekh mahabharat main bhi dekhne ko miltaa he. yah devataaon tathaa se jude hai inkaa sambandh durjey yoddha ke rup main yakshon se bhi he. prasarak ke rup main inkaa ullekh he.
 inaki utpatti ke bare main bhi kai mat hai, kuch kehete hai ki inaki utpatti prajapati se hui, kuch kehete hai brahma se, kashyap se, kuch kehete hai ki inaki utpatti muniyon se hui.
 buddha samskruti main ye gandharv kuch sabase nichle varg ke devataaon main se ek hai. ye devtaa chini dev katuramaharadikakaiyika main vargeekrut hai tathaa inkaa sambandh raja dhritarashtra se bhi he. ek gandharv kii tulana main kisi behatar janm main paidaa hona ek buddha sadhu ke liye sharmnaak mana jaataa he.
 gandharv hawa ke madhyam se ud saktaa he or sangeet ke rup main apane kaushal ke liye janaa jaataa he.vah pedo or phulon se jude hue hai tathaa chaal or ras main bhi vaas karte hai. ve ek akele tapasvi ko pareshaan karte hai.
 gandharva or yaksha dono hi kabhi kabhi ek samaan arth ke liye upyog kiye jate hai parantu yaksha ek devataaon kii kism ke rup main zyada saamaanya shabd he.
