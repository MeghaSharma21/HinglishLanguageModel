 dil dhadkane do ek bhartiya bollywood film he. jiske nirdeshak joya akhtar va nirmaataa ritesh shidwani or farhaan akhtar hai. isamen anil kapur, farhaan akhtar, shefali shah, priyanka chopra, ranvir singh, anoushka sharma, rahul bose mukhya bhoomika main hai. yah film 5 june 2015 ko cinema gharon main pradarshit hogi.
 farhaan akhtar ne iske nirmaan kii taiyaari zindagi naa milegi dubaara ke baad se hi 2011 main shuru kar diya tha.
 is gane ke sangeet ko shankar ehsaan loy ne banaaya or iske bol ko javed akhtar ne.
