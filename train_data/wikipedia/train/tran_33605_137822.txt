 kuanka kendri spain main swytt samudaay kastil-laa maancha kaa ek shahar he.
 kuanka naam arabi shabd قونكة se liya gaya he jo pahile is shahar ke sthapit hone se pahile ek alakasbe ko kaha jaataa tha or baad main yah ek shahar ban gaya.
 2009 kii janaganana ke mutabik is shahar kii aabaadi 55,866 thi jisamen se 27,006 mard or 28,860 auraten thi.
 garmiyon main taapmaana sardiyon ke mukaable main zyada garm ho jaataa he. garmiyon main ausat taapmaana 30°C tak pahunch jaataa he.
 kuanka bada girjaghar san 1182 se san 1270 ke darmiyaan banaaya gaya.
