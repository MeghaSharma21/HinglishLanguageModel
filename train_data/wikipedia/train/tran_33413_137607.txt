 electronic voting mashin 1999 ke chunaavon se bhaag main electronic voting ke karyanvayan ke liye bhartiya general or rajya chunaavon main or haal hi main puure desh ke paanch rajyon main yojit 2017 ke rajya chunaavon main istemaal ho rahi he. iviem ne bharat main sthaneey, rajya or saamaanya chunaavon main paper matapatron kaa sthaan liya he. iviem kii tampyta or suraksha ke bare main pehle daavon kaa daawa kiya gaya tha jo ki siddh nahin hua he. dilli ucch nyaayalay, sarvocch nyaayalay ke faislon or vibhinn raajnneetik dalon se maang ke baad, chunaav ayog ne matadaata satyapit paper audit trail pranali ke saath iviem lagu karne kaa faisla kiya. bhartiya loksabhaa chunaav 2014 main ek payalat project ke rup main 543 sansadeey nirvachan kshetron main se 8 main voter vairifoyble paper audit trail pranali ko pesh kiya gaya tha.
