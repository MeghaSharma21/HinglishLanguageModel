 uttam prachlan aisi paddatiyaan, takneekein yaa vidhiyaan hai jo saamaanya-rup-se-sweekrut, anaupchaarik rup se manakikrut hoti hai. ye samay kii kasuti par parkhi or khari utari hui hoti hai. ye praayah 'saamaanya buddhi' par aadhaarit hoti hai or vahaan prayog kii jaati hai jahaan koi vishesh vidhi manakikrut nahin hoti. sanskrit main isi par ek sookhti he-
 
 arth: jivan jeene ke asli maarg ke nirdhaaran ke liye koi susthaapit tark nahin he, shrutiyaan bhi bhaanti-bhaanti kii baaten karti hai, aisa koi rishi/chintak/vichaarak nahin he jiske vachan pramaan kahe ja saken. vastav main dharm kaa marm to guha main chipa he, yani bahut gudh he. aise main samaaj main pratishthit vyakti jis maarg ko apanaataa he vahi anukaraniya he. yahaan pratishthit yaa bade vyakti se matlab usase nahin he jisane dhan-sampada arjeet kii ho, yaa jo vyavsaayik rup se kaaphi aage bad chukaa ho, yaa jo prashasnic athwa anya adhikaron se sampann ho, ityadi. pratishthit vah he jo charitravan ho, kartavyon kii avhelnaa na karata ho, dusron ke prati samvedansheel ho, samaaj ke hiton ke prati samarpit ho, aadi-aadi.
