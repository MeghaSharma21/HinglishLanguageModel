 sar shivsagar ramgulam marishas ke pratham mukhyamantri, pradhanmantri evam chathe governor-general the.
 ve 1961 se 1982 tak pradhan mantri the. ve hindu dharm ke anuyaayi, hindi bhashaa ke pakshdhar or bhartiya samskruti ke poshak the. unke kaarykaal main hindi ke pathan-pathan main bahut pragati hui. vipareet paristhitiyon or abhaav ke rahete hue bhi unhone hindi ke vikaas main koi kami nahin rakhi. unhone hi sarvapratham vishwa hindi sachivalay kii sthapana kaa vichaar diya tha. unke putr naveenchandra ramgulam bhi pramukh rajanyik hai. 
 shivsagar ramgulam ko unke mata-pita bachapan main 'keval' kahkar bulaate the. unka janm 18 sitambar 1900 ko morisus ke bel reeve main hua tha jo koiri jati ke the. pita kaa naam mohit ramgulam tha jo bhartiya pravasi shramik the. mohit ramgulam ne 'basmati ramacharan' naamak ek navayuvati vidhwaa se vivaah kiya jinke do putr hue, nakchhedi hiraaman or raamalaal ramacharan.
 shivsagar ramgulam kaa bachapan bhartiya samskruti, darshan or hindi vaataavaran main sthaneey 'baitka' main hui.
