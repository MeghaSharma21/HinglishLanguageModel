 pigmi aise maanav jaateey samooh ko kehete hai jiske sadasyon kaa ausat qad asaadhaaran rup se kam ho. yah naam aksar madhya africa main basane vaali chote qad kii jatiyon ko diya jaataa he, jinamein aka, efe or mbuti shaamil hai. in jatiyon main purushon kaa ausat qad 150 semi se kam hota he. yadi yah maapdand badhaakar 155 semi kar diya jaye to kuch anya jatiyaan bhi pigmi maani ja sakati hai, jinamein australia, thailand, malaysia, bharat ke andaman dweep samooh, indonisia, philipinoz, papua new gini, bolivia va brazil kii kuch jatiyaan aati hai.
