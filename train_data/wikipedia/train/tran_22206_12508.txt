 britain kii rajdhani landan ke madhya main tems nadi ke kinare bane is bhavy qile kaa nirmaan san 1078 main wiliam ne karaayaa tha. iske nirmaan main lage pathar frans se mangaaye gaye the. iske parisar main kai imaaraten hai. ek samay yah britain kaa shahi mahal tha. isi parisar main rajasi bandiyon ke liye karagaar bhi tha. yah kila anek mrityudand tathaa hatyaon kaa sakshi rahaa he. henry ashtum ne apni rani ain bolin kaa 1536 main yaheen sar kalam karaayaa tha. vartmaan samay main britain kaa rajpariwaar is qile main nahin rahata he lekin unke shahi jawaharaat yaheen surakshit rakhe gaye hai.
