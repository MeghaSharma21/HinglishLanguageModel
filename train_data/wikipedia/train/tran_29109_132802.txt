 karthik sivakumar, jinhen apane parade ke naam karti se janaa jaataa he, ek bhartiya film abhinayta hai. vah abhinayta surya ke chote bhai he. unka kaam tamil filmon main kendrit he. unhone 2007 kii film pruthiviran se pardaapan kiya tha. payya, naan mahaan allaa or siruthi se unhone safalataa arjeet kii. flop filmon kii shrinkhalaa main dikhaai dene ke baad unhone biriyani, madras or thojhaa/oopiri se dobaaraa safal filmein di.
