 
 kepler-452 naamak tara ek ji shrenee kaa tara he jo prithvi se 1400 prakashavarsh dur he. yah signs naamak taramandal main sthit he. iskaa taapmaana surya ke taapmaana ke lagbhag baraabar he. isaki aayu lagbhag 6 arab varsh he or yah surya se 150 karod varsh purana he or surya kii tulana main yah 20 pratishat adhik chamkila he. surya kii tulana main iskaa dravyaman 4 guna or vyas 10% adhik he.
 grah kepler-452bi isaki parikrama karata he jise kepler antariksh yaan dwaara july 2015 main khoja gaya tha. vaigyanikon kaa maananaa he ki jivan laayak prakaash, garmi or vaataavaran banaae rakhane ke liye ye apane taare kepler-452 se uchit duuri par he or is vajah se yahan hamare grah prithvi jaise jivan kii sambhaavna ho sakati he.
