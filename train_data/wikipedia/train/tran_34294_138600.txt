 misri ret samudr uttar africa ke sahaara registan ke libayai registan upbhaag main sthit he. yahan ret hi ret he or uske anant lehren or tile aise lagate hai jaise samudr main lehren, jis se is kaa naam padaa he. isamen kuch ret ke tile 100 meter tak kii oonchai rakhte hai . misri ret samudr libayai registan kaa 25% hissaa he.
