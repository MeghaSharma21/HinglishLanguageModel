 barabati qila odisha main mahanadi ke kinare bana hua he or kubsuurati se taraashe gaye darwaazon or nau manzila mahal ke liye prasiddh he.
 barabati qile kaa nirmaan gang vansh ne 14veen shatabdi main karavaaya tha. aisi manyata he ki yuddh ke samay nadi ke donon kinaaron par bane qile is qile kii raksha karte the. vartmaan main is qile ke saath ek antarrashtriya stadium he, jo paanch acre main faile is stadium main lagbhag 30000 se bhi zyada log baith sakate hai. yahaan khel pratiyogitaaon or saanskrithik kaaryakramon kaa ayojan hota rahata he.
 barabati qile ko raja mukund dev ne san 1560-1568 main nirmaan karvaa kar vishaal qile kaa rup diya. san 1568 se 1603 tak yah qila afghaaniyon, mughalon or maratha ke raajaaon ke adheen tha uske baad san 1803 main angrejon ne is qile kon maraathon se chin liya. baad main ve bhubaneshwar chale gaye or yah qila upekshit padaa rahaa.
