 gopal ganesh agarkar bharat ke maharashtra pradesh ke samaaj sudhaarak evam patrkaar the. ve marathi ke prasiddh samachar patr kesari ke pratham sampadak the. kintu bal gangadhar tilak se vaicharik matbhed ke kaaran unhone kesari kaa sampaadakatv chhodkar sudhaarak naamak patrika kaa prakaashan armbh kiya. agarkar, vishnu krishna chiplunkar tathaa tilak "dekan ajukeshan sosayadi" ke sansthaapak sadsy the.
 agarkar ji kaa janm maharashtra ke satara jila ke tambhu gaanv main ek garib parivaar main hua tha. unki praathmik shiksha karad ke primary skool main hui thi. karad ke ek nyaayalay main clerk kaa kaam karne ke baad ve ratnagiri chale gayi kintu vahaan shiksha grahan na kar paye. unhone 1878 main bie tathaa san 1880 main em e kiya.
