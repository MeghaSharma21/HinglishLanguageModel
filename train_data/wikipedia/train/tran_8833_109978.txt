 
 dow shridhar bhaskar varnekar sanskrit ke vidwaan tathaa rashtravaadi kavi the. unka janm nagpur main hua tha. 
 'shrishivparajyodaym' unki sarvaadhik prasiddh rachana he. is par 1974 main unhe sahitya academy puraskaar pradaan kiya gaya tha. yah 68 sargo main varnit ek mahakavy he. yah pustak sangh lok seva ayog ke civil seva pariksha ke liye nirdhaarit sanskrit sahitya ke paathyakram main bhi sammilit kii gayi he. unki duusari prasiddh rachana he - 'sanskrit vaanmay kosh'.
 'bharataratnashtakam' tathaa 'swatantryavirshatakam' unki anya rachanaayen hai.
 dow shridhar ko raashtrapati puraskaar, kalidas sammaan, birla foundation kaa saraswati sammaan aadi pradaan kiye gayi. jagadguru shankaracharya ne unhen 'pragnabharati' sammaan pradaan kiya.
