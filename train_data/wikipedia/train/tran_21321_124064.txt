 simlipal rashtriya udyaan bharat ke odisha rajya ke mayurbhanj anusuchit jila main sthit ek rashtriya udyaan he tathaa ek hathi abhayaarany he. is udyaan kaa naam simal yaa laal kapaas ke pedo kii vajah se padaa he jo yahan bahutayat main paye jate hai. yah park 845.70 varg kilometre ke kshetra main failaa hua he or isamen joranda or barehipani jaise khubasurat jharne hai. is udyaan main 99 baagh, 432 haathiyon ke alaava gaur tathaa chousinge bhi vaas karte hai.odisha ke mayurbhanj jila me avasthit mayurbhanj rajya ke purvavarti shaskaon kaa yah shikaar sthal project tiger ke antargat shaamil kiya gaya he. 1956 main iskaa chayan aadhikarik rup se tiger rigerv ke liye kiya gaya tha. baripada se 60 kilometre dur sthit isko vanyajeev abhayaarany ghoshit kiya gaya jo 2277.07 varg kilometre ke kshetrafal main failaa hua he. ghaney jangalon, jharanon or pahadiyon se samrudh is park main vividh vanyajeevon ko najadik se dekha ja saktaa he. baagh, hiran, hathi or anya bahut se jeev is park main mulat: paae jate hai.
