 jeepiyu kaa upyog imbaded system main, mobile phonon main, pisi main, worksteshnon main, or game consolon main hota he. aadhunik jeepiyu computer graphics se sambandhit kaary karne tathaa chhabi sansaadhan main bahut daksh hai. iske alaava, parelel processing karne ke liye bhi inkaa upyog saamaanya CPU kii apeksha bahut upyukt he kyonki inaki sanrachana atyant parelel he. udhaaharan ke liye, intel ke ek saamaanya procesar main 4 se 8 tak kor honge, jabaki NVIDIA ke GPU main hajaaron CUDA kor ho sakate hai. kisi pisi par jeepiyu uske video card par ho saktaa he, yaa uske madraboard par hi sammilit kiya hua ho saktaa he. kuch-kuch CPU ke andar hi jeepiyu nirmit kar die gayi hai.
