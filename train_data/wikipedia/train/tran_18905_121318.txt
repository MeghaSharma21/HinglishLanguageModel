 edward bernastine germany ke ek rajaneta tathaa marxvaadi siddhantkaar the. ve germany ke soshal democratic party ke ek sadsy the. unke karl marks or fredrick angels ke saath ghanishtha sambandh the, lekin unhone marxvaadi soch main trutiyon ko dekhakar marxvad dwaara yojit vichaaron kii prakhar aalochana karaate huye marxvaadi bhautikwaadi siddhant kii jaanch karne kii chunauti di. unhone marxvaadi siddhant ke mahatvapurn hisson ko khaarij kar diya jo hegelian aadhyatmik tatwon par aadhaarit the, iske saath-saath unhone hegelian dwaitvaadi pariprekshya ko bhi khaarij kar diya.
 bernastine ne praarambhik marxvad ko apane aparipakv rup main pratishthit kiya or apni party ke marxvaadi paramparaagat samaj ko sanshodhit karne kaa prayaas kiya. iskaa uddeshy party ke vyavhaar ko vaastaviktaa ke kareeb laana tathaa marxvad ke antarnihit siddhanton ke anuroop prerit karnaa tha, kyonki unki party roodhiwaadee marxvad ko samarthan detii thi or ve roodhiwaadee siddhanton kaa virodh karte the. unhone auron kii tarah na keval marxvad ke is tanaav ko mahasoos kiya varan un donon dhaaraon ke antar ko mahasoos kar ek kaa chayan bhi kar liya or unhone marxvad main kranti kii aprihaaryta ko asweekrut karne ke baad bhi swayan ko sachcha marxvaadi kahana na choda.
