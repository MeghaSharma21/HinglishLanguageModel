 anekon samskritiyon ne tark karne kii sookshmatisookshm pranali apanaayi tathaa maanav ke sampoorn chintan main tark upasthit rahaa he. kintu tark karne kii spasht or vidhivat pranali mukhyat: or mulat: bharat, china or yunaan main hi viksit hui. inamen se keval bhartiya or unani tark paripati hi aadhunik kaal tak bina vyavdhaan ke vikaas karti rahi.
