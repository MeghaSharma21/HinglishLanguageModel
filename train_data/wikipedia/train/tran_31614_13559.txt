 chumbi ghati tibet ke shigatse vibhaag main sthit ek ghati he. yah us sthaan par sthit he jahaan bharat ke sikkim rajya, bhutan or tibet teenon kii seemayen milti hai. haalanki tibet ek thanda pradesh he, chumbi vaadi main garmiyon main mausam acha hota he or sthaneey vanaspatiyon main bahut phool aate hai. kootnithik drushti se 3,000 meter par sthit chumbi ghati kaa bahut mahatv rahaa he kyonki bharat par tibet se akraman karne kaa yah ek aasaan maarg he. 1904 main bharat kii british sarkaar ne tibet par chadhaai karke chumbi par nau maheene kaa qabza kar liya tha. british sarkaar ke andar chumbi ko aupchaarik rup se bharat kaa bhaag banane ke liye kaafii khichaatani chali lekin ant main ise tibeti sarkaar ke hawaale kar diya gaya. bharat or tibet ke bich ke do mahatvapurn pahari darre - nathu laa or jelep laa - ke ek taraf ke mukh chumbi vaadi main hi khulte hai. 1950 ke dashak main janavaadee ganatantr china ne tibet par qabza kar liya or 1990 ke baad nathu darre or jelep darre tak chaudee sadaken bana di jis se bhartiya samikshakon ke anusaar bhavishya main yudhsthiti main yahan ke raste chini senaayen tivrata se bharat par dhaava bol sakati hai.
 chumbighati himalay kii dakshini dhaalon par samudratat se 9,500 foot unchi yah ghati bharat ko tibet se milaati he. iske purv main bhutan or pashchim main sikkim hai. yadhyapi raajnneetik rup main yah pehle tibet ke or ab china ke aadhipaty main he, tathaapi bhugolic drushti se ise bharat kaa ang haana chahie. isi maarg se 1904 i. main british mishan gaya tha. yah uttar-uttar-purv main dangla darre tak faili he. is ghati main torsa nadi bahati he or isi nad ke dwaara bane chote chote samtal kshetron main jo, gehun or tarkariyon kii kheti hoti he. aamo chu nadi par chumbi ghati kaa prasiddh nagar chumbi basaa he, jiske 54 kimi dakshin-pashchim kalimpang sthit he. vartmaan bharat-china sangharsh kii drushti se is ghati kaa sainik mahatv bad gaya he.
