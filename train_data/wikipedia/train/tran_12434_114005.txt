 araku ghati bharat main andhra pradesh rajya ke vishakhapattanam jile main ek parvatiy sthaan he. yah ghati purvi ghat par sthit he or kai janajatiyon kaa nivas sthaan rahaa he. araku ghati dakshin bharat main sabase kam pradooshit kshetron main se ek he tathaa vanijyik rup se kam upyog kiya hua paryatak sthal he.
 araku 18.3333° uttar or 82.8667°purv kii disha main sthit he. isaki ausat oonchaai 911meter he.yah vishakhpattanam se 114 kilometre kii duuri par sthit he or urisa rajya kii seemaa ke kareeb he. anantgiri or sunkareemetta arakshit vanakshetra araku ghati ke abhinn ang hai. yah ghati gaalipond, raktakonda, sunkareemetta tathaa chitamogondi jaise pahaado se chaaro taraf se ghiri hui he. gaalikonda parvat kii oonchaai 5000 foot tak he, jo andhra pradesh rajya main sabase unchaa he. yahan par ausat varsha 1700 millimetre he, jisamen sarvaadhik maatra june se oktober ke maheenon main praapt hoti he.
 araku apane coffee poudharopan ke liye prasiddh he. adivaasiyon dwaara utpaadit bharat kaa pratham jaivik coffee brands san 2007 main jari kiya gaya. araku main utpaadit uttam kism kaa carbonic brands coffee araku emerald kii bikri vaishvik str par kii jaati he. araku janajatiy kshetra ke hajaaron adivasee coffee ugane vaale majdoor athwa chote kisaan hai.
 vishakhapattanam shahar se is ghati kaa sampark sadak va rail donon madhyamon se he. purv tat railway ke vishakhapattanam sambhag ke kottavalsa-kirundul maarg main araku or araku ghati naamak do railway stesan hai. bhartiya rail ke network main similiguda railway stesan samudr tat se 996 meter kii oonchaai par sthit he.
 yahan kii prakritik saundarya ne tollywood kii kai filmon jaise hapi days katha main apni jagah banaaya he. san 2010 relies hui darling film kii shooting araku main hui he.
