 bangnee line dilli metro rail kii ek line he. yah line oktober 3 ko madhya dilli main sthit kendriya sachivalay or dakshin dilli main sthit sarita vihaar ke bich armbh hui thi. oktober tak is line ko badarpur tak badhaaya jaayegaa. yah line 15 kimi lambi he or isapar kul 13 metro stesan he jo is prakaar hai:
 vilet line ko mul rup ,main march 2010 main armbh hona tay tha. 12 july 2009 ko jamarudpur ke nikat iske uparigami maarg ke ek bhaag ke gir jane ke kaaran chah logon kii mrityu ho gai evam 15 ghayal ho gaye. is kaaran iske chaalu hone main vilamb hua. badarpur stesan se ek vistaar 6 sitambar 2015 ko faridabad ke liye chaalu hua.
  
 dilli metro kii yah line 2010 rashtramandal khelon ke udghaatan vaale din arthath 3 oktober, 2010 ko khol di gai thi. is line par hi jawaharlal nehru stadium padataa he jis kaaran line ko is din tak chaalu karnaa aavashyak tha. haalanki is din kendriya sachivalay se sarita vihaar stesan tak hi ise kholaa gaya or aage badarpur tak kii 5 kimi lambi line ko iske 10 denon baad yani lagbhag 13-14 oktober 2010 tak kholane kaa praavdhaan kiya gaya he.
