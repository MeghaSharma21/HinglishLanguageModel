 uttrakhand kaa rajya-chihn athwa uttrakhand kaa pratik-chihn, uttrakhand sarkaar kii rajkeey mohar he, jiskaa upyog rajya dwaara sabhi prakaar ke prashasnic evam rajkeey kriyakalapon main uttrakhand rajya kaa pratinidhitiv karne ke liye kiya jaataa he. ise uttrakhand rajya kii sthapana ke samay dinaak 9 navambar 2000 ko rajya kii navgathit antarim sarkaar dwaara adhikrut kiya gaya tha.
 uttrakhand kaa rajya-chihn shwet prishthbhumi evam neeli paridhi sahit chaar jaldharaon se yukt heere ke aakaar vaala pratik-chihn he, jise himalay parvat ke shikharon dwaara sahaara dete huye darshaya gaya he.iske upree bhaag par bani kalgai main laal prishthbhumi yukt bharat kaa rashtriya chihn, ashok kii laat aakrit he, jiske neeche sanskrit main bharat kaa rashtriya udghosh, satyamev jayate sanlagn he.rajya-chihn ke nichle bhaag par nile rang main "uttrakhand rajya" ankit he.
 rajya-chihn ke upree bhaag main darshaayi gayi laal rang kii prishthbhumi, uttrakhand rajya aandolan ke dauraan shahid huye rajya aandolankariyon ke rakt kii pratik he.shwet rang kii prishthbhumi shantipriy prakruti vaale uttrakhandsiyon kii pratik he.parvatshikhar himalayan rajya kii bhugolic evam parsthitik prakruti ko darshata he tathaa chaar jaldharayen rajya kii chaar badi nadiyon; ganga, yamuna, kaali evam ramganga ko darshaati hai.nila rang rajya kii pavitra nadiyon ke shuddh jal kaa pratik he.
