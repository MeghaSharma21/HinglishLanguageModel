 bhashaa sampark us sthiti ko kehete hai jab do alag bhashaaon yaa upbhaashaon ke bolne vaale aapasi sampark main aaen or sanchaar yaa vaartalap karen. yah vishwa main ek sarvyaapee sthiti he: bahubhashikta puure itihaas main dekhi gai he or aadhunik duniya main adhiktar log bahubhashiya hai. jab bhinn bhashaaon ke log aapasi sampark main rahete hai to unki bhashaayen ek-duusare ko prabhavit karti hai - yahi kaaran he ki angrezi main tamil bhashaa ke "mangai" shabd kaa "mango" rup aam ke fal ke liye maanak ban gaya or mongol bhashaa kaa "naukar" / "naukari" shabd hindi or farsi main pravesh kar gaya.
