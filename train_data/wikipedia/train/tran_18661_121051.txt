 isisi vishwa cricket lig divijan aath isaki 2009-14 or 2012-18 chakra ke liye isisi vishwa cricket lig pranali ke nimnatam vibhaajan tha. anya sabhi divijanon kii tarah, dablusiel divijan aath ek staneylon toornament ke rup main nahin balki ek vaastavik lig ke rup main chunaav ladaa tha.
 udghaatan divijan aath toornament 2010 main yojit kii mejabani kii or kuwait se jeeta tha. 2012 toornament samoa dwaara yojit or vanuatu se jeeta tha. 2010 or 2012 se pehle the dablusiel paanch divijanon ko aakaar chota kiya gaya tha, donon kii visheshta aath tiimon jo kshetreey ghatnaon ke madhyam se kwalifai saath hi sanskarnon main yojit kiya jayega. vanuatu, bhutan, or surinam donon toornament main bhaag lene ke liye hi tiimon the.
