 betatron ek prakaar kaa chakree kan twarak he. iskaa vikaas norway ke vaigyanik rolf vidro ne kiya tha. betatron ko ek transformar mana ja saktaa he jisaki secondari ek turn vaali nirvaat pipe hoti he jiske andar electron chakkar karte hue twarit hote hai. transformar kii primary main prayavarti dhara pravaahit karne par nirvaat pipe main chakkar kar rahe electron twarit hote hai. betatron mashin pehali mashin thi jiske dwaara saadhaaran electron gan se praapt hone vaale electranon kii apeksha adhik urja vaale electron praapt kiye ja sake.
