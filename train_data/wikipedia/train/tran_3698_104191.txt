 daulat beg oldi bharat ke jammu va kashmir rajya ke laddakh pradesh main sthit ek sthaan he. yahan bharat kii ek sainik chaowkee he or yah aitihaasik rup se bharat or purvi turkistan ke bich ke vyapaarik maarg par ek padaav hua karata tha. is se thik dakshin main purv se pashchim bahane vaali chipchap nadi guzarati he. daulat beg oldi ke naam kaa parivarni banaakar ise kabhi-kabhi dibio bhi kaha jaataa he.
 mana jaataa he ki 16veen sadi main turkistan ke yarakanda shahar kaa ek dhanwaan, daulat beg yahan se nikal rahaa tha lekin karakoram darre se utarane ke baad usne yahan dam tod diya. 'daulat beg oldi' kaa matlab he 'daulat beg kii mrityu ho gai he'. kehete hai ki use apane khazaane ke saath yaheen dafana diya gaya he lekin ajtak koi uski qabr nahin dhund paaya he.
 daulat beg oldi 5,100 meter kii oonchaai par karakoram parvatamala ke sudur purvi chor par bharat-china sarhad se 8 kimi dakshin main or china-adhikrut aksai chin kshetra or laddakh ke anya bhaag ke bich kii vaastavik niyantran rekha se 9 kimi pashchimottar main sthit he. siachen glacier ko chhodkar yah bharat kaa uttartam sthai sainik adda he. yahan se kuch dakshin main murgo naamak bhartiya gaanv he jahaan mishrit tibeti-dardi mul ke balati samudaay ke log rahete hai jo khubaani ugane or yaks-paalan se guzaaraa karte hai. sardiyon main yahan taapmaana -30 centigrade tak gir jaataa he. 2001 main bharat ne ghoshnaa kari ki vah leh se daulat beg oldi tak ek pakki sadak bana rahaa he. 2013 main chini fuj ne seemaa langhakar is sthaan se kuch duuri tak ghuspaith kari jiskaa bharat ne virodh kara.
