 nav varsh ek utsav kii tarah puure vishwa main alag-alag sthanon par alag-alag tithiyon tathaa vidhiyon se manaaya jaataa he. vibhinn sampradaayon ke nav varsh samaaroh bhinn-bhinn hote hai or iske mahatva kii bhi vibhinn samskritiyon main paraspar bhinnata he.
 nav varsh utsav 4000 varsh pehle se babylon main manaaya jaataa tha. lekin us samay nae varsh kaa ye tyohaar 21 march ko manaaya jaataa tha jo ki vasant ke aagaman kii tithi bhi maani jaati thi. pracheen rome main bhi nav varshotsav ke liye chuni gai thi. rome ke taanashaah julius sijar ne isa purv 45wein varsh main jab julian calendar kii sthapana kii, us samay vishwa main pehali baar 1 janvari ko nae varsh kaa utsav manaaya gaya. aisa karne ke liye julius sijar ko pichhlaa varsh, yani, isapurv 46 isvi ko 445 dinon kaa karnaa padaa tha. 
 hebrew manyataon ke anusaar bhagwaan dwaara vishwa ko banane main saat din lage the. is saat din ke sandhan ke baad nayaa varsh manaaya jaataa he. yah din gregory ke calendar ke mutabik 5 sitambar se 5 oktober ke bich aata he.
 hinduon kaa nayaa saal caitra nav raatri ke pratham din yani gudi padva par har saal chini calendar ke anusaar pratham maas kaa pratham chandra diwas nav varsh ke rup main manaaya jaataa he. yah praayah 21 janvari se 21 farvari ke bich padataa he.
 bharat ke vibhinn hisson main nav varsh alag-alag tithiyon ko manaaya jaataa he. praayah ye tithi march or april ke maheene main padati he. punjab main nayaa saal baisakhi naam se 13 april ko manaai jaati he. sikh nanakshahi calendar ke anusaar 14 march hola mohalla nayaa saal hota he. isi tithi ke aasapaas bengali tathaa tamil nav varsh bhi aata he. telugu nayaa saal march-april ke bich aata he. andhrapradesh main ise ugaadi ke rup main manaate hai. yah caitra maheene kaa pehla din hota he. tamil nayaa saal vishu 13 yaa 14 april ko tamilnadu or kerala main manaaya jaataa he. tamilnadu main pongal 15 janvari ko nae saal ke rup main aadhikarik taur par bhi manaaya jaataa he. kashmiri calendar navareh 19 march ko hota he. maharashtra main gudi padva ke rup main march-april ke maheene main manaaya jaataa he, kannada nayaa varsh ugadee karnataka ke log caitra maah ke pehle din ko manaate hai, sindhi utsav cheti chand, ugaadee or gudi padva ek hi din manaaya jaataa he. madurai main chitraiy maheene main chitraiy thiruvija nae saal ke rup main manaaya jaataa he. marwari nayaa saal deepawali ke din hota he. gujarati nayaa saal deepawali ke duusare din hota he. is din jain dharm kaa navvarsh bhi hota he.lekin yah vyapak nahin he. oktober yaa november main aati he. bengali nayaa saal pohela baisakhi 14 yaa 15 april ko aata he. pashchim bengal or bangladesh main isi din nayaa saal hota he.
 islamic calendar kaa nayaa saal muharram hota he. islamic calendar ek purnataya chandra aadhaarit calendar he jiske kaaran iske baarah maason kaa chakra 33 varshon main saura calendar ko ek baar gum letaa he. iske kaaran nav varsh prachlit gregory calendar main alag alag maheenon main padataa he.
