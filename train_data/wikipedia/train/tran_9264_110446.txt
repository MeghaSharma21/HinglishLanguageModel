 miluo nadi china ke hunaan or jiangshi praanton main chalane vaali ek nadi he. yah lagbhag 400 kimi lambi nadi jiyangshi praant ke shiyushui zile main shuru hokar hunaan praant kii pingajiyang zile se guzarati he or phir prasiddh dongting jhil main bah jaati he. is nadi ke upree vistaar main vastav main do nadiyaan - mi or luo - sangam karti hai, jinka naam jodakar is nadi kaa naam 'miluo' padaa.
 san 278 isapurv main chini itihaas ke jhagadate rajyon ke kaal main chu rajya ke suprasiddh kavi chu yuan ke us samay ke bhrashtachar or chu rajdhani par dushman chin rajya kaa qabza hone kii vajah se is nadi main duubakar khudakushi kar lee thi.
