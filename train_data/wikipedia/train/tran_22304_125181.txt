 jab kisi sthaan par koi bada shahar ban jaataa he kintu us shahar kii jansankhya ke anuroop adhosanrachana kaa vikaas nahin ho paata to ise chhadmanagarikaran yaa chhadam-shaharikaran kehete hai. chhadmanagarikaran kii sthiti main nagar kii jansankhya to bad jaati he kintu awaas, shiksha, svachh jal, yatayaat, vidyut tathaa safai aadi ki samuchit vyavastha kaa abhaav hi bana rah jaataa he . aisi sthiti praaia: vikashshil yaa aviksit deshon ke nagaron main paayi jaati he.
