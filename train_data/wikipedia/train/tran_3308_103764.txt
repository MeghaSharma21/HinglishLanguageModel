 vuhan, china ke hubei praant kii rajdhani he or madhya china main sabase adhik jansankhya vaala nagar he. yah jianghan maidan ke purv main, yangtzi or haan nadiyon ke kataav par sthit he. teen baro, voochang, honkou or hanyang ke sangutikaran se utpann vuhan ko "nau praanton ke saarvajanik maarg" ke naam se bhi janaa jaataa he; yah ek pramukh parivahan kendra he or darjanon railmarg, sadaken or mahamarg is nagar se hokar jate hai. vuhan kii jansankhya 91 lakh he, jisamen se iske nagareey kshetra main 61 lakh log rahete hai. 1929 ke dashak main, vuhan, vampanthi kuomintang sarkaar kii rajdhani thi jiskaa netritv wang jingvei ne chiang kaai-shaike ke virodh main kiya tha. aaj vuhan kii pahchaan madhya china ke raajnneetik, aarthik, saanskrithik, shaikshanik or parivahan kendra ke rup main he.
 vuhan, hubei praant ke madhya main, 113°41′-115°05′ purv, 29°58′-31°22′ uttar, jianghan maidan ke purv or yangtzi or hanshui nadiyon ke sangam par basaa hua he.
