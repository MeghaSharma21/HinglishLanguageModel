 padaarth vigyaan main bahurupata kisi thos padaarth ke ek se adhik rup yaa crystal dhaanche main ho sakane kii kshamta ko kehete hai. udhaaharan ke liye koyala, grafite or hira teenon carbon ke hi bahurup hai. isi tarah kailcite or airagonite donon calcium carbonate ke bahurup hai. prakruti main bahurupata kai padaarthon main dekhi jaati he or rasinic tatwon ke str par yah aparupata se sambandhit he.
