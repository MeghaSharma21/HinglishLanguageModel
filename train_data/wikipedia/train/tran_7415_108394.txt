 anna arkadievana karenina liyo talsty ke upanyaas "aanna karenina" ke kendriya paatro main se ek he. karenina ek khubasurat, mahaan petersburg kii mahila, steve obloski kii bahan, alexi karenin kii patni he.
 tie e bers ke anusaar, "charitra or jivan se nahi, lekin dekhne se is kaa prototip", maaria alexandovna gartung thim.lekhak kii bahan maaria tolstoy kaa bhagya nayika ke samaan tha. ek anya prototip, vronski-raiveski ke prototipon main se ek kii premika ucch samaaj se ek mahila thi, jis ne use pyaar kiya or uske dhokhaa dene ke kaaran aatmahatya kar lee thi.iske alaava, ek or mahila bhi thi, jisaki aatmahatya kii kahani ne tollstoy ko prabhavit kiya tha.
 anna pyaar or bhaavnaatmak eemaandaaree ke maarg par chalati he or samaaj use vyabhichaari samajhate bedakhal karata he. wo bahut dukh jhelti haior aakhir main aatmahatya kar leti he. anna har tarah se ek sundar vyakti he: budhimaan or sakshar, vah nirantar padhati he, bacchon kii kitaaben likhti he, or kala kii saraahna karne kii sahaj kshamta dikhaati he. shaareerik rup se abhi tak dilkash he, vah ucch samaaj main vastav main har kisi kaa dhyaan khinchati he. anna pyaar main vishwaas karti he - na sirf romantic prem balki parivaar ke pyaar or dosti main bhi, jaisaa ki ham uske apane bete se gaharaa moh, doli or oblonski kii vivaahit samasyaon ko sulajhane ke liye uske utsaahi prayaason or apane dehaat vaale ghar main doli kaa garmjoshi se resepshan se dekhte hai. anna phencupan se zyada or kisi ko nafrat nahin karti, or vah apane pati, karenin ko, nakli, bhaavanahin paramparaon kaa avatar maanati he, jise vah tuchh jaanati he.
