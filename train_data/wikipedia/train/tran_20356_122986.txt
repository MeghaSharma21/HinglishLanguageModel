 arqbishap jon barva, esavidi. katak-bhubaneshwar ke roman catholic archidosis kii sevakari arqbishap he.
 
  unka janm 1 june 1 9 55 ko gbira, odisha, bharat main hua tha.
  14 april 1 9 85 ko unhen district verde sosayadi kaa catholic prestard kiya gaya tha. unhen 4 farvari, 2006 ko rourkela ke sah-abhiyojak bishap niyukt kiya gaya tha or 1 9 april 2006 ko bishap kaa padbhaar sambhala. rourkela ke bishap ke rup main unka kaarykaal 2 april ko shuru hua pope benedict XVI dwaara 11 farvari 2011 ko katak-bhubaneshwar ke roman catholic archidosis ke arqbishap ko niyukt kiya gaya.
