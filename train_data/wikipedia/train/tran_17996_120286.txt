 jaya devi ‘green ladi of bihar’ ke naam se mashhoor hai. 
 jaya devi ne apane ilaake or uske aasapaas kii mahilaon ko ekajute kar samuhik kaam karne ke tareeke sikhaye. 
 iskaa asar ye hua ki 285 swayamsevi samooh taiyaar ho gaye or in logon ne barish ke pani kaa sanchayan karnaa sikh liya.
 is tarkeeb se aasapaas kii kareeb pachaas hazaar hectare banjar zamin ko upjau kar kheti ke liye taiyaar kiya gaya.
 35 saal kii jaya devi ne iske alaava gaanv ke logon ke shramdan se pani ke kai tanks banawaye.
 jaya ke mutabik aasapaas kii suvidhaon ko behatar karke palaayan ko roka ja saktaa he. jaya ko 2010 main bharat sarkaar kaa ‘neshnal youth sammaan’ mil chukaa he.
