 operculam haddidaar machhliyon main chlom ke upar sthit haddi kaa ek dhaap hota he jo chlom ko surakshit rakhata he. adhiktar machhliyon main operculam kii pichhali dhar sir or dhad ke bich kii vibhaajan rekha bhi samajhi jaati he.
 operculam kii sanrachana chaar judi hui haddiyon se hoti he: operkal, prioparkal, interoparkal or saboparkal . raksha ke alaava, operculam kaa ek ore kaary bhi hota he. ise hilaakar machhlee apane chlom kii ore pani dhakelti he, jis se usamen se oxigen praapt ho sake.
