 tajmahal 1963 main bani hindi bhashaa kii film he. is film ko us varsh ke sarvshereshth sangeet niyojan ke liye sangeetkaar roshan ko filmphir puraskaar se sammanit kiya gaya. lata mangeshkar ko jo vaada kiya wo nibhaana padega geet ke liye sarvshereshth gayika kaa puraskaar mila to abhinetri veena ko unke abhinay ke liye sarvshereshth sahaayak abhinetri kaa filmphir puraskaar praapt hua.
