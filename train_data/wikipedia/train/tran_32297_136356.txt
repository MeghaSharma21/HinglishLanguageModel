 tok pisin papua new gini main boli jane vaali ek creole bhashaa he. yah papua new gini kii kuch sthaneey bhashaaon or angrezi ke mishran se 19veen sadi main viksit hui thi. haalanki iskaa naamak angrezi ke 'tolk pigin', yani 'pigin boli' kaa vikrut rup he, yah bhashavaigyanik drushti se vastav main pigin nahin balki creole kaa darja rakhti he, arthath zyaada sthai or viksit he. tok pisin ko papua new gini main sarkari manyata praapt he or yah us desh kii ek rajbhasha he. yah papua new gini kii sabase adhik prachlit bhashaa bhi he haalanki ise bolne vaale adhiktar papua new gini kii kisi anya bhashaa ke maatrubhaashi hote hai. anumaan lagaaya gaya he ki papua new gini main 700-900 bhashaayen boli jaati hai isliye tok pisin bhinn bhashaa-samudaayon ke aapasi sampark ke liye kaafii upyogi he.
