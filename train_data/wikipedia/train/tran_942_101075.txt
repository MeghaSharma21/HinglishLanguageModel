 chaithyabhumi mumbai ke dadar sthit bhartiya samvidhan ke nirmaataa bharataratna dow bhimrao ambedkar ji kii samadhi sthali or buddha dharm ke logo kaa aasthaa kaa centre hai. dadar ke samudr tat par chaityas bhoomi sthit he.
 dilli main apane nivas main 6 disambar 1956 ko ambedkar kaa mahaparinirvana hua or unka parthiv mumbai main laayaa gaya, chaithyabhumi main 7 disambar 1956 ko bhadant anand kaushalyayan ne ambedkar kaa buddha parampara ke anusaar dah sanskaar kiya tha. ambedkar kaa dah sanskaar ke pehle unhen sakshi rakh unke 10,00,000 anuyaayion ne buddha dhamm kii diksha lee or yah diksha unhen bhadant anand kaushalyayan ne di thi.
