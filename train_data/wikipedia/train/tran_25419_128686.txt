 niharika yaa nabula antartaarkeey madhyam main sthit aise antartaarkeey badal ko kehete hai jisamen dhul, hydrogen gas, helium gas or anya ynikrut plasma gaise upasthit hon. puraane jamaane main "niharika" khagol main dikhne vaali kisi bhi vistrut vastu ko kehete the. akashganga se pare ki kisi bhi gallexi ko niharika hi kaha jaataa tha. baad main jab edwin habal ke anusandhan se yah gyaat hua ki yah gallexiyaan hai, to naam badal die gaye. udhaaharan ke liye andromeda gallexi ko pehle andromeeda nabula ke naam se janaa jaataa tha. niharikaon main aksar taare or grahiy mandal janm lete hai, jaise ki chil niharika main dekha gaya he. yah niharika nasa dwaara khinche gaye "pilrs of kriyeshan" arthath "sristi ke stambh" naamak ati-prasiddh chitra main darshai gai he. in kshetron main gas, dhul or anya saamagree kii sanrachanaayen paraspar "ek saath judakar" bade dheron kii rachana karti hai, jo anya padaarthon ko aakarshith karata he evam kramshah sitaron kaa gathan karne yogya paryaapt bada aakaar le letaa hai. mana jaataa he ki shesh saamagree grahon evam grah pranali kii anya vastuon kaa gathan karti he.
 "niharika" ko angrezi main "nebula" likhaa jaataa he. yah ek latini bhashaa kaa shabd he or iskaa arth "badal" hua karata tha, jiskaa bahuvachan "nebyalai", "nebyale" yaa "nebyalas" he.
 is baat ke pramaan uplabdh hai ki doorabeen ke avishkaar ke pehle se maaya logon ko niharikaon ke bare main pataa tha. mrug nakshatra ke aasapaas ke akaash ke kshetra se sambandhit ek lokkatha is siddhant kaa samarthan karti he. kahani main ullekh he ki dadhakati aag ke aasapaas ek dhabba he.
 lagbhag 150 isvi purv cladius tolmi ne apni pustak aalmaguest ke VII-VIII ank main niharikaon main prakat honevaale paanch sitaron kaa ullekh kiya he. unhone saptarshi taramandal main ek badalon se yukt yaa dhundhale kshetra kaa bhi ullekh kiya tha, jo kisi bhi taare ke saath nahin judaa tha. taragucchon se bhinn pehali vaastavik niharika kaa ullekh farsi khagolvid abd al- rahamaan al-sufi ne apni "sthit taron kii pustak" main kiya tha. unhone andromeeda gallexi ke sthaan par "ek chota badal" dekha tha. unhone omricran velorum nakshatra punj ko "nebyalas star" yaa aspasht taare evam anya aspasht vastuon ko bruchi'ja cluster ke rup main chinhit kiya tha. 1054 main arab or chini khagolavidon dwaara craib nebyal SN 1054 kii rachana karne vaale supernova ko dekha gaya tha.
 agyaat kaarnon kii vajah se al-sufi orion nebyal ko pahchaanane main vifal rahe, jo ki raat ke akaash main kam se kam andromeda akaash ganga ke baraabar spasht dikhaai detaa he. 26 navambar 1610 ko nicholas-claude fabi de piresk ne ek doorabeen kaa upyog kar orion nebyal kaa avishkaar kiya. 1618 main jon beptist seast ne bhi is niharika kaa adhyayan kiya. haalanki, 1659 tak arthath isai higence jo apane ko niharikaon yaa is khagoleey dhundhalake kaa avishkaar karne vaale pehla vyakti maanate the, se pehle orion nebyal par koi vistrut adhyayan nahin hua.
 1715 main, edmund heley ne chah niharikaon kii ek suchi prakashit kii. jin philip di chasox dwaara 1746 main jari 20 kii ek suchi ke sankalan ke saath shatabdi ke dauraan, yah sankhya lagaataar badhati rahi. nicholas lui di lacale ne 1751-53 main kep of good hope se 42 niharikaon kii suchi banaai. jisamen se adhiktar pehle agyaat thim. iske baad charles mesier ne 1781 tak 103 niharikaon kii suchi banaai, haalanki unke aisa karne kii pramukh vajah thi - dhoomaketuon kii galat pahchaan se bachanaa.
 iske baad wiliam harchel or unki bahan caroline harchel kii koshishon se niharikaon kii sankhya main atyadhik ijapha hua. unki kaitalong of van thaujend new nebylai and cluster of stars 1786 main prakashit hui. ek hajaar kii duusari suchi 1789 main or 510 kii teesri tathaa antim suchi 1802 main prakashit kii gayi thi. apane adhiktar kaam ke dauraan, wiliam harchel ko yah yakeen tha ki ye niharikaayen sitaron ke aviksit samooh maatr the. haalanki, 1790 main, unhone aspashtataa se ghire ek taare kii khoj kii or yah nishkarsh nikaala ki yah adhik duuri par sthit samooh na hokar ek vaastavik ghatatope yaa niharika thi.
 1864 ke aarambh main, wiliam haggins ne lagbhag 70 niharikaon ke spectra yaa shrenee kii jaanch kii. unhone paaya ki unamem se lagbhag ek tihaai main gas ke samavesh kii vistrut shrenee thi. baaki main ek satat vistrut shrenee dikhaai di or inhen sitaron kaa ek samooh mana gaya. 1912 main, jab vesto sleefer ne yah darshaya ki merope taare ke aasapaas kii niharikaon kii shrenee pliyedas khule taraguchh se milti he, tab inamen ek teesraa varg joda gaya. is prakaar niharika taron ke prativimvit prakaash dwaara chamakta he.
 sleefer or edwin habal ne anek visarit niharikaon se inaki vistrut shreniyon ko ekatra karnaa jari rakhaa tathaa pataa lagaaya ki inamen se 29 utsarjan spectra dikhaate hai or 33 main taron ke prakaash kaa satat spectra tha. 1922 main habal ne ghoshnaa kii ki lagbhag sabhi niharikaayen sitaron se judy hai or unki roshni taron ke prakaash se aati he. unhone yah bhi pataa lagaaya ki utsarjan spectrum kii niharikaayen lagbhag hameshaa B1 yaa usase adhik garm se judy rahati hai, jabaki satat spectra yukt niharikaayen apekshaakrit thande taron ke saath prakat hoti hai. habal or henry noris rasel donon ne yah nishkarsh nikaala ki garm taron ke aasapaas kii niharikaayen kisi na kisi prakaar se parivartit ho rahi hai.
 anek niharikaon kaa gathan antartaarkeey madhyam main gas ke aapasi gurutvkarshan kii vajah se hota he. apane nijee bhaar ke tahat dravya ke sankuchit hone kii vajah se centre main anek vishaal sitaron kaa gathan ho saktaa he or unka parabangnee prakaash aasapaas kii gaison ko ynit kar prakaash tarangon par unhen drushtigochar banaata he. rojet nebyal or pelicon nebyal is prakaar kii niharikaon ke udhaaharan hai. HII kshetra ke naam se parichit is prakaar kii niharikaon kaa aakaar, gas ke vaastavik badalon ke aakaar par nirbhar hota he. yahi vah jagah hai jahaan sitaron kaa gathan hota he. isse gathit sitaron ko kabhi-kabhi ek yuva, deele cluster ke rup main janaa jaataa he.
 kuch niharikaon kaa gathan supernova main honevaale visphot arthath vishaal or alp-jivi taron ke ant ke parinamswarup hota he. supernova ke visphot se bikharanevaali saamagree urja dwaara ynit hoti he or isse nirmit ho sakanevaali thos vastu kaa gathan hota he. vrish taramandal kaa craib nebyal iskaa sravshreshth udhaaharan he. varsh 1054 main supernova kii ghatana darj kii gayi or ise or SN1054 ke rup main chihnit kiya gaya. visphot ke baad nirmit thos vastu craib nebyal ke kendra main sthit he or yah ek neutron star he.
 anya niharikaayen grahiy niharikaon kaa gathan kar sakati hai. prithvi ke suraj kii tarah, yah lo-maas arthath dravyaman taare ke jivan kaa antim charan he. 8-10 saura dravyaman vaale taare laal daanav taron ke rup main viksit hote hai or apane vaataavaran main spandan ke dauraan dhire-dhire apni baheri parat kho dete hai. jab ek tara paryaapt saamagree kho detaa he, tab iskaa taapmaana badhata he or isse utsarjit parabangnee vikiran iske dwaara aasapaas phenke hue nebyal ko ynit kar saktaa he. niharika main avshisht saamagree sahit 97% hydrogen or 3% helium he. is charan kaa mukhya lakshya santulan praapt karnaa he.
 niharikaon ko chaar pramukh samoohon main vargeekrut kiya gaya he. pehle gallexion or gol taragucchon ko bhinn prakaar kii niharikayen samajha jaataa tha. gallexion kii sarpakar sanrachana kii vyakhya ke liye sarpakar niharika kaa upyog kiya jaataa tha.
 is vargeekaran main badal jaisi sabhi gyaat sanrachanaayen shaamil nahin hai. jiskaa ek udahaaran herbig-haaro object he.
 sitaron ke paas kii visarit niharikaayen pratibimb niharika kaa udhaaharan hai.
 adhikansh niharikaon ko visarit niharikaayen kaha ja saktaa he, jiskaa arth he ki ve vistrut hai evam kisi sarvamaanya paribhaasha kii seemaa main nahin aatin. dikhaai dene yogya roshni main in niharikaon ko utsarjan niharika or pratibimb niharikaamein vibhajit kiya ja saktaa he, jo is baat par aadhaarit he ki hamen dikhaai denevaale prakaash kii rachana kis tarah hui he. utsarjan niharika main ayanit gas hota he, jo utsarjan kii dhundhlee rekha banaati hai. in utsarjan niharikaon ko aksar HII kshetra kaha jaataa he, "HII" shabd kaa upyog vyavsaayik khagol vigyaan main aksar ynit hydrogen ke liye kiya jaataa he. utsarjan niharikaon kii tulana main, pratibimb niharikaayen swayan paryaapt maatra main dikhaai dene yogya prakaash rekha nahin banaatin balki apane aasapaas ke sitaron ke prakaash ko pratibimbit karti hai.
 andhri yaa gahari niharikaayen visarit niharikaon jaisi hi hai, lekin unhen unke dwaara utsarjit yaa prativimbit prakaash dwaara nahin dekha ja saktaa. iske bajaae, unhen dur ke taron yaa utsarjan niharikaon ke saamne ke gahare badalon ke rup main dekha jaataa he.
 haalanki ye niharikaayen prakaash tarangon par alag-alag dikhaai detii hai, par ve sabhi infrared yaa avarakt taragon par utsarjan ke ujjwal strot hai. yah utsarjan mukhyatah niharikaon ke bheetar kii dhul se aata he.
 grahiy niharikaayen, ve niharikaayen hai, jo lo-moss ananthasparshi vishaal shakha sitaron ke safed boune main parivartit hone ke samay unase nikalanevaale gas yukt khol se gathit hoti hai. ye niharikaayen, dhundhale utsarjan yukt utsarjit niharikaayen hai jo sitaron ke nirmaan kshetron main pai janevaali utsarjan niharikaon jaisi hoti hai. takneekee taur par, yah HII kshetra hai kyonki adhiktar hydrogen ynit hoga. haalanki, grahon kii niharikaayen adhik ghani evam sitaron ke nirmaan kshetron main pai janevaali utsarjan niharikaon kii apeksha adhik thos hoti hai. grahon kii niharikaon ko yah naam isliye diya gaya he kyonki jin pehle khagolavidon ne in vastuon kaa adhyayan kiya tha unhone sochaa ki niharikaayen grahon kii tastariyon yaa discon jaisi dikhti hai, haalanki, ve grahon se sanvandhit nahin hai.
 ek proto planetary nebyal ek khagoleey vastu he, jo bhootpurv ananthasparshi vishaal shakha kii sthiti or uske baad ke grahon kii niharika ke charan ke bich ek taare ke twarit taarakeey kramik vikaas kaa alpakaaleen prakaran he. ek PPN shashakt avarakt vikiran utsarjit karata he or ek pratibimb niharika he. ek PPN ke grahon kii niharika banane ke vaastavik bindu ko kendriya sitare ke taapmaana dwaara paribhaashit kiya jaataa he.
 jab ek hai-moss sitara apane jivan ke ant tak pahunch jaataa he tab supernova ghatit hota he. jab sitare ke mul yaa centre main parmaanu sanlayan band ho jaataa he, tab sitara vighatit ho jaataa he. andar risnevaali gas yaa to pratikshipt hoti he athwa itani adhik garm ho jaati he ki yah kendra se baahar kii ore failati he tathaa taare ke visphot kaa kaaran banati he. gas kaa failaa hua khol, ek vishesh prakaar ke visarit niharika supernova avshesh kii rachana karata he. haalanki, supernova avshesh kaa adhikansh prakaash evam eks-re utsarjan ynit gas se utpann hota he, radioutsarjan kaa bada bhaag synchroton utsarjan ke naam se parichit non-thermal utsarjan he. yah utsarjan chumbakeey kshetron main dolyman ucch-veg yukt electronon se utpann hota he.
