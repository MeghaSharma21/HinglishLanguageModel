 moropant ek marathi kavi the. unhen 'mayur pandit' ke naam se bhi jaanate hai. marathi sahitya ke itihaas main prasiddh 'pandit kaviyon' kii shrunkhlaa main ve antim pandit kavi the. 
 unhone 108 ramayana likhane kaa sankalp kiya tha kintu 94-95 ramayana hi likh paye. inamen se kuch ramaayanon main keval ek yaa do yaa paanch pad hi hai. unhone ek aise ramayana kii rachana kii jisamen pa-varg ke sabhi vyanjan anupasthit hai, is kaaran ise 'niroshth ramayana' kehete hai. unke dwaara rachit kuch ramaayanon main keval ek hi chand prayukt hua he, jaise vibucopriy-ramayana or panch-chaamar ramayana.
 ve aryaa chand tathaa prithvi chand main siddhhast the. kekawali unki antim pramukh kriti he jiskaa samaapan unhone 1790 ke dashak main kiya.
