 
 yttrium ek rasinic tatv he. yah ek chaandi-jaise rang kii sankraman dhaatu he jiske gun lanthanide samooh se milte-julate hone ke kaaran ise bhi kabhi-kabhi usee durlabh mrudaa tatv samooh kaa bhaag mana jaataa he. prakruti main bhi yttrium hameshaa unheen ke saath khanijon main paaya jaataa he. yah prakruti main kabhi bhi shuddh rup main nahin miltaa. yttrium ko fosfar banane ke kaam main laayaa jaataa he, visheshkar cathod kiran nalika or prakaash utsarjak diod main dalane vaale laal fosfar ke liye. yttrium ke yogic maanav-sharir ke liye hanikaarak hote hai or unase shaareerik sampark se shwasan tantra ke rog ho jate hai.
 san 1787 main swidi rasayanashastri karl aranius ko sweden ke ek iyuttarabi naamak gaanv ke paas ek nayaa khanij mila jiskaa naam unhone gaanv ke upar 'itterbite' rakhaa. 1789 main finni rasayanshastri yohan gadolin ne is khanij namoonay se yttrium oxide praapt kiya or is rasinic yogic kaa naam 'itria' rakhaa gaya. 1828 main german rasayanshastri freederikh vohalar ne sarvapratham shuddh yttrium tatv praapt kiya.
