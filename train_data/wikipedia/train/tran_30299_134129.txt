 belapur durg maharashtra ke navi mumbai main sthit ek durg he. is durg kaa nirmaan zanjeera ke siddiyon ne karavaaya tha. kaalaantar main is par portugali samrajya kaa or fir maratha samrajya kaa aadhipaty ho gaya. 19veen shatabdi ke armbh main yahaan british raaj kaa adhikaar hua. british logon ke is kshetra par adhikaar karne or fir bombay presidensi ke vistaar hone se is durg kaa mahatva kam ho gaya. or yah prayog se baahar ho gaya.
 1560-70 ke dashak main jab portugaliyon se panvel creek ke nikat ke is kshetra kaa adhikaar siddiyon ne chin liya or is kile kaa nirmaan armbh kiya. 1682 main portugaliyon ne punah ise waapas adhikaar kiya or is kile kaa nirmaan kiya tha, to kile main 5 garh or deevaaren thim. yah kila 75 feet kii oonchai par sthit tha or yahaan 20 bandooken thim. maraathon ne 31 march, 1737 ko narayan joshi ke netritv main kile ko gher liya or 22 april 1937 ko kile par adhikaar kar liya. panipat yuddh ke baad, nanaaji ne belapur ke kile main sadashivbhau yaheen november 1778 main pakadaa tha evam punah belapur kila jeeta. 1779 main vadgaanv ke shilalekhon ke anusaar, angrejon ko harkar kile ko maraathon ko waapas dena padaa. 12 april 1780 ko angrez captain cambel ne belapur kila phir jeeta; kintu 1782 ko phir haar gayi or angrejon ko kile ko maraathon ko phir waapas dena tha. 23 june 1817 ko captain charles grey ne kile ko antatah jeeta or british samrajya main shaamil ho gaya.
 mumbai, thane, dombivli or kalyaan se nagar bas seva niyamit rup se basen chalaata he. inase belapur bas stop par utar kar kile ke udyaan ke nikatavarti burj tak kaa paidal rasta he. yaham dakshin ore ek tile tak jane vbali pakki sadak bhi he, jahaan se kile main jane hetu seedhiyaan bhi hai. yahi maarg aage retibandar tak jaataa he.
