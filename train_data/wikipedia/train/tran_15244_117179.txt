 prasad harichandan, prasad kumaar harichandan ke rup se jane jate he ek oriya raajnneetijna or odisha pradesh congress samiti ke maujuuda adhyaksh hai.
 unka janm 12 may 1964 odisha main, puri ke nankera main hua tha. vah gyarahavin, barahavin or chaudaavin odisha vidhaan sabha ke liye chune gaye the. 1999 main rajya ke sabase kam umr ke mantri, banaae gaye the or ghar, vigyaan, praudyogiki or samskruti vibhaagon kaa aayojan kiya. 2009-2014 main 14 veen vidhansabha ke congress vidhayak dal ke mukhya sachetak the.
