 sanket yaa ishaara gaiyr-maukhik sanchaar yaa gaiyr-mukhar sanchaar kaa ek rup he jisamen shaareerik kriyaayein vishesh sandeshon kaa sanchaar karti hai, yaa to samvaad ke saath yaa samvaad kii jagah. ishaare main haathon, chehre yaa sharir ke anya hisson kii chaal shaamil hai. ishaare vyaktiyon ko vibhinn bhaawanaaon or vichaaron ko vyakt karne kii anumati dete hai jisamen avmaananaa or shatruta se lekar anumodan or sneh shaamil he. aisa aksar bolte samay shabdon ke alaava shaareerik bhashaa ke saath hota he.
