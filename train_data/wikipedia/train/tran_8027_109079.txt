 irkutsk oblast rus ke siberia kshetra ke dakshinpurvi bhaag main sthit rus kaa ek sangheey khand he jo us desh kii shashan pranali main oblast kaa darja rakhata he. yah angaara, lenaa or niuanaaya tunguska nadiyon kaa jalsambhar kshetra he. isaki rajdhani irkutsk shahar he or is praant kii aabaadi 2010 kii janaganana main 24,28,750 anumaanit kii gai thi. irkutsk oblast kaa kshetrafal 7,67,900 varg kimi he yani lagbhag pakistan ke kshetrafal ke baraabar. irkutsk oblast kii sthapana soviet sangh ke jamaane main 26 sitambar 1937 ko hui thi.
