 nimbu-vansh ek padap vansh he, jo rutacy kul ke pushpiya padpon kaa vansh he. inaki utpatti duniya ke ushnkaribandheey or uposhna dakshin purvi kshetron main hui he.
 is vansh kii vargiki or krambaddhata jatil he or isaki prakritik prajatiyon kii sateek sankhya aspasht he, kyonki kai gyaat prajatiyaan kruntakeey rup se viksit sankar prajati hai or is baat ke anuvanshik sakshya hai ki kuch junglee, vishuddh-prajnit vastav me sankar, mul kii hai.
 nimbu-vansh ke krishi yogya fal multah chaar paitruk prajatiyon se sambandhit hai. vanijyik rup se krishi yogya prakritik or mul sankar prajatiyon me mahatvapurn fal hai, santara, chakotra, nibu, naarangi or kinnu.
