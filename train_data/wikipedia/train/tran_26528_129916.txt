 vidyut shakti ke sampreshan or vitaran ke sandarbh main, ankiy rakshi riley us tantra ko kehete hai jo vidyut dosha kaa pataa karne ke liye software-aadhaarit kalan vidhi tathaa ankiy tantra kaa upyog karata he. atah inhen microprocesar prakaar ke rakshi riley athwa neumerical riley bhi kehete hai. ye vidyut-chumbakeey rakshi riley ke sthaan par sidhe prayukt kiye jate hai. inaki vishesh baat yah he ki software aadhaarit hone ke kaaran ek hi ankiy rakshi riley dwaara anekon rakshi-kaary karaaye ja sakate hai. iske atirikt, inkaa upyog metering, sanchaar, swa-pareekshan aadi ke liye kiya ja saktaa he.
