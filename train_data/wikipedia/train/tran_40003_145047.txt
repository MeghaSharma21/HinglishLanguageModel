 abdurrahim khan-e-kaanaa mughal badshaah akbar ke navratnon main se ek hindi ke suprasiddh kavi the. rahim kaa makbara dilli ke nizamuddin purv main mathura road par sthit he. is makbare kaa nirmaan rahim kii patni dwaara karavaaya gaya tha jiskaa nirmaan kaary san 1598 i main purna hua. san 1627 main agra main mrityu ke baad rahim ko is makbare main bani kabr main dafnaaya gaya tha.
 rahim kaa makbara mughal badshaah humayun ke makbare or sufi sant hazrat nizamuddin kii dargah ke najadik grand trunk road par banwaaya gaya tha. ye makbara mughal sthapaty kaa ek utkrisht namoona tha.lekin 1753 main dilli main safadarajang ke makbare ke nirmaan ke liye is makbare kii khubasurat or nakkashidaar sangemarmar or balua pathar kii tiles ko nikaal liya gaya jiske baad ye makbara ujaad khandahar main tabdeel ho gaya.
 sadiyon tak badahaali jhelane ke baad inter globe foundation or aagaa khan saanskrithik trust ke sanyukt prayaas se vartmaan main is aitihaasik imaarat ke sangrakshan kaa kaary chal rahaa he.
