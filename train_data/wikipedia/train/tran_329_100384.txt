 hafoos or kannada main aapus, aam kii ek kism he jise mithaas, sugandh or swaad ke maamle main aksar aamon kii sabase acchhee kismon main se ek mana jaataa he. europea bhashaaon main iskaa naam alphonso, afonso di albukark ke sammaan main rakhaa gaya he. 
 pakane ke baad ise lagbhag ek saptaah tak rakhaa ja saktaa he or iskaa yah gun iskaa niryaat sugam banaata he. keemat ke maamle main yah aam bharat ke sabase mahange aamon main se ek he or ise mukhyat: pashchim bharat main hi ugaaya jaataa he. iskaa mausam april se may ke madhya hota he or prayek aam kaa vajan 150 gram se 300 gram ke madhya hota he.
 hafoos aamon kii sabase behtareen kism maharashtra ke konkan ilaake main sthit sindhudurg jile kii tehsil devgarh main ugaayi jaati he, saath hi sabase achche aam sagar tat se 20 kilometre andar kii ore sthit jameen par hi ugte hai. iske alaava maharashtra kaa ratnagiri jila, gujarat ke dakshini jile valsad or navsari bhi hafoos kii paidaavaar ke liye prasiddh hai. 
 bihar ke kuch kshetron main bhi hapus kii paidaavaar shuru kii gayi he.
