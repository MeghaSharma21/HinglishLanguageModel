 kazakhstan or rus ke sambandh, kazakhstan or rus ke bich dwipaksheeya videshi sambandhon ko sanderbhit karata he. masco main kazakhstan kaa duutavas he, saint petersburg, austrakhan or omsk main vaanijya duutavas general sthit he. rus kaa duutavas astana main he, almati or uralsk main rus kaa vaanijya duutavas he.janvari 2005 ko rus ke raashtrapati vladimir putin or kazakh raashtrapati nursultan najarabaev ne seemaa ke aadhikarik nakshe ke samjhaute ke anumodan par hastakshar kiye. 23 may 2009 ko donon deshon ne kazakhstan ke atyrau or rus ke austrakhan praanton ke bich apana pehla seemaa 7,591 kimi marker rakhaa. yah seemaa duniya main sabase lambi sthal seemaa he. simakan puraa hone main 10 se 15 saal tak kaa samay lagane kii ummid he.
