 kavi vah he jo bhaavon ko rasabhishikt abhivyakti detaa he or saamaanya athwa spasht ke pare gahan yatharth kaa varnan karata he. isiliye vaidic kaal main rishay: mantradrushtaar: kavay: krantadarshin: arthath rishi ko mantradrushtaa or kavi ko krantadarshi kaha gaya he."jahaan na pahunche ravi, vahaan pahunche kavi" is lokokti ko ek dohe ke madhyam se abhivyakti di gayi he:"jahaan na pahunche ravi vahaan, kavi pahunche tatkaal. din main kavi kaa kaam kya, nishi main kare kamaal.." 
 shaayari likhane vaale kavi ko shaayar kaha jaataa he. shaayari bhartiya upmahadweep main prachlit ek kavita kaa rup hai jisamen hindi-urdu bhashaaon main kavitaayen likhi jaati hai.
