 kendriya khanan evam eendhan anusandhan santhaan dhanbad, jharkhand main sthit he. yah bharat kii vaigyanik evam audyogic anusandhan parishad kii ek angeebhoot prayogshaala he. yah khanan se khapat tak arthath sampoorn koyala urja shrinkhalaa ko anusandhan va vikaas se sambandhit nivesh uplabdh karaane ke liye samarpit he.
 siefarai tathaa siemarai ke ekikaran ke baad siimer kaa gathan hua. ranchi, bilaspur, nagpur va rudki main is santhaan ke unit hai.
 kendriya eendhan anusandhan santhaan kii sthapana san 1946 main hui. yah ek antararashtriya str par khyatipraapt koyala anusandhan va vikaas prayogshaala thi. ise sansaadhan gunvattta moolyankan, koyala vinirmaan, carbonikaran, pradahan tathaa nimna gunvattta vaale koyale ke gasikaran, paryaavaran prabandhan tathaa udansheel raakh ke prayog ke kshetra main visheshgyata praapt thi. iske atirikt ise koyala, sinthetic eendhan evam rasayanon par moulik anusandhan karne kaa bhi anubhav praapt tha. 
 kendriya khanan anusandhan kendra kii sthapana 1956 main hui thi. 1994 main iskaa naam kendriya khanan anusandhan santhaan kar diya gaya. yah prayogshaala koyala evam khanij udyogon ko puri suraksha evam mittavyayita ke saath lakshyabadhd utpaadan praapt karne ke liye anusandhan va vikaas samarthan, takneekee seva, praudyogiki utkraman main sahaayta, praudyogiki anukulan aadi uplabdh karaane vaala desh kaa ek agrani santhaan tha.
