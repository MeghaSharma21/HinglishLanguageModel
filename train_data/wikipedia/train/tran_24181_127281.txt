 chanyu madhya asia or china main shashan karne vaali bahut-si kanaabadosh jatiyon ke samrajyon ke sarvocch shaasha kii oopaadhi hua karti thi. is oopaadhi kaa istemaal china ke jhou rajvansh kaal se armbh hokar lagbhag 800 saal tak chalaa. chin rajvansh or haan rajvansh ke samakaleen shiyongnu logon ke luanadi rajpariwaar ne iskaa bahut prayog kiya or us jati main iskaa prayog karne vaala sarvapratham shaasha tuman tha. chanyu kaa prayog samay ke saath-saath kam arth rakhane laga or chote-mote sardaar bhi iskaa prayog karne lage. 402 isvi ke baad isaki bajaae samraaton ne kagaan shabd kaa prayog karnaa shuru kar diya.
